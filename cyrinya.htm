Neopets Games::

First on our list is the FEATURED GAME! It changes everyday, and it's always double the regular amount of np given so if you're lucky, it could be your fave game and you can get a lot of np!
1000np x 3 plays =3000np

Next we play ZURROBALL. You think it's tough? Psh! It's really quite easy, and it pays well too! It's very simple to play. Choose any ball (I recommend the energy ball) and when you start clicking on the ball, make sure you click it very close to the floor but make sure it doesn't fall. This way, every time you hear a clicking sound, you get a "grounder" trick which is worth 10pts. Keep it up and you'll reach 200pts which is equivalent to 1k np. Just remember: practice, practice, practice!
1000np x 3 plays=3000np

Alright, now we play FASHION FEVER. Super easy 900np in a day. Just load it, and end game as soon as you start. Presto! 300np.
300np x 3 plays=900np

After, we play BOUNCY SUPREME. Very easy, just requires a lot of practice! Bounce carefully, but don't waste your time! This game requires some estimation... Play until 1000pts to get 1k np!
1000np x 3 plays=3000np

Well, next, we play SMUG BUG SMITE which is a very simple. All you have to do is click on the bug as soon as it pops up.
(around)500np x 3 plays=1500np

Afterwards, we play KASS BASHER, where you whack a kass. :) The key is to hold and release at the right time for max distance.
(around) 1000np x 3 plays=3000np

Next up is HASEE BOUNCE.This one's really easy, just make sure that you do lots of combos, don't skip out on special doughnutfruits and avoid yucky stuff. If you're good, you can reach 250-300pts.
(around)1000np x 3 plays=3000np

WARF RESCUE TEAM is next on our list. It's kinda easy, full of estimation. The more combos you make, the better! Also try to avoid all sorts of things from the tree. If you're good, you can make about 300-400pts...
(around) 900np x 3 plays=2700np

TURMAC ROLL... This is a very simple and easy game to play. Keep an eye out for the berries which give out lots of points, while dodging things like tree stumps and tar. Reaching 500pts is really easy which makes you a quick 1k!
(around) 1000np x 3 plays= 3000np

Next up is: EXTREME POTATO COUNTER! It's a little difficult, but I like playing this game with my friend/s, so I can get a second opinion on the potatoes. If you're alone (or don't have any friends) you must have a keen eye, and don't be distracted by anything, focus on the game and don't count stones and carrots!
700np x 3 plays=2100np

EVIL FUZZLES FROM BEYOND THE STARS! Keep shooting those fuzzles (: Try and maximize the bonus multipliers. Reach 410 points for 1000np
(around)1000np x 3 plays=3000np

Now we have WINGO BALL. It's all about reaching the other side, watching out for the red-hatted gnomes and timing. =)
(about)600np x 3 plays=1800np

Last, but not the least: DICE ESCAPE! Using a guide, you can get up to 1000 points playing this very easy game.
1000np x 3 plays=3000np
