Resource-Use Rules

Before exploring the resources that Bedazzled has to offer, please read and follow these simple rules. After you make note of all the rules, use the links to the right to navigate through the resources.

The Rules

Please give proper credit to Bedazzled for every resource that you use. See below for how to credit correctly.
You may edit the resources. You can recolor them to match your preferences, add text to bases, and so on.
Do not claim the resources as your own, or redistribute them on your own site.

Crediting: Do's and Don'ts

DON'TS:
Don't generalize. Please don't simply write: Some resources by Bedazzled.
Don't forget to include a link. If you are choosing not to put a link in your credits, don't forget to put a link on the resource itself.

DO'S:
Do be specific. Name the specific resources you used, like this: Bullets, envelope, and background by Bedazzled.
Do link the resources. I have coded an example below of how to properly link a resource back to Bedazzled.
