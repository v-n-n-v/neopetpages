What is a Mystery Capsule?
A Mystery Capsule is a NeoCash Item which when opened, grants you a random set of NC Item(s) that are available for purchase at the NC Mall (also known as buyables). Reading the description of the Mystery Capsule will give you an overview of what item(s) you can receive from the particular Mystery Capsule.

On top of that, when you open a Mystery Capsule, you may stand a chance to get a Limited Edition Item! Refer to the screenshot to the right for a sample. However, please take note that you are not always guarenteed a Limited Edition item for every Mystery Capsule you open. Limited Edition Items are awarded randomly.

Some good terminologies to note are as follows:
Cap: Short for Capsule | LE: Limited Edition | Buyable: Item that can be bought in the NC Mall | FF: Future Fashion | RR: Rerelease

*NOTE: Future Fashions; access is granted on a monthly basis when a user has spent over 10,000NC the previous month.
*NOTE: Rerelease; retired items which are released once again into the public.
*IMPORTANT: Different Mystery Capsules will reward different Limited Edition Item(s). Usually, there are more than 1 possible Limited Edition Item per Mystery Capsule!

Types of Mystery Capsules
There are many different types of Mystery Capsules and this guide will break down the categories below:
A: Gift Box Mystery Capsules
Gift Box Mystery Capsules as the name suggests mainly awards Gift Boxes when opened! They retail at 200NC a Capsule and will grant you 1, 2 or if you are really lucky... 4 Basic Giftboxes and 1 Limited Edition Designed Gift Box! That's a total of 5 Boxes maximum!

On top of getting Gift Boxes, you will be able to get NC Mall Buyables up to 200NC (or more if you are lucky!). Typically speaking from general NC Chat consensus, GB Caps will grant you 1-2 Gift Boxes. Odd of getting 4/5 Boxes are pretty slim. Nonetheless, it's a fun way of trying out your luck!

*RANDOM FACT: Gift Box Mystery Capsules used to give out 3 Basic Boxes back in the past but it seems like it's very rarely (or no longer) been given out lately.

» Right Screenshot:
Summer Popsicle Gift Box Capsule | 4 Basic Gift Box + 2 Random Buyables + Bonus LE(Summer Popsicle Gift Box)
B: Seasonal Themed Mystery Capsules
These Themed Capsules are released yearly with new designs. They are released on special days/seasons/events like Spring, Valentines, Birthday, Holiday etc. They retail for 150NC a Capsule and will grant you with 150NC worth of buyables from their respective Themed Shop.

On top of that, if you are lucky, you may receive a RR(Rerelease) Item and a LE(Limited Edition) Item! So basically when opening a Seasonal Capsule, you will get buyables of up to 150NC and if you are lucky, 1 RR Item and 1 LE Item! You will only get a RR Item if you get the LE in the same Capsule.

*IMPORTANT: Each "Seasonal" Mystery Capsule will have a specific pool of Retired Items it can rerelease and cannot rerelease just any Retired Item. You may want to look up on your Capsule in this guide to see what item(s) it can specifically give out to be sure.

» Right Screenshot:
Summer at the Beach Mystery Capsule | 2 Random Buyables + RR Item(Dripping Ice Cream Cone) + Bonus LE(Wavy Long Wig)
C: Normal Mystery Capsules
These Mystery Capsules are basic generic Mystery Capsules that do not fall in the category of either Gift Box Mystery Capsules or Seasonal Mystery Capsules. These Capsules will usually award you with NC Mall items worth up to 150NC (or more if you are lucky!) and a chance at a Limited Edition Item.

These capsules do not have a pool of rereleased items and cannot award gift boxes.
