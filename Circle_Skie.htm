Common Problems

Text areas in the wrong place
1. If you are trying to put a blog on a Pet Lookup (not a Pet Page), the text areas will always be wrong because position codes are disabled..
2. If you are trying to put a blog anywhere else and the text areas are wrong, your top and left numbers are incorrect. To fix this, focus on one text area at a time. Determine which direction you need to move the text area, then follow this guide.
Left: subtract from the left number
Right: add to the left number
Up: subtract from the top number
Down: add to the top number
Play around with the top and left numbers until the text area is in the right spot.

Blog repeats/resized/too small
1. If you uploaded the image to Photobucket, upload it somewhere else instead (http://www.imagecave.com). Photobucket resizes large images.
2. You might have the height and/or width wrong in the code. To find the correct height, right click on the image and choose Properties. Beside Dimensions is the width x height.

Blog background doesn't show up, but text shows up
1. Make sure you are using the correct address. An image's address looks like this:
//www.neopets.com/image.gif
It should not look like these: [img]//www.neopets.com/image.gif[/img]
img s r c="//www.neopets.com/image.gif
2. If you've upload the image to R i p w a y, you need to use a different host. R i p w a y is blocked on Neopets.
3. Try putting your image's address into the address bar and pressing "Go". If you don't see the image or get an error, you have the wrong address. If you do see the image, but there is other text or images on the page, right click on the image and choose Properties to get the real address.

Horizontal scrollbar at the bottom, or no scrollbar
1. If the scroll bar appears at the bottom, you have a long word without spaces or an image in your div that is too big. Add spaces to the word or resize the image and the scroll bar at the bottom will disappear.
2. A scroll bar will only appear if there's enough text in the blog for it to need to scroll. If you only have a few words ("TEXT HERE") you won't see the scroll bar.
