THIS! is you. Your bestest friend HAPPY! You control him by using the WASD keys (Keys are subject to change with each platform). W for jumping, A for moving to the left, S to crouch, and D to walk to the right!

Simple huh? Of course it is! Oh look how cute you are smiling. Also your friend here, Smile, will provide you emotional support! As well as find things for you occasionally.
Sometimes you'll come across angry petpets, neopets, and bosses, they'll try and attack you- Some even have Ranged attacks. You'll need to charge up your Smiley Attack with the right mouse button. You can level it up as you play to make your attacks more potent! Bigger enemies will take more attacks to make Smiley. You can't use this attack on 'Quest Saddy's' they require you to finish their quest to become happy, you can shield smaller attacks- but you'll have to use your smarts to dodge bigger ones. Don't worry! If you're hurt you can sip a healing potion.
This is the shopkeeper! You can find them between levels and before boss battles. You can use the neopoints you've gained from completing Saddy quests and battling. You can also level up your moves here! Those don't cost neopoints though, they cost special Smiley Face Points! Which you gain from completing objectives.
The last thing you need to know is there's all sorts of puzzles and enemies you have to defeat with special powers! To gain these powers you can buy and find Morphing potions that give you a certain amount of times you can use them. To Sip the morphing potion press space on the equipped! To use the special power associated with them you press the right mouse button. There's all sorts of items and special powers you can gain! But remember- have fun! And make sure you use these powers wisely ;)! There's also rare power-ups you can get from making special petpets smiley, completing very hard quests, or finding treasure chests, these often come with the names 'Droplet of rainbow paint' which will give you a temporary power-up of some kind as soon as you touch it! If you get one of these special power-ups you'll still be in whatever form you were in before- so it won't waste morphing potion charges.

☻ ITEMS TO LOOK OUT FOR! ☻


BLUE UNI MORPHING POTION: Adds magic horn that magically destroys small obstacles. Can also be used as a ranged weapon and gives you the LVL 1 feather fall effect. This is a level one item and can only be used three times per level.

RED KOUGRA MORPHING POTION: Adds a +2 to your jump! Making you jump higher by pouncing, it also adds the ability to slash at medium plant obstacles to break them open! This is a level one item, and can only be used three times per level.

PURPLE SHOYRU MORPHING POTION: Gives you a +3 to your feather fall, as well as a windy weapon to blow away ranged weapons with your wings! When unable to reach a higher obstacle, you can use your special flying power to fly up with 2 extra double jumps! This is a level 2 item and can only be used twice per level.

GREEN HISSI MORPHING POTION: This adds a special slither power to get through small holes where treasure hides! It also gives you a slashing option to break medium sized rock obstacles, and your special hissi abilities make it so when you stay still- Saddy enemies will not attack you because you naturally blend in with the ground! However it removes -1 from your jump and speed. This is a level 4 item and can only be used twice per level.

DROPLET OF STEALTH: A small rare item found occasionally before stealth missions! The droplet gives you the +10 to your stealth for ~20 seconds! Making it extremely worth it- but be careful once it rubs off because it will immediately remove the +10! and for 5 seconds add a -1 to stealth!

DROPLET OF PINK: This gives you a temporary +10 to your base attack- meaning your smiley attack will be extra potent against bosses and harder to beat saddys. This often shows up in the middle of boss battles or in mystery boxes! It's pretty rare!

DROPLET OF RAINBOW: One of the rarest droplets in game, this special drop gives you a +30 to your smiley attack, as well as making you temporarily invincible to all attacks. This item is only able to be found by finding certain mystery boxes. The effect lasts the longest- 45 seconds. Finding this right next to boss's is extremely rare- but useful if it happens!

DROPLET OF GREY: This is a droplet no one wants to come across, they can be accidentally consumed when falling to the grey drops- and can be ran into when opening mystery chests. When consumed it gives you a -10 to your smiley attack, making Happy sad makes any petpet that was Smiley back to a saddy. Be careful!
