welcome

❥ What are NeoBadges?
NeoBadges are little images you can award yourself and your pet to show off your accomplishments on neo! You can collect different ones for different categories. Did you zap your pet robot? Take the "Zapped to Perfection" badge! Did you get goldbanned in the Beauty Contest? Take the "BC Champ" badge. Collect as many as you can!

❥ Where can I put my badges?
I have seen people put them on individual pet pages in a section labeled "Badges" or "Awards. I personally use them on my character portfolio.

❥ Can I use them for other purposes?
These are essentially like adoptables, so if you want to use them for your application or something else, that is okay! Please remember you may not remove credit! Always link back to this page, or my userlookup.

❥ Can i suggest new ones for you to make?
You can of course send me suggestions and feedback for badges to make! These aren't requests, so I can't guarantee that they will be made though! Sorry! I will do the best I can though.

❥ How do I resize the image?
All of the badges are saved and uploaded as a 300px by 300px image. In the coding, you can change width="PIXELS", to any size below 300 to fit the coding or aesthetic of your page. The default number in the coding, and the size of the badges on this page is 200.
