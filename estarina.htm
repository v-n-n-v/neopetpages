Welcome to Sugoi! A semi-blind button request site run by me; Reena; who strives to provide you with a high quality custom made button. As per the word semi is implied, this request site is for someone willing to put almost 100% of their trust in the button maker to make them a button to their liking. Case in point, do you trust me to make you a button that fits your preferences? Remember, this is a semi-blind button request site. If you wanted a costumed one, please request your buttons from the other amazing sites listed in my affiliates. Thank you for your understanding.
