Foster Felines

Welcome to Foster Felines! We are a foster Adoption Agency providing Aishas, Kougras and Wockies a chance at finding their forever homes. Our agency fosters multiple felines that have been abandoned or stuck in the pound. Our mission is to temporarily house them, feed them and zap or paint them a nice colour to increase their chances at finding a permanent family and home. To make things even more exciting, occasionally our agency has feline customs and fountain dips that are available for adoption.

The founder of Foster Felines, Stasia, originally created Foster Felines as a Kougra adoption agency and came up with the idea after having stray kitties come to her home seeking the same shelter, kindness and love our fosters are. Exactly one year later, in October of 2023, Foster Felines opened its doors to Aishas and Wockies. While it's known you can't always foster and house a handful of kitties in real life, our agency became aware you certainly can with the feline species of Neopia!

We are rapidly growing and our fosters are being adopted as quickly as they are entered into our agency. If you have a love for felines and would like to help, consider joining our agency team! We truly appreciate every single amount of your support.

Applying

PLEASE READ:
1) Filling out the application form below is more than sufficient when applying for one of our felines, please no petpage applications

2) We are NOT a first come first serve agency, fosters will be re-homed
only when a suitable owner is found

3) Our agency mission is to send our fosters into permanent loving homes, thus applying with the intention of re-trading is greatly frowned upon

4) Trading a feline you've adopted from our agency will result in you being denied any future adoptions and dream wishes

5) If you are chosen to adopt please accept your transfer ASAP as agency members can have multiple transfers to send out each day

6) If you stop feeling attached to your new feline we are more than happy to foster them back into our agency


Note: Where you will send your applications varies as the agency has multiple foster pawrents. Please click on and send apps to the specified person under the felines name

If you are interested in adopting one of our felines, please read through and fill out our application form. All applications should be titled "Application: PETNAME"
