Hey! And welcome to HUNGRY!, a little site dedicated to housing my made-up fast food painted pets.

I've always loved food themed things, but especially Neopets! But why do Chias get all the food love? I thought it would be fun to create a brush that lends itself to a wide variety of savory treats (because the sweets market is bloated!). Taking some inspiration from popular, real-world fast food chains, each pet takes on a unique flavour! Be sure to eat look at them before they cool off!

Questions for the Chef

Where'd the idea for HUNGRY! pets come from?
I've always really liked food themed things, but especially Neopets! There are definitely some options out there, but they are either something sweet or a Chia haha! So I thought it would be fun to make some savory pets! I also figured there'd be a lot of fun things to reference in the fast food world!

What program do you use to make them
I draw all of them in procreate! If I ~really~ had the time, I would probably make them in illustrator after the initial drawing in procreate, buuuuut, until I have more time, they will just live as raster images!

How long do they take to make?
Most take no more than a couple hours! Simpler ones can be done in about 30 minutes. This is just a fun project for myself (and hopefully for others to enjoy!), so I kind of just go for it!

They are so cute!! Can I display them on my _______?
Sure thing!! I'd love to see all these HUNGRY! pets living throughout Neopia! If you do happen to put it on your Userlookup, petpage, or whatever, please just be sure to credit back to this page!

Can I request a new color or custom art?
Feel free to send it, but other than taking some user suggestions for new food/pet combos, I don't have much time to do anything else! But it doesn't hurt to reach out!

Would it be ok if I drew some fan art of your designs?
I would LOVVVVE to see any art you do of any of these cuties! Please be sure to neomail me if you post it somewhere! Might even feature it on this page! :)
