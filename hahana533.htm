Welcome to hahana's Art Directory!

Hello! Welcome to hahana's petpage/art directory! My name is Caroline (just call me Cah) and I'll be your host for today ♥

I'm no artist myself but my sweet Hannah (hahana533) here is, and she and I would both love for you (yeah, you) to share with us (and the world) your precious drawings, sketches, paintings, graphics and/or any forms of art c:

I had never seen an art directory going around before making this one so here it is! I'm sure many people will enjoy seeing each others art and I would be very glad to help advertise your page! There is no need for you to link me back in order to be listed (although if you want to I'll be very thankful) ♥ I don't care how many art pieces you have up on your page, if you update it regularly or not, if you have just started or do art for a leaving, or if you won't do trades/requests - everyone is welcomed to list here!
