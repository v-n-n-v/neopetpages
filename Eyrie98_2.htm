Welcome to Pascal ♔

Welcome to Pascal, a premade layout site hosted by Grace. I mostly offer CSS layouts for you to edit to your heart's content, though you may find one or two graphic layouts as well. Drag and drop the layouts to the address bar for a bigger view. Hover over the plus at the bottom right corner of each layout to find out more information about it. If you have any questions about how to use these layouts, feel free to neomail me and I'll respond as soon as possible.
