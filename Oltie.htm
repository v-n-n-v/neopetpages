Add a Pet

It's easy to add a pet and there are only TWO rules!

Rules:
1. Your plush must be a permanent pet.
2. Please remove your pet if you trade or adopt out.

Add a pet form:
Name: pet name here
Species: pet type here
Username: username here
Owner: your name here
Bio: short fun fact/bio here*

* Bio is optional.

And you're done! Send the form here: neomail Ash.

Example Form:
Name: Oltie
Species: Lenny
Username: sailhs
Owner: Ash
Bio: He is very squishy!



Common Questions

Q: How do I navigate the page?
A: See the small, black boxes on the side? Click them!

Q: Can I add my MSP Poogle?
A: Yes! They are listed under "MSP"!

Q: What if I trade my pet?
A: No worries, just send me their name and I'll remove them.

Q: Is this for converted or UC Plushies?
A: Both, this page is for ALL Plushie pets!

Meet the Host
welcome home

Your gracious host is the magnificent plushie bird who goes by the name Oltie. I had my eye on him for years before actually trading for him. The first time he was offered to be, I rejected. Well, I immediately regretted that. When I got offered him for a second time, I decided to accept.

This page used to be called Handsewn Plushies and I've recently decided to reopen it in 2022 to celebrate Oltie coming home. I'm a bit of a Plushie collector myself (really an UC Plushie fan, sadly). You'll find my 3 Plushies listed throughout the directory.

Anyway, enjoy the directory and don't forget to add your own Plushie pets on your way out!
