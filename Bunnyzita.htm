About Cybunnies

About

Cybunnies are native of the Terror Mountain, the coldest region of Neopia. For that reason they are very used to the cold, and love playing in the snow. Their thick fur coats keep them warm, it's also believed that the basic white color of their fur helped them camouflage in the snow. Cybunnies are 32cm on average, making them one of the smallest species.

Cybunnies are a Limited Edition pet, meaning there are only a certain number available to create. They are usually obtainable on their day (April 27). Another way to have on is through the Lab Ray or via Morphing Potions. Despite this predicament, cybunnies have remained one of the most loved species.

Unconverted Cybunnies

As explained above, after the 2007 revamp some colors of pets were given the option to convert or not. The chosen colors were mostly colors where the pet has a unique pose. They are quite rare since you couldn't make one past the conversion date, nobody was warned previously, and the colors themselves were rare/expensive, so you get the idea. In time the community created a pet trading system amongst themselves, making it possible for people the achieve a UC pet, however there is a very high demand. Below are the UCs Cybunnies:

    Notable Cybunnies


    Cylara

    Cylara was introduced in chapter two of The Return of Dr. Sloth. She was simply enjoying a vacation in the Space Station with her family, when she was whisked away by Gorix, and her family kidnapped by Commander Garoo! After helping defeat Sloth, she was offered a full-time position in the resistance.

    She also features in the games Moon Rock Rampage, Escape to Kreludor and Time Tunnel, though the adventures depicted there seem to have little connection to her actual achievements.



    Lulu


    Lulu is the cousin of AAA and Abigail. During the third Daily Dare event, she decided to set up a store in the NC Mall to sell game-themed items. Unlike her cousins, Lulu isn't a big fan of games. Instead, she loves collecting NC Mall items - to the point where she will sometimes break into things in order to gain them.




    Princess Lunara

    Princess Lunara is the daughter of the Emperor of Shenkuu, and future Empress. She is beautiful and well-respected, but sometimes finds the life of a princess to be lonely and dull, despite the best efforts of her loyal petpet Kazeriu, named Umbra.

    One day her younger sister, Princess Terrana, suggested a game of hide and seek. But instead, Lunara pretended to be kidnapped, which led the Emperor to start a search for her. She was later found and the truth was revealed. Later, Lunara discovered an ancient magical lapis amulet, with the ability to let her listen in on far away conversations.



    Sasha


    Sasha is known in Altadorian legend for her optimism and undefeatable spirit, as well as her talents for music and dance. Known as the Dancer, she is one of Altador's twelve protectors and has a statue in the Hall of Heroes.

    In the Darkest Faerie game, her statue had the inscription, "Muse of music; mistress of the stage. Let your entertainment fill us with joy."
