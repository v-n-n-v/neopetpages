There are two ways to request a Euphoric button: a custom or a premade base request. If you would like to request a button, please make sure to read the rules below and fill out the correct form for the type of request you're making.

rules

  • To request a button, fill out the correct form for the type of request you're interested in. The subject of your NM should be Euphoric Request. Only request when requests are actually open!
  • Limit one button per request per person.
  • Credit for my buttons is required.
  • I will not make a button for another button request site, and I will not make a button if you already have 10 or more buttons that already say the same thing.
  • The site you are requesting your button for must already have content. I do not make buttons for empty or future sites.
  • I reserve the right to reject your form if you don't follow the rules, your site has stolen content, or your request isn't appropriate for Neo.
  • You will be contacted when I receive your request and when your request is finished. Also, I will contact you if your request is denied and explain why it was denied.
  • Only apply with one of the forms provided, and please use the correct form for the type of request you're making. If you have any questions regarding either form, feel free to contact me.
  • If you read the rules, add "bibliophile" somewhere in your form.

For custom requests:
  • You must choose an image for your button, but I reserve the right to use a similar image in its place. All images must be from Neopets. You are only required to provide one image, but options are very welcome.

For premade base requests:
  • Premade base buttons are first come first serve. Please understand that the base you're interested in might have already been claimed.
  • Please be sure to clearly describe the base you're requesting (color, imagery, etc.) so there's no confusion. Please don't refer to them by their order in the available bases, as the number will shift regularly.
  • Please consider whether the text you'd like on the button will fit before you submit your request. Not all premade bases will be able to accommodate long text.
  • For certain premades I will have an idea for the text I'd like to have on it (short vs. long, single word vs. many, etc.). For this reason, I reserve the right to deny any request if I think the requested text won't complement the premade base.

euphoric: a retrospective


Euphoric began more than a decade ago (a fact that wounds me to admit). In 2012 I was in my junior year of high school, deeply in the closet and desperate for both support and a creative outlet. The site community on Neopets was surprisingly the perfect place for both, as I found a community of wonderful, supportive friends and discovered my enduring love of making buttons.

Because Euphoric was created at an emotional and tumultuous point in my life, it holds a very tender place in my heart. While most of Euphoric's decade-long existence has been in hiatus, even when I wasn't on Neo I would periodically return to stroll down memory lane. Looking back at Euphoric (and revisiting it on several occasions) is deeply meaningful to me.

Thus, I hope you can forgive me for any perceived narcissism you might pick up on when looking at this retrospective on my time with Euphoric. This site it truly one of my favorite accomplishments, as silly as that sounds. I hope that looking back at Euphoric's history might brring you a bit of the joy it brings me. (I doubt it will, to be honest, but a girl can dream.)
