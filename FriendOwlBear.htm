A bid for some Vandagyres

Thank you for visiting this pet page!

I am very excited to share my idea for a couple of Vandagyres. I love the look of this raptor/owl based pet, and I wanted to make designs for currently unavailable colors that could potentially boost this pet's popularity. So, let's begin with--

The Valentines Vandagyre

By creating as many heart shapes within the body that I could while adhering to the color's design standard, this Vandagyre has appeal similar to a Care Bear Cousin without the copyright infringement XD. Snuggable, huggable, and all around lovable, it's a perfect sweetheart for anyone that loves teddy bears and pink.

The Maraquan Vandagyre

The animal I had in mind when making this idea was the Sea Angel, a deep-sea slug with a semi-transparent body. The shape of the sea angel is incoporated quite easily into the basic Vandagyre design, and I would absolutely love to see schools of these buddies swimming on the ocean floor.

I sincerely hope these suggestions are taken into consideration by TNT. Thank you for your time!
