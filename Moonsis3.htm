About

Welcome to the MOON, your friendly pet chat source for
Marvelous Outstanding Original Neopets* B)

(*acronym expansion subject to spontaneous meteor blast and subsequent evolution)

How it works:

The MOON base is on the boards and is open to all. Neopians are welcome to post their up for adoption and dream pets on the board (or send a Neomail) and they will be added here, as written, in chronological order, with the latest at the top of the page. (Posts may be edited slightly to write out pet colours/species.) The peachy pink colour is for UFA/T pets, the blue is for pet wishes and the purple is for special requests. (Pets can be UFA/T, but not UFT only. UC pets/styles may be listed UFA. UC dreams are not listed.)

Each box includes a username at the top, to whom the pets in the box apply. Be sure to read the listing in full and note any conditions the person has set. Then simply click the username to go to their lookup and send them a nice Neomail asking about the pet. How your write it is up to you. A well kept account and details about your plans usually help.

*** RULES ***

1. Be kind! :)

2. Please update!
Posts older than 1 month will be removed unless updated. As always, your post can stay up as long as you'd like. Just send a note on the board or via NM to confirm it's still up to date and a time stamp will be added. If your post is removed, you're welcome to re-add it anytime.

3a. Be accessible.
Kind of a no-brainer, but please keep lines of communication open. If your inbox is full or if your Preferences are set to Neofriends only, people can't contact you. If someone sends you a random item, it probably means one of the above. Take action.

3b. Nuts and Bolts Jelly.
If I find that I can't reach you for one of the above reasons, I will send you this jelly. If your status does not change in a few days, your post will be removed.

4. Don't ghost.
As a common courtesy, if you get any offers, please do take a minute to respond, either way, so the other person knows you got it. Even a simple yes or no, thank you is plenty. No one likes to be left hanging.

5. Honor tradition.
When chocolate pets get adopted (coughorpostedcough) we celebrate with a real life chocolate treat :)

History:

Hello, Dumpling here! Back in April 2020, I started a board to adopt out my own pets and invited others to post theirs. Bumping was terribly boring at first, but then we got talking about food, started sharing recipes for fun and made people smile. I figured it'd help others if all the pets are on one page, rather than spread across 20, so I made a simple layout and copied the posts over. Pets and food naturally go very well together and here we are :)
