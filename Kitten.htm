rules: pls don't claim as your own, that's all. ^_^ if someone compliments the font, you don't have to but you could direct them to my userlookup or page to stroke my dopamine receptors. (* ᴗ͈ˬᴗ͈) ꕤ * .ﾟ ♡ ♡ ♡

font "unreadable?" some of y'all hate the light grey. change the 3 digit hex code prior to "neohtml" in the first line. darker colors are listed in the scrollbox on the left. i also use size 1 for all my fonts. change the number from "s=1" to "s=2", "s=3", "s=4" in the same tag aforementioned.

want different colors? i have made a list of hex codes in the "pop-up" to the left you can use, but if you'd prefer me to match a font to a specific avatar, mail me and i will. (´｡• ω •｡')
