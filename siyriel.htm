welcome to the popsicle stand

Welcome to the Popsicle stand, a directory run by Jewlz. This is a unique directory that lists sites essential for graphic makers, site owners, or those looking for anything related. Please don't hesitate to neomail me if there are any problems or questions about the site.

I have gotten rid of the site key with this new update of the popsicle stand. All the sites here are listed as the same. Each section does have a recommended section of sites though.
