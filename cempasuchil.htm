Welcome to Ixi Gala

The Ixi Gala is a place for all of the amazing and fabulously dressed ixi to come and parade along the red carpet and show off their fabulous outfits. All ixi are welcome at the Gala, they just have to be sure to be dressed fabulously. Their dress can be fancy or it can be casual, but it should be fabulous either way! If you'd like to be a part of Ixi Gala, click the link for Gala Tickets above to see how to add your beautifully customized ixi!
