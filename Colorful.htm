Welcome to Dream Colors! This page is dedicated to showcasing some of the amazing user created pet designs and paint brush colors that are out there. Recently, TNT has made amazing user-made pet designs official, the Maraquan Lutari and the Maraquan Kiko. While Neo users have been creating their own fake brushes and pet colors for years, it's amazing to see TNT listen to community praise for the designs and make some official.

The goal of this page is to organize the amazing designs that users have created, and offer it as both a fun gallery to peruse and as a resource for TNT to reach out to artists in the event they would also like to make their designs official site pets. If you would like to submit a pet, you can find information below to add them to the page!


Amazing pets made official by Synth and Chanty.


SITE RULES

  • Only submit art you yourself have created or edited. We cannot accept submissions on another user's behalf, we ask you submit your art or pet ideas yourself.
  • To submit, please submit a mail with the form below. Please link to a petpage which hosts the images of your pet ideas you would like to add.
  • If you have created a fake paintbrush image to go with your pet ideas, you can include that and we will add it to the page. If you are creating pet ideas for existing pet colors, we will use the site brush image. If you have not designed a brush, we will use a neo item relevant to the color instead.
  • You may submit entirely new brush colors, pet ideas for existing colors, or alternative versions of pet colors that already exist. We ask however you do not submit new pet species, just colors for existing pets.


    • FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

    CAN I SUBMIT A BRUSH COLOR THAT SOMEONE ELSE ALSO HAS DRAWN?
    A: Of course! Your interpretation of a brush like alien, angelic, or valentine is unique. We're happy to host anyone's fake brush ideas, regardless of it it has already been submitted before.

    DO I HAVE TO MAKE A FAKE CUSTOM TO SUBMIT MY PET?
    A: Nope! Although some of the pets listed have awesome fake customizations to show what the pet would look like if real, we just need either a transparent image of the pet or a square fake custom to be added to the directory.

    HOW MANY COMPLETED DESIGNS DO I NEED TO BE ADDED?
    A: You can submit your pet idea whether you've drawn one species, or every single pet species! Please submit each color as a separate mail for us, but we're happy to list any you make.

    CAN I SUBMIT PETPET DESIGNS / CUSTOM PET DAY CLOTHES / CUSTOM ITEMS HERE ?
    A: At this time this page just focuses on custom pet designs. It may potentially expand in the future to include pet clothes or items, but at this time we are only accepting pet colors.
