1. Trudy's Surprise

Profit: ~500k per month

Who should do this: Everyone!!

How: It is cmulative so you must log in daily to spin it. Every 25 consecutive days will reward you with 100k. Add that to all the neopoints you earned in the previous 24 days and you'll average 500k a month.

The Trudy notification has been inconsistent since the switch to the Beta theme so I suggest reminding yourself to spin it another way. For me, I have it included in my dailies bookmarks folder that I run through daily. Do whatever works for you.

Avatar: Awarded the first time you recieve 100k

Trophy: NA

3. Battledome

Profit: ~500k to 1.5mil per month

Who should do this: Everyone, but best once you can afford to train a pet.

How: You can only make 1500nps a day battling, but you can make much more than that selling your 15 daily prizes. As you are starting out, do whatever 1P battles your pet can manage.

Your end goal should be to predictably beat Koi Warrior, or Jetsam Ace if you have premium. If those are too strong, work your way up the Kreludan Defender Robot first.

4. Food Club

Profit: Depends on account age. Generally you will average a profit of 1.8x what you bet. With my 18 year old account, that's around 2.5mil per month.

Who should do this: Everyone can do this, but it's especially good for those with accounts 100 months or older. I suggest you wait until you have enough neopoints to afford to "tank" (lose everything you bet) at least 4 times in a row.

How: I suggest everyone starting out follows an established bettor. Many people post their bets daily for others to copy. Here are two of the most popular and best for the average person:

Garet: ~Boochi_Target
Lefty: ~Innocent

Garet is particularly good for beginners because he's quite conservative (plays it safe) and explains his bets everyday. Even if you don't plan to follow him, his page is worth a thorough read.

Bets are designed to work together, so you must place all 10.

So many people consistently post their bets everyday that there's no need to ever learn the mechanics of the game or how to bet yourself, unless you want to.

This is a betting game akin to horse racing, except in this case it's an eating contest. The game is RIGGED POSITIVELY so if you know what you're doing, or copy someone who does, you will always profit OVER TIME. Most established bettors have a lifetime RTO (return on investment) of about 1.8x what they put in.

If you're feeling overwhelmed, here is a guide on how to follow a betting page: ~OrvinnTheFirstMate

There is always an active board on the Help Chat called "Do You Play Food Club" that lists guides, bettors to follow, and is a good place to go if you have any questions. There is also an active board on the Games Chat.

As this is a betting game, sometimes you will lose everything you put in. Sometimes you'll lose everything for days in a row. But eventually you'll win it all back, and then some. As I said, unlike a normal betting game or horse races, this is rigged positively, and you will always profit over time. There's no reason not to do it, and if you have an aged account and aren't doing you're missing out considerably.

5. Stock Market

Profit: approx 300% of your investment, depending on sell point.

Who should do this: Everyone once you can afford to spend 15k a day.

How: Put simply, buy low, sell high. The minimum you can normally* buy at is 15nps/share. Then you wait, sometimes months or even years, until it reaches your sell point. You can choose any sell point you want, but to get the most out of it I suggest no lower than 60nps/share or 300%. I sell around 75nps/share.

This is extremely, extremely slow, but like Food Club if you stick with it you'll always profit. Buy 1000 shares of a stock at 15nps/share everyday and then wait, it's that easy.

If there aren't any stocks at 15nps/share, you can wait and check back later, or buy at 16 or 17nps/share. In the grand scheme of things, the few neopoints difference doesn't matter, and it's better to buy higher than not buy at all.

*There is an Obelisk boon that allows you to buy stocks at 10nps/share for one week. More info on the Obelisk and boons here: jellyneo.net/?go=battleground_of_the_obelisk

If you want more guidance, here is a guide: ~SilverCyanide2
