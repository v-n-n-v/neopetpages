...the crew behind the bakery, Love At First Bite...

Streusel the Plushie Doglefox

Pastry Baker

Roycio, or Royce as his friends call him, is a faerie born from wishes made on birthday candles. He is a perpetual sweetheart that can be a bit shy and soft-spoken at first. With just a few smiles though, he will be your friend for life.

Royce runs a bakery called Love At First Bite. He is the head baker, decorator, and mastermind behind each delicious recipe. Although, he does not have a head for business, so he leaves all the confusing logistics to Eiloh - his best friend.

His friend Eiloh runs the store front, while Floury - a tiny fae that came to life from a bag of flour - assists in the kitchen. His doglefox, Streusel, is often seen napping in the storefront window and will gladly accept belly rubs from customers.

Zinnia the Spring Faellie

Barista

Eiloh is the rational minded half of Love at First Bite. While Roycio is the mastermind delicatessen behind the bakery - Eiloh is the one that keeps the doors open. When he isn't busy reigning in Roycio's crazy ideas, he works as a cashier and barista in the bakery with Meugi. While he became a barista out of necessity when the bakery was at its beginning, he has learned to love the craft. He still prefers tea to coffee however, and even makes his own tea blends. Just ask him for a sample next time you stop by!

Zinnia, Eiloh's faellie, has taken up residency within one of the hydrangea bushes and is known to braid flower crowns for her favorite regulars.

Periwinkle the Spring Ona

Gardener

Meugi's day job is cashier at Love at First Bite, but his real passion is in gardening. When he was first hired, the patio behind the bakery was a sad plot. Now it is a stunning, flourishing garden thanks to Meugi, and one of the many reasons why patrons return to Love At First Bite. One day, Meugi hopes to open his own landscaping business.

Toffee the Birthday Quadrapus

Sweet Tooth

As one of the best confectioners around, Popswirl is a bit of a culinary celebrity. He's won many awards and competitions - some of these competitions have even been televised. His perfectly coiffed hair and adorable demeanor has garnered him a small fan base.

Popswirl takes sugar confectionery very seriously - he even went as far as studying chemistry to bring molecular gastronomy into the world of candy making.

He also specializes in sugar sculpture - elaborate, decorative pieces created from just sugar. He often teams up with Roycio to create stunning cake toppers and embellishments for some of Royce's artistic special occasion cakes.

Oddly enough, Popswirl doesn't have much of a sweet tooth. He always explains that he isn't making candy and chocolates for himself, but to bring smiles to others.

sugary the Birthday Barlow

Fae Baker

Floury is a brownie - a type of faerie, not the delicious dessert - that came to life when a bottle of faerie dust was accidentally spilled on top of a bag of flour. No taller than a spoon and extremely shy, he is rarely seen by anyone besides Roycio and Biscoffs.

Brownies usually come out at night and perform household chores while the other residents are asleep. In keeping with this instinct, Floury starts the prep for the next morning's baking while Roycio snoozes in his loft upstairs. He takes great pride in his work and is secretly pleased when Roycio thanks him every morning.

Floury tries his very best to help Roycio in the kitchen whenever he needs it during the day, but sometimes it gets too busy and loud and he retreats to the cabinets for a break. His very best friend, Biscoffs, is helping him gain more confidence so he can help Roycio even more.

Hidden in one of the kitchen's least used cabinets is Floury's secret treasure hoard; buttons, sequins, and anything else small and shiny that customers unknowingly leave behind and Floury collects once everyone has left. He loves his treasure almost as much as his favorite treat - a crouton dipped in milk.

Stuffles the Plushie Morkou

Fae Cookie

Each year on Gnorbu Day, Love At First Bite makes a stunning display of gnorbu shaped cookies in over 20 different flavors. Occasionally, less than perfectly shaped cookies come out of the oven. They get placed in the reject pile and given to all the employees after their hard work on the bakery's busiest day.

One year during this hectic time, Floury was so overwhelmed by all the people and commotion. He hid deep in the cabinets and wished for a friend that was his size and nice, and not loud and scary.

At the same exact moment Floury wished that wish, Roycio sneezed faerie dust on a cookie that was a little too rotund with legs a little too short. The cookie came to life in an explosion of sprinkles and a new member of the family was born.

Unlike his best friend Floury, Biscoffs enjoys customers' attention and enjoys it even more when they give him some whipped cream from their lattes or a taste of icing from their pastries.

Sorbet the Bikiwan

Delicatessen

Sherburts is a "frozen confectionaire". She sets up a gourmet popsicle and sherbert stand next to the bakery during the summer. At the first sign of autumn, Sherb flees to the sunnier beaches on the equator and sells her wares there.

Honeydew the Custard Spardel

Honey Faerie

From the cafe's patio, one can see a curious section of land walled off by shrubbery, and another section of open land covered in wildflowers. Placed in the corner furthest away from the cafe is what appears to be multiple beehives. These hives are lovingly tended to by Tisfe, a honey faerie with the sweetest disposition.

He loves to chat with Meugi about pollinator gardens and giving out bottles of his bee's honey. He seems to subsist purely on cups of Eilho's tea, heavily sweetened with honey - at least that's what Biscoffs will tell everyone.

Even though he is a being made entirely out of honey, Tisfe magically doesn't leave any honey behind at his touch. Although, if you're in a pinch for something to sweeten your tea - he'll gladly spare a drop of honey for your cup.

Boxwood the Woodland Yoakie

Green Thumb Faerie

Behind the cafe, past the patio and Meugi's garden, is a line of perfectly kept tall hedges signaling the end of the cafe's property and keeping the other side out of view. However, there is a door-sized gap in the line, flanked by two conical topiaries and punctuated by a rustic arch trellis covered in beautiful wisteria. A stone pathway encourages patrons to wander from the patio and into a huge, stunning English garden. There are even two greenhouses within the garden and rows of award winning rose hybrids.

The mastermind behind this tranquil haven is a horticultural fae named Shrubberys. Shrubberys is an old soul of little words. He spends all day tending to his garden and generally avoiding those who visit it. He's happy that his passion can bring joy to others, but he doesn't need to talk to them about it. The only person he begrudgingly speaks to on a regular basis is Meugi, who often comes around for gardening tips and to pester Shrubs for new clippings.

The secret to his horticultural success is that he can actually hear the wishes and needs of his plants as they whisper to him whenever he is near. His wisterias are full of wisdom, his roses a bit demanding, and his topiary a bit vain - but he loves them all the same.

Polli the Carmariller

Bumbly Bee

Chubby keeps the bees out of trouble and raises the alarm when trespassers come too close. He has a few long standing grudges against several local racoons and one skunk. Fire Goddess

Long ago, longer than she cares to remember, Nhout was chosen as a sacrifice to appease the gods and save her village from an impending volcanic eruption. She was caught between the rage of two gods, and fell to her tragic death in the lava below. Before passing on to the underworld, her soul was intercepted to become the new deity of the volcano.

Nhier the Hoggir

Magma Familiar

Before Nhout became a goddess, when she was a mere mortal, she loved with her whole heart. She was known for her loyalty and selflessness that made the world seem like a better place.

When she was taken from the mortal realm, her love was reciprocated in kind. Those of whom her kindness had touched mourned for the loss of such a bright light. They prayed to whomever would listen that she could feel just a sliver of that love in return.

The gods must have listened. For not soon after she crossed into the immortal realm and danced with fire - a being came forth from the molten core of Nhimra. The heart of the mountain itself fell at her feet and pledged eternal companionship.

Revna the Mauket

Northern Goddess

Seriliya is a goddess that is as cold as the lands she rules. Those that live in the harsh conditions of the North Sea only survive because they are equally as rough and tumultuous.

These are Seriliya's people - the devotees that believe her mercy keeps them alive. It is said that meeting one's end in battle is the greatest way to honor the goddess.

Seriliya watches over her favorite warriors and blesses them with boons in battle. Warriors that have gained her favor and pride but have met their untimely end in the throes of battle are given a second life serving at her side for eternity.

Sherr the Biyako

Chosen Warrior

There comes a time when heroes become legends and no one is sure if they actually existed.

There was a time when Dulfrost was a fearless leader and brave warrior. He was so favored by the goddess Seriliya that he seemed almost untouchable in battle. But all heroes met their end eventually.

When his spirit entered the afterworld, Seriliya ushered him to her side, as she could not bear to lose her favorite warrior. She stretched her arms across the weather and grabbed his spirit with her ice cold hands. His essence froze at the touch as Seriliya dragged him to her side. She blessed him with a corporeal form, but as his spirit was now as cold as his goddess, his form was forever encased in snow.

His ceaseless devotion to the goddess has earned him the position of Seriliya's most trusted advisor and confidant. He relishes every minute by her side and will go to the ends of the world for his goddess.

River the Lutra

Freshwater Sprite

Winterdeer is a creature that no human has ever had the privy of viewing - a water daimon. Halfway between an incorporeal being and the force of water itself. His terrestrial form appears as spindly deer with a mane of flowing water. Any body of water is his domain; from lakes and rivers, to puddles and bogs. With a touch that purifies, his eternal duty is to protect the life-force of the forest.

As his spirit is bound to water, he can never step foot on land. Instead, he travels by dissolving his form back into the water and then reemerges in a different body of freshwater, no matter the distance or size. He speaks very little, but when he does it is almost a whisper; like ripples on the surface of a pond during a calm day.

Webster the Apple Spyder

Forest Sprite

Remote and obscure, within a dense forest, pines give way to a corse of quaking aspen. As the aspens dance in the breeze, sunlight filters through the canopy and dapples onto the glade beneath. Wildlife is slow to wake in the mornings in this idyllic haven, but eventually the deer start grazing and the ruffled grouse pluck berries from low branches.

In the heart of the glade, in the heart of a hawthorn bush, a curious little sprite wakes each day to the songs of robins. The size of a newborn fawn, a mane of clover, skin the texture of bark, and intricate wings like a dragonfly's - this is the heart of the glade, Woodvales.

Born of nature itself, Woodvales is a protector, a botanist, a guide, a breeze on a hot day, a stream to quench the thirst of deer, a thicket of brambles to hide from the red tailed hawk circling above.

In the heart of his domain, a brook bubbles forth from the ground and spills into a stream that chases itself downhill, over rocks and through trees. The ancient freshwater sprite Winterdeer was responsible for the birth of this stream at a time before the aspens even took root in the area. The flurry of life that follows in the wake of water is what gave birth to Woodvales.

While his glade could easily be described as an idyllic haven, Woodvales knows better than to disturb the realities of nature. All things living must come to an end in order to nurture the next generation of life.

Vasek the Christmas Gruslen

Leshy

Kiyrasu is a Leshy - a guardian spirit of the forests. His ambivalence towards people is sometimes seen as temperamental, and as such many are wary to cross him. Especially since he can control the trees; often moving them to trap or disorientate those who travel too deep within the forest.
Not many have actually seen him, preferring to make up tall tales to spread fear around campfires. If they were to meet Kiyrasu though, they would know he has pale white skin, bright green eyes, and a mane of living plants.

He dwells within the deepest, darkest part of the forest with his companion Vasek, a white wolf he found abandoned as a pup. They speak in the sounds of the forest; in the songs of birds, the rumbling of bears, and the rustling of leaves.

Devious Cupid

Holart doesn't shoot arrows to make people fall in love. No, he doesn't need arrows to do that. (Although he is a fantastic marksman, if you ask him)

Watching people fall in love is boring, it's predictable, and it isn't what Holart was created to do. No, Holart doesn't create love, he tests it.

Astron the Starry Feepit

Celestial Wisher

Solarkik has seen the birth of many stars, and has seen just as many blink out of existence. He was there when they named the stars, when they arranged them into shapes and called those shapes names. He was there when they realized the stars have power - just like the grass they walked on or the water they bathed in.

He was there when the power of the stars was first harnessed under a moonless night and a new branch of magic was born.

He was there when they realized there was something greater dictating the flow of the stars' magic. His name was born then, from a language that rose and fell in the blink of his eye. He was there when they formed a cult to exalt him and his whims. In another blink, temples were built in his name and the cult was no longer considered a cult.

He was there when a bit of his magic first clung to a newborn child and marked them for a life intertwined with his power. He was there as this happened again and again and these children grew to be his oracles - messengers of his whims and listeners of the ebb and flow of the stars' magic.

He was there for it all, and he will continue to be there, even when this world blinks out of existence, he will watch as another is born.

Sediment the Sand Bogie

Guardian Statue

Sentiar first took form on this world as a mass of marble in the workshop of a well renowned craftsman. It took little over a year, but the stone was sculpted into an exquisite sculpture that entranced all who looked at it.

Admired by many, it eventually was sold and moved into the foyer of an estate owned by a wealthy guild leader. It spent a few generations there, and then another few in a different foyer in a different estate. Decades passed as the exquisite statue was sold and bought again and again. Over the century or two since it emerged from an unshapely section of solid earth, it had developed a colorful provenance.

A life journey of thousands of miles, it was exchanged one last time - from the hands of a rich devout merchant, to the Temple of Nhimra. A considerable offering, the statue was placed in the main chamber, amongst the dark stone walls and high ceilings and far behind the obsidian altar.

The temple was built into the base of Mt. Nhimra to be closer to its goddess and to be a physical part of her domain. Nhout takes note of all offerings made to her with varying degrees of interest. On the day Sentiar was placed within the wall of her temple - she took immediate notice. When the halls emptied, she stepped inside the main chamber for the first time in a long time.

Not only did the beauty of the statue intrigue Nhout - with the ebbs and flows of the earth played out amongst its skin - but it also called to her. The earth it was carved from called to her. It resonated within her and she knew it was meant for something more than a mortal's status symbol.

So, she let the earth within her call back, and the statue came to life at the recognition. Sentiar, she called it, became her sentinel. Astute, resolute, and severe - Sentiar stands guard over Nhout's realm.

These are the travelers of the Herd. Each has their own reasons for being on the move across the realm. Whether it be on an endless quest for meaning, knowledge, solace, vengeance, or to keep the demons at bay - these few have no place they can truly call home.

Quill the Hermiteese

Seventh Son

Septvn is named as such because he is the seventh son of the seventh king of Woolington.

Septvn was brought up without much purpose or direction - the tutors needed to direct their attention to the children most likely to take the throne some day. Knowing that he was the spare of spares led Septvn to cultivate "less useful" pursuits such as music, storytelling, beer brewing, wilderness survival, and more.

While his other siblings (four brothers and two sisters) have found pursuits they each excel at, Septvn never found anything he truly excelled in. Even Hexon, the sixth child, is an up and coming champion in dressage. Septvn has a lot of hobbies, but he loses interest easily and bounces to whatever new thing catches his fancy.

He dreams of exploring the world outside his country's borders, collecting stories and sleeping under the stars.

Septvn wishes his family understood that storytelling was a talent worthy of praise. He could entrance anyone willing to listen to his stories. His tales were borrowed from accounts recorded in the royal library, but he longed to have his own experiences to weave into an entrancing tapestry, instead of embellishing the dry accounts of others. Oh, if only he could go on his own adventures! But alas, he mustn't stray too far from the castle, least the kingdom needs a spare spare.

Red the Quintilc

Sword Master

Brcck is a well known master of the sword arts. Her quick feet, laser like focus, and cunning technique have been unrivaled for years. In the past, she taught pupils under strict guidance. However, that came to an abrupt stop after one fateful day.

She once leant her expertise in the appraisal of blades to a university that had inherited a large collection of swords from a mysterious old man. During the inspection of one peculiar blade - Brcck unknowingly bound herself to this cursed blade and changed the course of her fate forever.

The cursed sword is one of the finest in the world, using its dark magic to slice through anything its blade cannot. This blade has stirred envy and dark intentions within its admirers for centuries. With so many of its wielders meeting their ends by nefarious deeds, the sword now craves endless, unrelenting revenge.

Afraid of what endless revenge would do to her mind and heart, Brcck sought desperately for a loophole that would allow the sword to stay quiet and her conscience clear. Now she is a bounty hunter traveling the land to help others in their quests for revenge.

Rage the Crokabek

seeking vengence

The name "Lavendetta" has been whispered across bar stools and alleyways throughout the country. The name has morphed into a curse, not unlike the boogeyman. When a member of the shadier side of society goes missing - rumors begin to spread that Lavendetta has struck again. Thieves and crooks fear for their lives when their name is whispered as Lavendetta's rumored next victim.

But Lavendetta has a list. A list of members that belong to the Greater Northern Shipping Guild, the legal entity that serves as a front for a dangerous crime syndicate. It is an open secret who really runs the Guild, but political pockets are stuffed deep with bribes to look the other way.

Born as Lavender to a well-to-do family of merchants, she had a comfortable life growing up. However, one month her family refused to pay the 'protection' fees of the Guild, putting their foot down and refusing to bend to the Guild's will. They ended up paying the price of that defiance, and became a nefarious example to others to never cross the Guild. Lavender was traveling that day, in another city visiting with friends. Many called her blessed and lucky - but she only saw the end of her world and the beginning of a rage that burned brighter and brighter.

Grief gave way to the beginnings of a plan for revenge. She had heard tales of the swordmaster Brcck, who carried out revenge for others with her cursed sword. Lavender traveled across the land to meet with Brcck and present her request. Brcck refused, however, citing that the mindless slaughter of an entire group of people was not revenge, but madness. After Lavender refused to leave, Brcck realized nothing would stop her. The burning fire of rage in her heart had grown too large for it to simply smolder out on its own. Brcck took her on as an apprentice, more tough on Lavender than she had been with any of her other students. For she knew Lavender's quest ahead would be long and dangerous, and any mistake would lead to her end. had grown Lavender, so she mentored her until she was almost as good with a blade as Brcck.

Years under the tutelage of Brcck, Lavender was born again with a new purpose. Lavendetta is now taking out the trash one by one, and has the criminal underworld looking over their shoulder in fear of being next.

Mr. Higgins the Plumpy

Travelling Merchant

Shydalu is a traveling merchant that specializes in rare wares from all reaches of the globe. Her wares travel with her in her enchanted caravan.

The enchantments were achieved after a tense, hours-long negotiation with Alchemysts. Shydalu parted with a stash of her extremely rare potion ingredients for the powerful enhancements needed to upgrade her caravan. Both women admire the steadfastness in each over, but will never admit it lest they lose the upper hand.

The caravan has been extended on the inside to be as large as a brick and mortar store. Products on display are magically encased to prevent shoplifting. Extra stock is locked in a back room behind a reinforced door. Also included in the deal was a magic safe that only opens for Shydalu.

The store does not have a counter with a till, but rather a table where interested parties negotiate a sale with Shydalu. In the back corner of the room is a spiral staircase that leads to Shydalu's sleeping quarters. For obvious reasons, the staircase is strictly off limits to visitors.

The caravan is expansive and secured with the strongest magic in the realm. Bandits have learned the hard way that it is an impenetrable fortress even an army would have trouble taking.

Hijinx the Mibblie

Troubling Shadow

Ajinks is a reformed cutpurse. No, seriously, he is. His pickpocketing days are behind him. Well, every now and again he comes across someone just begging to have their stuff stolen. But reformation is a process, that's what Mohpps says anyways. And really, if you think about it, he's teaching those people to be less obvious targets. It's all about giving back to the community.

Ajinks also knows a lot - well, he 'overhears' a lot. It's not his fault his ears immediately tune into whispers. And it's not his fault people don't notice him as he gets closer to 'overhear' better. Never mind that as a former sneak thief, he is quite good at sneaking.

Shydalu might frown in distaste, but Mohpps pays well for the information he collects. She can't be disappointed he's making honest money! Well...semi-honest money. Mohpps doesn't ask where the information comes from as long as it's reliable. But Ajinks can picture the disappointment on Shydalu's face and the sigh that would follow. She might not actually be his older sister, but she sure acts like it.

It's not like she's the perfect angel everyone claims she is. They say that just because her smile can turn the toughest soldier into a harmless kitten. He's seen the way she subtly manipulates people to tip a trade in her favor. Ruthlessness lies beneath that pretty exterior. That's why Ajinks likes her so much and why traveling with her is always entertaining.

Roombert the Goyalbotnik

Tavern Keeper

At the edge of a mid sized city, on the side of a mid sized road, sits a mid sized tavern. A humble sign hangs above the front door, reading in faded letters, "Cheshire Inn & Pub".

Despite its apperances, every night this tavern teams with patrons, as if it was a large sized pub in the center of a large sized city.

The secret behind the popularity of this middle-of-nowhere establishment? The proprietor - Mohpps.

Most tired travelers don't have high expectations for an inn. All they want is a place to rest and refuel, never mind the customer service or the flavor of the food. But Mohpps goes above and beyond.

There isn't a face or name Mohpps has forgotten. He greets everyone as if they're a long, lost friend. The drinks are cold and the food is served hot. Lodgers can ask for a blanket without receiving a snear, and baths are delivered hot and steaming instead of lukewarm.

Not only is the Cheshire Inn & Pub known for its excellent service, but it's also known as a major information center. Gossip tends to run rampant when a few brews are involved, and since there are so many regulars, the bar becomes a hotbed of local news.

A visitor can sit at the bar for an hour and learn much of the comings and goings of many individuals - but if they're wanting to know something specific, all they have to do is ask the man behind the bar.

Mohpps tends not just drinks but also information; Lavendetta's next victim, Alchemysts' latest feat, how to contact Brcck with a bounty, or the goods Shydalu is currently selling. And if the quality of the information reflects the quantity of the visitor's tip? Well then, Mohpps is nothing but a successful businessman.

Undo the Zombie Farnswap

seeking a future

Trovus just needed some stamps. To send a mortal letter through the mortal post to a mortal.

It shouldn't be so confusing to send a missive through the mortal ether, Trovus thought as he cursed bureaucracy.

How did he manage to reanimate an entire corpse when he was just trying to summon the correct postage for his missive? The mortal world will crumble because of bureaucracy, Trovus thought as he tried again to summon stamps; now with a half sentient being off to the side in his workshop.

A roll of stamps appeared in the middle of his summoning circle. His relief was dashed when he picked up the roll and realized, as all the stamps fluttered off the roll and onto the floor, that the adhesive failed to materialize. He sighed, this really shouldn't be so complicated. His gaze lifted to the body standing awkwardly across the room. Of course, Trovus chastised his stupidity, dragging a spirit from the ether into this realm has disturbed the balance of my magic. The Reporter Gene

EmGFP stands for Emerald Green Fluorescent Protein. This is a twist on the classic GFP. GFP is a protein that was originally discovered in the jellyfish Aequorea victoria. This species uses the green fluorescent protein to provide flashes of green bioluminescence.

Scientists studied this jellyfish and were able to identify the gene that produces this protein. Knowing this piece of genetic code was exciting because now scientists now had a reporter gene they could insert in tandem with a gene of interest into cell cultures, bacteria, and other organisms. If the gene of interest was successfully introduced into the cells, the GFP gene would also be expressed. If the organism glowed green - the experiment worked!

EmGFT is a next-generation GFP that has been optimized for mammalian use. EmGFP (the gnorbu, not the protein) is living proof this reporter gene works! He seems normal at first, but shine blue light at him and he lights up like a glow stick!

A living fossil is a species that superficially looks like an ancestral species that we only know from fossils. Swengu is not actually millions of years old. His lineage just hasn't changed much in that time.

It does not escape him, the irony, that he is a paleontologist with a prolific career. He has published many seminal works that have solidified hypothesized taxonomy or brought current classifications into question. At the time of writing this, he has 18 extinct species named after him.

One of his major works was the discovery of the common ancestor of the canidae petpets; the doglefox, droolik, manjeer, puppyblew, spardel, warf, and zomutt. While it had been long hypothesized that they were part of the same clade, Swengu found the fossilized remains of the species confirmed to be their common ancestor.
