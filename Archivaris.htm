FAQ

What is Ugly Sweater Week?
Ugly Sweater Week (USW) is a user-organized theme week in December for the Beauty Contest (BC) in which people draw their pets in ugly festive sweaters and then enter those drawings into the BC.

What qualifies as an ugly sweater?
There's no hard and fast rules, but ugly sweaters are sweaters that are tacky and/or ugly in some kind of way and themed after Christmas. Think puns, clashing colours, lights, music and/or sequins. You can look at previous editions (2015, 2016–2018, 2019, 2021, 2022) or search online for inspiration/examples. Do note that you don't necessarily need to go with a sweater! You could also go with a dress, suit, scarf, whatever. As long as it goes with the spirit/vibe of an ugly sweater, you're good!

When is the Ugly Sweater Week?
Generally, in early or mid December. This year, the USW takes place from December 8–15, 2023. Entries should be submitted in the preceding week: December 1–8, 2023.

I want to participate, but those dates don't work for me
No worries! You can always enter a week or two later (or earlier!) if that fits your schedule better. You won't technically be part of the theme week, but you can still get a place in the archive.

How do I particpate?
Draw your pet according to the theme (ugly sweater) and enter the drawing into the Beauty Contest here between Friday December 1st 12:30 PM NST and December 8th 11:30 AM NST. When noon (12pm NST) rolls over on December 8th the theme week will officially have started and you can advertise your entry on the neoboards and vote for others' entries.

Can I win anything by participating?
The usual BC prizes (pet trophy and avatar) apply, and you can also pick up this sweet trophy I made to display on your pages. You can also get a spot in the archive, if you want!

What do I need to do to end up in the archive?
Simply let me know the following information: a link to a page with your entry image, your pet's species and name (full petname and optionally a nickname), plus your name and username! Whether you post that info on the USW board, neomail me or hire a skywriter does not matter, as long as it gets to me!

It's my first time entering the BC. What do I need to know?
There's four components to the BC: creating your entry, submitting it, advertising and voting. It's important that you know and follow the rules for all of them.
The official FAQ is located here
Additionally, /~Helpy is an excellent summary of the BC rules. /~cotton_kandy_ is a great guide to the BC, even though it's a little bit outdated in some areas (most notably about where you are allowed to advertise).
Other useful pages include /~Snapsdile to make your (manual) voting life easier and /~Colress which has some historical data about the popularity of pet species in the BC. /~Moonie_LightHeart and /~artprompt are useful for finding previous, current and upcoming theme weeks.

If you're unsure about anything at all, please do not hesitate to ask other users on the art neoboards!

Is this a new theme week for the BC?
No, the USW is an (almost) annual theme week. You can see some entries from previous years here (2015), here (2016–2018), here (2019), here (2021) and here (2022).

Who is hosting this theme week?
It changes from year to year! This year (2023) your lovely host is TaNi (_help_de_jaguar_). If you're curious about me, you can check out my portfolio. I also hosted the 2021 edition and I co-hosted the 2018 edition with Elsa (hermelijntje) c:
