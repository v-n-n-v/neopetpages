NEW BATTLEDOME 1P opponents' icons multiplier table since Dec 2015

In the New Battledome 1P opponents are not limited to a 16X multiplier for damage and defence

The values between [ ] are estimated (not tested)

For a given opponent the multiplier is proportional to its difficulty

ex. Balthazar difficulty 320 the multiplier is 12.5 At double difficulty 640 the multiplier is double 25


Fractional icons show up in the battledome as full icons, but they don't do that much damage.

Ex. Werelupe Claw Necklace. It does 4.66 icons of fire, 4.66 icons of dark, 3 icons of earth and .2-5 physical icons

Damage is rounded up. Ex 4.1 of damage will be rounded up to 5

For comments and corrections, please contact me (claudej) Updated Feb 2nd, 2018

A special thanks to neo__rainbow for pointing to differences between his table and mine. So I checked them all and made a few corrections.
