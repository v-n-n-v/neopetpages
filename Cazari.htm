Hey there! I'm Ivy! I started Enchanted Untaken Names in January 2014 when I found it difficult to find an untaken name for a new pet. The site has been on hiatus for a while, mostly because recaptchas made it hard to deal with everything, but I thought it was time for a revamp!

I started this as a small page to help a few close neofriends with their untaken names, and it grew from there! Over the years, this page has grown far more than I ever imagined. Thank you if you've ever used a name from Enchanted! You are the reason I am constantly motivated to bring you regular updates! It's a wonderful thing when I see a pet on the boards with a name I listed here, it makes my entire day!

As Enchanted is now visually updated, there's no need to let me know when you take a name! All I ask is that you enjoy your new pet! Feel free to share this page around if you like what you see!

Thank you so much for visiting Enchanted!

Pets made since the revamp in Feb 2023: 743.
