ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm Seera,And I have a passion for pixels! The 8-bit Neopet is an interesting concept! However, it's not a color that has many truly 8-bit properties!
Yes, Most of them go out of the realm of 8-bit. Most 8-Bit Sprites consist of 4 Colors (Or 3 Colors and a transparent color!)
Yet the official Neopets seem to go outside that range- Not that there's anything wrong with that! I just want to use the limitation for my designs!

Limitation leads to inspiration,as I say. ^_^ However- I will be also making Expanded Palettes to match the official 8-Bit pets as well!
This is all just for fun,after all! I want to Improve my pixel sk1lls.
