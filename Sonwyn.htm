A random collection of untaken petnames

I felt bad hoarding all of these names to myself, so here ya go! Most were found using either a fantasy name generator, an "unusual words" generator, or are a variant of a scientific name (thanks, Biology degree!)

Fair warning: I didn't think up any of these names with trading in mind. If they end up being desirable, great! But I just thought they were neat.

I'll try to check back every now and again to see which names have been taken, but if you notice that a bunch (5+) have been grabbed and the page needs an update, feel free to NM me (tdoglover) and I'll do a sweep. Thanks and happy naming!!
