Welcome! (Or welcome back!)

Find A Laugh is a directory of screenies (screenshots) and other entertaining diversions. I'm Weapon and it is my goal to provide you with all the tools you need for many minutes of laughs, distractions and procrastination.

Get listed?

Drop me a neomail with the address of your page to get listed. Find A Laugh only lists 1) Screenie pages and 2) Humorous or entertaining pages. Linking back with a button or text link is appreciated but by no means compulsory. Thanks!
