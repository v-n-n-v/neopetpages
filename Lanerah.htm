Intro


Somewhere in the deep depths of Mystery Island lays a magical place just for the seafolk of Neopia. The bay is called Secret Cove. At the cove neopians, who live underneath the waves, have a safe place to meet other merpersons, regain their strength, rest, and play in the crystal clear waters of the cove. Every second month, among the the merpeople, they choose one lucky person who will receive the title Cove Queen or Cove King.

Welcome!

Hi there and welcome to Secret Cove a maraquan directory! Maraquan pets can't wear normal clothes and sadly there aren't many specific wearables for them. They are a bit harder to customise. This is why I created Secret Cove!


Here at the cove, you can admire Maraquan neopet fashion and have some inspiration. Every other month one lucky pet will be chosen to have the honor to be part of the royal court of Secret Cove and he or she will receive the title Cove Queen or Cove King and a spiffy trophy! Will your pet be the lucky one? Just send me a request if you want your pet(s) added to our expanding directory!

REQUIREMENTS/RULES:
Your pets have to be painted maraquan.

Your pets have to wear at least two wearables.
