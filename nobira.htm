Want to Collaborate?

Bejeweled will collorate with anyone! You do not need to own a site. My only request is that you can provide your own coding or graphics. This means you cant use coding from someone elses site or someone else graphic image. Collaborations are a split between both parties. One will make the graphics and the other provides the coding. I'm okay with doing two kinds of collaborations:
  • 1. One party provides a graphic for the other party to actively code. eg. Collaborations between Bisou and Beetle were made for each respective layout. The coding and graphic are 1 of a kind, not made from premade bases as each party contributed.
  • 2. Only one party is active in the collaboration process. This means that the active party is using the other parties content and pairing it with thier own. eg. Collaborations made with Silent Serenity and Whitespace are made this way. I use their premade coding to pair with my new graphics.
