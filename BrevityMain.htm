intro

Brevity is run by myself, Britt, and was originally opened in August of 2014. After a long hiatus, it was reopened in March of 2021. I have been on Neopets for quite some time and I've always enjoyed coding here. I opened Brevity to improve my coding abilities and provide new ideas to the community. The premades I offer are all inspired by something different, which has created an interestingly diverse portfolio.

I openly welcome feedback and suggestions for new content. If you would like to know more about me, have an idea you'd like to share, or have any questions or problems, please don't hesitate to neomail me.

terms of use

As long as you leave credit, you may:

  • Alter images, colours, font faces, etc. as much as you'd like. Please feel free to make my layouts your own!
  • Use my code as a skeleton for your own personal layout. Credit can be in the form of a link or a button.
  • Use my code (or pieces of it) for premade layouts. Credit may be left on your site, or on the layout itself.
Basically: Give credit where credit is due.

If you have any questions, or if you see someone violating these terms, don't hesitate to contact me.
