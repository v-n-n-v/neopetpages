One Day...

you find yourself traveling through the forest outside Brightvale... You make your way through golden mist, shrouding the forest floor. As you forge through foliage, the sun soars higher above the mountain ridge. With the sunshine gleaming through the trees, the mist clears- You notice cloven footprints in the dirt...


Following the prints leads you down to a stream. Trickling water rushes over a bed of smooth stones, and you bend down to splash your face. Just then you hear a *rustle*...

You quickly glance across the stream and make out what seems to be the silhouette of a large Ixi. The creature steps into the light: his body glitters like facets of crystal from head to toe, in the most lush color of green. He smiles and says, "Greetings, traveler... If you complete a Quest for me, I may offer you a reward..."
