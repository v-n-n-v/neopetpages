Pet Roulette Basics:

Pick one or more of your vwn trading fodder (Xxxxxxxxxx name format, pronounceable 10L or less) that you don't mind trading RIGHT THIS SECOND.
List your pet on the Roulette board to be added to the pot.
People will offer their pets on yours as fast as they can.
You get ONE PASS. This means you must take your first valid offer OR use your ONE PASS and take your Second valid offer. The bids must be by separate users. The third offer and all other bids received after are ruled invalid.



Compound names (Up to 13L), Names 4 Letters or less, uncapped names (10l or less and pronounceable), Pets that cannot initiate the trade CAN be added to the Roulette pot
However these are not considered VWN pets. If you offer any of these on a Roulette pet, it will not count as that person's pass if they reject.


Additional Rules:

---- No neomails. We do it all on the board.

---- Don't post unless you're willing to play and trade this pet for a VWN basic! You never know what you will get, so don't put a pet into the pot that you would regret playing with!

---- You may put multiple pets in the Pot.

---- You can offer your pet in the pot on another pet in the pot.

---- You don't need a pet in the Pot to offer and you can offer on a pet at any point during the game.

---- You may offer multiple pets on a pet in the Pot, but this counts as ONE offer. If you're rejected on that pet you may not offer again on it.

---- If you leave, please list your pets so we can remove them from the pot.

---- If all of the pets someone bids on a pot pet cannot initiate the trade, this offer will NOT use up the listers ONE PASS.

---- If you put a custom in the pot or offer a custom on your offers, you MUST state the exact amount of that custom (Example: Dawg the Spotted gelert + 1 mil custom)
