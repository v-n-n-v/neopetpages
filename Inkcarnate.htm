Purple Melton


First up is the Purple Melton! The inspiration for this Petpet is Wraith Neopets such as the Wraith Acara, and the Blue Melton. When I look at the Blue Melton, I can't help but think how awesome it'd look in that Wraithy pinkish-purple. So I drew it.

Disco Ghostkerchief


Next is the one I've wanted for a very long time. A bit of a different take on Disco, this one focuses solely on paisley, as found on a classic Hankerchief or Bandana. I always thought of it in red, though a dark blue would work as well. Just so long as you get that classic paisley feel. The designs are based on some hankerchiefs I own. The paisley around the closest eye is inspired by the Spotted and Starry Ghostkerchief.

Possible Release Holidays

  • Kau Day - June (because cowfolk)
  • Halloween - October (because spooky)
  • Disco Neopet release - any month (because disco)

Zombie Bloop


What I love about Bloops is their iconic, clean, and cartoony style. As I was thinking about more Petpets I could design, I came across the Bloop, and thought, there's something there. Their eyes in particular have that classic black and white animation style. So I came up with the idea of honoring that spririt by creating an undead Bloop with "x" eyes. I thought about adding some bandages to the tail, but in the end decided against it. There's just something about Bloops that screams simplicity, and I wanted to stick with that here as well.

Possible Release Holidays

  • Petpet Day - June (because petpet)
  • Halloween - October (because spooky)
  • Zombie Neopet release - any month (because zombie)
