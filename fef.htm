You were wild once, don't let them tame you.

Hello! Welcome to Little Uglies, a directory for mutant, snot, swamp gas, and zombie Neopets! This directory mainly serves as a place to adore these odd pets; so there's not much information about them. Candy (candy_fizz) runs this with her darlin' Fef, and we hope you enjoy this site as much as we do!

Why call it Little Uglies?
Because even if they may be conventionally ugly, or a little ugly, they're still very, very, very cute! It is meant to be an endearment.

Why did you open Little Uglies?
Fef and her older brother are both mutants, and after seeing the stigma around Neopia against mutants and other similarly odd Neopets, she wanted a little place where they could show off! Candy finds these colours somewhat underrated, so kudos to all of you who own one of these awesome Neopets.

How can I get a Little Ugly?
Candy provides information to the left of each section, but she's also collated some helpful pet pages and put them at the bottom of the sitely.

Here, the pet images link to pet lookups, pet names link to petpages, and owner names link to userlookups. All the Neopets are grouped by colour, then in alphabetical order; hovering on some pets will give you more information.

Got a mutant pet? Why not see if you can help model for JN and DTI.

Want to peruse without looking at other peoples' pets? Click on one of the images below.

Check out some directories for specific mutants!

We hope you enjoy your stay here! - Candy & Fef

Rules & Form

Want to get listed? Please wait until Candy posts a board. Just fill read the rules and fill in the form! Once you're done, send it to Candy who'll then put it up for you. Feel free to check Candy's userlookup to see if she's active.

EXTRA! EXTRA! Fef can approve your pet! If you would like your little ugly to be a friend of Fef's, please say so and I'll put her button in their blurb.

DISCLAIMER: All little uglies are automatically approved by Fef.

Don't have an ugly pet? Got an adoptable instead? Candy will list them too!
