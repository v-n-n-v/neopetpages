【 WELCOME! 】


Welcome to The Prancing Unpopular !

A project designed to give visibility and affection to pet species from Neopia that aren't that popular amongst users!

Although some people might disagree with the referenced species on our page, our main goal is to host pets that aren't among the TOP tier pets, especially when it comes to customization where Aishas, Blumaroos, Draiks, Ixis, Kacheeks, Peophins, Unis, Usuls and Xweetoks tend to be people's favorites.

Here you can find:

➥ Directory of the species we are hosting;
➥ Featured Pet we choose a new one every month;
➥ Polls to get feedback from our community;
➥ Customization Contest with monthly rounds;
➥ Gallery of the species we are hosting



〔 Read more about those on their pages 〕



【 RULES 】


PLEASE READ THE RULES CAREFULLY BEFORE ADDING YOUR PET

➥ The pet must be a Buzz, Jetsam, Jubjub, Kiko, Koi, Moehog, Nimmo, Ogrin, Scorchio, Skeith, Techo, Tonu or Tuskaninny
➥ The pet must belong to you and be permanent
➥ The pet cannot be a lab rat
➥ Invisible pets will not be eligible

--------------------

DISCLAIMER ABOUT NOSTALGIC PETS
Once again, we have decided to change our policy about UCs/Notalgic Style pets due to the latest events in that field.
We now have 2 distinct directories: one for regular, fully customizable pets and the other one only for Nostalgic pets.

NOTES
➜ We will check weekly our listed pets to look out for any pet that has changed its species. Ideally, if you change your pets' species, do let us know!
➜ None of the listed pets are UFA/T, so please don't bother their owners about it!
