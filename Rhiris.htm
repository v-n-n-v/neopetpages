RULES
1. Your account must be at least 4 months old.
2. You must have a pet the SAME COLOR as the clothes you are seeking (otherwise the clothing will not transfer).
3. You MUST have an IN and an OUT transfer for the month (any pet that's pounded loses its clothing).
4. If you read the rules, please mention your favorite petpet somewhere in the mail.
5. Remember that pets can only be user-to-user transferred once per day so I will have to return your pet to you the following day.
