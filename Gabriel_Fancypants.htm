About This Page

Oh, I should mention that this page is dedicated to the study of Neopia's unique foods and their properties! Windstar has been very curious about them for a while, and I'm honored to help her by listing them here, of course! It's clever, really, she not only reviews the description of the food itself, but the other dishes that said food is used in- in order to come up with a more conclusive description... She may take a description request if you ask nicely!

A

AZZLE



Properties: Mildly Smelly, Sweet, Strong

Ripe upon splitting, this fruit is often paired with bitter ingredients for interesting results!


B

BAGGUSS



Properties: Dried, Diluted, Faintly Chocolate, Gassy unless dried, Stinky

Bagguss is filled with gas and is otherwise unappetizing unless degassed/dried fully. It's prone to popping if mishandled prior to becoming ripe. Some boil ripe bagguss, however...



BLUCHARD ROOT



Properties: Bland, lol (this is its only descriptor)

It's incredibly bland, and used best as an ingredient for stew... Though, people DO often pickle them and serve them in dumplings or as a platter of sorts!


C

CHOKATO



Properties: Chocolatey, Tomatoesque, Versatile, Non-Spicy, Fruity, Distinct "Salad/Dessert" Smell, Non-Salty

Neopians are obsessed with the flavor and smell, and put chokato in literally Anything! It's even used for babyfood, makeup, bath products, desserts, snacks, and so forth...


D

DARGIL MEAT



Properties: Fruit, Meaty, Hairy

Looks can be deceiving, and perhaps taste can be as well! This "meat" is, in all actuality, a fruit— and is used in some baked goodies!

I

ISLANDBERRY



Properties: Juicy, Signature Smell, Healthy, Fresh, Tasty, Crunchy

Commonly found on Mystery Island, it can be served grilled, baked, and has an interestingly multicolored appearance in most beverages!


J

JUPPIES



Properties:
Cocoa- Chocolate, Decadent, Addicting
Gold- Refreshing
Red- Spicy
Purple- Sour, Intolerable
Teal- Bitter, Intolerable, Healthy

There's a different juppie for EVERYTHING, folks! Cocoa and Gold are among popular choices for baked goods or warm drinks... Red gives a spicy kick and is best for cheeses.... and even the less appetizing of juppies, purple and teal, have something to offer as well in the form of healthy snacks!

P

PHEAR



Properties: Icy, Tangy, Tasty, Fresh

Phear is a happy result from having crossbred pear with snowberries-- and is perfect for a variety of refreshing drinks! Said drinks range from juice, to coffee, to even MILK!


Q

QANDO FRUIT



Properties: Cool, Refreshing, Distinct, Sweet

This Qasalan fruit is highly favored in both breads and baked goods, as well as drinks such as punches and teas.. boasting inclusion in over 18 known cooking recipes across Neopia! It's a Fun Ingredient for Ice Cream and Pizza as well!

S

SNOWBERRY



Properties: Icy, Cold, Tasty, Intense Fruity Flavor, Refreshing/Fresh

A Tropical Fruit with a taste that lies on the polar opposite from that of the heat, Snowberries are not only a popular flavor in frozen treats- but also as icing for baked goods, as well as a flavor for Teas and Drinks! Snowberries are also one of two of the components to the Crossbred Fruit, Phear.



SOURMELON



Properties: VERY Sour, Tangy, Refreshing, Healthy, Strong

Sour is no exaggeration of this fruit, with Sourmelon being the very definition of its namesake! Regardless how extreme, Neopian have taken to the fruit and include it in baked goods, Teas and Brews, and even Stew to add that sour kick! Sourmelon is also a component to Co-Melon, Neopia's "response" to Kiwifruit!



T

TCHEA FRUIT



Properties: Dual-flavored by color (blue likened to banana; yellow likened to blueberry), Bold, Creamy, Sweet, Healthy, Fruity, Fresh

Popular for its dual-flavoring, it is a common Sakhmet delicacy! Neopians play with the taste for both meal and dessert, and may even eat them straight up! Sometimes served aged or as various teas and brews...



THORNBERRY



Properties: Juicy, Sweet, Sour, Bold, Fresh, Sugary, Fragrant

Thornberry, being the ever-sweet and ever-juicy fruit that it is, is used in a wide array of Candies, Desserts, and Brews alike... making it ever so apparent that Neopia can and WILL fight the thorns of this fruit just to eat it! The thorns are annoying at best and not super sharp, but will still get the better of one if they're not careful. Other observed uses include pasta dishes, as well as being paired with Lemon Flavored items!



TIGERSQUASH



Properties: Tangy, Tasty, Distinct, "Ferocious", Tropical, Thick, Healthy

Presumably makes one ROAR with delight! Commonly used for both its taste and thick texture for things like jam and icing— making it perfect for desserts!


U

UMMAGINE



Properties: Root, Grows FAR Underground, Rare, Sweet, Fresh, Healthy

Ummagine is rarely dug up from where it grows. It is commonly served grilled, and also as juice or tea!


V

VOIDBERRY



Properties:Odd", Scentless

An Indescribable taste or lack thereof...
...can be extracted from as a cure for Spyder Bite.
