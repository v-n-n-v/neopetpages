Welcome

Welcome to The Forest, a pet directory dedicated to all Woodland pets. I aim to list as many woodland pets as possible so all of these pets have so time in the sunlight. Why not take a peek at and get ideas for your own pets? We currently have 152 pets listed with a goal of listing listing 250 pets.

Listing Your Pet

To list your pet, please read the following rules:

#1 - To list, neomail me the name and species of your pet. I don't have a preference of how you send this information, just be sure that the information is there!

#2 - Your pet cannot be UFT/UFA/being labbed. I will only list permanent pets. If this changes, please neomail me to remove your pet.

#3 - I have no restrictions on the amount of pets you can list but they all must belong to you. I will not list a pet without the owner's permission.

#4- By default, I will link to a pet's lookup. If you would rather have me link to your pet's petpage, please let me know and I'll see what I can do.

#5 - Please check to see if your pet is already listed. Sometimes pets are moved around and I am not notified of it.

#6 - Pets wearing a pet style to look like a unconverted robot will go to the crosspaint section. Additionally, any pets that are wearing a pet style for a colour other than robot but are still robots without the style will go to the crosspaint section.

FAQ

Is there a limit to how many pets I can list?
There is no limit to how many pets you can list, as long as they all belong to you!

My friend is holding some of my pets for me on their account. Is that okay?
Yes, as long pet belongs to you and you're not listing somebody else's pet.

I change my pet's customization a lot. Do I have to neomail you when I change my pet's customization?
No. With the very rare exception, I can use the URL that automatically updates your pet's image here whenever you change it.

My pet isn't wearing any clothing. Can I still list them?
Yes, your pet's state of dress doesn't matter.

My pet doesn't have a character. Can I still list my pet?
As long as your pet is painted woodland, they don't need to have any sort of character. Only pets that are not painted woodland need some sort of character.

My pet has a ___________ character. Does this still count?
Possibly! I go case-by-case when looking at characters, so please neomail me and I'll decide after looking. However, your character definitely has to have something to do with the forest. They might be part of the forest, they might have their entire lives based in the forest, etc.

Your inbox is full! Where else can I contact you?
My inbox is pretty big but if it's full, you can either toss an item at me or you can try neomailing any of my sides: snowflakes_side, dioji_, a_l_l_3_y, and lemongummiesbears. However, I do encourage you to wait or toss an item at me because my main is the fastest way to contact me.

Woodland Characters

Do you have a character that belongs in the forest or is part of forest? Are they painted a colour other than woodland? Then this is the place for you! Here, we list all of the pets that have forest-based characters, without being painted woodland.

#1 - Your pet must still follow the rules on the welcome page.

#2 - Your pet must have evidence of your character somewhere, whether it is a full petpage or a quick blub on their pet lookup. Customization are not enough.

#3 - You must tell me where your character is. If you have a lookup blurb, then I'll link to your lookup. If you have a full petpage for your pet, then I'll link to your petpage.

I will go case-by-case for all characters so neomail me to see if I'll accept! We are currently listed 6 characters.
