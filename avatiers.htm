This is Avatiers, the one-stop avatar reference guide for collectors, lenders, and random passersby alike! For far too long the goal of Avatar Chatters has been to obtain The Big Three. What kind of goal is that?! If you ask me it's not a goal worth having when there are 348 total obtainable avatars out there just waiting for you to collect them.

Use this guide to help you decide what avatar you should try for next, or to help you decide whether someone has put enough effort into their account to warrant you lending them, or for anything else you can think of. New features for optimized usefulness will be added regularly- if you have any suggestions, neomail them to tizzlestix!

Oh, and here's a button for you to use if you'd like!
There are avatars that are considered "clickables." These avatars cost no neopoints, call for no minimum account age, and require no effort other than visiting a specified location or typing in a certain phrase. These avatars are must-haves, and there is zero excuse for them eluding your collection.

Last, but not least, is the worst of the worst- the hardest of the hard. The avatars that make up Tier 4 require what is considered enormous amounts of time, effort, and dedication. A few avatars listed here are lendable- and considering their cost, that is how most people obtain them. Lending is part of the spirit of avatar collecting, and if you've made a valiant effort to show your dedication to collecting, your time for these lends will come! Lendable Items

Here you have them- The Big Three. Though the prices are always changing, each of these items costs more than 100m at present. If you only listen to one recommendation from this guide, let it be this one: Do NOT overtly seek lends for these items before you have completed all of Tier 1, all of Tier 2, and most of Tier 3. If you paid a ton for an item, would you really want to lend it to someone who still hadn't spent 40k on the Wishing Well avatar? Probably not.
MSPP

Equip your Grarrl with a Bony Grarrl Club. Super Attack Pea!
