✘ If you are not on the art chat (or premium boards), you are not allowed to bump your board with a bc signature. That means no smilies, no X, no bump!, no random lyrics from a song, you are only allowed to post with a bc sig when you are "CONTRIBUTING TO THE CONVERSATION".
