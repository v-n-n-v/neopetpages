Welcome

Hello there, and welcome to The Fortunate Ones Adoption and Dream Granting Agency! Here we aim to connect pets with their forever homes and help Neopians to get their dream pets. We offer adoptions, pet zapping, magma dipping, and, on occasion, Rainbow Fountain dips or species changes. Let us know what your dream pet is, and we will help you get it!
We try our best to have this page updated within 48 hours of notification of any changes - if you mail about a pet and they have already been adopted or you do not receive a response right away when updating a pet, we do apologize for the inconvenience. Please remember to be mindful that we all have jobs and other outside commitments that may pull us away from neo at times - Thank you!

Want to Join Us?

We'd love to have you join our group if you would like to help us grant those dream pets! Do you have a lab ray? Do you know your Magma Pool time? Would you like to just help us advertise and bump our boards on the PC? Let us know! If you're interested please send us a neomail. :)

Our History

The Fortunate Ones was started by our founders, Otto and Mia. They put in a ton of time and effort to conceptualize, create, organize, and run the agency to get things off the ground. The name Fortunate Ones was chosen specifically to carry on the legacy of their former long-time running guild, Fortune's Footpath, which they ran many years ago and hold very dear to this day.

Due to real life situations, our well known page location is currently inactive. Our Adoption Agency page is now located here at /~FortunateOnes and our Dream Granting page can be found at /~Clurisa. We apologize for any confusion or inconvenience this may cause. However, we are eager as ever to help you with your adoptions and dreams!

Rules and Information

Please take a minute to read through this section

1. Please contact the owners of each pet directly. As an agency we do not have any say in the final choice of the owner of any pet in question. Each owner is solely responsible for the decision of who to adopt or trade their pet to.
2. In order to "apply" for a pet, please neomail the owner with a little bit of information about yourself, potential plans for the pets, etc.
3. If one of your pets is adopted, please let us know. This way the page can be updated and you will not continue to receive messages regarding your pets that are no longer available.

Add a Pet

If you would like to have your UFA pet added to our agency please send a neomail to us with the pet's name and your username you'd like to be contacted on. You do not need to be in our guild to list in the agency. You can list as many pets as you would like, just be sure to update us once your pet has been adopted! Pets may be removed if they have been listed with us for more than 30 days. If you notice that your pet has been removed but is still UFA just let us know to re-add them!
