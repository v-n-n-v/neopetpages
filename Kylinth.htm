Welcome

My name is Ella and I'm unbelievably name-picky. If a pet's name is the most important thing to you, then this petpage is for you (and me)! It lists stuck pets that are VWN/WN—names that are capitalized, pronounceable, and with no numbers or underscores. However, they are not separated by species or color.

Did you adopt one of these pets? Want to submit a stuck pet? Send me a NM!

Last update: June 26, 2024

Key

(RW) Real Word
(SBD) Pet has some BD stats (level, strength, defense, or hit points 20+)
(aged) Pet was born before April 26, 2007 (Y9)
