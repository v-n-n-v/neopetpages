Intro


Somewhere in the corner of Faerieland lays a magical meadow just for the Faeries of Neopia. The place is called Flutter Field. Neopians, blessed with magical faerie wings (whether they were born this way, had a magical paintbrush, got a fountain faerie quest, or were lycky with secret laboratory), have a safe place to meet other faeries, regain their strength, rest, practice their magic, or admire thousands flowers on the field. Every other month, among the Faerie neopets, they choose one lucky person who can join the magical Mesmer League and win the title Mesmer of Flutter Field.

Welcome!

Hi there and welcome to Flutter Field, a directory dedicated to faerie neopets! Faerie color has been one of my favorites from the day I discovered Neopia. Gorgeous pastel colors and the fact your pet will gain magical faerie wings, make this color really special. This is why I wanted to create the directory! Flutter Field also has a sister site called please check it out!


Here at the field, you can admire faerie themed fashion and have some inspiration. Every other month one lucky pet will be chosen to have the honor to be part of the Mesmer League and he or she will receive the title Mesmer of Flutter Field and a spiffy trophy! Will your pet be the lucky one? Just send me a request if you want your pet(s) added to our expanding directory!

REQUIREMENTS/RULES:
Your pets have to be painted Faerie.

Your pets have to wear at least two wearables.
