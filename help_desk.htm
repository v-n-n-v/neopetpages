*BOOCHI *

Boochie is a baby bruce that runs around the site randomly zapping a player's active neopet into a baby. This is the cheapest way to get a baby neopet (I mean come on it is free) but it is also very random. There is no way to get Boochie to zap your pet other then luck of the draw. So if you look at your pets one day and notice that one has turned into a baby while you were not looking, the pet most likely got zapped by Boochie. He only zaps your active pet, so keep your least expensive painted pet as your active pet just in case. ~wyckid
