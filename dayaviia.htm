Gorgeous Gelerts

Welcome to Gorgeous Gelerts, a pet directory all about Gelerts! My aim is to list as many Gelerts as possible, as every Gelert needs their time to shine! Every picture is linked up to the Gelert's lookup, so you if you're looking for ideas for your own Gelerts, why not have a peek? We currently have 1396 Gelerts listed and we hope to list 2500!

If you want your Gelert to be listed, just make sure you follow some simple rules:

#1 - To list, neomail me the Gelert's name and colour! I have no preference with how you list this but make sure the information is there.

#2 - There are no restrictions to how many Gelerts you can list but they all must belong to you. I don't list Gelerts without the owner's permission. If you think your friend would be interested, please send them the URL instead.

#3 - Your Gelerts must not be UFT/UFA or being labbed. I will only accept permanent Gelerts. Please let me know if your Gelert will be traded/adopted out so I can remove them.

#4 - I will link to your pet's lookup by default. However, if you would prefer to be linked your pet's petpage, let me know.

#5 - Please check to see that your Gelert isn't already listed. Sometimes, Gelerts get moved around and traded and I'm not always notified of this. Thank you!

**Don't bother the owners of the pets listed in this directory, please! These pets are not UFT/UFA!**

FAQ

Can I list my Gelert in your directory?
You don't need permission to list Gelert! Just neomail me and I'll take care of the rest!

Is there a limit to how many Gelerts I can have?
Nope! List as many as you would like so long as they are permanent pets.

My friends are holding some of my Gelerts. Is that alright?
I don't see a problem as long as there is an agreement between you and the person holding the pet. These gelerts have to be yours and not random Gelerts that you're adding to my directory. I do not like listing pets without the owner's permission.

Your inbox is full! Where else can I contact you?
My inbox is pretty big but if it's full, you can either toss an item at me or you can try neomailing any of my sides: snowflakes_side, dioji_, a_l_l_3_y, and lemongummiesbears. However, I do encourage you to wait or toss an item at me because my main is the fastest way to contact me.

I have some gelerts but I plan on painting them. Should I list them now or later?
That's completely up to you. I can always list now and move your gelerts around later. However, if you plan on painting in the near future (in a couple of days or maybe even a couple of weeks), it's easier if you listed later.

Will my gelert's image change here if I change their customization?
Most of the pictures here will update as you do. There are a couple of times where it doesn't seem to work but for most pets, it should update automatically without me touching anything.

Does my Gelert need to be customized?
Nope! This isn't a customization directory. I understand that not all owners like customization.

My Gelert is a crosspaint. Which colour will it be listed under?
I list pets according to their base colour.

Gelert Hoarders

This is the collection of people who have big Gelert families and want to show them all off in one place! If you want to list your family, follow the rules below:

#1 - You need to have at least four Gelerts for your family to be listed here.

#2 - To be added, neomail your name, your Gelert's names if they are already listed, and your favourite item to act as your icon. If your gelerts are not listed, you will have to list according to the rules on the welcome page.
