LIVING AMONGST THE STARS

Few ever get to actually see the Space Faerie, but everyone knows and appreciates how well she protects Neopia. Through the silent vacuum of space she flies, always wary of forces that may attack from outer space (namely, Dr. Frank Sloth).

Watching over and protecting both the Neopian moon of Kreludor and the floating Space Station has made it hard for her to keep her eyes on everything that's going on in the space around Neopia, but through her tireless efforts, the Space Faerie has repeatedly halted Dr. Sloth's attempts to overthrow Neopia. If you look up at night, and think that you see a shooting star whiz through the sky, it just may be the Space Faerie flying through the cosmos.

Hello there, Neopian, and welcome welcome to Katie's Fonts, run by yours truly. Katie's Fonts was established on December 21st, 2009, and has truly blossomed as a site since then. Please, do take a look around. Maybe you'd enjoy browsing the premades? Perhaps request a font? Have you come to get your font reviewed? Whatever you need, Katie's Fonts is here to guide you!

    FONT REVIEW FOR Amy


    Fontfaces: The font for the NeoHTML is good and easy to read. I also like the fontface that "Amy" is in, however, I don't recommend it for the part that says "The fish lover". I feel like Georgia italics size 1 or 2 would be good for this part of the font. (3/5)

    Colors: Sadly, the colors have no correlation to the avatar in use. I recommend Sunnyneo's eyedropper for tasks such as this. (0/5)

    Words: Normally, I wouldn't recommend putting something like that in your font, but it goes pretty well with the avatar and your pet. (4/5)

    Overall: I think that the current font is a good idea, and with a couple of coloring and fontface tweaks, the font will end up being very pretty. (3/5)
