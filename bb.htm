MY TOY BRUCE

TOY BRUCE

Inspiration

My inspiration for this design comes from the fluffy plushie baby penguin toys around when I was a kid. With movies like March of the Penguins and Happy Feet, emperor penguins became very well known, especially their adorable fluffy babies.
The beak and feet patterning come from the sparkle material used by TY Beanie Boo plushies which tend to use a sparkly, sequiny looking material in elements of their plush.
The tartan bow I chose as a reference to those used on many teddy bears, to give my design a classic and cosy, and "tie up" the design.
I believe the natural colours of this toy Bruce will give emperor penguin lovers an outlet and allow many fun customisation options as the greys should match with many different wearables!

Made official for Bruce Day Y24!

How did they see your design?
I made this petpage with the original art and myself and others who liked the design shared it and requested the design be made on the monthly suggestions board in site events.

Did you get any compensation?
I received a toy paint brush, and items from my gallery wishlist posted in my gallery - Vullard, Black petpet paint brush and Tyrannian Mallard.

The reception of the toy bruce has me in such warm and fuzzy feels today. Getting shouted out on the news page, AND facebook/twitter by the official neopets pages, and literally rocking up to the boards to see one titled "thank you draik_bianca" and people needing to immediately trade for a toy bruce just has me like *blush* (and I think Adler did a great job with their version, I love my pet I painted, but a lot of people saying they like my design better so matter-of-factly is just super flattering)

Thank you TNT for finding inspiration from my design and creating such an awesome pet from it!
