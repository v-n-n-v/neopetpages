welcome to the parade!

Welcome to the Neopets Beauty Contest LGBTQ Pride Parade! This is a user-hosted BC theme week! Users can submit their pride themed artwork to the Beauty Contest for the week of June 24th to July 1st 2022!

The first Pride Parade was a BC theme week event from February 12, 2021 to February 19, 2021 where Neopians showed off their LGBTQ pride via art! This theme week was in honor of TNT reversing their stance on LGBTQ topics and on the site. Although Pride is in June, LGBT Neopians felt it fun to celebrate our pride earlier than usual to celebrate the lifting of the ban.

The second Pride Parade was held during Pride Month of that same year from June 11, 2021 to June 18, 2021!

Celebrate LGBTQ Neopians!

For the longest time on Neopets, LGBTQ topics were completely banned by the Neopets staff, as it was thought to fall under politics that interfered with the "family-friendly" site. While members of the LGBTQ community know that reasoning was discriminatory for while, the rules finally changed in 2020. On December 18th , 2020 TNT heard the peoples' request for change and lifted the ban on LGBTQ topics. The Pride Parade in the Beauty Contest is an official TNT accepted celebration of our LGBT pride.

Thank you!

Thank you for celebrating pride with us and reading! I hope you enjoy the lovely artworks here!
