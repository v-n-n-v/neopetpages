Roo of the month
~ July 2023



Pet Name: Muihlla
Colour: Woodland
Owner: emifero

Hello and welcome to Fangrools, a Blumaroo fan club / customisation directory collaboration between Daisy and Georgie. We created this space on 12th August 2021 (Fangrools' birthday!) for Neopia to showcase their lovely Blums.

Fangrools is a mash-up of Fangirls and Blumaroo, so if you're here to admire the Blumaroos, you can also count yourself as a Fangrool!

We welcome Blumaroos of all shapes and sizes to this directory. We just ask that your Blum be...

  • fully customised
  • a permanent pet

    • Your Blumaroo will be removed from the lookbook if they change species or aren't customised ~ sorry!

    If this sounds like your 'Roo, please send the following information in a neomail to _georgi_03_ or haisydeath:

  • Blumaroo name(s)
  • Blumaroo colour(s)

    • Your 'Roos can be UC or converted. If you would prefer to list your Blum without their name being public, we are happy to do so! Just let us know when neomailing. ^^
