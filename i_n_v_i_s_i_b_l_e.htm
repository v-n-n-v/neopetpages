Invisible Art Directory

  • I'm obsessed with collecting and seeking out adoptable art on Neopets. Building a giant adoptable directory showed me that there are so many ways to draw invisible pets! Some require a dark background to be seen and some need the opposite. Sometimes it's a blank image, which is fun too!

  • To view the invisible adoptables click here, BUT we warned the second half are in a white box which may be hard on your eyes after scrolling through the black section. Sometimes PB images don't load, sometimes they do.

  • After creating i_n_v_i_s_i_b_l_e a pirate/invisible crosspaint Aisha, I decided to give my prior built collection its own page. Invisible Aishas are on his lookup.
