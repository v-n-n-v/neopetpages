A Cry For Custard

Custard is a color that needs a revival, there are so many yummy custard desserts that aren't shown true justice on Neo.

Mmmmm THIS is custard

Pudding or Ice cream, whatever custard means native to you, either way custard is shiny smooth and has delicious cute toppings like fruit or caramel. There ARE appealing delishous custard items on neo so why not for painted pets?


Custard Cheesecake anyone?

So why Custard? Why of all things does this need attention?
To revive and respect an old color
No one thinks about custard. How many Custard pets have you seen that aren't abandoned, crying. Forgotten, or quickly zapped or painted over. Who is asking for custard dream pets?

Did you know Custard became a zap-able color in 2004? That is old!....

You didn't know there wasn't actually a Custard Paint Brush did you?


But there IS one for petpets!... Does this brush look like a dessert to you?

If you were colorblind and I said this was a clay or dung brush do you think you may believe me?



See, in conclusion all I want is instead of leaving old designs in the past, to create something new and refreshing. Bring it up to today's standards. Make Custard an EXCITING zap. Make people WANT to own these cuties .

Make Custard color people want to eat up!

!!! DISCLAIMER: I will not try to redo the old existing designs, but to ask to take the pets that are not Custard already to be taken in this direction!!!
