F.A.Q
Frequently Asked Questions
My page fits into more than one section on The Directory. How do you choose where to list it?
Your page will be listed under all of the categories it fits under!

How is the order of the buttons determined?
They are listed in the order they are added.

What if I don't have a button, but I want to get listed here?
Don't worry, I still list pages that do not have buttons yet! Pages with buttons usually get more visitors though, so you might want to consider getting one!

I am listed with no button, but I have a button now! Will you update my listing?
Absolutely! Mail me and I will gladly update your spot with your brand new button!

What are the requirements for being a recommended page?
To become recommended the petpage need to be up-to-date and have a nice organized layout that is easy to navigate and understand with proper crediting and link back buttons. Pages with outdated information, broken links, lack of credit, or difficult/broken layouts are not eligible.

I am already listed here but I'd like to become a recommended page. What do I do?
If you would like to suggest your page to be upgraded to recommended, please mail for me to review! Please note that not all pages can be considered recommended, do not take it personally! If there is a problem with upgrading your page to recommended I will mail you to discuss the issue!
