Acronym Guide

Name Formats

(Note: names are subjective - formats are a guide only!)

RW = A real-life word (note: may be very obscure)

4L = Four letter name

4C = Four character name (includes one or more numbers/underscores)

VWN = Capitalized; Pronouncable; 10 letters or less

WN = Uncapitalized and/or longer than 10 letters; Pronouncable

Compound = Consists of two words in either XxxxXxxx format (maximum two capital letters) OR Xxxx_Xxxx format (maximum 1 underscore)

DN = Unpronouncable OR More than 2 capitalized letters (including all-caps) OR Framed names (e.g. _Xxxx_ ); Maximum 2 underscores, no numbers

BN = Everything else

Other Acronyms

BD = Battledome stats

Y2 = Born in year 2 (2000)

Y3 = Born in year 3 (2001)


P2 = Petpet
(On some species, you can't tell their age from petpages. If they have a petpet I've included its age instead - the pet will be at least as old as its petpet.)
