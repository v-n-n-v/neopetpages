Have you ever wondered why people put links on their petpages? People don't actually click on those, do they? Yes, actually. Some of us have discovered the joys of spelinking.

Main Entry: Spel•ink•ing
Pronunciation: spel INK ing
Function: noun
Date: 2007
Etymology: From 'spelunking' (the exploring of caves) and 'link' (in the context of the Internet) coined by Neesboy.
: the exploring of the Internet, especially petpages on Neopets, by following links to new pages- often involves the creation of many tabs or windows

How does it work? Start with one or more of the nifty petpages below. Enjoy the art and the content, then move on to any links the page may have. Make a new tab or window for any links that seem interesting, and repeat those steps for all your new pages. WARNING: On some computrs your brower may crash if you open too many tabs or -blocked- Firefox, and sometimes Safari, will often save your tabs before crashing and give you the option of restoring them. Stay clear if Internet Explorer, because it is evil.

There's more room on this quilt, so neomail me with suggestions. The pages should be generally enjoyable. Art, stories, and other cool stuff is encouraged. Sloppy things and bad/nonexistant coding are discouraged. All pages need a 50x50 link.
