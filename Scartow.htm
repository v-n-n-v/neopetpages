Welcome!

Cosplay Pets is a page dedicated to showcasing all of the talented
owners across Neopia that fashion their pets into iconic characters!

This is in honor of their dedication to their fandoms.
Hopefully you'll gain some inspiration and we'll be able to add your pet someday!

(Pssst, thank you Queen Potema for helping with the coding!!)

If anyone would like to help spruce up the page,
please let me know! You will be fully credited!
