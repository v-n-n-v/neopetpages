The Art of An Unusual Crossover

This is the roster of characters so far for my Neopets fiction. It is *supposedly* in the order that these characters appear, but I do not guarantee that.

Main Characters

Keemiter

Name: Prince Keemiter of Hathidesa
Age: 18 years old
Land of Origin: Shenkuu
Family: The Raja and (late) Rani of Hathidesa
Friends:
Enemies: Colorful
Story:

A young prince of a small mountain nation underneath the vast empire of Shenkuu, Keemiter once lived a peaceful, pompous life, until the fateful day when a monster came to his nation, ravaged his city, wrecked havoc in his family's palace and committed the ultimate sin. Ever since, he has sworn to take revenge on this Kougra who came by, and left his kingdom to train and track the wretched shadow witch down. He has been following along the path of destruction she has been leaving behind for months now, which has been leading him down the tenebrous Haunted Woods, an evil forest he has only heard of in stories before. Where and how will this quest end? He doesn't know, all that he knows is that it is too late to turn back now, and return to the life he once had.

Story:

This young witch was once the single child of an infamous Altadorian patrician family, notorious for having sided with the Darkest Faerie back on the day, and, despite its head having asked for forgiveness of King Altador himself, had become a social outcast in Altadorian society. So it was no wonder that her disappearances, and later on the family's and their house's demise went on unnoticed for months on end. As a matter of fact, it was assumed she had too perished in the fire that burned on the countryside house, until, that is, reports of a strange creature with shadow powers similar to her father's, but far more dangerous and uncontrolled and powerful, started showing up in other places, including on a small Eastern province of the Shenkuuvian Empire where a small country of Elephantes exists. Other rumors also went around previously, of a similar creature ravaging a small village North of the Haunted Woods, at the foot of the mid-continent mountain range, where an outcast Dark Faerie lived. But a more recent reporting places her and her shadow projections somewhere in the depths of the vast Haunted Woods, seeking helpless victims, and heading towards Neovia, unfortunately.
