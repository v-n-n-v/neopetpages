A Grarrl Directory

Grarrls are ferocious creatures, or at least they try to be. With lots of care and attention, Grarrls can grow to be some of the strongest Neopians. They are known for their sharp claws and teeth, and their powerful jaws can crush even the toughest bones. If you are looking for a pet that is both strong and loyal, then a Grarrl is the perfect choice for you.
