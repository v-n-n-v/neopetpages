» D is for Dress to Impress

Get into customisation! It's fun to dress your pets and gives you a chance to be creative. You can also enter and vote in the Customisation Contest!

» E is for Employment Agency

Have your pet sign up to do jobs at the Faerieland Employment Agency to earn neopoints. There are both basic jobs and super jobs (which require either a job coupon or a Brightvale Job Coupon) available.

» F is for Food Club

Food Club is a popular way to earn neopoints. It is a gambling game and involves some risk, but long term offers impressive profits. Check out this guide to learn the fundamentals of the game. Many players follow an experienced bettor when they start playing. Feel free to check out my guide for bets and other resources.

» G is for Games

Beginning in July 2023, the new TNT leadership team brought back 50 classic flash games by incorporating the Ruffle emulator. A list of these games is found here.

» H is for Hidden Tower

The Hidden Tower, run by Queen Fyora, sells the most prestigious items in Neopia, with prices to match! From elite weapons, to exclusive paint brushes, and more, you'll find them here. Every third Wednesday of the month there is a 3% discount in the tower (for accounts 60 months and older). If you're really lucky you might get the RE which awards a 10% discount!

» I is for Intelligence

Read books to your pets to increase their intelligence. They might even get smart enough to qualify for the Neopian Book Award and/or Booktastic Book Award.

» J is for Join

Consider becoming a member of a guild. There are many diverse guilds on the site where members share a common interest. It's a great way to make friends and to participate in guild activities. There are also groups on the neoboards that you could join.

» K is for Kadoatery

Check out the Kadoatery! You can earn trophies and an avatar by feeding enough kadoaties. Be sure to check out the current kad feeders board in the Games forum for guides, refresh times, and support.

» L is for Loyalty Perks

Did you know that there are perks available for experienced players (i.e., older accounts)? In addition to the HT discount mentioned above, there are extra Neotitles, favorite games, NM extra inbox space, pet transfers, extra trade lots, and more!

» M is for Making Others Smile

A nice thing to do is to be kind and hopefully make another player's day! Random acts of kindness, such as gifting, Secret Santas, quest help, and such will help put a smile on someone else's face.

WHAT IS THERE TO DO ON NEOPETS?

Introduction

Welcome to my ABCs guide about things to do on Neopets! I got the idea from seeing topics cropping up on the neoboards asking for ideas for stuff to do on the site. Much of the time it's new players who ask, but it's not uncommon to see returning players and long time players ask for suggestions as well. The key point is that there are many, many things to do in Neopia. This guide is not intended to be all-inclusive, but it is reasonably comprehensive. Visit Jellyneo for more in-depth guides for specific features, games, events, etc. Regarding navigation, please note that letters after C continue in the left and then right sidebars.

A is for Avatars

Avatar collecting is a favorite pastime for many players, old and new! It's fun and challenging to earn secret avatars. Many are easy to obtain. However, a lot of avatars require time, dedication, or perhaps a ton of neopoints to earn them. If you make the high score list you can also earn a trophy.

B is for Battledome

Train a pet for the Battledome! (Guide 1, 2). There are many reasons why it's worth the effort to train a pet. For starters, if a plot should come along and it features a BD component then you'll want a trained pet to be able to earn plot points. Another reason is that you can earn NP and prizes from daily battles, plus win boons by participating in the biweekly skirmishes.

C is for Coding

Learn HTML and CSS in order to edit your user lookup, pet lookups, shops and galleries, and pet pages. Not only is it fun to be able to code these areas on the site, but it is an actual real life competency that you can use at work or at home. Honestly, if it hadn't been for the opportunity to learn to code on Neopets I doubt I would ever have tried it.

Stay Informed

NEW PLOT!!! Visit this link to participate in The Void Within plot. Also be sure to otherwise Keep up to Date about current happenings. Read the New Features regularly so you are aware of event announcements, new items, new pet colours, contest results, etc. Also, it's a good idea to read the weekly Editorial in The Neopian Times for answers to user questions by TNT staff members.

Keep reading for more things to do.

Site Events

Watch for announcements for site events and plots and take part! Examples include annual events such as the Advent Calendar, Altador Cup, Negg Festival, Faerie Quest event, etc. Plots are less frequent but they are a lot of fun when they happen.

Neoquest I and II

Do you crave adventure and excitement? Have some time on your hands? Try tackling Neoquest I and Neoquest II! JN has good guides, including related maps. Earn avatars and trophies while you play.

Charity Corner

Charity Corner has become a highly anticipated event on Neopets. Many players hoard items in their SDB (especially r90-99) to donate during the event. In return you can win rare items or perks (note: the rewards system has changed over the years).

Gourmet Club

Feed your pet gourmet foods (r90-99) and have a chance at another trophy! Once you get to a certain point it gets quite expensive to acquire the foods. Getting on the HST is an accomplishment!

Pet Trading

The Pound Chat is where it's at if you are interested in pet trading. You will quickly notice that unconverted (UC) pet trading is especially active. Here is a basic guide. Check out shuffles on the PC as well.

Neocash Trading

Do you have Neocash (NC) items that you don't need and would like to trade for something else? Then NC trading may be the answer. Visit The Neocash Hub for basic information, NC values, lingo, and other help. You can also find NC shuffles on the NC neoboard.

Art and Design

Are you the artistic type? Love to draw and paint? Create artful layouts? Submit your artistic creations to the Art Gallery or the Beauty Contest, or enter one of the site spotlights.

Neoboards

Like to chat? Have a question? Want to show off your latest avatar? Then spend some time on the Neoboards. Be sure to stay on topic and follow the chat rules. Set preferences here.

Pet Adoptions

Many players enjoy surfing the pound in search of pets to adopt. Often they will zap the pet until it is painted a nice colour and then return it to the pound or adopt it out. Another pastime in the pound is looking for stuck pets.

Stamp Album

Stamp collecting is a challenging and expensive hobby, but can be quite rewarding as well. Currently there are 23 stamp album related avatars available, and you can also earn a trophy! Collect stamps, charms, evil coconuts, coins, sea shells, and scarabs.

Plushie Tycoon

Ever want to be a tycoon? In Neopia you can become one by playing Plushie Tycoon! Each month a new competition begins, so it is best to begin on the first of the month. Be sure to visit the current Plushie Tycoon help board in the Games forum for helpful guides and support.

Neodeck

Start a card collection and put them in your Neodeck. Collectable cards restock at the card shop. You can remove these cards from your neodeck at any time if you decide you want to sell them later (unlike stamps). If you collect enough cards to get on the high score table you may be eligible for a trophy!

Rubbish Dump

The Rubbish Dump in Meridell was recently optimized (April 2022). If you are both lucky and persistent at refreshing in the dump, you may be able to score the Rubbish avatar, and possibly a rare item in the process! The Rubbish avatar can be awarded by obtaining either a petpet, fading bottled faerie, paint brush, apple core, Everlasting Apple, or dung catapult.

Almost Abandoned Attic

Another loyalty perk, the Almost Abandoned Attic is accessible ONLY to accounts 36 months and older. The Attic is located in Neovia and specializes in stocking r80-99 items. There is a chance of restocking at 7 minute intervals. Be really FAST, because the Items can sell out quite quickly. Also, there is a limit of restocking 5 items per day.

Gallery

Start a Gallery! Many Neopians have collections of items that they display in nicely decorated galleries. What will it be: plushies, neggs, rainbow items, faerie items, gnomes, or something else? There are tons of items to collect. Galleries can provide a great sense of accomplishment. You could even submit your gallery into the gallery spotlight.

Quest Log

Quest Log was launched as a new feature on Oct. 24, 2023! Complete the five random daily quests to win prizes (NP & items). After seven consecutive days of completing these quests, you will be rewarded with a grand prize. It will then reset for a new week.

Monthly AMAs

Check out the monthly AMAs where TNT staffers discuss current and future happenings connected to World of Neopia, and also answer questions submitted by players. Be sure to watch for appearances by TNT Dom, the CEO.

Jordle

The folks at Jellyneo bring you Jordle, a Neopian take on the popular word game Wordle! Head on over to Jellyneo and give it a try! There are threads on both the Site Games and Help forums where you can post your daily results.

» N is for Non Flash Games

In addition to the playable classic flash games (see "G is for Games"), there are plenty of non flash games available to play. Refer to this guide or this one for links. Try for some new trophies and avatars!

» O is for Obelisk Skirmishes

Participate in the biweekly Obelisk Skirmishes. If your faction wins you can earn an avatar and special boons. Need help picking a faction? Try this guide.

» P is for Petpets

Why not give you pets their own petpets to care for? There are many species and colours to choose from. If your pet keeps it's petpet long enough it might win the Petpet Protection League trophy! While you're at it you could also give the petpet it's own petpetpet.

» Q is for Quest

The faeries are known to give out random quests for items and in return will bestow your active pet stat increases, or in the case of the rare Fountain Faerie Quest, the opportunity to paint your pet almost any colour you want!

» R is for Restocking

Learn to restock in the Neopian shops for big profits! Here is one recommended restocking guide with lots of info and tips.

» S is for Stock Market

Learn to invest in the Stock Market to earn lots of NP! Be aware that the stock market is a long term investment endeavor so patience is required. Here is a good guide.

» T is for Trophies

Work on earning trophies for your userlookup (games, site events, spotlights, contests, etc.), as well as for your pets (eg., Beauty Contest, PPL).

» U is for Usukicon

Usukicon is an annual theme day celebrating Usukis!

» V is for Virtupets

Explore the Space Station, and learn about Dr. Sloth!

» W is for Writing

There are various opportunities to engage in creative writing on Neopets. Consider writing an article, story, or comic to submit to the Neopian Times. Or you could enter the different writing contests: Storytelling, Caption, and Poetry.

» X is for Xandra

Xandra was a character in the Faeries Ruin plot. Visit Jellyneo's Book of Ages to read about the fall of Faerieland, as well as other historical happenings in Neopia.

» Y is for Yooyuball

Yooyuball is the principal game in the annual Altador Cup.

» Z is for Zapping (Lab Ray)

Acquire all nine pieces of the Secret Laboratory Map to gain access to the lab ray. Once you have access you can zap one pet per day. The lab ray can change species, colour, gender, and stats of you pet. Results are RANDOM.
