Themed Beauty Contest Weeks

Themed Beauty Contest weeks are casually user run events that require very little effort to create. Make a board advertising the theme and chosen week. Post the board at least a month before the chosen week. Check the boards to make sure there isn't a themed week during that time. If possible, plan it spaced apart from other upcoming themed weeks (if there are any). Artists can get burnt out participating in multiple BC weeks in a row. Bookmark and bump the board as often as you can, but not more than once a day. Optional: you can make a petpage to gather themed entries. You can also make a badge or trophy for participants to collect from your BC week page.

More Beauty Contest Trophies?

I highly suggest repurposing art of your pets! If you drew a pet for the Art Gallery, got rejected, and don't plan on resubmitting it, then submit it to the Beauty Contest. If you drew your pets for any other reason, you can put them in the BC too.

Just keep these factors in mind: only the species in the art must match the actual pet. The design/color can be completely different. Your pet can't be too human (such as a person with Aisha ears) and your art can't be too realistic in style. An example would be a wocky that looks a lot like a real cat.

Keep a folder of art that won a trophy and a folder of art that didn't. Once a piece of art wins a gold trophy or gets into the Art Gallery it cannot be resubmitted to the BC or AG. Unless you really have your heart set on gold, don't feel pressured to be actively advertising. Unless your species of choice is particularly popular, you're likely to get a trophy.

If you plan on resubmitting a piece until it gets a gold trophy, I recommend spacing it out by at least a couple months. Even longer if you plan on advertising. Even though you are allowed to resubmit entries that didn't get gold (or an AG trophy) it is frowned upon to resubmit it often and you'll get fewer votes.

Beauty Contest Trophies For Pound Pets

You can adopt a pet from the pound, give them a BC trophy, put them back into the pound, or advertise them as UFA on the boards. You can go off of species and/or names you find inspiring!
