Welcome to Celestial

A directory home for Fallen Stars, Aliens and Brave Space Explorers.

Welcome to Celestial. A directory home for all Fallen Stars, Aliens and Brave Space Explorer neopets.

My name's Anna. Since coming back from a hiatus in late 2016, I had been thinking of creating a pet directory of some sort, but never had a solid idea. Around the time of mid 2017 my love for spacey things grew dramatically and I found myself with many space themed and related pets. my star children. Fast forward to late 2018/ early 2019 and I find myself talking with my good guild mate, and friend Olive. We were discussing one day, about pet directory's, and she had showed me hers, and I felt inspired to really do one this time! It was an obvious choice what my theme was going to be! With that, Celestial the space themed and space related pet directory was created!

My Goal for Celestial is for this to be a place to list Permanent Space pets. Pets that qualify for listing are the following: Starry pained pets, Eventide Painted pets, Alien Aishas, and Customized Space themed pets.
My only other requirements for a pet to be listed here is they must be a permanent pet. And are required to be wearing atleast a background! As we dont want our pets listed here getting lost in space!

If you own a neopet that fits one of these categories you may join! If your pet is a listed colour and also a space themed customization they will be automatically listed by their colour!.
Please note: pets applying for the customization section are subjective. I reserve the right to decide whether they fit with the directory's theme. If you are unsure with what this entails please jump down to the customized space pets part to see my pets, and others, who fit the theme!

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Some answers for your questions.

    Q: Why do Grundos not have a specific listing area?
    A: I understand that Grundos are the token space/alien pet of Neopets. But for this directory i personally wanted this to focus more on the colours, and customization than a whole species. I did try to work in Grundos in the beginning but i personally didn't like the idea of having a species section, with only 1 species in it. To me it looked a little weird, and outta place. Grundos can totally still be listed though, provided they fit into one of the categories above!!
