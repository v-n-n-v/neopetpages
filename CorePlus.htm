Welcome to Core+

/kôr/ noun: the part of something that is central to its existence or character.

　 　Greetings and salutations, and welcome to a graphic resource & premade site ran by Taylor. Core has been designed to meet all of your basic editing needs for Neo. While this site offers an array of graphics, these layouts tend to be darker in nature & alternative in theme.

　 　A note from Taylor: ❝ I have been making graphics for on-site unofficial Neopets pages for over 15 years. From guilds to petpages, I have done it all. I am extremely excited for the launch of Core Plus as this officially marks a brand new era of graphic making & design for me! The plus in the name indicates a premium experience and I hope that you as the viewer feel that you get this type of experience from the site. Thank you for stopping by and I hope you might find something that you're looking for! If you have any questions regarding my content, my inbox is always open. ❞
