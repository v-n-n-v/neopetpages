Maelin's Untaken Names

Est. June 4, 2023

Welcome to Maelin's Untaken Names, curated by Nina. This page originally started as a way for me to share names I came across when searching for names for my own pets. As people started to create the names and bring them to life with customizations, I found that helping people find a name for a pet to love was something I derive a lot of joy from. I hope you find one here!

Under the Video Games and Real Names sections, you can hover your mouse over an image (on desktop) to see more details about the origin of the name.

If you don't see a name you like but have a theme in mind, I am happy to take requests! Under each section, I have specified the criteria that will help me most easily search for each type of name. You can Neomail me with your request by clicking the link in the sidebar.
