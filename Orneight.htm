Hello! This is a collection of all the customization items that can be acquired with neopoints!
We focus on the items that all species can wear (*except vandagyres sometimes. Vandagyres can only wear the headwear/clothing/accessories/handhelds that were released after November 14, 2014, when the species itself was released).

If you come across any wearables whose names have a period (.) in it, then please take it out when searching that item. The period is placed there because neopets filters have blocked a certain word from being used. An example would be the Grap.efruit Necklace - simply take out the punctuation mark.

Did you get a nostalgic token for your pet? You can still use items in the Backgrounds and Elements sections, but the items in other sections will not appear/be available.

I am planning to keep this page updated whenever a new non-species-specific wearable comes out :). In case you see the "items updated through" has a future date, that refers to the most recent item's release date according to JN's Item Database.

Please feel free to contact me with any questions, comments, or omissions you may find :).
Neomail me (@forfeitreality) or comment on my board!
