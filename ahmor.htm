Rules

1. Everyone is welcome to participate! You do not have to have a Rainbow pet to submit! They can be whatever color, there are so many different and creative ways to show your pride!
2. Submissions are limited to two (2) pets for the Pride Parade, and two (2) item names per person for the Pride Quilt. Please use the submission form directly to the left of this box!
3. Pride Parade: You can submit any of your pets that you would like, including on your side accounts! I will be linking to their pet details page and using the image from there (you may send me a link to a Dress To Impress image if you would prefer), and you can include some info that you would like to share with everyone about your pet! It can be about anything, from why you picked their outfit, to their favorite hobbies and activities, a short story about them, etc. You can submit one or two pets if you would like.
4. Have fun! I hope everyone enjoys this little project I've put together. I appreciate each and every person who stumbles upon this page!
