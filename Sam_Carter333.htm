Welcome

Hi there! Welcome to the homepage of Amorphous Adoptions, the adoption agency of my Mom, Karen. I'm Sam, the overseer of this project. Sure, Mom might be the big boss, but I'm the one who oversees the day-to-day operations. After all, I have plenty of experience with the lab ray, since I'm a lab pet myself!

Here you'll find the pets who are currently undergoing lab ray treatment and those who have been happily re-homed. After a pet has been zapped, Mom follows these steps:
  1. Checks her guilds' lists of dream pets: ZYDP and The Fortunate Ones.
  2. Checks other dream pet lists (see sitely).
  3. Posts about the pet on Neoboards.
  4. Continues zapping the pet until they turn a different color, then repeats the process again!
If you have any questions, or would like to adopt a pet, just neomail Mom and let her know!
