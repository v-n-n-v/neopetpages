Homes for Every Lost Pet

Where lost pets get a second chance

We're a team of Neopians looking to zap pets found in the pound and give them a second chance! But you don't just have to be a zapper to join us, we still need people to advertise, bump the board, work on CSS, seek out people to adopt our pets, and more! Interested in becoming part of our team? Consider joining us today!

We are an application free adoption agency. All we ask is that you neomail the owner and introduce yourself a little. You can include whatever you like, whether the pet is your dream pet, if you like the name, any future plans, how excited you are... As long as you're polite! We work on a first come, first served basis for the most part, so please neomail the owner right away! Of course, the final decision lies with the owner, so if you're rude, don't have transfers, have a history of adopting just to trade, or have adopted several pets from us, the owner may decide to adopt out to someone else. Most of the time, however; owners adopt out to the first person to neomail them expressing interest!

Our pets are UFA to everyone, not just guild members! You are, of course, welcome to join the guild for any reason but you don't have to be a member just to adopt our pets. (Joining also won't give you an advantage since we work on a first come, first served basis!)

If you have any questions please neomail any council member or post on our board on the PC!

List a Pet

How to add your pet

We prefer (but don't require) that you be a member of the guild to have your pets listed on our petpage. It makes it easier to keep track of all the owners this way. Also everyone is welcome and encouraged to post their UFA pets on our board on the PC (you can find a link on the guild main page)!

Also please remember that if you have any pets listed on this petpage, you need to neomail Evie sometime between the 1st and the 7th of each month to let her know you're still active. When you do so, please double-check the petpage to make sure everything is accurate. We just want to make sure the petpage is up to date. If you do not do so, your pets will be removed. Thank you for your cooperation!


Owners are responsible for verifying the accuracy of their listings!


To have a pet listed on the petpage simply neomail Evie using the forms below! They specify what information is needed depending on the section. Please put multiple forms in the SAME neomail. And update Evie as soon as a pet has been promised to someone!
Evie always replies to let you know when your pet(s) have been added/updated, so if you don't receive a reply please message her again! (Neomails can be glitchy.)



Please double check the spelling of your pet name before you send me the form!
Please make sure you include all information needed, or I won't be able to list your pet. It's best to please fill them out as-is, it really helps me update the petpage as quickly and easily as possible!


Form for UFA pets:
  • You MUST include your username. Each time, no matter what!
  • You do NOT need to include the field names!
  • You can simply put a space between each form entry, instead of putting them all on a new line, if you wish!
  • e.g., Proud Shadow Kougra Male 1st November (Y14) 0o0slytherinpride0o0 Pride is PERFECT
  • Simply copy and paste the birthday directly from the pet's lookup, no changes needed! (When logged into the account the pet is on.)
  • You MUST include Notes: before any notes. And please try to keep them brief!

  • You MUST complete the entire form for EACH pet

FAQ

Check here for the most commonly asked questions!

Questions for members and potential members! Questions for potential adopters!
Q: Can I join the Homes for Every Lost Pet guild?
A: You sure can! We love new members! H.E.L.P. is an open guild and you can join at any time. You don't even need to have the lab ray or any pets UFA! We would just love to have you join the community. The guild is located here. We also welcome side accounts!

Q: What are the requirements to join?
A: We don't have any requirements! You can be as independent as you want, there's no need to post in the guild or on our board in the PC. Of course, you can if you like! We do have some chatty members! However, a lot of our members just neomail Evie to have their pets listed on the petpage. The only real "requirements" are that you do your best to check-in once a month if you have pets listed on the petpage. This is just to make sure the petpage is up to date! Please see the List a Pet Section for more information! Some of our members stay inactive for a while then have pets they want to adopt out - that's fine! Other people join temporarily just to adopt out some pets and then leave the guild (and sometimes come back later!) that's fine too!

Q: Can I list my pets in other adoption agencies or advertise them on the PC?
A: Of course! Our ultimate goal is to help people get their dream pets, so we try to do that the best we can! Just make sure you update us when a pet has been adopted!

Q: I have a pet to list but I'm not a member of H.E.L.P. Can I still list them?
A: Yes, although we prefer you be a member of the guild, you can list your pets even if you aren'!

Q: How do I adopt out my pets through H.E.L.P.?
A: It's pretty simple! You neomail Evie to have your pet listed on the petpage (see the List a Pet Section for the handy dandy links and forms) and respond to any neomails you get from people who want to adopt your pets! The final decision is 100% yours. There's no oversight from guild leadership on the adoption process. We won't ask you who you are adopting your pets out to, we only need to know when they are adopted so the petpage can be updated. However, if you ever need advice or have a question about the adoption process, don't hesitate to reach out to leadership!

Q: What if I have a problem with a potential new owner?
A: Please let Pride know if something happens. We're all independent members but if anything happens, it's best to let her know so she can try and help. We all just want this to go smoothly with no troubles!

Q: Are there any colours that people don't want to adopt?
A: A good rule of thumb is: if the colour costs more than 1 million Neopoints to buy the Paint Brush for, it's a good colour to put up for adoption. For instance, it's not much use listing Snow Neopets. The Snow Paint Brush is worth very little, so most people are not seeking Snow Neopets. Unless your cheaply-painted pet has a REALLY great name that people might want to adopt, you will probably not receive any interest in them. Other cheap/unpopular colours include Split, Checkered, Speckled, Glowing, Snow, Striped, Starry, Purple, Strawberry, Orange, Sketch, Swamp Gas, Cloud, Electric, Fire, Disco, Ghost, Gold, Silver, Relic, Biscuit, Camo, Brown, Christmas, most Pirates (the morphing potions are very cheap due to Anchor Management), Rainbow, Skunk, and all basic colours (Red, Blue, Green, and Yellow). If your pet zaps any of these colours, you probably want to continue zapping to get something better–otherwise it may be very difficult to adopt them out. Please also keep in mind that some Transmogrification Potions (to turn a pet Mutant) are very inexpensive, so not all Mutant Neopets are popular. For instance, the Moehog Transmogrification Potion is less than 100,000NPs.

Q: So what are some good colours to put UFA?
A: If we're thinking about colours that cost more than 1 million Neopoints, we're thinking things like Zombie, Water, Stealthy, Candy, Faerie, White, Darigan, Desert, Woodland, Elderly...etc. Some colours that cost less than 1mil are also popular, like Baby and some Spotted pets (think the cool patchwork on the Spotted Lupe). There are also a bunch of colours that can only be acquired through the lab, and these are also popular. Snot, Ice, Burlap, Robot, Jelly, Chocolate, Mallow, MSP, etc. In fact, MSP can ONLY be acquired through the lab. It's not even available at the Rainbow Fountain from a Fountain Faerie Quest!

Q: I found a name I think is pretty cool that hasn't been created yet. Can I create the pet and adopt it out through H.E.L.P.?
A: We prefer if you not do this, although we understand that there are a lot of reasons to adopt out pets besides ones you adopt from the pound. The point of this guild is to find pets a new home who need one. Whether that means adopting a pet from the pound to zap, adopting out a former permie, or creating an LE pet on their day to zap, let's just help people get their dream pets and find new homes for any pet who needs one! 		Q: How do I adopt a pet from H.E.L.P.?
A: Just neomail the owner! You can include as much or as little information as you want. In general, please write something more than "Can I have your pet?" because those types of neomails seem rather rude. Even something as simple as "Hi, I'm (name) and I've always wanted a (colour) (species), could I adopt (pet)?" can be enough! Some of our members might want to look at your account or want more information. The key is just to be honest and polite!
General tips:
  • Tell us your name
  • Use the foster parent's name! It makes it feel more personal
  • Use the pet's name
  • Use the correct gender of the pet (if you want to change it, that's fine, just say so!)
  • Tell us why you're interested. Is it for the name, the birthday, the colour, the clothes1, the species, all of the above?
  • Tell us if you plan on keeping on your main or a side account
  • Tell us if you plan on morphing/repainting2
1If someone has a pet UFA for a while with no interest and they have clothes, the foster parent may more than happy to give the clothes away and start zapping again. It doesn't hurt to ask!
2For example, someone wanted a Robot pet that Pride had UFA–they wanted to use their Premium Perk on the foster pet because they wanted a Robot pet of another species. However, you don't need to start with a Robot pet to get a Robot via premium perk! If you let us know why you really want the pet, we might just be able to help in unexpected ways.

Q: I see two pets listed that I just love! Can I try to adopt both of them? I've also adopted from H.E.L.P. before... Is that okay?
Of course! Adoption decisions are up to the individual owner, we don't have any limits!

Q: What if I have a problem with a guild member?
A: Please let Pride know if something happens. We're all independent members but if anything happens, it's best to let them know so they can try and help. We all just want this to go smoothly with no troubles!

Q: I don't see any pets UFA that I like right now. How often is the page updated?
A: Evie updates the page roughly once per day. So check back tomorrow and you may see new pets UFA!

Q: I want to adopt a pet from the page, but I don't have any space right now until I can afford a new pet slot/adopt out a pet I own/etc. Will the owner hold the pet for me?
A: Maybe! It doesn't hurt to ask! But it is up to every individual owner whether they want to hold a pet for someone. They do not HAVE to hold a pet for you. This guild is first-come, first-serve, and it is within the owner's rights to adopt the pet out to someone else rather than wait for you. Most of the guild members want to quickly make room for new foster pets. Assume that they won't hold the pet, ask politely, and if you're pleasantly surprised, then that's great! But otherwise, respect the owner's decision to adopt out immediately rather than wait for you.

Q: I don't like the colour/species/stats of this pet, but I like the name. Will you do a custom paint job/morph/BD training for me?
A: No. Absolutely not. We are adopting these pets out for nothing at all, just the goodness of our hearts. We're not receiving anything in return, unlike in a trade. If you don't like the pet the way it is, don't adopt it--or else paint/morph/train it yourself after you've adopted.

Q: Can I adopt a H.E.L.P. pet for a trade?
A: Please, do not do this. While we can't actually stop you from doing what you will with the pet after you adopt it--we really want these pets to go to permanent homes. Please don't trade a pet you adopt from H.E.L.P.. If we find you've adopted a pet just to trade it, you will be banned from adopting from us again. If, after a while, you realize you don't want to keep the pet you adopted--please, adopt the pet out to another user to pass on the giving spirit of H.E.L.P..

Q: I REALLY want a specific colour/species of pet but I don't see it UFA right now. Is there any way you can tell me if you zap one?
A: Yes! We have a dreamie wishlist page, and whenever we get a good zap, we check this page to see if someone has already wished for one! Head over to our Dream Pet List for details. You are also allowed to wish for non-zappable pets, like Babies and Plushies, because occasionally we have users putting hand-painted pets UFA.

Q: I'm really hoping to adopt a Lutari...they're so cute!
A: Aww, Lutaris are cute! And they can be transferred now! YAY! So you can absolutely adopt them from us but only with a user-to-user transfer. Lutaris still run away when they are dropped in the pound. :(
