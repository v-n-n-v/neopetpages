I have no more dreams of my own, but LOVE to trade, so I thought instead of aimlessly trading I can help others. I hope this project goes well!     Rules

    Please read through these rules before listing or granting a dream pet!!

  • You may list UP TO THREE (3) pets to feature on this page. After one (or more) of your dream pets found, you are able to list another.
  • Anyone and everyone is able to grant wishes and list dream pets. If you would like to join the Dream Traders Guild click here
  • If a DreamTraders board is active on the PC/ Pet Trading boards, PLEASE post your listing rather than NMing for the duration of the board. The OP will announce once the board is closed. At that time, please continue to NM your wishes.
  • You will be notified by NM if your dream pet is found. You are not obligated to accept, but please let the person know ASAP
  • If you have a pet that can grant a lister's dream, please refer to Dream Granting
  • Please NM @spearbin if you found your dream pet through DreamTraders or in another way, so that I can remove it from the list.
  • If your dream is granted and you are given the chance to adopt out a pet, PLEASE try to not use it as fodder or retrade. If you grow unattached or no longer want the pet, I ask that you adopt it out to someone else who seeks that pet. You can find them through this project or elsewhere
