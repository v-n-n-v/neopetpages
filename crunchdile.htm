ABOUT
　
Welcome! I proudly present the Krawk-o-Matic. Want to make your very own unique Krawk adoptable? You came to the right place!

But.. how? Once you're done you simply press the PrtScn button and copy it into an image editor like paint, crop it so that there's no green border left and save it as .PNG. And you can have your very own pet Krawk!


All the possibilities...

There are an amazing amount of combinations that you can come up with! Whatever you create is most likely the first of its kind!

FAQs

Q. Who drew everything?
A. All Krawks are drawn by Semmy! The clothes are either drawn by Semmy or edited from official image source. Con had provided additional work as well.

Q. Can I draw for the Krawk-o-Matic as well?
A. Due to the inability to assure the same style of art, I have to say no.

Q. Why does some of the _________ look horrible?
A. Most of them were made in 2013. It's very dated.

Q. Can I suggest wearables?
A. Go ahead and neomail if you like~ I don't guarantee to draw anything, but I will try to when I have time!

Q. Do you make custom adoptables?
A. How much more custom do you need?! I take customs when I'm in the mood.

Q. Feedbacks/Comments/Loves?
A. Just neomail.
