    • Stocks

    The best advise I can give for stocks is buy at 15 sell at 60. When picking what stock to buy the only column you need to worry about is the 6th column labeled "Curr." You can buy 1,000 shares a day, if you buy at 15 you will invest 15,000nps a day. You can check your portfolio to see how your stocks are doing. When deciding on what sticks to sell, you only need to focus on column 4, labeled "Current Price." As long as the number in that column is over 15, you've got a profit.

    To buy stocks, simply click "Buy Stocks" at the top of the page, type in the 3-5 letter ticker. This can be found in column 2, labeled "Ticker." The best way to find stocks selling at 15 is to use the Bargain Stocks page.

    To sell your stocks, you need to be in your portfolio. Click the arrow in the "Icon" column. This will then pull up a subsection of the table showing how many shares you own. In the last column labeled "Sell" you will enter how many shares you want to sell, scroll down and click "Sell Shares.

    This is a waiting game, so don't expect big returns fast. Best practice is to set a sell target and do not sell before you hit that target. Stock links are listed below!

    Battledome

    My go to is Turned Tooth x2, and Lens Flare. I can one hit take down Jetsam Ace on hard with that.

    If you got a Viera Is Bomb, that is a good one too paired with a healer and Static Cling. You definitely have to battle an opponent that you can beat.

    If you are still training and have low level stats, I would battle Chia Clown. If your stats are a little higher, you can go against S750 Kreludan Defender Robot on easy. If you are not Premium, other than the Kreludan Robot the best battle to Codestone ratio comes from battling the Koi Warrior on medium or hard, whereas the Robot also gives out Neocola Tokens. So pick based on what you're mainly going after. Other than the battledome, I am just going to link other player's guides. Battle Ground of the Obelisk guides will be added as well.
