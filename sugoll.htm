Tiki Tack Tombola Tally

I started researching Tombola because I was curious about what prizes could actually be won and how frequent winning tickets were awarded. Like my page on All the Wheels of Neopia, I collected data on 300 spins of the Tombola wheel. Below are MY results with MY prizes. Your prizes will vary. I don't claim that this is a complete guide to all possible prizes, just the prizes I've received over 300 spins. If I find new items, I will add them to this page, but not add them to the tally.

My first surprise in research was that there are only 21 tickets available. Spinning the wheel I never got the sense that the number of tickets was so limited, but if you keep track you'll notice that only 21 tickets are possible. Of those 21 tickets only 4 are winning tickets, because they end with the number 0, 2 or 5.

After tallying the results, Tiki Tack Tombola is truly random.
