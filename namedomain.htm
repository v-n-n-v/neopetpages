about us

Why hello there! Welcome to The Name Domain or TND for short. We were created to offer fresh and unique pet names to Neopians! Nobody wants to have to call there pet.. Jelly One Five Eight Nine Three in public! That would be terrible. Here at the Name Domain we hope to offer a variety of names for YOUR future Neopets. We hope that enjoy The Name Domain, and we hope you find a name that suits your next Neopet! I only ask that you follow the rules below to show some courtesy to myself and all the people that contribute to The Name Domain! Enjoy, and happy naming!

rules

Here are the very simple rules of The Name Domain. These rules help The Name Domain run smoothly so we can provide YOU with the best experience.

1. If you use a name, neomail namedomain, so we can take the name down.
2. If you stumble upon a name that IS taken, please neomail namedomain, so we can remove them.
3. Spread the word of The Name Domain to your Neopian friends!
4. Post a button or a banner on your pet's page to tell your friends about The Name Domain!
5. Be considerate of the work we do here, it is important that you follow at least rules ONE and TWO to ensure everybody has a good experience at The Name Domain!
5. Have fun, and happy naming!
