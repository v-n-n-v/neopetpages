Welcome
Welcome to the LOTR Directory, the first directory made for LOTR pets! Here you will find lots of pets with Lord of the Rings themed names!
I created this Directory from my love of J.R.R Tolkien's Lord of the Rings trilogy, the movies directed by Peter Jackson, and of all the AWESOME people in my guild who have LOTR themed pet names.


Being Added to the Directory
If you have a pet with a LOTR themed name then
please neomail me with his/her EXACT name, species and who (or what) they are named after in LOTR and I will add them :) Any names inspired by any Tolkien language (elvish, dwarvish, ect.) are welcome as well! Please don't send your neomail twice. I will neomail you to let you know once I've put your pet up. Please link back to the directory so more people will be notified! Thanks!
