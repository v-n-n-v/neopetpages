Character Corner

Welcome to the Character Corner, a directory of character pages.
Basically, these are biographical pages based on the characters that users have given to their neopets.

The only requirement to be listed is that your page must have information on your pet's character.
This includes works in progress, multi-character pages, character-based project pages.
There's no minimum amount of content, and if you don't have a 50x50 button, I can use your pet's official neo portrait.

If you want your page listed, please fill out the form below and neomail me.
