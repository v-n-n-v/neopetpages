MASS COLLECTOR FRIENDS

Welcome!

Hi friends!

I love encouraging other Neopians with their goals, and I was inspired to create a page to share the amazing mass collections others are putting together. The goal of this page to keep it fun, simple and supportive.

If you'd like to have the items your collecting added - I'd love to include you!

Simply send me a neomail and include the name of the item and if you have a goal for the number of items you'd like to reach.

A few things to keep in mind:

1- First and foremost, this list is about being kind, and respectful + following TNT rules.

2- The item(s) being collected is under 1.5K NP based on JN's item database.

3- I'm excited about managing this page! However, if it does become overwhelming or no longer fun I'll have to close it.
