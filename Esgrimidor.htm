That's right folks, it's April again, which means it's time for my favorite site event ever... If you're wondering what on Neopia I'm talking about, then let me introduce you to the Daily Dare. Good luck and happy gaming!


Important Note:

Key:
  • Items with light blue backgrounds are wearable items.
  • Items with brown backgrounds are Neohome 2.0 items.
  • Red behind an item means it was part of a Double Dare Day. For more about Double Dares, please see the "Double Dare Rules" section.

Tips:
  • Hover over the items to see their names.
  • To use the codes, simply type the word shown into your game screen. All codes are one-use unless otherwise stated.
  • The scores needed to beat each challenger are underneath the items you win by scoring said amount against said challenger.
  • Wearable items have previews. Preview 1 will be the item on a two-legged pet; Preview 2 will illustrate a four-legged pet.

Do I get a prize for playing every day?


If you complete a challenge on the day it is released, you will receive a bonus prize with your other prize that day. You won't receive this prize if you complete the challenge after the day on which it is released.


Do I have to be logged in or signed up to play Daily Dare?


Yes. Your scores won't be counted and you won't be able to earn prizes or trophies unless you're logged into Neopets.


How long will Daily Dare last?


Daily Dare will run for 18 ½ days, starting on Wednesday, March 7th at site sync and running through Sunday, March 25th at 11:59:59 PM NST. (NST is equal to U.S. Pacific Time.)


I challenged AAA, but I can't seem to match or beat his score on today's game -- can I still win a prize?


Yes, if you match or beat Abigail's score, you will win the Abigail prize for that challenge. You will not receive this prize until the end of the event in case you end up beating AAA's score. You will win AAA's prize if you beat or match his score on another day. However, you won't get the bonus prize if you cannot beat or match AAA's score on the day the game is launched. Also, if you don't eventually beat or match AAA's score in every challenge prior to midnight on March 25, 2012, you won't qualify for the gold trophy tier.


I challenged AAA, but I changed my mind. Can I switch to Abigail?


Sorry, no. Once you have selected a challenger, you cannot change. Make sure you practise before making your choice, so you know which opponent you want to challenge.


What are the Daily Dare trophy levels?


There are four trophy levels this year for Daily Dare:

Gold: Beat AAA at each challenge.

Silver: Complete all challenges before the end of Daily Dare, beating AAA at least once.

Bronze: Beat Abigail at each challenge.

Participant: Beat or match at least one AAA or Abigail score by the end of the event.

Whether or not you participate every day does not play into the trophy tiers.


What if I miss a day?


You can still earn the prizes for that day by matching or beating AAA's or Abigail's score for that game on another day. However, you won't get the bonus prize for that day.


What is Daily Dare?


Daily Dare is a gaming event that runs from March 7th through March 25th. You can challenge either Games Master AAA or his sister Abigail. Then, if you match or beat AAA's or Abigail's scores, you will win prizes! You can also earn a trophy at the end of the event.




What is Double Dare?


Double Dare is a special challenge during Daily Dare. To take the Double Dare, you must challenge AAA and match or beat his score. If you succeed, you'll receive AAA's and Abigail's prizes for the day! If you fail, however, you'll receive nothing.


I challenged AAA on a Double Dare Challenge day, but didn't beat or match his score that day. Can I still collect my prizes if I defeat him on a different day?


Yep! You can complete a Double Dare Challenge any other day and still receive AAA's and Abigail's prizes. However, you will not qualify for the bonus prize.


When are the Double Dare Challenges?


The Double Dare Challenges will occur once or twice a week throughout the entire Daily Dare event. You'll just have to keep an eye out to discover the exact days!



Just something worth noting here. You MUST click on the Team Challenge button within the Daily Dare page in order to participate in it. IF you choose a challenger without going through the Team Challenge first, you won't be able to complete the Team Challenge for that week.



Can I withdraw an invitation if my Neofriend has already accepted it?


No, sorry. Once an invitation has been accepted, you must complete the Team Challenge with that Neofriend in order to win the Team Challenge prize.


How many teams can I create or join in each Team Challenge each week?


You're only allowed to do one Team Challenge per week. That means if you accept an invitation from a Neofriend for a Team Challenge, you'll only be able to participate in that particular Team Challenge for that week.


I beat the Team Challenge and so did my Neofriend, but my prizes aren't showing up in my Inventory. What's wrong?


For Team Challenges, you must collect your prize on the Daily Dare main page. Then your item will appear in your Inventory.


I challenged Abigail in Friday's Daily Dare. Can I challenge AAA in the Team Challenge?


No, sorry. The challenger you choose for Friday's Daily Dare will be the same challenger for the Team Challenge.


I don't have any Neofriends. How can I participate in the Team Challenges?


You have to invite a Neofriend in order to play the Team Challenge. Do you have any siblings, other family members, or close friends that you can ask to join you? If they're not registered players on the site, that's okay. Ask them to sign up for an account and start playing Daily Dare!


I sent five invitations for a Team Challenge this week, and it's telling me I can't send any more invitations out. Why?


That's because you can only send out five invitations at any given time. If you accidentally sent a Team Challenge invitation out to the wrong person or want to invite different Neofriends, you'll need to withdraw some of the invitations you've sent out.


I took the Team Challenge but decided not to invite a Neofriend to play it with me. Can I still get the prize?


The only way you can earn the Team Challenge prize is if you invite a Neofriend or accept a Neofriend's invitation. Then, both of you must match or beat either AAA's or Abigail's score (depending on the opponent you challenged) by 11:59 PM NST Sunday night to qualify for the Team Challenge prize.


I took the Team Challenge but decided not to invite a Neofriend to play it with me. Can I still get the regular Daily Dare prize if I beat or match AAA's or Abigail's score?


Definitely. You don't have to complete the Team Challenge to earn the regular Daily Dare challenge item for the Friday game. As long as you beat or match AAA's or Abigail's score, you'll earn their prize. In fact, if you beat or match AAA's or Abigail's score on the challenge day and have accepted the Team Challenge, you'll also earn the bonus prize for beating or matching their score on the day the challenge was released.


What are Neofriends and how do I find one?


Neofriends are other Neopians who have either requested to be your Neofriend or who have accepted your request to be their Neofriend. You can get to know other Neopets players on the Neoboards, guilds, etc. When you meet someone you'd like to be Neofriends with, just send them a request. If they accept it, you're Neofriends! It's a good idea to Neomail other players before sending them a Neofriend request, just to introduce yourself and make sure that you have plenty in common.


What happens when I withdraw an invitation?


If you withdraw an invitation, your Neofriend will no longer be able to play the Team Challenge with you. They might see your invitation on their Team Challenge page, but when your Neofriend clicks on it, they'll be notified that the Team Challenge is no longer available.
