Oooh... what a wicked Ruki. He has stolen all the dice from all the board games in Neopia! Think of all the children on Christmas morning who won't be able to play their christmas presents! No more Adventures in Neopia, no more NeoQuest: The Board Game... just boring old TV :(

You must enter his evil dimension and free the dice! Control each dice by using the arrow keys. The topmost face of the dice must match the exit tile in order to finish the level. Every move that you take will make your bonus smaller, so try and complete the levels as quickly as possible. You will lose a life if time runs out, and you on ly get three lives.

In this guide there are screen shots of each level in the game Dice Escape. on each picture I have added red arrows to show the direction you can go to finish the level, with the best scoring path (to my knowledge) displayed.

In some cases there are two paths shown for on e level - this depends on the value of the coin, and if it will increase your score to get it or not.

Recently jamesters has neomailed me a lot of advice for the guide, and i have finally got around to updating. Here is a little bit of what he sent me that you might find interesting:
STEPS: each step taken minuses 4 points, you are given 100 step points to start off each level so after 25 steps you'll be at 0.

TIME: your given 19 time points to start off with, every three seconds minuses 1 point. the timer starts off at 59, so, 57-59=19 points, 54-56=18 points, etcetera. so 0-2 seconds is 0 points. and no u don't die on the 0th second, u still have 1 more second to win the level b4 u die.
