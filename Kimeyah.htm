In this hypothetical situation the eleven puzzle-makers and Zika are holidaying together on Mystery Island. They decide to have a tournament of Mynci Beach Volleyball, each game having only two players.

The changes the players' variables have on their base sk!ll can stack. The changes are also permanent, unless otherwise indicated. There were no ties at any point in the tournament. In the third round the three remaining players each have a game with the other two. One of them won both of these games, resulting in the structure seen on the left of the graph.

The graph should show the results of the tournament, but it is incomplete. Complete the graph to discover who ended in the first, second and third places. This will be your answer, to be added onto the end of the address in your address bar (e.g. /~Kimeyah#challenge958matthew-khalid-mote).
