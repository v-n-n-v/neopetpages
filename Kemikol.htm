About Names Unlimited

Names Unlimited is owned by keily95 and co-owned by ardatia. It started when Ardatia was searching for stuck pets for the Lost Pets Directory. She came across over hundreds of unused name and had no where to send them since NNI was closed down. I decided to take on the job to have people still provided with many nicely unused names. So now NU is taking on the challenge.

a-b-c-d-e-f-g-h-i-j-k-l-m-n-o-p-q-r-s-t-u-v-w-x-y-z

Please do Neomail me if you have taken a name and it is no longer available so others don't have to search through them. I know most people won't but I will FULLY appreciate it.

For all of Kalico's Latest News! I also make an entry for when letters are went through and all the used names are cleared out.

FAQ

How can I help?

- You could look up names for me that are not taken and have no _'s or numbers then Neomail me with them. You could also find names that are taken and let me know so I can remove them.

When did this open?

- May 30 of 2006 was the Grand Opening.

Who do I contact for questions, comments, names, or to remove a name?

- Kalicofaerie even though my main account is Keily95 it helps to keep me organized.

What inspired you?

- The great people on the Charter Directory Board and No Numbers Inc.

Can I be an affie?

- No NU does not accept affies. We only have the 4 listed at the top and that's it. Sorry. But you can display one of our buttons to link to here.

Not finding the right name?

- Try The Need-A-Name Center for more names!
