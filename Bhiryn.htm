F.A.Q.

Is your question not here? Feel free to neomail me, so I can add it to this list!
1. Do I really have to leave credit, even after I've edited the resources?
Yes, yes, and yes! It does not matter how much you alter my resources. They are still my work and you still must leave credit! Remember to make sure to read the rules for each section.
2. I caught someone stealing/using your graphics without credit!
Firstly, don't contact the offender. Please contact me, so that I can deal with this issue.
Secondly, thank you so much for informing me. I am truly grateful!
3. Can I be affiliates with Aurora?
Yes, of course! If you're interested in becoming affiliates with Aurora, please note that I will reject you if you:

Have stolen content.
Break Neopets rules.

If you follow these guidelines then we will have no problem. Unless I have reached 25 affiliates and have no openings that is. You will then have to wait till I sweep a closed/inactive site.

I am currently at 23/25 affiliates. Want to apply?
4. Can I review Aurora?
Hmm, maybe. I may or may not except a review, because of the fact that I am a very sensitive person. It really only depends on if you are kind and pitch it right to me. If that is so, I will most likely be swayed to except your review. Kindness goes a long way with me!
5. Where do you find the images you use on your graphics from?
I find most of my Neopets images at CC Images, Charly's S.W.F. Collection, Background Bonanza, and Neopets Backgrounds. I use Poogle to find the rest, both neo-related, and non neo-related.
6. What program do you use to make your graphics?
I use GIMP and an online photo editing site that allows you to do most the things programs like GIMP do.
7. I found a spelling/link/image error.
Oh gosh, thank you so much for finding my error! I really appreciate you informing me about it. Please neomail me the mistake I made, so I can fix it as soon as possible.

Dusk's Story

I made Aurora to have fun and to push myself to improve my graphic abilities. Right now my expertise level isn't that amazing, but I hope by creating this site I will hone my abilities, and become as close to mastering them as I can! I named this site Aurora, because I think the name is just lovely, not to mention the aurora borealis/australis are so beautiful, and I also use the name for my continent in my role-playing world with my boyfriend. I was going to name it Aurora Borealis, but I thought that was to long, so I shortened it to Aurora. I plan on keeping Aurora up and running for as long as I am on Neopets, and that should be for a long time.
