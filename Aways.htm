moonbeam

Welcome to Moonbeam a wearable recolors site run by Fly. Moonbeam was opened May 15th 2014. These are by no means official or items that are expected to be released. Please have a look around and send in your votes/ratings for your favorite items, as well as suggest what you would like to see recolored next!

I thought it was much more exciting to make a dress up version, but that comes with the flaw that I had to choose a species and stick with it. Since that was the case, I decided to go with the Aisha to match with my affilate Sunbeam an Aisha directory. C:

dyeworks were released june 30, 2014!!

Using the image

You are welcome to screenshot and use the image of the pet you create anywhere! All I would ask is that you please either use one of the watermarks, a link back on the image or a link back button where-ever you use it so others can find their way back here. C:
