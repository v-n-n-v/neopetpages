Rules

Rulez shmools, obviously we're all rebels here right? Alas, I still have a few to go over!

1. Permies only! I realize that you may change your mind and trade/adopt out down the road - but do not list pets you are considering trading soon or actively trying to trade!

2. Provide link back to directory, so that other's may find us easier as well! We want to show these pretty names off, right?

3. Do not solicit trades from this page
Do not solicit trades from this page
Do not solicit trades from this page

4. Please only list recognizable MS. It doesn't have to be a "close" MS, but if it has to be explained, I don't think it should be listed.

5. Mail the following form to list your pretty babies:

Pet name:
Username: (please only list your main account. You may also send a nickname if you'd like!)
Fun fact: Optional
Category: (Name | Word | Place)

6. Not a rule, but more of a request. Since MS are so largely popular, if you are sending in multiples, it makes it much easier and efficient for me if you send them alphabetically/by name or word.
Again its not a rule! But it is SO appreciated ♥
