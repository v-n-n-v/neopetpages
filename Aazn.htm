What is Dreamy?

Welcome to the grand opening of Dreamy! Created and ran by Star!

I used to run a premades/resource site in the past, but ended up losing my account and quitting neo for a few years. In that time, I got really into digital art, which is what Dreamy is all about.

I recently decided to give neopets another try. I had to completely start over, but I noticed that there are still some active sites. I wanted to join in and offer some content of my own; namely some adoptables and resources!

So what is Dreamy? I offer some cute little chibis/items that you can use on your profile or within your site for aesthetic purposes.

I am also offering buttons, bullets, etc. that are drawn/created by me!

For now, I am not taking requests. I would like to establish the foundation before I take on too much work. I am, however, always open to suggestions! Click the envelope below to send me some mail! I would be very happy to hear from you.

How to use:

Use CTRL + A to select the code, then CTRL + C to copy.
There are 3 different size options, the one displayed is the medium size (183x237). Feel free to resize if needed. I have chosen to not add a signature to my chibis so that they look nice; please do not remove the link to my page.
If you have a suggestion for a chibi you would like to see, feel free to mail me!
