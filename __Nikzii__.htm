Hello!

I wanted backgrounds for my pet pages that matched the actual background they were wearing. While I found a lot of great ones, they were scattered and my neurodivergent brain didn't like that. All backgrounds are 1920x1081. I have slightly edited some images to remove the noise created from running them through a converter to enlarge after downloading from DTI. Send any requests to evilgirl28666 please, and if any image needs to be edited that I missed.

News

June.12.2022 Finally found a layout I like and will be editing and adding images. Starting with NC backgrounds first.

The code I use on my pet pages for the backgrounds is:

Change the parenthesis to greater/less than for it to work and place outside the style divs.
