WELCOME

Hi! My name is Shark. I've recently returned from a lengthy seven-year hiatus, and, since coming back, one of my favorite part of Neopets has been picking out petpets and petpetpets for my permies.

I've seen a lot of fun matches from other Neopians, and I thought it would be fun to launch a directory of all the wonderful matches you guys have come up with!

If you have a petpet combo you're proud of and you want to see it listed here, shoot me a NeoMail with the form to the right. You don't need a P3 to be listed, but your petpet should match your Neopet in some way, shape, or form. If you are submitting more than three pets, please place them on a petpage first. It helps me a lot if they're organized by colour.

I hope you enjoy scrolling through the Petpet Matching Directory! ♥♥♥

I AM NOT CURRENTLY ACCEPTING SUBMISSIONS! Sorry, I underestimated how long data entry for this project takes and I don't have the time or energy to continue updating at this time.
