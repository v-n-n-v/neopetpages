An introduction to the Park:

Petpet Park is a new section of Neopets where you can create a Petpet and play and interact with it in a cute enviroment. You can actually see your petpet walking around and see other user's petpets too, and talk to and play with them :) .
You don't need to create an account in order to play it, if you have a Neopets account you can log in with it. There you have a number of fun activities you can do, just like in Neopets. In the Park you create a new petpet, but you have to choose from the ones available: pinixy, hifflo, kassegat, etc, basically the ones that appeared in the Petpet Park plot.

Getting food:

Every petpet needs to eat, right? And eating is important because it improves its mood, so now I'm going to tell you some ways to get your petpet some food without spending all your PPP in the process ;)
Have you noticed that the Park is full of trees and bushes? Well, some of them can be very useful. All you have to do is get very close to one of them, position your mouse over it until it changes from an arrow to a hand and click to shake it. Easy, right? If you are lucky (it may take several clicks) a fruit will fall of it and go into your inventory! Now you can drag it to your petpet and it will eat it.

General questions:

What is Petpet Park?
It is a new section of Neopets where you can create a Petpet and play and interact with it in a cute enviroment. You can actually see your petpet walking around and see other user's petpets too, and talk to and play with them.

Do I need to create an account in order to play it?
Not necessarily, if you have a Neopets account you can log in with it.

Do you create a new petpet there if you currently have a petpet on neopets, or can you make a character from your current petpet on here?
In the Park you create a new petpet, but you have to choose from the ones available: pinixy, hifflo, kassegat, etc, basically the ones that appeared in the Petpet Park plot.

What are servers for?
When you are connected to a server, you can see all the petpets that are in the same server as you, so, if you want to play with a frien, make sure to choose the same server both of you.

Why are there soo many servers to choose from?
Well, think about this: imagine there are 100 people playing at the same time, and since you can see all the petpets that are in the same server as you, the game would become very laggy. With more servers this shouldn't happen.

How do you check your petpet's mood?
Just click on the button with the ID card in the down-left corner of the screen.

Why do some words become red when I type them in the chat box?
That's either because the word isn't allowed or it hasn't been added yet to de dictionary yet. If you think the word should be allowed, just send a bug report or feedback.

Why ins't the post office/university/mall/adoption center/ some other place open?
Because the park is new and TNT is still working on some of the features.

What are expansions and patches?
They are updates, they usally include new items and places to visit.

Items:

How do I use my items?
Well, it depends: if it's something edible, just open your inventory (click on the backpack-like image in the lower-right part of the screen) and drag it to your petpet, so simple as that. If it is a piece of clothing, just clcik on the closet button (the one that has a 'coat hanger' on it) and customize your petpet from there.

Can I sell/discard/donate/delete my items? No, you can't do that right now. Let's hope TNT would make that possible in the future ;).

Petpet's mood, habilities and etc:

How do you improve your Petpet's mood?
You can feed them (just drag the food item from your inventory to your petpet).

How do you improve your petpet's abilities?
Petpet Park is still beta so this feature is not active yet.

How do you unlock new tricks?
It seems that you have to level up the abilities of your petpet (this isn't available yet).

How do you change your petpet colour?
First, you have to go to Petpet emporium (it is located in the Market lane) and buy a bottle of ink. Then go to Groomtopia (in the fashion field) and enter the 'color change' station. Then, dragg the bottle onto the colour station and you are ready!

Petpet Park Points:

Why are the payouts so low?

This is because TNT is still balancing the game, so they raise the game's payout little by little. After all it wouldn't be fun to have a lot of PPP and can't spend them on something fun because you've already bought every item in the game ;) (trust me, this was the feeling during the closed beta test where the item prices were pretty low...).

Is there a limit on how many PPP (Petpet Park Points) can you make a day?
There's a limit of 1200 PPP per day. What are the best ways to make PPP?
Well, it depends on various factors: how fast is your computer, what your gaming abilities are and what games you like. If you are good a it, skeedadle is a good way to make points, some people prefer wardrobe wars and some other are good playing blocktacular. Doing quests is also a good way of making points and you can also get items at the same time!
