➺ welcome to dyeworks by maya
THINGS I WOULD LOVE TO SEE DYED AND SOME SUGGESTIONS FOR HOW

I started this page to satisfy my own curiosity on the 17th of June, 2019 and it's quickly become my first real petpage project!

I recently decided to begin the search in earnest for my own Great Big Outdoors Background and I knew from the get-go it would be a long journey, so I wanted to play with the idea of getting my hands on a Dyeworked version and thus, Dyeworks by Maya was born.
We love collaborating with Val who undertakes seasonal Dyeworks Polls for us as a community!
I'm honored and very proud that people have responded to enthusiastically to my work and that I've inspired a new wave of people to join me in the recoloring fun!

Here's hoping we're able to make a difference with each newest batch of Dyeworks!

f.a.q.
Some really nice and inquisitive people have asked about my goals and my process so I'll attempt to answer a few here!


THE MAIN GOALS

✶ STAY TRUE to the original color values and saturation: let's try not to have more Oversaturated Wigs and Backgrounds with muddy toxic water

✶ make it INTERCHANGEABLE: I hope everyone gets to customize with the items they love and not have to sell a kidney for that chance

✶ make it VERSATILE: TNT did a fantastic job with the Polka Dot Holiday Dress and this is the kind of thing I hope also to do with some of my recolors

✶ create REALISTIC expectations: I could go wild and make an item that was monochromatic into a rainbow item, but we would Never Actually get it

✶ pick A COLOR and stick to it: if I need to look at the item name to determine what color the item is supposed to be, yeet
