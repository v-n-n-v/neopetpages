Neopet References Guide

Welcome to my reference guide!

On this guide, you will discover the movies, shows, memes, books, and other pop culture media that inspire The Neopets Team.

What is a reference, you may ask? Well, it's sort of like an aspect of Neopets that was based off of something in, or hinting at, the real world. An easter egg, if you will.

Neopets is teeming with images that seem like traditionally wacky TNT-style ideas, but on closer inspection, those images are actually references to popular culture in today's society!

I hope you enjoy my findings! :D
