ABOUT NC EVENTS

NC Events are PUBLIC customization events for EVERYONE to attend to.
The NC Events Committee are the only members in the NC Events Guild.
We plan and host all NC Events and welcome both NC AND NP Customizers!
@ncevents is the official guild account for NC Events.
This is the only account we will have the The NC Events Fund shop on.

The Head Planner and Co-Head Planners are the only members in the guild right now.
The guild will only consist of the Head Planners and the rest of NC Events Committee.
At this time, we are NOT seeking to expand The NCE Committee and recruitment is closed.
Interested NC Event goers can sign up for Neoinvites.

NC EVENTS FAQ

1. Want exclusive Neoinvites to NC Events?
Send a Neofriend Request to @ncevents and you'll get one sent straight to your inbox.
We will also NM you for reminders of an event as it nears, in case you forget!
You won't want to miss out on all the fun!

Otherwise, please bookmark this page and check back here for NC Event updates!

2. Got a NC Event idea?
Please drop a Neomail to @ncevents with your suggestion and we can talk about making it happen!

3. Want to help NC Events out?
Place a donation at the The NC Events Fund to help us out!

4. Do I need to join the NC Events guild to attend events?
NO! The guild is just members of The NCE Committee.
The NCE Committee plans and host events for EVERYONE to attend to!
No guild membership required! However to stay updated,
please sign up for Neoinvites and bookmark this page for any other news.

5. I love what The NC Events Committee does and I want to join. How do I apply?
Thank you for your interest! At this time, our recruitment is closed for expansion.
However, please feel free to send a Neomail to @ncevents anyways to express your interest
and the Head and Co-Head Planners can discuss for consideration! :)

6. What is The Album?
The Album is an archive of all NCE hosted events that include each event's invitation, guest book, and major highlights.
You can find The Album at neopets.com/~TheAlbum

7. I want to join your ____ event, but I can't make it due to a schedule/timzeone conflict! Can I still include my pet?
YES - You can! Please neomail @ncevents the below form:

Subject: *Insert NC Event Name*

Username:
Pet Name (actual name, no nicknames):
Neo IMG Link:
Caption (1 sentence):

8. How do I grab the NEO IMG LINK?
To obtain the Neo IMG Link for us to include in the album:

1. Customize up your pet
2. Go to your userlookup
3. Right click on your (customized) pet's picture
4. Click "Copy Image Address
5. And THAT is your Neo IMG Link :)
