PETITION FOR #KEEPTHEOLDART

I will start off by saying we love the idea of NC UCs being brought out into the neoworld! It is a great idea and allows everyone a chance to obtain the artwork we all miss! We do not want original UCs from the 07 conversion to be affected by this update in any capacity.

This petition will state that the old art should stay as is without being autoconverted and that the new artwork should not be implemented on original unconverted pets. If a change must be made then the linework for pastel, light lines, especially the UC Faerie Ixi, must be reviewed for userbase feedback.

Want to write feedback on the demise of UC Trading and that ucs will actually cease to exist historically, go sign Kaliqs petition and tell us how important these ucs are to you!
