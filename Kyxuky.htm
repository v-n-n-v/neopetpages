Simple. Just send a neomail (Gold__Bunny) and I will add him/her. Please include on the neomail the pet's name and color.

Please note:

* Your pet MUST be a Eyrie already. Don't ask if you are going to get an Eyrie soon because you will not be added.
* None of these Eyries are up for adoption. Don't neomail their owners begging for one of them.
* No insulting the owners of these Eyries.
* No lab pets. It has to be a permanent Eyrie.
* There is no limit on how many Eyries you can submit.
* If your Eyrie changes color, please tell so I can change it :)
