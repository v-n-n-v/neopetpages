about

Hello, and welcome to Noceur. I'm Britt, otherwise known as the ghoul running this show.

I opened this site to put to use my critical nature. Feedback is important to site owners, and I've noticed a distinct lack of site services being provided as of late. I've decided to change that.

I aim to provide fast, quality feedback to those who need it. Browse my rubric section, and see if anything jumps out at you. If you require any additional services, don't hesitate to neomail me. I'm happy to help in any way I can.

Things of Importance

  • Note: Reviews are done around my work schedule, which changes daily. Please be aware that at times, I may therefore be unable to strictly adhere to the wait times listed on each rubric.

  • Please try to avoid changing the content related to your review, unless you don't mind it not being reviewed, as it may be missed depending on the progress made.

  • Reviews are done on Google Chrome by default, on a 15" laptop. Firefox, Edge, or mobile reviews are also available by request.

  • There is no limit on how many reviews you may request at once. The one exception is an in-depth review, simply because it contains all other rubrics' content.

  • You may request as much as you want, regardless of how long it's been since your last review.

  • I reserve the right to deny your request if I see fit. This includes, but is not limited to: sites which break Neopets' rules; sites with stolen content or uncredited coding; sites using content in a manner that breaks the Terms of Service of its creator.

Argenté

Type: First Impression
Length: Short
Wait Time: Less than 1 day
Ideal For: Everyone

My first impression of your site is what determines whether or not I will choose to stay and look around or leave. In an Argenté review, I will give you a general opinion of each page or area of your site, from the layout to the content.

This review includes:
  • Layout overview - aesthetics, organization
  • Content overview - quality, uniqueness, if I would use it
  • Quick error check - grammar, spelling, coding problems, etc.
  • Suggestions on how to improve
  • Recommended Status


Ténèbres

Type: Link Sweep
Length: Varies
Wait Time: Less than 1 day
Ideal For: Directories, Sites

Broken links can really affect the quality of a site. In a Ténèbres review, I'll check all of the links on your site, and return a list of any problems I ran into.

This review includes a sweep of:
  • Navigation and sub-navigation links
  • Any additional site-related links (neomail links, extras links, etc.)
  • Affiliates links + activity status
  • Directory: a full sweep of all sites/shops listed, complete with activity status and any relevant changes.


Rampé

Type: Content
Length: Varies
Wait Time: Up to 1 day
Ideal For: Sites, Shops, Galleries

The content is the most important part of a site. In a Rampé review, I will look solely at the content of your site and provide feedback on its overall quality as well as providing an individual review of each section.

This review includes:
  • Overall quality
  • Uniqueness - Would I remember your site based on its content?
  • Individual review of each section or piece of content.
  • Consistency - If there is a theme, does your content suit it?
  • Shop - Are your prices reasonable? Do you have a nice assortment of items?
  • Quick error check - grammar, spelling, coding problems, etc.
  • Suggestions on how to improve
  • Recommended Status


Effrayant

Type: Shop & Gallery Specific
Length: Varies
Wait Time: Varies

Running a successful shop or gallery is not always an easy task. In an Effrayant review, I will provide several services tailored to your needs. All the services I provide for shops and galleries are listed below - those with an asterisk are optional.

You will need to specify what, if any, optional services you require in your request form.

This review may include:
  • Overall quality
  • Uniqueness - Would I remember your shop or gallery?
  • Consistency - If there is a theme, does your content suit it?
  • Quick error check - grammar, spelling, coding problems, etc.
  • Suggestions on how to improve
  • A spreadsheet of items which fit your theme, arranged by price (roughly)*
  • Shop - Price check*


Étoile

Type: Error Check
Length: Varies
Wait Time: Less than 1 day
Ideal For: Everyone

Errors are deal-breakers for many site visitors. In an Étoile review, I will thoroughly check your entire site for all types of errors.

This review may include a check for:
  • Spelling and grammar errors
  • Coding and organization errors
  • Content-specific errors (if applicable)
  • Broken images, links, or anchors


Foncé

Type: Layout
Length: Short to Medium
Wait Time: Up to 1 day
Ideal For: Everyone

The layout is what draws me into a site. If the layout is lower quality, it can affect whether or not I stay at your site. In a Foncé review, I will provide feedback on different aspects of your layout such as organization and navigation, and offer suggestions on how to improve it.

This review may include:
  • Overall quality
  • Aesthetics - Is it visually appealing?
  • Organization - Is it well-organized/easy to navigate?
  • Consistency - Does the layout flow well?
  • Errors
  • Suggestions on how to improve


Revenant

Type: Mix-and-Match
Length: Varies
Wait Time: Varies
Ideal For: Everyone

Do you want to request a review, but none of the other rubrics quite describe what you need? Maybe there were additional aspects you didn't want, or you wanted one aspect from a few different rubrics.
For example, maybe you only wanted the consistency of your layout to be reviewed, not the entire layout. Or, maybe you wanted an Argenté review, but you also wanted a link sweep just for your affiliates.

Fear not! In a Revenant review, you can ask for up to 10 specific services you require. Alternatively, you can also ask up to 10 specific questions for me to answer.

You will need to specify your services or questions in your request form.

Hanté

Type: In-Depth
Length: Long
Wait Time: 2+ days
Ideal For: Sites

This is as deep as it gets. In a Hanté review, I will be looking at everything. All of the rubrics on this site are included. You will receive a rating out of 5 stars as well.

5-star Rating Advantages:

  • A special button/graphic to display on your site
  • A perpetual spot on the Noceur Hall of Fame
  • Skip the Form - Once you've received a 5-star rating, you no longer have to submit formal request forms. Neomail me casually and let me know what you want (i.e.: "Hey Britt, I'd like a Hanté re-review"). This only applies to the site that received the 5-star rating.

This review includes:
  • Layout overview - aesthetics, organization, overall quality, consistency
  • Content overview - quality, uniqueness, individual content reviews, if I would use it
  • Error check - grammar, spelling, coding problems, link sweep
  • Overall impression - if I will revisit or regularly use your site
  • Suggestions on how to improve
  • Recommended Status + Rating
