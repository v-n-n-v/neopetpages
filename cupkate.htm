Intro

Hello! Welcome to Lua, Anh's (rationalizing) user lookup dump. I started this page in 2013 when I started receiving neomails from people asking for permission to use my lookups. I decided that displaying them all on a site would be a lot easier than having to bring up a code everyone time a layout was requested, so ta-da. Enjoy.

As of March 26, 2016:
Wow, my last update on here was nearly two years ago. I had disappeared because I was starting my first year of university, and now I'm almost at the end of my second year! I logged back in during some idle time while on Spring Break, and realized that I still get neomails about Lua. It's very humbling. Thank you to everyone who has supported Lua and me throughout the years, and thank you to those of you who continue to use my layouts even when there are so many premades sites that offer a lot more sleek and minimalist styles than I do! You all are wonderful. That being said, this site will no longer be updated, as I am only active on Neopets sporadiclly, if at all. The content will remain here indefinitely, though, so feel free to use it! I probably will not be replying to e-mails regarding coding, though.

FAQ

  1. Do you take requests?
    2. I do not.
  2. I remember a lookup you had, but it isn't here! Can you please add it?
    3. Sometimes I create lookups and I forget to save the coding on my computer, which is why some are missing from this page. I could recreate the layout, but since I usually don't use a layout more than once, I would rather not spend my time doing so. My apologies! Another reason you might not see a previous lookup up is because I was using a premade!
  3. Will you teach me how to code?
    4. Unfortunately I can't. I do not have a large amount of time to commit to teaching coding. My advice would be just to practice! Read lots of tutorials and tips, and always experiment with coding; it can do wonders. Besides, I'm still learning myself!
