Spare a Vote in the BC? (Dec 15th, 2022)

Welcome! I'm Kathy, and you're vieiwng a new directory for artists to come together and share their collections. This page is designed and inspired heavily by Artistry by sophiehaku, who unfortunately was frozen some time before its creation. I hope she and her wonderful directory can come back soon, but I decided to try and make a new one in its stead. I hope I can do the concept justice for you guys!

Artists are listed alphabetically by username, with their chosen alias displayed on their button image. You can navigate to them all using the sidebar on the left! For simplicity's sake I'd like to only list by usernames (rather than nickname), but you're welcome to use any of the accounts that you prefer to be contacted by.

monthly spotlight

Every month, I'd like to highlight one person in the directory for their hard work and artistic merit - just like Artistry did in the past! Being listed here earns you one of the trophies below (coming soon), as well as a warm fuzzy feeling, I hope. It's my way of thanking ya'll for supporting the directory and its growth. Please feel free to drop me a line if you'd like to suggest a new trophy image!

get listed

That's it! You can leave the editing of your card and everything else to me. What's nice about this directory is that it's totally okay if you don't have a small link button - nothing like that is required to be listed. You're welcome to mail me again anytime you'd like to update your card, too!
