Hello friends! Welcome to The Jub Hub, a place where people from all over Neopia can come and show off their precious JubJubs. JubJubs are a seriously underrated pet species, and they deserve a directory to call their own. I mean, they look like cotton puffs with smiley faces. What's not to love?



Do you have a JubJub you'd like to add to this directory? Neomail me the NAME and COLOUR of your JubJub by clicking the mail button above. The only requirements are for your JubJub to be a permanent pet, and to be wearing at least a background. If your pet is listed here, then be sure to grab a button below to add to your JubJub's lookup!




Jub of the Month
