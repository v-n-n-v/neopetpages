Misspells

Ateeiz

Ateeiz is my little way of expressing my enjoyment for ATEEZ here on Neopets. Becoming an ATINY in early 2019 was such a whirlwind for me, and I honestly enjoy all of their music and find their journey very inspiring. One of my favorite songs is Illusion, so I wanted to try and create a pet around that theme, and out came Ateeiz! Since he's invisible, I've also made a lot of looks for him to represent their songs, and it's been a blast! He's my way of channeling my ATINY~

Visit Ateeiz's petpage for a CUTE ATEEZ inspired lookbook!

Kyungsou (Kyungsoo from EXO)

Kyungsou is a misspell of "Kyungsoo", my bias from EXO. I created him in Y19 as a bruce (because for some reason Kyungsoo has always been associated with penguins), painted him skunk (because Kyungsoo's favourite colour is black lol.. shadow just ain't right tho), and dressed him up all kokobop-y because kokobop was released just after he was created. That's how he came to be! I honestly spent the longest time looking for a Kyungsoo name/misspell (even mailed anyone with similar names etc), and I think Kyungsou is a perfect one :')
