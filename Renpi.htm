What is this page about

Hey, I am the artist Chanty (@BoredChanty)! In the first month of 2018 I decided to create ideas for Neopets that are missing colors. I think Neopets can get wonderful ideas as Maraquan since it is one of my favorites colours, so I started my project around the internet hoping they can be implemented in the game. Spoiler: 3 of 8 goals achieved!

F.A.Q


- How do you plan to make your project turn real?


My method is to create posts and pray for people to like them, lol. Sadly, I can't say the names of the offsites where I have been posting, but you can create a project page like this one if you have any idea to share with the community and TNT. Good luck! I have some tips that I believe it can work:

- Make people share a lot, people has to tag TNT or sending messages to them.

- You can send suggestions for that editorial from the Neopian Times. Be sure to suggest the colour AT LEAST ONE MONTH away from the Pet Day, and not one week away like many people think. TNT needs a good spare of time to draw the Neopet so they cannot draw them in a rush and make them look awful, I am sure you don't wish that!

- Aesop monthly creates a topic asking for "Pet Day Suggestions" in the Site Events Neoboard. You can write something like "Maraquan Jetsam by @BoredChanty" or "Maraquan Nimmo by neopets.com/~Renpi". It is important to tag me if you want MY concept to become true. (This seems to be the most effective tip)

- Fanarts are also welcome to help spread the idea. (Thought I never received one for my Maraquan Project, hellodarknessmyoldfriend )

I created the project, but they'll only turn into reality with the power of the community, showing to TNT they really want that concept as their pet. Me alone will never make the projects turn real, I just start them and pray!

- Will you draw the Maraquan Vandagyre and the Maraquan Gnorbu?


No, because that's not the point of my project. My project is to convince people that aquatic Neopets can also be Maraquans. There are other artists that also can create awesome ideas, deserving some attention and prizes for these two Neopets missing their aquatic form. I hope one of them can be a squid/octopus/cuttlefish based, I mean, we already have the Mutant Jetsam as Octopus, but it doesn't have the cuteness appeal.

- How did TNT notice your project?


I have no idea lol They even found my username before I was creating this petpage, I got a bit scared hehe. But I believe it was due the shares my project got from external sites.

- The Quiggle bothers me a bit because bettas and goldfishes aren't saltwater, but I suppose the Maraquan forms have already crossed that bridge before.


HMMMM Good point, I never paid attention to this :0 And you are right!! We already have the Maraquan Kacheek as goldfish too as a pet that is from fresh water, I can't think any other pet.

- Aquatic Neopets as Maraquan don't make sense.


Hello, I understand your point of fishes turning into... Fishes again? Well, once TNT said in an editoral they would never create the Tyrannian Grarrl because Grarrls are already from Tyrannia, guess what? Years later they released the Tyrannian Grarrl, maybe they forgot their own rule and now my Maraquan Neopets should exist too! Not gonna lie, I am curious to see the Tyrannian Chomby, hehe.
