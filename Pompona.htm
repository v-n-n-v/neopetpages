WELCOME


Welcome to Mythic, a mythological pet directory run by Rico. The pets in this directory represent the gods, goddesses, monsters, heroes, and notable names of the ancient world. Currently, Mythic only lists pets named after figures from the pantheons of Egyptian, Greek, and Roman mythology, though this may expand in the future.

Please enjoy perusing the many pets that Mythic has to offer, and if you have a mythological pet of your own, please consider listing it with us! The rules and listing form are to the right, should you need them!

Mythic was last updated on 6/4/24!
List your pets!
To list your pet with Mythic, please take a look at the rules and neomail the form below!
  • All pets must be named after an Egyptian, Greek, or Roman mythological figure. All names must be correctly spelled, but common variant spellings are also allowed.
  • Any pet you submit must be your permanent pet. Please do not submit trading fodder or another player's pet.
  • Mythic accepts all pets, with or without customization!
