Welcome to the Grim Faire

a helping guide to all things haunted

Welcome to the Faire! Grimfaire is a pet created on Halloween for the purpose of bringing joy to those who love spooky things. This page is to help make Halloween accessible to more Neopians.

This page contains suggested neopoint wearables to help make your pet spooky on any budget. There is also a section that can help you choose a new name for your pet if you are creating one. Suggestions are always welcome for both sections!

Grimfaire is your guide this area, and between me and you, he's pretty knowledgeable. Check out our info and check back often since we add more frequently!

You can also check out our sister site, Wishdoe's Winter Holiday Guide!
