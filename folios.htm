    about crumble

    Crumble was a site idea thought up by Jewlz in January 2014. She wanted to open a new edible adoptables site and who better to open it with than her Neo-Bestie Hayley. Jewlz came up with the site design and theme, while Hayley designed the focus of our site layout: the lovely Teapot and tea cup. Jewlz then designed the background and menu to fit with Hayleys design. When it came to came to naming the site, Hayley had some doozies(-insert gibberish here-). So Jewlz produced a huge list of delicious sounding names to consider. Hayley LOVED "The Crumb Cafe" since she felt it is what would be left if Sopramus ever broke in or if Jewlz finally gave into the temptation that is Folios. However, it was pointed out the we do not serve coffee since this is a tea-party so it could not be "Cafe." So they decided to shorten it to Crumble. It still worked with the previous idea and with the theme of the site. After working for weeks on the site and content, Hayley and Jewlz finally opened the doors to Crumble on February 10, 2014.
