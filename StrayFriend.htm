Welcome to the directory of pets who have a Gruslen petpet! Doesn't matter the age, or color or what we would love to list them here. Gruslens have gone under appreciated for too long so this page is dedicated to them!

You can see the categories on the side, colors, PPLs from Gruslens (special group) and the highlight of the oldest gruslen of any color.

Labbed gruslens are welcomed, but they do get their own category.

If you are interested in my other gruslen themed pages I do have two galleries: Gruslen Haven Rescue Center and Gruslen Showcase. Please check them out.
