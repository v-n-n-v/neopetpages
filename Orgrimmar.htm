faq

Hover over the title bars of each preview for individual notes!

How do I get a counter?
The regular counter is from boingdragon. The active user counter (the one with the little person on it) is from whos amung us.

Why can't I use your counter?
Because it will mess with both of our stats, and that doesn't provide either of us with accurate information. I check mine often so I'll mail you if you're using mine.

How do I get the little images of my pets?
*In order to find your pets' avatar images just use:
//pets.neopets.com/cpn/petname/1/1.png
And obviously change petname to your pet's name. I will mention that they probably won't all be positioned perfectly in the center because all species are positioned differently; which is I specifically centered mine. I still suggest that you make and crop your own images if it really matters that much to you. But the code has been updated for lazy people.

How do I get my [habitarium/nc album/shop] to show up?
In the css there should be this giant chunk of stuff that all has the display:none property. Just find what you want to show, and remove it (and the comma.) For example #ncmall,#habitarium,#usershop etc; they're pretty straight forward. Just remember that it may mess up positioning and look lame. Alternatively, if one of those is showing, you can add the id to that chunk of css to remove it.

How do I center this lookup?
You didn't read the hover notes, did you!? Scroll back up to the layout you want to center and read the instructions! xD
