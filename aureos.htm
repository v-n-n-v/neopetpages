Welcome

Rules

2. Please do not redistribute any of the content on this site. Storing edits of these fonts on petpages is allowed as long as it is for your personal use only. If you are found to be linking edits of premade fonts from your own petpage, you will be reported.

3. You are allowed to get these fonts rated, but please be sure to mention that the font you are using is a premade font from Canvas. Linking the site is not required; however, it is appreciated! 4. If you fail to properly submit a font request form, you will lose privileges such as: the opportunity to exclusivity and the opportunity to receive post-request edits. Please, make sure to take the time to fill out the proper forms correctly or to simply apologize for your mistakes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How Can I Remove the Periods/Dots in My Font?

For PC Users:
1. Highlight each period/dot individually, then press Alt+255 (must be done with a number pad). This will produce an Invisible Space that CANNOT be copied or pasted.

2. Search for the Character Map App on your PC, type 2007 in the Unicode Box, select and copy "Full Space" Character. This will produce an Invisible Space that CAN be copied and pasted for your use only.

For Mac Users:
1. Highlight each period/dot individually, then press Command+Space. This will produce an Invisible Space that CANNOT be copied or pasted.

2. Highlight each period/dot individually, then use Option+Space. This will produce an Invisible Space that CANNOT be copied or pasted.


How Can I Remove the White Lines in My Font?

For PC and Mac Users:
1. Try to increase your font size to size 3 or size 4.

2. Try to change the color of your font to something suitable for your specific computer monitor.


How Can I Use Truncated Hexcodes in My Font?

For PC and Mac Users:
1. As an example, use #135 instead of #123456; #ACE instead of #ABCDEF; #123 instead of #1A2B3C; etc. The key is to use every other alpha-numerical character in the un-truncated hexcode.

2. Remember, using a truncated hexcode may result in a slightly different color than expected due to a lack of specificity.

Requests

Before requesting a font, please understand that your request may take a few days or hours depending on the amount of requests sent in and how busy my schedule is.
