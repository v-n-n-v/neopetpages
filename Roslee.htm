the lookbook index

Welcome to The Lookbook Index, this page aims to list the different lookbooks that can be found around neopia in one single petpage.

A lookbook, for the purpose of this page, is a petpage where you upload the customizations that your neopets are wearing or have worn in the past.

How to list?

1. Pick a book item* (i.e: Diary of a Forgotten Neopian)
2. Fill the following form and send it here

*Community lookbooks/customization directories do NOT have to pick an item

FAQ

1. Can I list more than one lookbook?
Yes, you can, as long as you pick a different item for each petpage.
2. What's a community lookbook?
It is a lookbook that showcases neopets from different users.
3. Can I pick an item that has already been chosen?
No, please pick a book item that is not taken already.



DISCLAIMER: This petpage is meant to look for inspiration, please be creative enough and do not copy customizations from other users :')
