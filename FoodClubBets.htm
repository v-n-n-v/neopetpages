WHAT IS FOOD CLUB?

Food Club is a gambling game and is one of the easiest ways to earn NP everyday.

In this game, you bet on various pirates who participate in a daily eating competition (and hopefully correctly guess the winners!). There are a total of 20 pirates that you can bet on in 5 arenas. You are allowed to make 10 unique bets every day and they can feature any combination of pirates in different arenas that you choose.

On the Main Pirates page, you can find a list of all of the pirates along with relevant stats such as their Strength and Weight (as well as links to their individual descriptions, where you can also read their favorite foods and their allergies).
On the Next Round page, you can find links which give you information on each arena for the day, such as which pirates are competing and what foods are being served in each arena. By comparing the foods being served and each pirates' favorites and allergies, you can determine their Food Adjustment for the day. All of these stats factor in to who has the best probability of winning.

Finally, on the Place a Bet page you can view your Maximum Bet amount (which is calculated based on the age of your account), the arenas and pirates competing in each arena, and most importantly each pirates' Odds for the day.

The odds go from most likely (2:1) to least likely (13:1) and are determined by multiple factors - mostly, the stats of each pirate which you found on the Main Pirates page.

Your potential winnings from a single bet are multiplied by these odds - for instance, if you bet 1000 NP on a single (2:1) pirate, your potential winnings for that bet are 2000 NP, and the probability of that bet winning are probably quite good.
Alternatively, if you bet on a single (13:1) pirate with the same amount, your potential winnings for that bet are 13000 NP, however the probability of that bet winning are much lower.
You can even bet on a (2:1) pirate in one arena and a (13:1) pirate in a different one, both at the same time - your potential winnings in that example would be a whopping 26000 NP, but since BOTH pirates must win simultaneously in order for that bet to win, the probability of it actually winning is A LOT lower.

Once again, you are allowed 10 unique bets everyday, and they can feature multiple combinations of different pirates in different arenas. There are many different methods and strategies that people use while creating and placing their bets.

Ideally, you want a good balance of risk and potential profit - and importantly, you should place bets that are comfortable to you.

This is a very, VERY basic run-down of the game.
There is an excellent and much more in-depth Food Club guide located at /~Jawsnapper0991.

How do I read your bets?
You read them like a table from left-to-right.
At the very top, you can see the names of each arena. On the furthest left-hand side, you can see each bet is numbered 1-10. You read Bet #1 from left-to-right, selecting and checkmarking whatever pirate I listed underneath each specific arena (or skipping if I left a specific arena blank), then you read Bet #2 left-to-right, then Bet #3, and so on, and so on.

When can I bet?
Each round ends and a new one begins at about 2:15 pm NST*. (Due to lag issues, the round now actually ends as late as 4:15 pm!)
You may start betting for the new round at that time.
Betting closes at about 1:45pm NST everyday.
(It reopens at 2:15pm NST and due to lag the round does not actually reset until later - so if you're late you have a second chance!)

How many bets do I place?
You should place 10 bets a day.
If you are following someone else's bets instead of making your own, you should follow all 10 of their bets - mixing and matching will make your bets less effective.

How much NP do I bet?
I strongly recommend you bet your Maximum amount for each bet.
You can find what your daily maximum currently is here, in bold letters.
There are really only two exception to this...

  • If you do not have a lot of NP or think that the bets are too risky for you, then you might prefer to bet less than maximum.
    Just REMEMBER: If you bet less, you win less.
  • Also, if the specific bet you input makes the Total Payoff say 1,000,000 NP. This is the maximum cap NP you can win on a single bet, so there is no use in spending more than you have to. In that case, decrease your Bet Amount accordingly.

My side account is really old - can I use it to play Food Club?
No. You can only play games on your main account.
This question was addressed in the editorial 469.

I placed my bets earlier and then I won, but it says I won a different amount - why?
The odds of specific pirates change throughout the day relative to how many or how few people bet on that pirate.
For example, if Gooblah was listed at (4:1) when you first placed your bets, but then everyone bet on Gooblah, his odds might change to (2:1) by the end of the day (while the odds of the other pirates in his arena might also change in the opposite direction).
