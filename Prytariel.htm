　Altador Cup Basics　　　　　　

New to the Altador Cup?

If you're new to the Altador Cup or just need to brush up on the fundamentals, you're in luck! There's lots of resources available for you to check out. Neopets-approved fansites SunnyNeo.com, JellyNeo.net, and TheDailyNeopets.com are great places to go to for rules, team backgrounds, support graphics, and more!

Now, let's briefly talk about some board lingo. If you're new to the boards, there are certain abbreviations used for the sake of efficiency:
YYB Yooyuball
SS or SLS Slushie Slinger
MSN Make Some Noise
SOSD Shootout Showdown
Pry Prytariel, TM's team captain


Game Info and Guides

There are some great game guides out there that make it possible for players to learn the ins and outs of each game. Please note there are minimum scores you need to meet for each game in order for your score to count. See below:
SLS 270
MaSN 2700
SOSD one goal/ten seconds
If you send scores below the minimum it may add to your games played, but you won't earn rank points for it and will just be wasted time.

There also caps for each game, or a maximum number of times per day you can play each game before it no longer counts toward your rank points. They are as follows:
YYB 35
SLS 65
MaSN 301
SOSD 301

Scorekeeping

The TOTM board has had a years-long tradition of keeping track of daily total scores for each game in order to motivate the players and show areas that may need some focus. However, we are out a scorekeeper this year, so please inquire on the boards for more information about posting your scores.
