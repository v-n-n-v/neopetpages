Frequently Asked Questions

These are questions I get asked a lot about Snuggle, so here are your answers! If you don't see your question here, than please feel freel to neomail me.

If I change my pets customization will their picture on Snuggle change?
Yes! Snuggle uses auto images, so any changes made to your pet will be shown.

I don't like my pets current customization, can I send you a different picture?
No, sorry. If I did this for one person, I would have to do it for everyone and that would be too much of a hassle. I apologize for any inconvenience.

Do you list UC babies?
Yes! They are just along side the rest of their species though, they recieve no special treatment.

How long will it take my pet to be added to Snuggle? I neomailed you X Days ago!
I like to add in batches, as it is easier. I update the site roughly every two weeks or so, it depends on the number of listings sent in.

My pet has a horrible name, will you add their nickname?
No, sorry. Snuggle will only add your pets official name, if your pet has a nickname, put that on its lookup.

Does my pet have to be clothed and/or customized to be listed at Snuggle?
No! You may list any baby pet, regardless of clothing or customization.

Can I list pets on my side accounts?
Yes! You can list any baby pet that you own.

Will you advertise my pet as UFT/UFA?
No sorry, Snuggle is for permanent pets only please.

How to Obtain
So how does one go about getting a baby pet? Well there are a couple different ways you can explore to get your baby pet; painting, morphing, and adoption. I will go over each one for you, so you can get on your way to having your own baby pet.

Painting

One of the easiest and most direct ways to get a baby pet is to paint one of your existing pets. Every species of Neopet can be painted baby.

How do you get a baby paint brush?
You can buy a baby paint brush at any time from the faerie queen at The Hidden Tower for 600,000 NP. Too expensive? Don't worry, you can almost always find users selling them on The Trading Post for little cheaper, at around 575,000 NP usually.

Morphing


So you want a baby pet, but maybe you don't already have a pet of the species you want turned baby? You can buy a baby morphing potion! Not every species has a baby morphing potion, but some do. Be aware though, some potions cost more neopoints than the actual paint brush. Make sure you do your pricing research before purchasing a baby morphing potion, to make sure you are getting the best deal.

Adopting

Last, but not least, adopting. There is two ways to go about adopting.

The Pound
If your account is over 4 months old, you can try you luck at The Neopian Pound. Finding a baby pet in the pound though is a gamble, you could search for hours and find none. It's also worth nothing that baby pets you find in the pound do not come with their paint brush clothes, so don't be expecting those adorable Baby Skeith Nighttime Pyjamas after you click adopt. Make sure you have enough NP on hand to cover the adoption fee.

User Transfer
Sometimes kind Neopians will put their baby pets up for adoption on the boards. You usually have to at least fill out a neomail app explaining why you want to adopt the pet, but some people go all out with their applications. This way of getting a baby Neopet is also very much a gamble, lots of other players are potentially applying for the same pet you are. It is completely up to the owner of the pet who they wish to adopt out to.
Once again even to accept a user transfer, your account must be at least 4 months old to get a baby pet this way. User to user transfer keep the babies paint brush clothes, so if the pet had clothes to begin with, they will still have the clothes when they are sent to you. You also still have to pay an adoption fee like you would at the normal pound.
