OUR GOAL:

The Neopians involved with this operation hope to find homes for all unwanted pets of Neopia.
What we do is find "unfortunately" named pets in the pound and zap them until they are more desired.

We advertise them for one week.

If the pet doesn't find a home within a week the foster parent has two choices:
free pound so the pet finds a random home or keep zapping for another chance.

Some may say that color doesn't matter.
Well just take a look in the pound, it seems names definitely do.
We want to give these pets a fighting chance!

HOW TO ADOPT A PET:

Clickable links are available to view the pet of your dreams before you mail the foster.
All pets contacts are listed below the pet.
If there are instructions for adopting the pet, please read and follow them so things go smoother.

EVERYONE CAN ADOPT OUR PETS!

We do NOT require applications or have lots of insane rules.
A friendly, polite neomail asking about the pet in question will generally suffice.
Please remember that the more popular pet colors will likely have several people trying to adopt.
It is at the owner's discretion to choose the right home for their pet.
