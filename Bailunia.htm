    • Introduction

    The Art Gallery is a Biweekly neopets event where artists across neopia submit their pieces fitting a specific theme for the chance to win prizes and (most importantly) have their art displayed on the art gallery page forever. This is a popular event, with many people entering weekly but don't worry! Even if you aren't very experienced, you can still get in! As an experienced Art Gallery Entrant, I will go through the basics of the Art Gallery for all neopians who wish to enter!

    Questions?

    Kikocat is the judge of the Art gallery and a moderator on the forums. She frequently talks to and takes into account suggestions from the users for the Art gallery. She also judges the beauty contest and releases the schedule for each month. If a question you have isn't answered in this guide. Message me at Mapleshading or ask the Art chat boards for help, we'd be happy to assist

    UPDATE: there is now an Art gallery FAQ on the neopets help page. https://www.jumpstart.com/support/np-classic under "Game Hint and Help."

    • The Rules .

    Before you can enter the Art Gallery, its good to know the rules on entering the Art Gallery and what you can, can't and what you are recommended to do. This page will outline the rules of the art gallery. Some are universally known and others are not well known.

    Basic Rules

  • You may not enter another person's work in the Art Gallery, All pieces must be made on your own.
  • You may enter up to 3 pictures into the art gallery per day. However, submitting the same picture multiple times does not increase your chance of being chosen
  • You may not include copyrighted images that are not neopets related in your piece.
  • You may not have an offsite related signature in your piece.
  • Your picture must be related to neopets and include a neopets character.
  • All images must be either GIF or JPG.
  • You cannot submit any Art gallery entries into the Beauty Contest if accepted, they must be unique to the Art Gallery.
  • Image filesize must be no more than 80kb, recommended image size is 300x300 - 600x600.
  • Any art medium is allowed, including 3D art mediums such as modeling, sculpting, traditional art and plushies

    • What You Should Create

    Honestly I have no business telling you what you should Create, but for the Art Gallery there are certain themes that are followed by the judge that will determine how she'll select the pieces. These themes are in the schedule which you can see on this page and the art boards! The following types of themes are...

    Pet Days

    Pet days are centered around a specific species of Neopet! For example, Monday the 9th is Pteri day, so all of the art pieces that will be accepted to the Art gallery will surround Pteris. So therefore, you should draw a Pteri or a neopets character that is a pteri! Pet days are the most common days. Specific pet days such as Lupe drum up the most hype of all of the days. There is usually an option to submit under you specific pet day in the submission page

    Holidays

    Holidays are similar to pet days as there is a specific theme but the theme in this case is the day that is being celebrated. For example, Halloween is a very popular holiday in Neopia. One person may draw a swarm of Krobats, while another may draw an aisha trick-or-treating! You have more freedom to draw what you'd like during these days so take advantage of it!. There is usually an option to submit under you specific holiday in the submission page

    Random Days

    Random days are the most fun of all! As you can draw anything neopets related! You can also resubmit rejected art gallery entries from the other to categories to random days! You can submit under any category, though the "other" category is recommended!

    Do

  • Resize your image after completion
  • Keep your image under 80KB
  • Keep your image size small
  • Submit 24-48 hours in advance to your chosen day

    • Submitting your piece

    Its the moment of truth! I'm so proud of you for getting this far! Submitting your piece is honestly the hardest part as the rules are somewhat inconsistent and confusing. But I'll try my best to guide you through it.

    File size

    This is the easy part! I highly recommend that you resize your image after you are done creating it! First you resize your image to a size that supports a file under 80 KB (I usually resize my image to 600x600). Then convert to JPG or GIF, this can be done online or through an image editing software. Finally you check if your file size is under 80 KB, if not, many websites can help you compress it. Also! Keep all of your file names lowercase in order to make sure kikocat sees it. For example, submit as kougra.jpg rather than Kougra.jpg

    How to submit

    When you come to the submission page of the Art Gallery, you'll be given two options: Submit your file and what category to chose. Submitting your file is self explanatory but choosing the category is important. If you are submitting under a random day, it doesn't matter but its recommended to choose "other". If you are submitting under a specific pet day or holiday, chose that day and submit under there.

    When to submit

    This is the tricky part, as submitting too early can overshadow your submission and submitting too late can mean you are too late! General consensus is that you should submit 24 to 48 hours in advance of your chosen pet day. I usually submit the night before but that general guideline is good to follow.

    UPDATE: According to Kikocat, the best time to upload is anytime from after the previous Art gallery uploads to on the date of your chosen Art gallery day

    What Kikocat judges on

  • Creativity
  • Effort

    • How Pieces are Judged

    Please note that this is all speculation. I cannot speak for Kikocat but as a 74 time Art gallery winner I do think that I have some credibility in this category. However, what we do know is that Kikocat is inclusive of all experience levels and mediums. .

    The Primary things that I think Kikocat judges on are creativity and effort. I think Kikocat wants to see your hard work and loves seeing diversity in the various art gallery pieces. What we do know for a fact is that Kikocat cannot see the titles, nor usernames of those who enter the art gallery, which is why sometimes two pieces from the same user get in by accident.

    When selection comes around, Kikocat can select anywhere from 12 to 36 entries to be accepted to the art gallery

    I do not fully know the process of selecting and judging pieces but I'd like to think I know what Kikocat values in the Art Gallery and I hope this helps in inspiring you when making your art pieces.

    UPDATE: Kikocat has stated, " I pick a diversity if I can, meaning not all the same colour, although I usually love far too many pictures to get them all in. But you are right, creativity, effort, connection to Neopets and that species."

    • Selection, Accepted or Rejected

    You'll have to check your inventory or the Art Gallery page manually to see if you have been accepted or not. A common cue that you have been accepted is 10,000+ Neopoints on your hand and a new item in your inventory.

    Accepted?

    Congratulations! That's amazing and we hope you come back to enter more. Now that you've won your piece is immortalized in Neopia forever and you have a chance of being on the front page to represent all of the art gallery entries.

    Now for your prizes. You'll win a beautiful trophy on your lookup, 10,000 Neopoints and a cool new item! This cool new item comes from a pool that all creativity contests share. This pool can be seen on jellyneo. Funfact: The most valuable prize you can currently win is the Candychan.

    Rejected?

    Don't worry! It happens to everyone on here! Its normal to feel sad about it but there's always another chance so don't give up!

    Remember that you can always enter your rejected piece into the beauty contest, a random art gallery day or even next year for a certain pet day or holiday. This piece is with you forever and can be reentered at any time! And remember, You and your art are valuable and you've done an amazing job!
