    NAME | Balderok
    AGE | Young Adult
    SPECIES | Cybunny
    COLOR | Royal
    GENDER | Male
    BIRTHDAY | April 27th
    SYMBOL | Shield with Pumpkin

    Balderok hails from western Tuva where farmland stretches from horizon to horizon. There, his family owns a large farm where they grow pototoes, carrots, parsnips and pumpkins (Balderok's favorite!) along with various other root vegetables. In addition to produce, they also raise several types of petpets, including Zebies, Nuks, Vacanas and Gobblers.

    Constantly overshadowed by his many brothers and sisters, Balderok always dreamed of far-off adventures and a place where he could make a name for himself. Though he loved working on the farm, he still yearned for individuality. After harvest time, Balderok would accompany his father and several older siblings to the city of Caer Tuva. There, they would set up a large stall in the central market to sell their wares. It was here where Balderok got his first glimpses of the esteemed Royal Guard, noble knights of the realm charged with protecting the citizens and upholding law.

    When he came of age, he said goodbye to his family and farm where he grew up and, along with his best friend Suchinn, set off for the exciting city.

    Royal pets were introduced to Neopia back in February 2004, and is one of the few colors that look different depending on if the pet is male or female. Of the 55 Neopets species, 51 can be painted Royal. Those that cannot are Chias, Jetsams, Ogrins and Vandagyre. Though it took quite a bit of time, Royal was eventually introduced for petpets in May 2011.

    Zafaras were the first lucky pets able to be painted Royal, followed quickly by Gelerts, Scorchios, Aishas, and Unis all released throughout March 2004. My personal favorites are the Korbats and Shoyrus, released in January 2005 and April 2005! The last pet able to be painted Royal before the conversion in April 2007 was the Elephante, and the Yurble was the first Royal released afterwards.

    Many would agree that the conversion took away much of the personality and prettiness that Royal brought to Neopia, and UC (unconverted, or old art) Royals continue to be highly sought-after and admired even today. However, given all the customization options we have now, regular converted Royals can be just as much to dress up and play with!

    How to get a Royal pet or petpet



    To paint a pet Royal, you can visit the Hidden Tower and buy the paintbrush at a set price of 1.75 mil. You can also buy the petpet paint brush version there too for an easy 400k. Royal can also be gotten through a Fountain Faerie Quest. Kyrii's are the only species to have a Royal morphing potion, both of which run a bit cheaper than buying the paintbrush!
