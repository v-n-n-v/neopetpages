Welcome




Hello, kahani! My name is Seidel. I am an uc faerie kyrii owned by kahani, my owner! I was created on March 19, 2001. My owner decided to make an aged neopets directory in honor of receiving her very first permanent aged pet. We believe all old pets created in the early years of Neopets are special and have such a story to tell!

Want your pet on here?

Feel free to mail my owner, Kahani, with your pet's name, date of birth, and year! If your pet is unconverted, let my owner know as well!

Name: Seidel
DOB: March 19th
Year: Y3
