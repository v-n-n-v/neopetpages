Welcome!

Hello there! My name is Mai, and this is the SpaceCats! I created this directory so people can both show off their Aishas and get inspiration for their own customs. Submissions are currently open so feel free to NM me to list your pet!

Rules

ʚ♡ɞ This is a customization based directory, so pets must be (mostly) fully customized. "Fully customized" is highly subjective, so I'll leave this up to you if your Aisha qualifies. However, they must at least be wearing a background in order to be accepted!

ʚ♡ɞ UCs/nostalgic pets are allowed, however your pet will go under the category of what color token they are wearing as opposed to their actual color. For instance, if they are wearing a Nostalgic Faerie Aisha token but are actually painted blue, they will still go in the Faerie section!

ʚ♡ɞ Please only list pets that are 1) permanent pets and 2) you plan to keep as Aishas. If a pet is traded/adopted out (and lose their customization), or is morphed into another species, I will remove them.
On this note, do NOT message anyone regarding adopting/trading for their pets.

ʚ♡ɞ Taking inspiration for your own customizations is allowed and encouraged! However, please do not directly copy.
