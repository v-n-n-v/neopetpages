The Adam Asparagus Guide

Welcome

Welcome to The Adam Asparagus Guide! Here is where you can find a list of everyday prices of the Asparagus on the Neopets' "About Us" page. I started this to help my friend and I find out the lowest price to get the Adam "Number 6" avatar. This guide can help you, too! If you go to the "About Us" page and see the price of the Asparagus, that is the exact number of Neopoints Adam has on hand at that moment, and the price drops and rises daily. This guide will help you to figure out when is the best day, and best price, to try for the avatar. Good luck, all!


This guide was started on Sept. 9, 2009.
