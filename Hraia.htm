The Shenkuu Directory

After knocking softly on the wooden door of a small, ancient building atop one of Shenkuu's many mountains, you're greeted by a Techo dressed in traditional clothes. She happily greets you, as if seeing an old friend, and puts a hand on your back to direct you inside. The lizard removes a big, elegantly decorated scroll from underneath the table which faced the door, plopping it atop the surface before finding his reading glasses.

– There we are! The Shenkuu directory. Here, you'll be able to find everyone who lives here. At least everyone who matters! – she chuckles before unlocking the paper archive, rolling it open in front of you.

– So? Who do you want to find?

About

  • Welcome to the Shenkuu Pet Directory!
  • This directory was created as a way for those with Shenkuu and generally East Asian themed customizations to cross-reference, gather inspiration and create a sense of community.
  • If you would like to be included in this directory, your pet only has to have a custom around those themes and to plan to keep that custom semi-permanently. I will be removing pets which do not fit the theme anymore as to maintain the directory cohesive. Please feel free to neomail me if you are interested.
  • If your pet has a non-permanent Shenkuu custom, you may submit it to be kept permanently as an image of the outfit, and not the pet. However, only one image per pet is allowed on the directory.
  • We are always looking to grow and expand. Don't fret to neomail me any suggestions or comments as well.
