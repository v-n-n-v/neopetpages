my petpet

Ezzie is my little soreen, gifted to me by my owner as a welcome home present when I was first adopted, and I just adore him. He doesn't do so well outside of water (soreens skins dry up very quickly!) so a lot of the time I have to leave him with a sitter or friend whenever I'm away. I'm lucky he's so easy to look after, except that he can get pretty demanding when he wants to play!

I did try the keep-him-in-a-jar thing briefly so that I could bring him along, but I ended up paranoid that I'd misplace him somewhere. Plus, I think it's kinda cruel when he's such a high-energy creature

I often get asked if I'm going to paint him, seems its such a common thing to do these days. I do kinda like the green and electric colours on soreens, but also kinda hope some new colours will be discovered, so I probably won't be painting him anytime soon.


besties

While a have a ton of aquaintences and some great buddies I catch up with once or twice a year, I don't have many close friends (because of this whole travelling lifestyle thing) so I treasure these guys with my whole heart.

Troodon

I'VE NEVER SEEN ANYONE GET PEOPLE HYPED LIKE HE DOES
Troodon is the only pet that's been here longer than I have, though he doesn't act it and is perpetually young at heart. He's the life of every party I've seen him at, and immediately lights up a room with his contagious energy.

Sometimes I'll be feeling depressed and then realise I haven't talked to him in a month. A quick phonecall fixes me right up.

I would protect him with my life. Speaking of which I have had to get between him and aggressive strangers on more than a few occassions when his bubbliness overflows and annoys some tough guy.

Go say hi to him, he's always up for a chat and honestly you're doing yourself a disservice by not meeting him.

Juaro

OH YOU SWEET SUMMERCHILD
I am very protective of gentle little Juaro. Not too many people know this but I actually hatched him. It's true! It's too long a story to go into right now, but making the decision to raise him afterwards was the craziest thing I've ever done.

You worry about me too much I think.
And you don't worry about yourself enough..."
He's all grown up now of course (draiks mature pretty quickly!) and I don't get to see him too often but I'll always see him as my baby boy.

Codeo

WHY CAN'T I TRUST YOU?
I don't fully know how to feel about Codeo. I mean he's fun to hang out with casually (at first) and I want the best for him, but I'll always end up starting some ridiculous argument with him. No one else brings out this side of me, I really don't like it. Maybe it's his immaturity, idk.

I think he's a bad influence on Juaro too. I was never too keen on them hanging out together, but they seem to be spending more time with each other than ever...

Dude, you just need to chill is all. You're always in such a hurry.
All those places they aren't going anywhere. Just be a cool guy for like two seconds?"
People ask what my problem really is with him and it's like, nothing? Can't I just not like how irresponsible and thoughtless he is? Idk man, I just can't live with him for more than a few days, and I know I'm not the only one.
