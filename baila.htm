Rules

    General

  • For all lends, please post your name, a and a little bit about yourself. We like to get to know our lendees, so stay and chat!
  • Please don't mail a lender unless you have already posted on the board. We do not lend through mail alone.
  • Begging will not be tolerated. All lenders have the right to lend whom they choose.
  • Paying NP for lends or gifting in order to receive a lend is against the rules, thus all gifts will be declined. A simple please and thank you will suffice!
    • Item Lends

  • All items MUST be returned to the lender in the same way they were sent to you. Failure to return an item will result in being reported.
  • To ensure a safe lend, please remain active on the lending board. If you need to leave Neopets for more than a few minutes, please let us know so the item isn't sent to you while you're away.
  • Please don't ask to be lent a specific item if you haven't been lent items of lesser value.
    • Pet Lends

  • All pets MUST be returned to the lender the same way they were sent to you. Please mail the lender to sort out the return of the pet.
  • Do NOT remove any pet/petpets, paint or morph a pet you have been lent. Doing so will result in you being removed from all chains.
  • If you are a part of a chain lend, please mail the next user in line to receive the pet. If they do not reply in 24 hours, mail the next user in line until a transfer can be arranged.
  • If you are the last person in a chain, please mail the owner of the pet to arrange a safe return home.
    • The Big Three

  • Your inventory must be nearly full before receiving one of the big three to ensure the safety of the item.
  • The item must be returned IMMEDIATELY. This means no posting on the boards while in possession of the item. If you would like to post the events, please copy/paste them and hit send AFTER you have sent the item back.
  • Screenshots are permitted.
  • Users that would like to be lent BGC must post with their Grarrl as active and paste its NeoLodge checkout time. You will not be lent if you fail to do so.

    Due to a bug, BGC will not be lent to users whose Grarrl's name has too many numbers or consists of numbers entirely.

  • When being lent MSPP, the only page you need to visit after accepting the item is your inventory. Please refrain from visiting other pages while being lent.
  • To be lent SuAP, please ensure that you have a Pea Chia as your active pet. If you need to be lent a Pea Chia, please let us know before requesting a SuAP lend.
  • If you have read all of the above rules for the big three, please post two !
