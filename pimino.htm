CD ROM; PIMINO'S PARTY STARTS UP

Featuring No one




You're greeted by a 'new game' screen, you're not allowed to name the save. It simply states it as 'player 1'.

A small cutscene plays, Pimino's eye opens, looking upon the world around him, a bright cheery place with flowers and homes as far as the eye can see. It was beautiful, but boring, Pimino blinks, walking quietly along the stone paths. Pimino could only think of the ways they could make it even nicer to live in. A tree sprouting teeth is quite the sight isn't it? Or an eyeball floating in the sky in place of the sun, they needed YOUR help to make things fun, skipping along the path enthusiastically, There's a couple of young neopets nearby singing 'ashes to ashes'. They walk into an oddly colored bouncy castle, and turn to look at you.


There's nothing to say, you click the textbox and the game simply begins, You point and click the items and things in your inventory to help Pimino with their plans. After each 'big' puzzle in the section is solved, they change the area, usually making it nonsensical, silly, maybe just a little eerie. You trust Pimino, as much as you can trust a fake character. At first, they seem to genuinely be having fun, they follow you into every room or 'stand behind' you. They get confused with large cluttered puzzles, and they make funny little puns and jokes. Pimino calls you their best friend.

But things start taking a weird turn when NPC's start to show up, none of them seem to like Pimino, none of them seem to appreciate their 'efforts' in sillying the place up. An oddly colored aisha tells you Pimino has been 'Trouble' since they showed up out of nowhere. But the game continues, you help Pimino anyways since there's not really much else you CAN do. It's the goal of the game anyway.

A character named 'Anyone' suddenly approaches you while you're clicking around a small room trying to find the codes to a lock. You can't really remember what it looked like. Their text box was scrambled and messy.



Then they were gone just like that. Like nothing had even happened. You think you can mention this to Pimino, but no options come up to imply the existence of the stranger.

Pimino continues to guide your playthrough. Occasionally getting into their own special danger. You honestly forget about the encounter with Anyone for a little bit. Pimino creates a forest of living trees that steal apples from other tree's after you finish a puzzle. Pimino giggles. ❝An apple a day keeps the doctor away!❝, How cute. You save and quit the game for the night. Thinking about the characters, it was a charming little game.

That night you have a dream about Pimino, Pimino is asking for friends. But everyone thinks clowns are scary. They ask you to be their friend, You smile and say yes, they're ecstatic, flapping their weirdly animated hands in excitement. They made a friend. A real one.

When you wake up, for just a second, you think you see an eyeball staring at you from your window. You wonder if that was real, must've been whatever was leftover from the dream land. You start the day, you find that the living room smells like vanilla, The whole day you feel odd. Sometimes you catch a figure at the corner of your eyes. Is Anyone there? But nothing replies.

You boot up the game again, A tired day has led up to this moment, getting back to the game, Pimino and you continue their journey. They introduce you to their pet 'Messier' A kookith with an freaky face. They tell you not to worry, Messier's face is a black hole, they say, they'll eat just about anything. But otherwise Messier is mostly harmless.

Days and days continue to pass as you play each day, your home is starting to seemingly be effected, at least you think it is. You keep feeling watched, you keep feeling cold breezes where it wouldn't be possible. The scariest thing was when you closed the medicine cabinet, only to see a creature with an eye for a head, just for a second, then it was gone. When at work you keep seeing things brighter then they should be, why is the sky so blue? Why is the grass neon? Maybe it wasn't the game, maybe you were driving yourself crazy. The further into the game the more distorted it gets as well, the harder it is to see any real 'story' line, you have no idea where this is going.

You're almost done with the journey, you can tell because your save says 85% Story completed. Pimino seems excited to view their end result, clapping their hands. At some point only a bit later, before a cutscene or the puzzle is finished, Pimino suddenly speaks to you.



The question strikes you as odd, out there? Where? You're not sure, but it's not like you can really ask OR answer anyways. You just continue on the path. You think you hear a footstep outside your door, when you look up, there's no shadow underneath, you shake your head and continue on the puzzle. This puzzle is hard. Pimino comments on the hardness of the puzzle too, claiming ❝the end is worth the trouble.❝

Pimino asks you some odd questions all day, some (but not all) include:
❝Would a friend really help you?❝
❝If you are a balloon animal, which one are you?❝
❝Is ashes to ashes your favorite song?❝
❝Why does the living room smell bad?❝
❝What kind of material is your house going to be.❝
❝Are you scared of clowns, too?❝

You just think it's because of the oddness of the game, you don't take it seriously. You close the game, 95% left.

You invite a friend over and tell them about the weird game, You show them the CD and the loading screen, how it was fun for the most part but it wasn't your style, it was stupidly nonsensical, which you liked at first, but it was hard to put a conclusive story to it, it's more of a joke game, or maybe some stupid 'deep' art game by someone who thought this had meaning to it, but you didn't think it was serious. You say maybe later they can try it out, but they think that the game isn't really their thing either, it's too weird.

You go to bed that night, you don't remember your dream, but you feel a sense of foreboding, you kind of remember the words 'This could be anyone' being said to you. You go on through your day. You boot up the game. You're greeted with Pimino who looks a upset.




The music changes from that boppy electronic tune to a solemn quiet hum. You go to the 'last' section, a small cabin, there's a banner made of newspaper clippings above the door that says 'WELCOME TO PIMINO'S PARTY'.

You click the door and enter the cabin, it's an absolute mess. Pimino stands by the couch and shifts. Looking back towards the screen.



You begin to explore the room

You explore the room, but something seems odd, there's no puzzle, just stuff to explore and look at, There's nonsensical pictures everywhere, there's a lot of stuff cluttering the ground, and there's not really much else, no real puzzles to be found, You're stuck like this for hours, You even look online for guides but find no one who's actually completed the game, it's so niche that most peoples copies were seemingly broken at this point. You finally, after what seems like eternity, find a little square, that seems like an out of place area for a clickable area to be in. Pimino doesn't say anything, but they open their weird sharp-toothed mouth. You Click. Your computer begins to smoke, and you hear a soft 'Thanks anyways'.

Soon after, your bedroom door creaks open.











About Pimino:
Pimino was once the anti-villain protagonist of a small CD-ROM, point and click video game called 'Pimino's Party', Pimino is a small clown who changes the world from normal reality, to a nonsensical nightmare, thinking that they're making it funner to be in, therefore doing good in a desperate attempt to impress and befriend the inhabitants of their world. However, Pimino got tired of this; as it wasn't working, sure enough they could MAYBE make friends in the real world instead, so with their reality bending skílls they escaped into the real world to do as they please. The person who accidentally let them escape is unknown. Pimino can still go into their little computer game when they want to, but they mostly just run around Neopia, All Pimino wants is a friend. Luckily out here, they have some.

Don't be fooled, he is NOT evil! Just a little clueless about how things should work. And maybe just a little stubborn.
