About Species Inclusion Originality Effort Cuteness

This page is all about celebrating and praising a wide variety of adoptables, including freebie awards. There are 4 awards each with its respective category. Each person can win up to 2 awards for one petpage. Many of them won 2! The awards I picked for any page reflect what I believe to be the strongest aspects of the adopts, not to imply that they lack in any other qualities.

All of the awards I drew traditionally. All of the adopts featured are gold, silver, or blue whenever possible.

Any adopt not shown here is not meant to imply that they aren't good art. This page is meant for celebrating the adopts that I personally want to give extra praise to. I have a large adopts directory where I'll share any and all adopts.

Featuring this award on your page is completely optional. If you do, I recommend adding width="100" to your image code.

Categories

Originality - Adoptables that stand out for their presentation in some way. Could be for the art style, props, and/or pose.

Effort - Adoptables that have a lot of detail and/or have been drawn in large quantities. Small differences between colors such as the transparency in jelly and the shininess of gold make your adopts even better. Some go beyond 1 full set for 1 species. Bonus points for animation!

Cuteness - Adoptables noteworthy for how cute they are! Any species can qualify for this award.

Species - Included less popular species. Must have drawn at least 2 instances of any less popular species listed on this page more then once. 2 different species both drawn one time count for this award.
