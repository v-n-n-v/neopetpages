Paddy's Guide to Nickelodeon Card Items


August 2014 - Nickelodeon cards have been discontinued and have been replaced by Neocash cards. The old Nickelodeon and realy old Neocash cards can still be redeemed, although the card redemption items will be from the old item pool, and not the new Neocash card one.
Items given from the original Neopets Neocash card items are currently the same as for the Nickelodeon cards (as listed on this page).


Previous items from the original cards can be found on Custard's Petpage, and current Neocash card items can be found on Drouwin's Petpage.

The Basics


Nickeoldeon cards were available in the USA, Canada, the UK, Australia, New Zealand, Mexico and Brazil.


With the latest update to the NC cards, all card denomiations now give out items from the same pool, so the item you get for redeeming a $25 card could be the same item as you could get from redeeming a £5 card. "Exclusive" cards (like the US $25 Monkey Quest card from GameStop) also give out items from the same pool (they are no longer exclusive items for those cards).


If you have redeemed a Neocash card and have received an item not currently listed here, please send a neomessage to dragonsflame_uk, and I'll update the page :)
