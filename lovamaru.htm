welcome


Greetings, welcome to Stitched Up - a premade NP & NC outfit site run by Caylee. Stitched Up is dedicated to having high quality outfits at a fraction of the cost for Neopians. If you have an idea for an outfit that you'd like Stitched Up to create, feel free to neomail your suggestion. This site is about YOU, and so we want you to have as many outfit ideas as possible. :)


about us


This section is about the guidelines we take in making our outfits.

  • We never have more than 3 neocash items in one outfit. Most of the time we will include a neopoint item as an alternative for a neocash item in case you don't have neocash.
  • Speaking of neocash, we always use buyable neocash items in our outfits.
  • If you are afraid about how much you will spend on an outfit - don't worry! All of our outfits are less than 250,000 np. You can always substitute a cheaper item for your outfit if you need to, though.
  • Many of the outfits are gender oriented. An example? Sorceress is meant for a female pet, while the Pirate outfit is meant for a male pet. If we think that the same style of outfit could suit both genders, but with some seperate accessories, then we'll do something like Spooky (girl) and Spooky (boy).
  • You may be wondering why all of our pet models are kacheeks. Our reason: Kacheeks are easy pets to show off how the outfit works and they are commonly used for customization.
  • Lastly, there are many pet-specific items that are similar to the items we use for the outfits. If you want to save neocash (for wigs, clothes, and accessories), then buy pet-specific items for your pet.

Hopefully these guidelines will help you understand just how much Stitched Up has worked to get you the highest quality outfits possible. :)
