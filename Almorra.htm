WELCOME, ADVENTURERS!

Introduction

Dungeons & Draiks is an adventurer's guild based in Meridell, made up of all types of scholars, magic users, and fighters! Each of the Neopians in the guild have their own special traits, and they are all listed here in the guild's membership list.

Dungeons & Draiks is a character directory meant for any pet that is based on the TTRPG Dungeons and Dragons. If your pet is based on another RPG and fits into the categories that are present, please feel free to list! For example, if you play Pathfinder, you are welcome to list in the class closest to your characters, or in the Other section. If you plan to list in any of the categories, you must have a custom on your pet - pets without any customization will be delisted.
This directory is organized by character class first, and then other categories. D&D monsters are welcome as well as anything that is related to the game - real words and names, misspells, themed customizations, anything you feel fits into the directory I am happy to list. A character for your pet is NOT required to list, but the pet should be clearly D&D/RPG themed.

Sidenote - the guild is part of my worldbuilding project! Your pets are not required to be a part of this fiction, but if you'd like them to be actually involved and have connections to my pet's stories, feel free to dm me! :)

How to List

Send the following information to Lup via Neomail!
► Pet Name:
► Section: [Class Based] [Monster] [Other]
► Class and Subclass (if Class Based):
► If monster or other, pet is based on:
► Petpet:
► Any other info: (short bio, links/buttons, etc?)

After listing, don't forget to grab a button for your pet!
