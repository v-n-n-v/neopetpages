Welcome

Zapping Your Dream Pets (ZYDP) is a group of Neopians who zap pets and then give them away to people on our Wishlist. Although our Wishlist is pretty extensive, sometimes we zap pets into color/species combinations that aren't currently wished for. To find homes for those pets, we put them in our Adoption Agency!

So, welcome! Have a look around, and see if any of these pets catch your eye. The pets featured on this page belong to a variety of ZYDP Zappers - make sure you contact the appropriate person!

Can't find the pet you're looking for? Follow the instructions on the ZYDP wishlist and submit a wish for your dream pet! Be sure to check out our Zappers' pages and see what guild members are currently zapping.

If you have any questions or concerns about this page, neomail Vanille. This page will be updated at least once a week. If your neomail was sent before the date listed on the side bar, try resending your message. Thank you for your patience and understanding!

Because of the sheer number of neomails I get for the Adoption Agency, I do not usually reply to you adding or dropping a pet. However, if you notice that the date of the last update has changed and your pet is still not added, please just sent me another Neomail.

Rules

Adding your pet:

  1. In order to add your pet to this page, you MUST be an active member of the ZYDP guild OR a member of the ZYDP Zapper's List. If you decide to only sign up for the Zapper's List, please make sure your name is ON it before mailing the Adoption Agency!
  2. Please do not add pets that you are still zapping - list your labbies on the ZYDP labbies page.
  3. Keep in mind that pets that are added here should be at their end stage of zapping and have a shot at finding a home if they currently are not wished for. We do NOT list just any pet.
  4. Recommended, but not required: Place a note on your pet's lookup that says they're UFA.
NOTE: Adopting out a pet through this page DOES NOT count towards the Happy Endings page or your Guild Rank!

Automatic removal from the Adoption Agency:

  • A deadline was posted with the pet, and the date has passed.
  • The pet has changed Color/Species.
  • The pet is no longer on the account listed as it being held on.
  • If you have not been seen for 30 days or longer.

Applying for pets:

Keep in mind that these pets all belong to different people, so each one is likely to have their own set of rules! If you're interested in a pet, contact the person who is listed as that pet's owner. They will be able to give you more information about their application process.
