FAQs　

How to play?
After signing up, you will be receiving your own bingo card shortly via neomail. The game officially begins for everyone as soon as the mail is sent out. Each card contains 25 uniquely generated categories. A category you have does not necessary mean it is on your opponent's card.

Sample Bingo Card


Next time when any players get into the Art Gallery you can see if their drawing matches to a description for the category on your card. You must declare your match in public on the Art Agency board in the Art Chat.

Simply post...
Player 1's Drawing: I am marking Cheap Neopet Color!
Player 2's Drawing: I am marking Lineless!
Player 3's Drawing: BUSTED!


This is done to prevent misunderstandings. For example, one player might consider the Neopet in the drawing is feeling "Sad", while that was not the intention at all. If a drawing does not fit any category on your card it is consider to be a BUST. All markings will be tracked on this page.

The first person to make a single line declares a BINGO on board and has to reveal their card on board!


What do I win?
Friendship and motivation! ♥ If you are the champion of the previous game of Bingo, you win the right to make your own category that will be used for the next game. This can be anything you want and as absurd as you would like. Winner's Category is very influential because it is the only category that is guaranteed to be on everyone's card.

What happens if multiple people declares a BINGO?
Tie-breaker will be based on who got into Art Gallery the most times during the duration of the game.

When does Art Gallery update?
Monday at ~10PM (no longer reliable). Friday at ~12PM.

How long does each game lasts?
Less than a month on average. The more players, the faster the game.

I have a drawing that fits multiple categories on my card!
This is where strategy comes to play. You can only pick one of the category to declare your marking. You have until the next Art Gallery update to change your mind.

I was busy and missed marking the previous Art Gallery update!
You are allowed to play catch up and still declare what your matches for the previous updates, but be HONEST when you do so. When other players are matching, they did not get the luxury of knowing what drawings will be in the next update, but when you are playing catch up, you do. Try to not let this influence your choice.

I am unsure what the drawing is suppose to be.
Any category markings can be disputed and you can ask the original artist on board for clarification. There had been multiple debated categories and those were settled through with on board discússions. (Never forget. #SlothisaChia #truthisoutthere)

I'm not an artist, can I still play?
Yup, you do not need to actively draw at all to join. It will make it harder for you to win though!
