If you do not have a page listed here and would still like to link back - THANK YOU! I have codes for several link buttons HERE.
1 - Your page must offer codes or useful information for everyone's use. Guides, graphics, codes, premade lookups, fonts, etc. all fall into this category. Take a look at the links and categories on this page for ideas of the kinds of links I'll include.

2 - Your codes/information must be on the petpage itself, and may not direct someone off-site to get the codes/info.

3 - Send the FULL LINK (for example //www.neopets.com/~YOURPETSNAME) to the petpage you would like added when you send your request. Just copy the link in the last sentence and change "YOURPETSNAME" to your pet's name to get the link to your petpage. If you just say "add my page"...well, I'm not psychic and I'm not going to go searching around trying to see what you meant. If you just state the pet's name, I would have to copy the name into the search box, click search, click on the pet, then click again to view their page. It doesn't seem like much trouble, but multiply that by 50 times and it's a lot of extra clicking. If you include the direct link, your page will get reviewed and (possibly) added much faster. I look at pages with direct links first. I don't know how else to say this - PLEASE PLEASE PLEASE send the link! Thanks!

4 - If you only have a large link back graphic, I may use a text link or alternate graphic link instead to improve page load time. THERE ARE A LOT OF LINKS HERE - SMALLER GRAPHICS ARE PREFERRED!

5 - I do not require a link back, but it is appreciated if you choose to! :D
