Welcome

As you step down in to the little burrow, you feel yourself beginning to tip forward. Before you know it your tumbling down the narrow hole until finally you stop as you hit the bottom. For many, as they lift their head they would suddenly get a dose of Blurred Vision and as you carefully lift your head, weary of the low roof, you notice your vision has been tainted. But it's not blurred, it has become a Divine Vision. In which everything you see has now become a pleasure to view.


Hello and welcome to Divine Vision - a site run by me, April (lostcontrol92). This site was set up with the aim of providing great quality graphics and layouts that are simple to use and easy to understand. The site was opened on the 9th of September, back in 2010, and has been growing ever since.



Rules & F.A.Qs

Rules


There are not too many rules, so I would appreciate it if you would follow the few rules I do have.

1. Do not remove any credit from any content of this page.
2. Always credit back for everything on this page using a link, either a button or text.
3. Do not redistribute or claim the layouts/graphics as your own.
4. Do not request graphics, unless the requests are open.
5. Do not copy the layout of this page.

F.A.Q

What programs do you use?
I only use one program and that is GIMP. I find it much simpler to use than such programs as Photoshop and it continues to help me create great quality graphics.

Does anyone else contribute to the site?
No, I (april=lostcontrol92) run this site alone, and I currently prefer it like this.

Can I change the colours/images of the layouts?
I do allow this, although Credit to Divine Vision must remain, preferably through linking back here using one of my buttons.

I've found someone using content from your site without credit, what shall I do?
Neomail me :).

What browsers do you use to check the codes?
I use Firefox and sometimes Google Chrome.

What browsers are your petpages suitable for?
I know they are definitely suitable for Firefox and Google Chrome, yet I don't believe there are any problems in using them on other browsers.

I'm trying to learn how to code, could you help me?
I do not mind helping those who have already got the basis of coding in their grasp, so please neomail me for help. But I will not be teaching you how to code step by step, I will just be aiding you through the process.

Be my affie?
Sure, just visit the 'Sitely' section of the site and apply through there.
