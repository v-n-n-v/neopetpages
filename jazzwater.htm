Welcome

Hey the_freebie_man_, I'm Jaaazzy and welcome to The Screen Savers, we're a directory of screenie pages. Don't know what screenies are? Screenies are screenshots or pictures of your screen and the images taken captures the events that you run into on Neopets. Still confused? Just check out some of the links below or go here for an example. If you need screenie help refer to the 'Need Screenie Help' section on the right sidebar.

Unlike other directories the quality of the site is high and will always remain so. I update the links frequently, so it is unlikey that you will find a deadlink listed here. If you do happen to come across one before I do, please send me a neomail informing me of it. Our goal is to be Neopets #1 Screenie Directory, help us accomplish it!

The Screen Savers' I know such a awesome name right? I must thank rikkuffx2theif for coming up with it!

I'm always glad to hear from my visitors so Neomail
me with any questions, comments, suggestions, ect.

Want to Join?!

Want your page listed here? We have a few requirements & rules.
Your page must have atleast 25 screenie images posted.
Your page must be tidy and organized.
You must link back before you apply.

If your screenie pages meets the requirements neomail me the application below. Again it is a requirement of being part of the directory to link back to this page, you can do this by posting our link back button (located on left sidebar) or by posting a text link, on your screenie page. I'll reply to your neomail as soon as you're added. Thank you.
