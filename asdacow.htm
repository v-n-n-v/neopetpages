Hi there, and welcome to the Altador Cup Repository!
This page contains a useful rundown of the stats for each of the 18 Altador Cup Teams. You'll find a complete listing of all the matches that have taken place in all cups, along with the final standings and the overall win percentage for each team over all cups. The page also has team specific win percentages, details on the Staff Tournaments, and other useful information, and is now spread across five petpages. I'm also now accepting affiliates if you like!


Last Update: July 2023 (After AC18).

NOTE: If you use any of the information on this petpage, it'd be charming if you linked back. This took a lot of work to get all the figures, so sticky fingers are not appreciated.



The first Altador Cup debuted in 2006. It put 16 teams against each other in a knockout tournament (Shenkuu and Moltara did not participate). Yooyuball was the only available game, so scores were based solely on this. When a team was knocked out, players from the losing team were able to rejoin one of the survivors.

The 2nd Altador Cup took place in 2007. It radically changed the format of the original, introducing two new side games to play alongside Yooyuball - Make Some Noise and Slushie Slinger. The tournament format also changed, scrapping the knockout system in favor of a double round robin, in which all teams played each other twice. This was followed by a finals round where the top four teams played to determine the podium positions.

The third Cup took place in 2008, and kept much of the previous year's format. The finals round was tweaked so that all teams took part. Using the results of the double round robin, teams were sorted into brackets of four. Teams then played each other within these brackets to determine the final results.
