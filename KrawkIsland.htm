~ Who are we?

Krawk Island Living Legends was founded shortly before AC II by the lovely Julie as a hub for all KI players. In the years since then, we have firmly established ourselves as a power house team, with the best average placement among all teams throughout the Cup's history, as well as taking home gold in AC IV! Our board is active in the Site Events section year-round.

During the Altador Cup, K.I.L.L. is both a score board along with a chat board to get to know your fellow KI teammates! Scores posted on the board during the cup will all be tallied by a scorekeeper and updates will be given throughout the day, with final tallies shortly after midnight once everything is calculated.

The K.I.L.L. petpage was formerly hosted on /~Bilieus, but as some pirates move on with their life - Tara took over the task from AC VI until shortly before AC VIII. The petpage is now hosted on KrawkIsland which was luckily grabbed in the latest purge.

Since Krawk Island is such a large team, we have another group - KI YYB. KI YYB focuses on (like the name implies) YYB with minimal chatting during the cup at the end of the day their scores are sent to K.I.L.L. and are added to our final total. KI YYB's petpage is hosted on /~jon_lennen.

Shortly after K.I.L.L. was formed, a guild was created so that the long-term members could stay in touch and have a more private place to talk. While the K.I.L.L. board is always open for conversation, membership in the guild was basically a sign that you were an active user on the boards throughout the year. Over the years, K.I.L.L.'s older members have moved on. The old guild is now a bit of a ghost town, unfortunately, but this opened up an opportunity for a newer, more active guild to be created and put to use.

During ACVI, we made a much bigger effort to organize the team, sending out daily strategies for each opponent. In ACVII, mailing out so many plans every day resulted in more than a few silenced accounts, so we decided to create a guild for the whole team. Starting in ACVIII, the daily plans were posted on the guild's homepage each day, allowing everyone to see our strategy privately without having to recruit a few people to send out mail every day at midnight. In addition to facilitating team organization, the guild is also another place for general chatting and getting to know your teammates, so it is active in the offseason as well.

The guild is called Armada, and there are no requirements for joining other than joining Krawk Island and understanding that the daily plans and other strategic discussions are to be kept within the guild.

The Pirate Lords are a group of very dedicated pirates who vow to go above and beyond every day of the Altador Cup. Pirate Lord signups are opened up before the cup begins and are open a week into the cup to help you better gauge your time and dedication.

Although it is difficult, Pirate Lords are expected to keep their oath throughout the cup and work on an honor system. (We can check your stats to see if you've been playing!) We understand that sometimes real life situations come up and take priority and this is completely understandable. If you feel you can no longer keep your oath feel free to drop me (neon3002) a neomail and things will be adjusted accordingly!

More information on Pirate Lord sign ups can be found on /~Saltbag.
