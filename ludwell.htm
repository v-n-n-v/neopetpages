105 free books

(and where to find them)


intro:

want to improve your pets but not sure know where to begin? or maybe you just do not have the funds to save up for codestones, avatar items, and items for your gallery. have no fear! this little guide is dedicated to tracking down over 100 books your pet can read for free.

if you want your pets to read books as fast as possible, then maybe this guide is not for you, but if you are patient enough, eventually your pet will have read over 100 books with out spending a single neopoint.

how it works:

quite simple. the list has been arranged alphabetically, and each book will have a link to direct you to the daily as well as a rating to help gauge how quickly and easily you will be able to read this book to one or all of your pets. in time, your pet will have read over 100 books, and all you had to do was click.

about:

i love reading books to my pet, but i also love avatars and wearables, both of which are very expensive interests. so, i decided to see if i could make a list of free books.from there, i started to track what books i was getting for free from dailies and how many of my pets have read them for free.

ratings:

each book will be given a rating, easy, medium, or hard. ratings are based off of how often a daily hands out a particular book, so even if they come from the same daily, your odds of receiving one book or another varies.

note:

the books on are from dailies that are available any time of the year. books from site events, such as advent calendar or game master challenge, are not included in this list as they are only available for free once, and the goal of this list is to have all of your pets read 100 books for free, not just one. if you participate in site events, your pet will certainly read well over 100 books than if you only used books from dailies.

that being said, key quest books are not currently listed. while you can still buy these book for cheap or find them at the money tree, you can not currently receive all of these books from a daily so they unfortunately do not qualify.

these are not all of the free books that exist in neopia, just the ones that i feel are most easy to obtain through regular dailies. this list and the ratings are based off of my own personal tacking of which free books that my pets have read and how many of them have read a particular book.

questions, comments, concerns?

please feel free to neomail me if you believe that i am missing a book, if i have mislabeled something, a link is wrong, or just to rant and/ or rave about this list.
