2) enable customization for specific zones on various maraquan & mutant pets

47 species are currently capable of being painted Maraquan, all species are currently capable of being transmogrified into a Mutant. Separate items have been created for a while now to allow these species to wear clothes (as long as the color/species combo existed before it was created), as their biology and pose typically differs from that of the original species biology.

There is, however, one clever species that seems to have figured out how to dress to the nines.

The Maraquan Mynci. Mostly because the biology and pose (boringly) does not differ from that of the original Mynci biology

There are actually multiple Maraquan and Mutant species that share similar poses and biology with their originals. Some not as close as the Maraquan Mynci, but enough similarity that specific zones could be enabled to allow some to wear specific items.

Why should the Maraquan Usul be forsaken from wearing wigs any other Usul could? Why should the Mutant Krawk be incapable of holding what any other Krawk can?

Why follow the original outline so closely if it doesn't benefit the pet at all? If these species can't have graceful or grotesque poses that really differentiate them from the original, they might as well be able to make use of it.

Additionally, species that are not bipedal already have a version of most handheld items that are designed to be on the ground in front of them rather than in their hands.

It would be great if these versions of handhelds could be used on Maraquans and Mutants that do not share hand biology with their original species (a bonus would be allowing these items to be used with Baby pets and UCs as well!).

3) avatars

Quite possibly the easiest content to produce that would create the most interest. People love collecting avatars. People love expressing themselves with avatars. Recently, however, there has been a severe lack of avatar content.

They only ever seem to be released through events, special days or mobile games (and these are almost always overly animated). The last avatars released that didn't fall into one of these categories were Royalboy Lutari and Robot Vandagyre on June 19, 2019.

This is unfortunate since avatars can drive engagement all over the site, creating short and long term goals for users to work towards. They can even bring the community together to bump brains, as was the case with Chef Bonju and the Wishing Well. Users came together over months and even years to try to figure out these avatars, it would be great to have some mystery to chase after.

Sure avatars have to be designed, but some of the best avatars are simplistic, with a touch of sheen or blinky text thrown in. The overly animated feature films we have been getting are difficult to express yourself with, less is more! And the thing is, a LOT of art assets already exist.

There are a vast amount of Buddy Icons that are already avataresque in design and size (some are even avatars themselves, or prototypes!). Nearly 800 of them. There are a mix of vibes and quite a few of them could be used as is to release a diverse set (in terms of style AND unlock criteria) of avatars!

There are also just over 600 of the larger buddy icons, which can be rescaled and touched up with a bit of care. I am NOT an artist or anything of the sort, but I have taken the liberty of editing a few of the smaller and larger buddy icons (spending ~5-10 minutes on each).

Some animation tweaks, a border and a flip are sometimes enough to make something look like an avatar (I am not capable of good twinkly text lol). A person with a better sk!llset could easily whip the Buddy Icons into great avatars.For more of my edits see www.neopets.com/~welch

As far as coding is concerned, a lot of different unlock criteria have been created throughout the years. It shouldn't be difficult to pull the code for various avatars and tweak them (a little or a lot) to work for new avatars. The time saved could be used to think up new code for new criteria!

For some unlock criteria made by my friend, head on over to www.neopets.com/~earthbender

4) activate/release unactivated/unreleased items

Sometimes items get added to the site but are not activated properly. They might show up in searches but cannot actually be obtained.

Sometimes PetPets are released but not properly activated and will not appear as options when you bring the appropriate Petpet and Petpet Paint Brush to The Petpet Pool. Sometimes they end up at The Petpet Pool despite being exclusive unpaintable colors!

For more on improperly activated items check items.jellyneo.net/item-error-list/status/

There are also hundreds of unreleased items. Some may be unreleased for specific reasons, but some seem like they could (and should) be released. It would be nice to break up the monotonous news of wearable releases and *checks notes* nothing.

There are quite a few items of various types and some even in sets. There are also quite a few battledome items (some only used by battledome opponents) which are unfortunately not so EZ to make if balance is a concern (but the art is already done, so half the work is there).

For more unreleased items take a look at items.jellyneo.net/search/?status=2
