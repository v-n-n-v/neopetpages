FAQ:
How do you find these names?
Manually! It takes a lot of time but I have that weird brain chemistry connection that lets me obsess over things.

How often do you add/scan names?
I will replace names mailed to me right away (unless I have a planned sweep in the next few days) - otherwise I will check the full roster monthly! I work 60 hours & six days a week, but I have time for Neo always :) I try to keep a minimum of 30 names per letter!
Last updated on: 5/4/2024 - replaced all taken names!:)!

Do you post on the Neoboards?
I do! And if you see me - I will post names not listed here so take a peek!

Can I share your page?
Please do! Finding names is the smallest piece of Neohappy that I have.

How can I let you know if I take a name?
To keep it nice and easy for folks, please just Neomail Me! ^^ If you don't feel like talking that day or not really in the best mental space, just send me any dung item from the account with the pet. I'll check it myself, swap out the names, and send you positive vibes (spiritually)!

Can you do name/specific requests?
Yes I can! Please send me a mail with what you are looking for. c: The fewer the letters the more challenging it can be, but I will always try!

Please NEOMAIL ME if you take a name. It's five seconds and keeps the page active.
Thank you!
