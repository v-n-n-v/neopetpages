Hello, and welcome to the Koi Pond! This pet directory is dedicated to the wonderful species known as Koi. This page was started by me Siotto, the UC Sponge Koi and Shelby in 2020 to help spread the word about the underrated species. Koi may not be the most popular species around Neopia but here you will find a bunch of proud owners and all of their lovely Koi!

This page has many things to offer, including; history of the Koi, a few of Neopia's famous Koi, all of the Koi related items, paint colors, Koi looking for their forever homes, a Dream Pet list as well as Neopia's lovely converted and Unconverted permanent Koi!

We hope you enjoy looking around and find inspiration to get your own Koi from this page! If you have any questions or concerns, don't hesitate to Neomail me. c:

    History of the Koi

    Koi were introduced to the site on the 6th day of Collecting, Year Two (known outside Neopia as October 6th, 2000). Its name was taken from the Japanese word for "carp". Initially, it was not a Limited Edition pet. The natural disaster that destroyed Old Maraqua led to Koi being unable to find a suitable habitat to settle; it's not known where they went, but Koi have been harder to obtain ever since.

    The Koi is a scaly Neopet. It has no conventional limbs, but uses its flexible forefins as makeshift arms. It is easily recognized by its filmy tail and crest, as well as the whisker-like eyebrows that extend on either side. At around 30cm, it is one of the smallest Neopets.

    Koi are native to Maraqua, both Old and New. They are highly creative and ingenious pets who love to apply their minds to new ways of solving problems; it was an unnamed Koi who invented the Utility Fish, tame sea creatures adapted for household tasks. Inhabitants of Maraqua since long-forgotten times, they are completely at home with underwater life and utterly unfazed by the local sea monsters like Chiazilla. They are quite attracted to treasures, such as the pearls and gems that can be found in the Maraquan ruins. It's uncertain exactly how they move on land; it's possible that they float like Peophins, but it's never been officially established. What is known is that they prefer to spend most of their time underwater, where their fins and tail allow them perfect maneuverability. If you don't mind visiting their aquatic habitats, Koi are highly outgoing and playful friends to have.

    Version I: Koi originally had very broad snouts and human arms and hands.


    Version II: Koi later had their arms replaced with fins, and the stripes on their backs were removed.


    Version III: The long, flowing fins of this version of the Koi gave it a much more graceful appearance.

    Famous Koi

    Fanciful Fauna Shopkeeper - The rare, oriental petpets found at Fanciful Fauna require a quiet and tender hand. This shy shopkeeper seems to have a gift for working with these petpets!

    Grandfather Koi - After the destruction of old Maraqua, this character became a recluse and spent his time writing stories. Many of these were collected in the Neopian book named after him.

    Karpoh - Karpoh works for pirates, and gathers treasure from the ruins of old Maraqua for them in the hopes of being allowed to join their crew.


    King Kelpbeard - Originally known only as the Koi King, Kelpbeard is the ruler of Maraqua. He refused to pay Dread's protection money due to bad kelp harvests, and as a result Old Maraqua was destroyed. Kelpbeard led the survivors to found New Maraqua, and successfully repelled a pirate attack some years later. Since then, he has become reclusive.

    Turo Rafels - Rafels was a defender in Shenkuu's squad. He is an excellent defender, but he is injury prone. Perhaps due to an injury, he was replaced in the 2008 squad by Antola Maeir.
    Koi Ghost - These ghosts roamed the Haunted Plains of Ancient Neopia, and attacked travelers while under the influence of magic from Two Rings Fortress.


    Koi Warrior - The Koi Warrior is a defender of Old Maraqua, valiant to the protection of the ruins.



    Kori - Kori appeared in issue #24 of the Neopets Magazine. Kori is a friend of Norell, who was scared away by his Arkmite, Snappy. A few days later, Kori returned to the house with Rissi and their Petpets.

    Maraquan Chef - This Koi was a member of the first wave of opponents for Rank 2 pirate warriors in the Curse of Maraqua Plot. Even normal citizens like him came to the fight to defend their city.

    Tonie Plessix - Plessix is the Maraqua goalkeeper in the Altador Cup. His large frame takes up most of the goal area, but he isn't very agile.


    Kipper - Kipper the Wonder Koi is a character in the Neopian book, Wonder Koi, who has amazing adventures.
