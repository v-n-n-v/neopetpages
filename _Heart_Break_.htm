It is that time again! It is time for the Fifth Altador cup! This guide is about the game Shootout Showdown. There are two methods on getting goals You ready to get a highscore? Ok lets go!

Instructions:
The point of the game is to get 5 goals. There is a defender defending the goal which can block some of your shots! To move your ball left and right use the arrow keys. To shoot hold down the space bar. The green bar will raise. The higher the green is the more power you kick the ball with.





First start a new game.


First method:

Both of the methods are easy. The first one is by holding down space bar and pressing the left arrow key to aim as far left as possible. Wait for the green meter to fill. Shoot and Score!



The second one is done by holding the right (or left) arrow until you are just left of the goal post (the bar that holds the goal up) Now space bar and wait for the green meter to fill. Shoot...



and Score!
