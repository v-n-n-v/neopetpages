What is cottagecore?

Cottagecore, also known as farmcore and countrycore, is an aesthetic inspired by a romanticized interpretation of agricultural life. It is centered on ideas of simple living and harmony with nature [...] Its visuals are based on the domestic lifestyle associated with living in this type of environment, with all images providing a sense of comfort and mild adventure. (Aesthetics Wiki, 2022).

This theme week invites participants to draw their neopets inspired by this aesthetic! Make your way over to the inspo section of this page for a visual guide on this aesthetic, along with sister aesthetics.

    Common keywords and motifs: naturalistic color palettes and natural lighting; longer and loose-fitting clothing, patterns like gingham and faded floral; embroidery, sun hats; overalls, puffy sleeves, lace; strawberries, mushrooms, flowers; picnics, baking, and gardening; domesticated animals like cats, cows, and ducks

    In addition to Cottagecore many sister aesthetics share similarities with other aesthetics. Feel free to peruse them below for even more inspiration!

    Bloomcore

    - flowers, gardens, gardening, nature

    Goblincore

    - frogs, snails, moss, mud, fungi, hoarding

    Honeycore

    - making and eating "rural" foods like honey, bread, waffles

    plant mom

    - plants galore!

    witchcore

    - spells, gems, horticulture

    Frequently asked questions

    Nebula will add frequent questions as they arise over the course of the next weeks!

    When is the theme week taking place? The theme week will begin at 12pm NST on Friday, October 7th and last until the beauty contest round ends on Friday, October 14th.

    Will you make a gallery somewhere of everyone who does this theme? Absolutely! The gallery will be hosted on this page, in the "entries" section.

    Are we allowed to do cottagecore aesthetics based on non-western countries? Of course, and thank you for bringing this to my attention so that I could include even more visuals for inspiration! At the end of the day, cottagecore is an idealization of simple, country/nature life.
