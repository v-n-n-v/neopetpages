I've chosen to do templates instead of premades because I want to encourage people to customize these pages*. Just remember to not delete the credit!

If you have any questions at all about editing or just coding in general, please don't hesistate to ask! I'm also open to suggestions for more layouts. You can mail me on my main here.

Requests are OPEN

When you can't find that perfect premade/template that fits your idea or just can't edit a layout to your liking, I think it's time for you to request one! But before you run off and mail me, there's a few things you should be aware of...

  • **You must have a specific idea in mind. I won't do a "faerie themed" petpage but I will do a layout with three faeries surrounding a single, anchored textbox.**
  • I will not do graphics but I may do drawings. What's the difference? For me, graphics are pictures that are put together using filters/various pictures from around the site/poogle. An example is a banner. Generally you see them being for lookups. A drawing is something like the book above in the fourth petpage template. That being said, you can have someone else/you make the graphics. I won't mind using them.
  • If you have a premade and just need help editing it, those requests will always be open. You must have everything picked out (ie colors, graphics/pictures, fonts, etc) and already have a layout though.
  • If I haven't sent you a version within 5 days from when I accepted your request, PLEASE MAIL ME. I'm probably being lazy and not doing it.
  • This isn't really a guideline, but after I send you the first version, please don't be scared to tell me what you want changed! I'd be more than happy to completely redo it. Just please be polite about it =)
  • I reserve the right to reject any request I don't want to do.
