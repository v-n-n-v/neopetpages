Information

The Loobook Directory serves as a reference page for personal individual lookbook and journal. A lookbook is a place where you get to display the customisations you have created. If you do not have one yet and you would like to create one, I invite you to consult the "Create your own Lookbook" section above (you should; it's a lot of fun!), and please let me know if you update your lookbook/journal so that I can update your preview!
If you have a premade site and propose premade lookbooks, please contact me and I will gladly add yours to the list.

If you have any question, feel free to mail them to me and I will do my best to answer!
Thank you very much!
