Send in your trades!

- Month the trade occurred
- Name, species and color of both pets involved
- Extra info such as HSD, custom done, trophies, particular interest, etc.

You DO NOT need to copy and paste a form, just be sure to include all of the information I ask for!
Examples: March 2020 VWN UC Faerie Aisha (3 trophies) for BN UC Grey Aisha
May 2020 2350HSD 4L Transparent Aisha custom for Xxxxxxxxxx UC Darigan Kougra


VWN: roughly 8 or less letters + capitalized + pronounceable/looks nice
WN: longer than 8 letters + capitalized + pronounceable |OR| lowercase + pronounceable/looks nice
DN: compound names |OR| 1-2 underscores/framed names |OR| 1 number + pronounceable
BN: more than 1 number |OR| more than 2 underscores |OR| unpronounceable/unappealing
VBN: multiple numbers + multiple underscores |OR| extremely long + unappealing/unpronounceable

If a name is listed as "V/WN" or "DN/BN" it means it falls somewhere in between depending on who you ask and therefore I have made the decision to list it this way to get the best point across and keep things as accurate (yet confidential) as possible.

Notes:
It is important to provide me with the exact name of all RW/RN pets. If you are keeping the RW/RN pet permanently and would like the actual name added to this page, please let me know. Otherwise the name WILL NOT be added, just vaguely described.

Please send as many trades as you can in one mail but be sure to separate/organize them so that is easy for me to understand!

Please do not send in trades that you feel are unfair. If you were forced to trade in a shuffle or whatever other situation there may be where you feel unhappy - there is no need to report your trade information. I want this page to be the best example it can be so please try to keep that in mind when submitting!

If you see a typo, error, missing entry after I have updated, or otherwise wrong information please let me know! Also please keep in mind that I do not list nameswaps.

Once again, this page is to help provide information on trades, not to tell you what you should or should not do. Always make trades you personally want and are happy with, regardless of what any page says!
