Q: What is a fandom pet/fanpet?
A: A fandom pet (or fanpet in short) is a pet made in honor of a certain fandom character. Whether your pet's name is correctly spelled or not, well-named or badly-named, as long as its purpose is to represent a fandom character, then that is considered a fanpet.

Q: What does fandom even mean?
A: It is a community of fans (or subculture) of a particular material (person, fictional series/movies, etc).

Q: Should my pet be fully customized in order to be included in the directory?
A: Not necessarily. It depends on the character your pet is based on and how you want your pet to look like. If you think your pet's look is already or almost complete (which only you can decide or tell), you may submit your pet/s for listing. By the way, using alternative items in your customization is totally fine!

Q: Can our pets be listed if they do not have a background but are wearing other wearable items?
A: Yes if that is your preference. As long as your pet is not totally bare.

Q: Can I list more than 1 pet?
A: Sure. The more, the merrier!

Q: I don't have a fanpet to submit. Is there any way that I can contribute to this page?
A: Yes. You can also submit your customization idea/s in this page if you would like to share it here.

Q: Can I submit more than 1 customization idea?
A: Yes of course!

Q: I have a pet that is not a fandom character but is a tribute to a certain fandom (place, object, etc.) Can I list that pet?
A: Yes. This page is for everything about fandom so you are welcome to enter them too.

Q:What about Original Characters (OCs) inspired by a canon? Are those pets included?
A: That is technically a form of a fanwork so yes those are include. Just include the name of your OC and which canon (fandom source material) is based on.
