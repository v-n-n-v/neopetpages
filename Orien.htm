Full name: Orien Clarke
Age: Early 20s
Place of Birth: Meridell
Occupation: Guard-in-training

Orien is a young Gelert with ambitions to follow in his father's footsteps and join the Meridell guard. He and his sister, Thorla, grew up in Meridell and in Altador, where their father, the decorated Lieutenant Koleman, still lives and works as a consultant to King Altador.

Growing up, the Lieutenant was as much of a tactician at home as he was in his battles against Darigan. Unlike his sister, Orien has always strived to meet his father's seemingly unreachable standards, never quite able to earn his full attention or approval.

In many ways, Orien isn't cut out for the guard: he's anxious, awkward with people, and a stubborn perfectionist. But he is just as determined to succeed in it as he is to finally earn his father's praise.

Full name: Thorla Clarke
Age: Late 20s
Place of Birth: Meridell
Occupation: Mercenary

Thorla and Orien have moved back to Meridell to start a life of their own, though they have very different ideas about what that should look like. Unlike her brother, who is nervous, serious, and full of ambition, Thorla responded to the pressure of their militant upbringing with chaos and flippancy.

Thorla is the kind of person who's great at a party and great in a fight, but really difficult to rely on. As soon as things get too serious, or the expectations get too high, she gets wigged out and abandons ship.

The mercenary lifestyle has fit her well, though her recklessness has landed her in quite a few sticky situations. Luckily for her, she has a plucky group of friends and a neurotic little brother to clean up her messes.

Full name: Roone
Age: Ancient
Place of Birth: ???
Occupation: Retired ???

The mysterious Roone arrived in Ashkary's café a couple weeks ago, and now seems to be always at a pretty premium booth window seat, to the aggravation of many a regular. Rumor has it she's an old friend of Ashkary's, from her whispered career as an adventurer.

While much about her remains a mystery, she has been observed re-warming her tea with a snap of her mossy fingers. Many common folks know a spell or two, but combined with that air of wisdom about her, it does make one wonder...

Full name: Likero Vithier
Age: Mid twenties
Place of Birth: Meridell
Occupation: Heiress

Despite the relative newness of her family's noble title, Likero has always lived a pampered lifestyle. Perhaps that's why her parent's pleas to find more 'suitable' friends for her 'station' have gone unfulfilled; she's never felt the pressure to prove that she deserves her nobility. It could also just be that she is friendly.

Likero is boisterous, theatrical, and surprisingly unsnobbish. While her privilege has given her definite blind spots, she is thoughtful in the way she knows how to be.

Full name: Mollax "Molly" Mazlovic
Age: Mid twenties
Place of Birth: Haunted Woods
Occupation: Barista/Necromancer

Molly is the moodiest barista currently working at Ashkary's Café. He can usually be found scowling at customers that interrupt his reading (is that book...glowing?) and making very good, very spiteful espresso.

Molly keeps his cards very close to his chest. Even his closest friends, Thorla, Likero, and Orien know little of his life before he moved to Meridell a few years ago. If it wasn't for his subtle Neovian accent, they probably wouldn't know where he was from at all.

Despite his grumpiness, he has been surprisingly down for the hijinks that tend to follow Thorla wherever she goes. He is quiet, sarcastic, and deeply cool in that cat-of-a-person kind of way.

Full name: Ashkary
Age: Middle Aged
Place of Birth: Meridell
Occupation: Shop owner/collector

Ashkary is the rarely-seen owner of Ashkary's Café. She first inherited the shop from her late parents over ten years ago, transforming it from a dilapidated farm and general goods store to a beloved pillar of the local village community.

Those who knew her in childhood say she was always chasing down mysteries and adventures, culminating in her leaving the village, full of zest and when she was no more than 16. When she returned all those years later to take over the shop, Ashkary was changed; cold, aloof, secretive. The rumors about her exploits in those years quickly became local legend, which created a nice bit of buzz for the shop's reopening.

The upper floors of Ashkary's, with their peculiar collections and mysterious apartments, are whispers over latte's and mid-priced local tea.
