FLAWLESS VICTORY BATTLEDOME SETS

STR 55 STR 85 STR 125 STR 200 STR 250 STR 300 STR 350 Exit

Hello and welcome to some battledome builds! This page is heavily inspired by Chips' 1P Battledome Weapons Guide but this guide focuses mainly on farming your BD dailies fast and without hassle, using items that are fairly obtainable. The goal is to give you a flawless victory every time, so you wouldn't have to heal in between rounds. Yep, no damage taken, clean beatdown, 100% of the time. For these to work, we deprive the opponent the chance to 1) block our attacks, 2) heal and 3) stun/disable us. For that, we use Faerie Abilities that stun, and freezing items. So all of these sets include Lens Flare, a Faerie Ability unlockable at Pet Level 50, and some, Warlock's Rage, unlockable at Level 200. So your BD pet has to be at those level to gracefully land the respective sets.
