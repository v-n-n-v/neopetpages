Welcome & Introduction

Welcome to my new Customised Usul Directory opened Oct 17 2010.
This page is dedicated to Usuls to show off the amazing things you can do with them while customising.
Usuls are in alphabetical colour catagories and then in the order they were added to the directory.
You are allowed to add as many Usuls as you own,
(if you know other Usul owners then please let them know and they can neomail me if they wish.)
You can add as many differents outfits on your Usul as you wish, just neomail me when he/she has changed and I will add the new image.
Thanks for visting and come back soon :)

Monthly Spotlight

November Theme: Food
Either dress up your Usul as food or have food in your customisation.
You can choose to put a caption with it or not, Goodluck :D


Click on the image to see this months entries.

Each month I will suggest a theme to customise your pet.
You have 2weeks to customise and submit your pet (only one look per Usul please).
The Usuls will only be viewable once entries have closed and then be decided which Usul has been customised best by other Usul owners on this page.
Neomail me to vote for the 3 Usuls you like best and put them in order 1-3.

1st - Theme Anounced and entries open
14th - Entries close @ Midnight
15th - Usuls visible and Voting starts
29th - Voting closes @ Midnight
30th - Winner Anounced
