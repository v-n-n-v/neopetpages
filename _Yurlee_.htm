Hello annika1211, welcome to Vanity Set. I'm Annika and I will be your guide for today. Vanity Set is a guild directory that is here to help the guildless find their true beauty(Find the perfect guild.) The possibilities here are endless, featuring young and old guilds, with many different sizes. My mission here is that you leave here feeling satisfied with your choice. To help you with this, I revamped the site so that everything is neat and organized to help your choice more clearer to you. I also provided you with more than enough information about each guild, so you won't have trouble choosing. Thanks again for visiting Vanity Set, hope I see you around!


Need help where to go? Simple start off by visiting the "Foundation Tab" to get you started on what guilds your looking for. If you have any more questions, contact annika1211. -Find your true beauty

Makeup Remover

Starting every 1st day of the month, all guilds listed will be going through the Makeup Remover. This means that I will check to make sure all links are up to date, your guild is still active, and I will see if any of the guilds information has changed, such as is it private, or has it gotten bigger. If your guild is no longer active you will be deleted.
