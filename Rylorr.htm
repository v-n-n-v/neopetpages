6/1/2023

Well, it sure has been almost a full calendar year! In case you haven't noticed, I haven't made a single badge since last year's cup! Not a one! See all those ideas I had, back when I was so bright-eyed and hopeful? Yeah, last year's cup ended in a bit of a... kerfuffle, I think is the literal nicest way of putting it. But I'm back to play again this year, and you can bet I'll be collecting badges again! Who knows? Maybe if this year's cup ends on a better note, I'll start making more badges again in the future. I really do like doing them! Let me know if you'll be collecting badges too--I like to see which ones everyone nabs, hehe. See you on the boards!

7/2/2022

And thus ends another Altador Cup! Everyone played amazingly this year! We'll have to wait until the final results are tallied to see where we land, but I'm still proud of everyone! We played hard, made new friends, and according to some of the other teams, we were the friendliest team in the cup. Regardless of our final standing, that's a reputation to be proud of!

Now, the reason we're all here: badges! I plan on making more for next year, and here are some of the ideas I've had so far:

-Scored the highest in YYB, SLSL, MSN, and SOSD
-Won the most Badges
-Scorekeeper Badge
-Referee Badge (host a maROOthon or some other game)
-PokeCup Participant
-Hurt & Heal Participant
-Hurt & Heal RI Wins
-Badges for # of games maxed across the Cup
