Welcome to the Toy Box

I'm a sucker for nostalgia. I guess that's a given since I'm 26 years old and still playing Neopets. One of the colours that I think has some great opportunities to play off of our collective nostalgia is toy, and we've already seen it with some of the more recent toy releases, such as the Vandagyre, Grundo, and Bori. This petpage is dedicated to sharing some of my own toy design ideas for the remaining pets. Feel free to take a look, and enjoy your visit to the Toy Box!

LupePoo-Chi Interactive Puppy

The Lupe design is inspired by one of my favourite toys from the early 2000s, the Poo-Chi Interactive Puppy. These puppies (pun intended) were all the rage back in the day, and I'd love to see they come to life on site!

Scorchio My Magical Kissing Dragon

Yes, this toy was really called the 'My Magical Kissing Dragon'. I never had one of my own, but I remember enviously playing with my neighbors version of this rare 90s dragon.

Nimmo Sand Stuffed Frog

Do these stuffed frogs have a formal name? I'm not sure, but I do know that EVERYONE had one of these. It was always either on the dashboard of their moms minivan, sitting on their dresser, or tucked away in their backpack!

Gelert Slinky Dog

Who doesn't love slinky dog? I'll tell you what I don't love: slinky dogs slinky. Why are slinkys so hard to draw? I don't know, but they are. Anywho, here's my concept for a slink dog gelert, with his sweet leather ears and annoying slinky tail.
