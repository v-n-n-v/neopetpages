Welcome to Jel's Untaken Names



Welcome to Jel's Untaken Name site. Our site is a work in progress and--ironically enough--we still don't have a website name. Our website started April 4th, 2023 and the inspiration came to me as I was searching up some untaken names for personal usage. While searching, I realized just how many unique names were up for grabs and I thought it's be sweet to share them with ya'll!

I used to own a bunch of websites for banner creations, css pages, and button requests back in the 2010's but took a break for college/adulting. But now I'm back! The site is still up and coming...Hmpf. In the meantime, feel free to check out our untaken pet names or see which pets have already been created. Confused? Check out our mini frequently asked questions here:

FAQ:

  • How do I claim a name? Easy! As long as you have an open pet-slot, you can create a pet here.
  • How do I know if a name has been claimed? If a pet image loads under your desired name, that name has already been claimed and created.
  • When do you add new names/remove old ones? Depends! I'll try to check-in bi-weekly.
  • Can I request a name or submit a name? Sure, send me a neomail and let me know what kind of name you're looking for/name you want to submit.
