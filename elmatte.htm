To heal this petpet, it will need to give it what he needs.


Now, first we need to understand how it works.
It is important the the first time you visit the pet, you don't click on "check the vaelous" also do not refresh or go to lenny's office and come back to see him or you will reset the game.
The petpet status refresh every minute, so the trick here is to give him what he needs on time + 5 seconds every time, for a few minutes in a row. For example, like this:
6:31:00 - visit the vaelus from the lenny's office {quickly, perform the action it's asking for}
6:31:05 - check in the vaeolus {quicky, perform the action it's asking for}
6:31:10 - check in the vaeolus {quicky, perform the action it's asking for}
6:31:15 - check in the vaeolus {quicky, perform the action it's asking for}
6:31:20 - check in the vaeolus {quicky, perform the action it's asking for}


This means it needs medicine.


This means it needs food.


This means it needs bandage.


This means you need to wait.

I highly recommend you taking note of time and what it asked for in a piece of paper. :)

After a few minutes, it should be healed!

A skeith from Petpet Protection League should be there to give you a certificate for your help :) Click the logo to find a new pattern.
