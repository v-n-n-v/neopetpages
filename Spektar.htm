WHAT IS SPOOKY?

I'm ever so glad you asked! Spooky is a customization directory originally created by Mexxy that features spooky-painted pets with drop dead GORGEOUS customizations.

RISE FROM YOUR GRAVE

Mexxy is currently on an indefinite hiatus, the original directory has been out of commission. However, spooky pets are still a beloved fixture on the site and they are especially dear to my heart. With Mexxy's permission, I have decided to revive the directory in her absence. You can still view the original directory here!

Spooky: Resurrection will be starting from scratch and maintained by Akito. If you were previously listed and want to be relisted, please do not hesitate to drop me a mail. Simply fill out the form and let me know you are re-listing! For new applicants Spooky will be following the same guidelines as the original directory!

PLEASE NOTE: I update on Thursdays, the featured pet is chosen on the first Thursday of each month. I will be sweeping pets on the first Thursday of each month as well! If your pet goes undressed for a month, they will be swept. If you wish to re-list simply drop me another mail! Also, I have decided to keep pets that are only temporarily wearing UC tokens or may be wearing them part time. They will be listed in the appropriate section, but at the end as they may be UC at random.

Requirements

Your pet must be one of the following colors: Transparent, Zombie, Mutant, Halloween, Darigan, Ghost, Burlap, MSP, or Wraith. Your pet does not have to be customized spooky as long as it's one of the above colours. Your pet must not be a lab rat and must be wearing three+ wearables. Keep in mind that first and foremost this is a customization directory!

If you are listing a UC, please let me know the following: Does the base color correspond to the nostalgic pet token and do you plan to swap between converted and UC or are they a permie UC? This will help me list your pets accurately!
