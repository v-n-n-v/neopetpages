Arrrgh!

Welcome to Arrrgh!, a pirate directory dedicated to all the pirate pets of Neopia! I'm Alley and I'm back for more directory fun! Please read the rules before submitting your pet to the directory. Currently, we have 54 pirates with a goal of listing 100 pirates!

#1 - Permanent pirates only, please! This means that your pet cannot be UFT, UFA, or being labbed.

#2 - I don't have a restriction on how many pirates you can list but they all must belong to you. I will not list pets that don't belong to you.

#3 - I will link to the pet's lookup by default. If you would rather have your pet linked to their petpage, please tell me.

#4 - Your pirate doesn't need a pirate lookup, customization, or character. Only the pirate base is required. Please note that this rule changes in the 'Pirate Characters' section.

#5 - Please check to see that your pirate isn't already listed!

If you agree to this rules, then please neomail me the name and species of your pirate to list!

FAQ

Can I add my pirate to your directory?
You don't need permission to list a pet - just neomail me after you read the rules on the front page and I'll take care of the rest.

Is there a limit to how many pirates or pirate themed pets I can list?
Nope! I'll list every pet that belongs to you, so long as they are not UFT/UFA/being labbed.

My friends are holding some of my pirates. Is that alright?
I don't see a problem as long as it's ultimately yours and you're not adding random pets.

Will people ask if my pirate is UFT if they're listed here?
This directory is for permanent pirate. They shouldn't ask you, but I can't guarantee anything.

My pirate isn't wearing the pirate brush clothing. Is that alright?
Completely, as long as the base colour is pirate! My pirates don't wear their pirate clothing either!

My pet isn't pirate coloured but has a lot of pirate themed wearables. Can I still list my pet? Pirate crosspaints or themed pets won't be listed in the main directory but they will be listed in the 'first matess' section!

Your inbox is full! Where else can I contact you?
My inbox is pretty big but if it's full, you can either toss an item at me or you can try neomailing any of my sides: snowflakes_side, dioji_, a_l_l_3_y, and lemongummiesbears. However, I do encourage you to wait or toss an item at me because my main is the fastest way to contact me.

First Mates

Do you have a pet that is pirate in all but their colour? Well, here, we recognize all the pirates - or first mates as I will call them here - and all of their glory! Simply read the rules below before neomail me!

#1 - all pets must be a color other than Pirate. All pets painted pirate will go into the main directory unless they were wearing a NC style.

#2 - pets can be crosspaints, have pirate themed customizations, or pirate themed lookups or petpages. For lookups and petpages, I will judge case-by-base.

#3 - if pets no longer pirate themed, they will be removed without warning.

We currently have 15 first mates listed!
