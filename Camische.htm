Welcome

Hi vanillerrybeitje and welcome to this UC faerie directory. We list all the unconverted neopets that are painted with the faerie paint brush.
If you are lucky owner of some of these mythic creatures, please consider adding them to the herd!
Do note that we do not accept faeries that are searching for a new owner (permanent homes only) and we only accept faeries that are wearing a background or more!
NOTE: I do not check if your pet has changed owners. Are you the new owner and see your pet listed here without your permission? Neomail me and I will remove it from the page or change the username to yours. I WILL actively remove pets that do not wear a background anymore.
