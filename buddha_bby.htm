    Directly. // About + Updates

    Welcome! Directly. was founded wayy back in the day in 2015 [8 years ago!] by Shaedi. One of my favorite things to do on neo has always been coding. We don't rank sites as recommended or favorites, simply because we feel that each site suits different people for different reasons. If we single out certain sites as "recommended" then it leaves room for you to overlook potentially great sites! We will always strive to bring you guys nothing but clean, working pages. We only ever remove sites if the owner requests it, they disappear, or become unusable due to too many broken images/coding. Check our most recent archive to see a list of all of the pages we've ever added or removed!

    Click here or on the link above, to get listed.
    Feel free to neomail me for whatever! -Shaedi
