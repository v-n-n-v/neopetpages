Gentle Giants

A Pet Directory for Elephantes & Chombies

Welcome to Gentle Giants. This is a pet directory for the Elephantes and Chombies of Neopia. I decided to make a pet directory after seeing all the lovely petpages dedicated to beloved pet species and colours. This petpage is dedicated to elephants, which are my favourite wild animal. But I think Elephantes and Chombies would be excellent herbivorous buddies, so those cute dinos are welcome, too.

Please read the rules before submitting your pet. Thank you, and have a great day!

Neomail your submission with the following:

  • Name
  • Nickname (optional)
  • Species
  • Colour
  • Owner (username or preferred name)
