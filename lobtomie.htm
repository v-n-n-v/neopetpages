Welcome to Ix-Sea! I'm Tomie. That's me on the right over there! I run and operate this awesome Maraquan Ixi Directory! Ix-Sea is a place where all the Maraquan Ixi of Neopia gather to play! Here you will find all types of Maraquan ixi, from the very young, to the very old, to the fanciest, to the plainest. But they are all well-loved and adored!

If you love maraquan ixi as much as we do around here, I'm sure you'll enjoy perusing all of the simply gorgeous maraquan ixi we have listed. If you have a maraquan ixi of your very own, you should list him/her here! We're always excited to have more ixi join our family!

We also have a section just for our Favorite Customizations! We love to see how other Maraquan Ixi are dressed up and so we've included this section just for that. If you have a beautifully dressed maraquan ixi, please send me a neomail so we can list it here! Maybe you'll inspire someone else to dress their beautiful Sea Goat!
