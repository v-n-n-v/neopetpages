Goal of the Game


Faerie Bubbles pop if you get three of the same type together, and it's up to you to clear each level by popping all of them. If you get four or more bubbles together you will set off a combo. There are good and bad combos. The less moves you complete each level in, the higher your score will be. Also look out for Nova and Rainbow bubbles, each with their own special effects! Just in case you didn't know - you can click the little X in the top left hand corner of the game board to pause the game. This also gives you options to turn the sound off, restart the game, send score or resume playing.


Different Bubbles


If you get three of these bubbles together, those bubbles will disappear. Except for the Nova bubble and Rainbow bubble, these are special bubbles and have other effects.


Fire bubble Dark bubble
Water bubble Air bubble
Light bubble Earth bubble
Nova bubble Rainbow bubble





Bubble Effects


To get a combo, you have to get four or more bubbles together.


Good Combos:
Fire Combo:
This burns all non-water bubbles that touch the combo.
Water combo:
This destroys all water bubbles on the screen.
Light combo:
This changes all bubbles touching the combo to a random colour.


Bad Combos:
Dark combo:
All the bubbles in the combo will turn to random colors.
Air combo:
This is BAD. Don't do an air combo, seriously.
It fills in extra space in any line where there are air bubbles.
Earth combo:
This makes a new line of bubbles appear, in random colors.


Special Effect Bubbles:
This bubble clears all the bubbles it touches.
It will clear al the bubbles within a two bubble radius.
This bubbles gets the color of the first bubbles it touches.
If you shoot it against a fire bubble, it will turn fire...... and so on.





Special Codes


Type the following words during your game to get special help!! Limited to one use per game.

stardust: changes the current bubble into a Nova bubble
faerieland: changes the current bubble into a Rainbow bubble
slumberberry: all your lines of bubbles go back to the top of the screen
bubbles: changes all the bubbles on your screen to one type


Game Tips


1. It's not important to get as many threesomes as you can in a level. The most important thing is that you finish the level with the least amount of bubbles shot! Sure, you get points if you make threesomes, but you get way more points if you finish the level quick, believe me!

2. This game is not being timed or anything. Yes, I know your lines are going down from time to time. The lines go down every five bubbles shot. This could come in handy if you want to figure out if you can just shoot that last bubble, to get some room again for the next!

3. Light bubbles look great and they are supposed to make good combos, but they are very unpredictable!! There is a chance that when you make a Light combo, all your Light bubbles will change into Air, Dark or Earth bubbles, and you probably don't like that!! So choose wisely, when you have some Light bubbles on your screen. (They can turn into good bubbles too, no one knows!!)

4. Make fire combos to get rid of those pesky, irritating Air, Dark and Earth combos!!

5. Sometimes, when you use the "bubbles" code, all your bubbles will turn into Rainbow bubbles. This is, however, very rare! If it does happen to you, you can only get rid of them if you shoot another Rainbow bubble at them. Use your "faerieland" code to get one.

6. You will always get a 250 points bonus if you clear level 20 (but you get more if you use my next tip!!)

7. Try to clear level 14 without using the "slumberberry" or "bubbles" code. That way you can use your "slumberberry" code for the next level (level 15). At least try to save the "bubbles" code and if possible the "faerieland" code for the very last level. If you reach level 20, and use the "bubbles" code, hope your bubbles will turn into something good (not Dark or Earth!) Then you can use the "faerieland" code and you can clear the level with one shot!! This means an extra 500 points bonus for you!! (Could be worth it when you want to get the avatar!)
