Machines in Motion

Date of collab: Late Summer (August?), 2024

Submission Timeframe: Late July/Early August. (After the previous Neopian Times Issue until the Monday before the Issue.)

Our next collab is Machines in Motion. A collab is when lots of Neopians agree to all submit to one issue of the Times with the same theme. You can find past examples here. This means that everyone will be writing something to do with that theme, however they choose to interpret that.

Anyone is welcome to join in the fun, whether this is your first or fiftieth time in the NT. We would appreciate you sending us a neomail, as listed in the Submission Form below, so that we can send you updates and reminders about this user-created special issue.

We keep a master list of everyone who is interested in participating on the NTWF, the certified fansite, because more than one person can edit the list that way. This does not mean you need to join to participate, we will neomail you with any news.

If you want help or advice with your entry, I would advise stopping by the Neopian Writers Board. There's often someone there willing to help.

Keep an eye out for other contests with the same theme, such as Art Gallery, Poetry Contest, Storytelling Contest, and Beauty Contest. It's up to you, though, to make them happen by submitting pieces to the theme!

Thanks for reading, and please consider joining in!
