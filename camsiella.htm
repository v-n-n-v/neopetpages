Yurbles are one of many different species that exist in Neopia. As a general rule Yurbles are curious, fun loving Neopets that love eating just about anything.

about featured pets:
the yurble feature/pet spotlight/yurble of the month/whatever u wanna call it is a little section i've made to showcase the creative sides of you lovely yurble owners! every once in a while, i'll send a neomail to one yurble owner asking if they'd like to be featured. i'll ask to use a piece of art of your yurble along with a short blurb about their character to post up on here. i also take neomail nominations!
