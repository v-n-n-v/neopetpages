Welcome!

This is a page dedicated to customs that various neopians have created, myself included! I spend most of my time on neo either playing around with customs / DTI, or trading NC items. As a result, I randomly decided to open a petpage where the main focus is on customs, whether they're made from NP, NC, or both. I also really enjoy seeing what other people have created, and because everyone has their own creative style, I thought it would be a fun idea to create a collection of people's designs! If you wish to have your pet / custom featured here, please neomail me using the submission form.

Pets with a * have no customs atm. Consider submitting!

Some things to know

  • You're encouraged to send in your custom to be featured here! More details below.
  • Please be aware that someone may replicate your custom. If you don't want this to happen, I do not suggest sending in your custom.
  • Each species section is organized by paint type.
  • Hover over the image to view the pet name and click to view their lookup.
  • Please make sure to scroll under each custom to view the full details.
  • Designs that use NP items will show (NP) beside the item name.
  • Links to TLs are broken (e.g., the DTI link is placed after the neopets url) but you can edit the link to find their TL/WL by removing the neopets portion.
  • Lastly, if you have a page of your own, I'd love to exchange buttons c:

Submissions are open

  • Fill out the submission form and send it to me via nm.
  • Use Custom Submission or something along those lines as the subject for the message.
  • Since the form is short, you can send in multiple customs at once.
  • The UN and pet (if applicable) you provide on the form will be used to link back to you.
  • You're welcome to submit a custom for a pet you don't even own; any of your designs can be displayed here!
  • You can also share your TL/WL link as well.
  • Please give me at least 24 hours to place your custom here. I'll reply as soon as I see your message and will typically upload at that time.

Additional Information:

  • Do you want your UN and/or pet linked? If not, it will display as anonymous.
  • Do you have a DTI link to the custom? This link will only be used by me to see the items/image.
  • If not, please list out the items used. I'll grab the image from the pet's lookup.
  • Did you use NP items? If yes, add (NP) beside the item.
  • Page = petpage, like if you have a directory of your own, attached to that specific pet!
  • You can share your TL if you'd like, if not, just leave it blank.
  • Tell me anything else that you believe may be useful.
