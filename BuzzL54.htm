Weapon Rebalance Proposition

The primary goal of this proposal is to adjust a handful of existing weapons to be more balanced for mid-tier league play. There are hundreds of weapons on the site, most of which are functionally useless due to being massively underpowered for their cost. Making minor changes to some of these items would allow them to see more usage and encourage purchasing, trading, and battling.

Details:
-All of the listed weapons already exist on the site; no need for new item artwork.
-All of the items proposed here are classified as ACTIVE on the site (not retired) and sold in official shops.
-No icons were removed from any items. Only additions were made, ensuring no loss of power/value to any current weapon holders.
-No weapons with attack icons were altered to have more than 13 total icons, ensuring no power creep on existing high icon weapons.
-To prevent massive changes all at once, a slow trickle of rebalanced species items can be announced on pet holidays (Bori Day, Lupe Day, etc).

Additional comments: I'm suggesting species-specific weapons as a good starting point because they wouldn't introduce a glut of new set options all at once. Because each item can only be used by one species, it doesn't fundamentally flip the meta, just allow 1-2 more options to play with alongside existing sets.

Special Consideration

These items currently do nothing. No attack/defense icons, no special abilities. Updating would allow them to actually be used in the battledome. As-is, they are just collectibles classed as weapons.
