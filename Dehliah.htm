OIL PAINT BORI TIME!
IT'S TIME. Bori Queen has conjured up her second ever fan made design. Toy Bori was a flop and honestly I don't blame them it was weeks before Bori Day and way too close to Starry in terms of colors. However, I have not given up. I have decided to make a second attempt, this time... we're turning to the canvas.



Ain't she a beaut? This is a design that's been bouncing around in my mind for ages now and I'm so proud with how it turned out. I'd be honored to have a pet color immortalized on site, especially of my favorite species of all time-- Thanks to pet slots I own 36 bori and I'd be more than happy to make my own design number 37.
INSPIRATION!!!!!


Well, you know how Oil Paint Cybunny is based off of The Starry Night by Van Gogh? I absolutely LOVED the idea of making more oil paint pets based off of actual paintings, sooooo...


I decided to base my design off of Van Gogh's Sunflowers! I really want to set a precedent and open the door to more Oil Paint pets being based off of real life paintings... Imagine all the dynamic colors and ideas you could use!!!

Spread this everywhere you can so the idea can gain traction!! And when October rolls around... PLEASE PLEASE PLEASE make sure TNT is aware of this petpage and my design!!!
