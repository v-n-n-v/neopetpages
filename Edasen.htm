Welcome to Noodle's Untaken Names. This page is for my fellow picky people. Here you will find mostly pronounceable 5L, 6L, and 7L+ names. Many are inspired by real names and even more are inspired by fiction or completely made up by me.

Feel free to take any names for yourself! If a pet's photo is displayed, that means the name has already been used. All names should pass through the filters, but if you see one that should be removed, please neomail me.
