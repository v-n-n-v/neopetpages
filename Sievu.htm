Frequently Asked Questions


Q: What's UC mean?
 A: Before customization was around, Neopets used to look different. When TNT redrew them into more uniform poses, some were given the option to keep their old looks. Today, those pets are called Unconverted (or UC) and cannot be made any more. For more info, check out this guide.

Q: So uh...how do I offer on these pets?
 A: This isn't a directory of UFT pets! It's a record of trades that have already happened. You can see what kinds of critters the pet you want has traded for lately, but I'm afraid you'll have to look elsewhere to find one that's UFT :)
Q: Your picture is wrong! My UC looks different from the one shown on your list.
 A: TNT did modify some of the UCs at the time of conversion (UC Plushie Quiggles and UC Grey Tonus in particular come to mind). I opted to use all of the old circle images for consistency, so they may not look quite the same.

Q: Most importantly, who is Dante?
A: Dante is our loveable Red Cyodrake mascot. This chipper little fellow can often be found roaming about the site. He looks kind of tough, but don't worry - we promise he's totally harmless!
