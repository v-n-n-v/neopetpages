Meridell:


^Overall. You start at Trestin in the Western Plains and head around kinda clockwise from there.



^Western Plains. Trestin is the starting town. Head over to the Dark Cave when you're about level 5.



^Trestin. Make sure you talk to Mother whenever you need her free rest.



^Dark Cave + Gold Mine. The Miner Foreman is your first boss. Defeat him and step on the teleport orb behind him to be transported to the start of the cave.



^Northern Marches. Head to White River City to rest and stock up on supplies, then get ready to grind some levels before fighting Zombom.



^White River City. Buy potions and a sword for Rohane and rest at the inn. The bridge appears after Zombom is defeated.



^Underground Cave. Follow the puddles. Pretty simple. Just be careful of the skeletons in here.



^Lost Island. Please don't tell me I need to tell you where to go? :P



^Mysterious Tower lvl 1. Start praying that you won't get attacked by skeleton knights.



^Mysterious Tower lvl 2. Keep on heading up...



^Mysterious Tower lvl 3. And up a little bit more...



^Mysterious Tower lvl 4. Time to fight one of the hardest bosses in the game. Hope that luck is on your side and Zombom won't decimate you too often. Catch the portal down to the island.



^Southern Meridell. Head round to Lakeside before going to the desert.



^Lakeside. Stock up on potions and talk to the Hermit to gain access to Phorofor.



^Phorofor. You won't be able to get in here without talking to the Hermit first. Sand Grundo is a pretty easy boss.



^Tower on a Hill lvl 2. Head down and out.



^Tower on a Hill lvl 1



^South-East Meridell. Go to Seaside for the usual rest and potions then go south to the Plains of Retreat.



^Seaside. This is the last town in the chapter so remember to get enough potions for your two fights against Ramtor, as well as for Leximp at the start of the next chapter. Also, here is where I normally buy 5 resurrection potions as this is as cheap as they get, and by now you'll have gold to spare on them.



^Plains of Retreat. The grass surrounding the castle (including the trees around the small lakes) up until the band of hills in the middle is a monster-free zone. Fight Ramtor for the first time in the castle then follow him to his tower.



^Meridell Castle. Fight Ramtor. Talk to the guard at the entrance afterwards for a free rest.



^Ramtor's Tower ground level. The grass at the entrance is monster-free. You have to head down through the dungeon to reach the stairs going up.



^Ramtor's Tower dungeon lvl 1. Come down the bottom stairs and go back up at the top. If you want to talk to Skarl go to the second dungeon level.



^Ramtor's Tower dungeon lvl 2. Yep, just Skarl here.



^Ramtor's Tower tower. The area down the bottom beneath the final statues is monster-free. Defeat Ramtor to finish the chapter.

Terror Mountain


^Southern Pass. You begin right on the left, defeat Leximp in the Damp Smelly Cave, go to Chia Oscuro for rest and potions, then head over to the Caves of Terror. Note: you are able to walk through these trees.



^Chia Oscuro. You can't do anything here until you've gotten the Wordstone from Leximp. After you do you can rest for free at the blue chia middle-left and get potions from the green chia up the top. Note: some people find themselves unable to beat Leximp because they're seriously underlevelled. In that situation, if you want, you could risk going straight on to the Mountain Inn on the other side of the Caves of Terror.



^Damp Smelly Cave. Leximp is a pretty hard fight - he is very resistant to Mipsy's magic attacks.



^Caves of Terror. Hurry through here to get to Talinia.



^Eastern Pass (overall). This map is also broken up into smaller sections if you want a bigger picture. You start on the right and work your way around. Collect Talinia at the Mountain Inn, fight Kolvars, stock up at Happy Valley and grind a few levels by the encampment before heading into the Lost Caves.



^Eastern Pass pt 1



^Mountainside Inn lvl 1. Pick up Talinia, rest at the green tuskaninny (closest pet to the door) and get potions from the white cybunny down the bottom.



^Mountainside Inn lvl 2



^Eastern Pass pt 2. Kolvars isn't really that hard. Once you get past him you are in a monster-free area which continues until about where the path forks into two down south.



^Eastern Pass pt 3. Do the usual town stop at Happy Valley and rejoice in the knowledge that there are no monsters to attack you.



^Happy Valley. Talk to Jualie then Niacha to get discounts at the shops.



^Eastern Pass pt 4. From the last 4-block wide part just below the Adventurer's Encampment up until you enter the cave it is a monster-free zone.



^Adventurer's Encampment. Rest for free at Allden (bottom left).



^Lost Caves lvl 1. The snowy area at the entrance is monster-free apart from the three tiles that directly touch the dirt ones. Just head up the stairs.



^Lost Caves lvl 2. And up the stairs again.



^Lost Caves lvl 3. Not too sure what a throne is doing in a 'cave'.



^Lost Caves lvl 4. Go on, talk to the Snowager. He doesn't bite. The lava can't harm you either as you make you way up and out.



^Mountain Peak. Defeat Scuzzy to finish the act.

Lost Desert


^Lost Desert Overall. You start at Sakhmet, go to the Temple of the Sky, back to Sakhmet, Ruined Temple, Sakhmet, Waset Village to pick up Velm, Palace of Ancient Kings, back to Waset, Hermit, 2nd Medallion Piece, Akhten-Ka, Gemstone, Zakharukh's Pyramid. The map is broken up into pieces below.



^South. The area immediately around Sakhmet and the Huts is a monster-free zone.



^Central



^West



^North-West. If you can't find the medallion piece, just follow the hermit's intructions: go due north until you hit the mountains, then due east until you hit more mountains.



^East. The gemstone is three steps to the left of the single mountain.



^Sakhmet. Talk to the labelled people for story info each time you get sent back there.



^The Huts. Go to tent 1 for potions and tent 6 for the inn.



^Temple of the Sky lvl 1. Just head up the stairs on the top-left.



^Temple of the Sky lvl 2. Be careful of Siliclast, he can hit quite hard. Catch the portal back to Sakhmet.



^Ruined Temple lvl 1. The usual heading up the stairs drill.



^Ruined Temple lvl 2



^Ruined Temple lvl 3



^Ruined Temple lvl 4. Fight Gebarn II, yell at your computer as you get sent back to Sakhmet, and run back up there as fast as you can to collect Velm from Waset.



^Waset Village. Time to read the best five words in the game: Velm has joined your party! The grarrl in the same tent is the inn and Erweb has potions. Lifira appears after you rescue her from the Palace of Ancient Kings. You get discounts when she's back if you feel the need to refill any potions you used since you were last there.



^Palace of Ancient Kings lvl 1. Step on the bed below 'GO DOWN HERE' to get down to the next level.



^Palace of Ancient Kings lvl 2. Fight the Revenant, rejoicing in the fact that you now have Velm with you, and talk to Lifira before taking the portal outside to head back to Waset to talk to Lifira again. All of the tiled area up the top is monster-free.



^Akhten-Ka. You won't be able to get in here if you haven't talked to the hermit and collected the second medallion piece. Make sure you follow the instructions Coltzan gives you after you defeat him and collect the medallion piece from the barrel behind him and then the gemstone from the place marked on the 'East' map.



^Zakharukh's Pyramid lvl 1. If the medallion is not completed then you will not be able to enter.



^Zakharukh's Pyramid lvl 2. Head up again to find Anubits.



^Zakharukh's Pyramid lvl 3. Fight Anubits and go on to the Haunted Woods.

Haunted Woods


^Haunted Woods West. You start out just above Shadow Gulch. Stock up/rest and head over to the Castle of Count von Roo (and sorry, but no, you aren't able to walk on the swampy tiles).



^Shadow Gulch. Green grundo in the bottom-left for potions, red kacheek in the top-right for the inn.



^Castle of Count von Roo lvl 1. Head down the stairs.



^Castle of Count von Roo dungeon. Defeat Meuka, talk to Roo to catch up on recent events and take the portal out.



^Stenchful Swamp. Weave your way amongst the trees and swamp tiles over to the cave.



^Cave of Dark Things. Defeat Spider Grundo, talk to him for more story and head out.



^Balthazar's Grove. Never thought you would miss grass this much, did you? To make the grass even better, it's a monster-free zone. Too bad it turns back to the usual dull colour when you're done there. Go into the house to find your last potion and rest stop and yet more story as well as the key to making the Four Faeries attack you. Be really careful when fighting them, there are four of them attacking you at once with several abilities each, making it feel like you're attacking other versions of yourself that have a lot more health.



^Happy Fun Non-Haunted House lvl 1. You have to head up to the second floor to get to the stairs that lead out the back the first time you arrive, after that the gates at the sides will have disappeared so you can take that route instead. The Brain Tree tells more story and tells you what to say to aggravate the Faeries into attacking you. Augur Faunt gives you free rest and the last potions for the chapter. Make sure to come back here after you defeat the Faeries to re-replenish your potions for your future fights against Hubrid Nox (if you're not skipping him) and the Esophagor.



^Happy Fun Non-Haunted House lvl 2. Come up the stairs on the left, go down the ones at the top.



^Mountains of Terror. I'd recommend not going too close to the tower until Mipsy's health is above 200 so she'll have a better chance of surviving against the Hands of Nox. If you wish to fight Nox, head up the tower.



^Nox's Fortress lvls 1 + 2 + 3. If you need to do any leveling, do it on these levels as there are no Hands of Nox until level 4.



^Nox's Fortress lvls 4 + 5 + 6. Start hoping that you won't get attacked by more than one Hand.



^Nox's Fortress lvls 7 + 8 + 9. Be prepared for a long fight if Nox heals continuously. Thankfully the portal saves you the effort of walking all the way back down.



^Goo Bog. Defeat the Esophagor to finish the chapter and go on to Faerieland.

Faerieland


^Faerieland. You start up the very top and make your way down and around through the Underclouds to the city.



^Northern Watchtower lvl 1. Rest at the shoyru beneath the stairs. Head upstairs for potions.



^Northern Watchtower lvl 2. The yellow elephante down the bottom has the potions.



^The Underclouds. Go through Cumulonimbus to get to the town of Cirrus and back up to Faerieland.



^Cumulonimbus. Naturally, just to annoy you, there are lots of stone clouds blocking the easy way through. The black line shows the path you take.



^Cirrus. Go to the fire faerie in the middle for the inn and the light faerie, bottom left for potions. Max out the potions you get from her for they'll have to last for three battles against the Faerie Thief, two battles against the two Pant Devils, and the first fight against King Terask.



^Faerie City. The light faerie at the entrance explains what to do. Basically, you fight the Faerie Thief upstairs in the inn to the NE, follow her when she flees to the building just to the west of the entrance (you have to go the long way around through the building on the left down the stairs to the basement though to get to where she is) and when she flees again defeat her at the gates to the Faerie Palace. Unfortunately there are no portals here so you'll be doing a lot of walking.



^Faerie City - Inn. Fight the Faerie Thief and follow her when she runs away.



^Faerie City - Basement. Come through here to fight the Faerie Thief the second time.



^Faerie Palace lvl 1. You weave your way through the rooms on the right up to the NE corner, go down through the garden in the middle to the SW corner, and do some more weaving up to the NW corner and across to the stairs.



^Faerie palace lvl 2. You do the two northern towers first to defeat the Pant Devils. They'll give you the key that unlocks the door to the SE tower where you will defeat King Terask for the first time. From him you'll get the key that unlocks the door to the SW tower where you fight King Terask for the second time.
Note: the stair in the SE tower that's circled in black is the one that you want. The others lead to dead ends two floors up.



^Faerie Palace lvl 3 North. NW tower on the left, NE on the right.



^Faerie Palace lvl 4 North. Fight the Pant Devils and talk to the faerie back on level 2 to make the key for the SE tower.



^Faerie Palace lvl 3 South. SW tower on the left, SE on the right.



^Faerie Palace lvl 4 + 5 South-East. Fight King Terask, receive your final weapons/armour, and spend those last skill points on maximising your skills before heading to the SW tower to fight him again.



^Faerie Palace lvl 4 + 5 South-West. Make sure you rest at Lyra for free so you don't have to walk all the way back up from the second floor of the palace. Stock up on all your damage, speed and slow potions (and healing if you need it which is very unlikely) and go defeat the bigger and badder Terask to win the game! If you're wondering where he is, well, let's just say I hope you're wearing a hard hat (and where the map cuts off is an indicator too). :)
