Introduction

HELLO THERE!

Hello and welcome to Niche! This is a comprehensive visual guide to rare, popular, and notable NC wearables! My hope is that this guide will give you a better understanding of what the more profound parts of NC trading. This page is an inspired remake of the previous Notable Visual Guides, /~Reeses and /~Loosi.

If there are any suggestions (for listing, see below), comments, and/or inquiries then please contact Chips!

Last Updated: Jul 6th, 2020 (We Announced Heavy Retirement On Jul 6th, 2020)

Highlight Key


Listed At Grandpops

Blue indicates that the NC Wearable is listed at Grandpops!
It's been communally voted to provide their trade reports as publicly shown for their popular characteristic.



Unstable At Waka

Red indicates that the NC Wearable is listed as "Unstable" at Waka!
These NC wearables are at unsteady/different values. Be wary when trading these and make a VC thread if needed!



Obtainable In The NC Mall

Green indicates that the NC Wearable is obtainable at the NC Mall!
The item is either buyable in the mall, a possible prize in the NC Archives*, etc.



Rereleased Before (1 Year Ago)

Fuchsia indicates that the NC Wearable was rereleased before within the last year!
The NC Wearable was once obtainable again through NC activities such as Retired Capsules for example.


* Due to NeoHTML coding limitations, we are only able to apply at max two colored borders, so multivariable wearables will be listed in order of the following:

Grandpop Popular (Blue) → Waka Unstable (Red) → NC Mall Obtainable (Green) → Rereleased ≤ 1 Year Ago (Fuchsia)

Item Changes & Voting

Rarities and nobilities are subject to change wherever and whenever.

Every three months or so, we will create a pseudo-quick voting thread in where the community and I will determine for NC wearables to be changed in category, added to the listing, or be removed completely (as there are over 1,000 items listed on this page as of now).

Important Subjects To Think About:

  • Would you consider this NC wearable Popular, Hard to Find, or generally Rare? If not, why so?
  • Is the NC item solely popular/rare for its public listed quantity, value, or desirably (seasonally or not)?
  • If this NC wearable was ever rereleased, how will that determine its listing?
  • NC wearables with a high/low value and/or public listed quantity does not always indicate that the item is Popular or Rare, but they might crossover to show their nobility.

    • If you believe an NC wearable should or should not belong on this page (or elsewhere), then please contact Chips with either of the following NM form to plan towards a discussion vote thread:

    SUGGEST AN ADD:
    		SUGGEST A REMOVE:
    		SUGGEST A CHANGE

    Renominations will be allowed through add form after four months if the NC wearable was voted off completely.

    Disclaimer

  • This is just a visual notable rarity guide. Guides are just guides, not law.
  • Even though values are not listed, trade at your own discretion and never feel afraid to create a Value Check (VC) thread to get an opinion and information about NC Wearables.
  • We are not updated 24/7, but we will try to update within reasonable time.
  • Elite Boutique and other redeemables are not included unless they showcase high nobility (Delicate Earth Faerie Lightmites Trinket and Rosy Cheeks Face Paint really).
  • We will not be listing items that were released under 6 months ago unless they undergo certain specifications varying its nobility (Bonus Items from NC Mall Collectible, Altador Cup Set Sails BGs, etc).
