Faerie Clouds

Hi and welcome to Faerie Clouds. A special pet directory for all things Faerie Pets, run by Luna Tsukino (aka Chibi_Tenshi)♡. I've always loved the Neopets Faerie pets since I was young and just starting out on Neopets, and now that I'm older and can actually earn the NP to get them I'm slowly filling up my account with Faerie pets. But not just that but with pet customs that have Faerie themes for pets that aren't always painted faerie. I wanted to find a directory for Faerie pets but fell short and not all my pets would go in a normal 'Faerie Directory' Since I have some pets painted Royal, Cloud and even Christmas that still look Faerie.

[ Faerie Pets ]

All Faerie painted pets are welcome to join our Directory, with or without customisations. Those pets without Backgrounds however will be displayed together at the bottom of each species section so those with customs can be displayed together.


[ Unconverted Faerie Pets ]

All Faerie Unconverted pets are welcome too, and much the same as the previous section these pets will be organised with pets w/o backgrounds at the end of each section.

As of 23rd January UC pets have become more accessible and more prone to change so if you have a UC pet and plan on keeping it permanently as a UC then it'll go in here. If your pet is likely to change between UC and converted faerie then I'll kindly ask to have the pet placed in the first section. If your faerie UC is not coloured faerie under the style and you plan on changing between the two I don't think this directory is right for your pet, sorry.

[ Other Coloured Faerie Pets ]

Unlike the previous sections, ONLY customised pets that have a faerie theme will be welcome here, however this section will allow pets of any colour as long as the theme is matched. For example, pets with wings or pets based on a certain faerie type such as a Fire Faerie, Grey Faerie or our Faerie Queen Fyora.
