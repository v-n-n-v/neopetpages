Welcome to Eclipse, the battledome pet trades database!

Last updated April 5th 2021


Have you wondered what your BD pet can trade for? or maybe have a pet and want to know what to ask for? You came to the right place! Here I will list trades that have happened so it's easier to know.
But to keep the data up to data I need your help! You can help by submitting your trades from the last 12 months with the following information:

Month/Year in which you traded:
Name (or format), HSD of the BD pet and if even/trainable/uneven/labbed* (+ color/species if important).
Name (or format) and color/species of the other pet.
Extras (RW/RN, specifically seeking xxxx, etc):

* If you're not sure about even/trainable/uneven/labbed just send me all stats and I'll see how I classify it.



Please note that I will only list your pet if the total HSD is over 300HSD, you can calculate with this formula:
HSD = Health + Strenght (up to 750) + Defence (up to 750)


And remember that to calculate HSD Strength and Defence are 750 at max because of boosts.
A pet with Health 800, Strength 800 and Defence 800 would have 2300HSD total.

Disclaimer

In an attempt to keep the database and BD pets values as close to reality as possible, trades that seem extremely unlikely won't be added. Rest assured, Uptrades and Downtrades always happen and they won't be refused.
Ex: BN 450 HSD E/T for VWN UC Tyrannian Draik would be refused



All pets listed fit on both Even/Trainable or Uneven/labbed unless its stated as only one of them.

BD pets with maximum strenght and defence & health over 750 are considered even for this guide because of caps.
This happens on most, if not all, 3001+HSD pets

About

Name formats

VWN: Short and easy to say, 7 letters or less.

WN: Up to 10 letters, easy to say, can be uncapped

DN: A bit harder to say, longer than 10 letters or include one or two underscores/numbers

BN: Hard to say, really long or have more than two underscores/numbers

VBN, Unpronounceable or have a lot of undescores/numbers


Even or trainable/Uneven or Labbed

Even: A pet whose Strenght and Defence is the same or very close (same boost)

Trainable: A pet you can increase the total HSD without having to train level or train very little.

Uneven: A pet whose Strenght and Defence are far from each other (different boosts)

Labbed: A pet trained by the lab, usually high health, medium strenght and really low defence and level.
