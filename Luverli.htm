Welcome to Sapphic Stars!

Hello! My name is Lavender and you've found your way to Sapphic Stars, a directory for sapphic pets that fit within the realm of butch/femme. I made this because I have a lot of pets I tend to fit into these categories, as a femme IRL, and I thought it'd be fun to see everyone's pets that identify as such and show them off!!! :)

Your pet can either have a character that IDs as butch/femme, or just be you expressing your identity through them. In the interest of avoiding discourse on neopets, I am keeping butch/femme a wide spectrum of identity within lesbian, bi, etc. My only request is please do not list here if you are openly trans exclusionary; I am a nonbinary femme lesbian irl and as such do not want anyone listed who might look down on me or others listed.
