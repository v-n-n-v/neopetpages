Scary Jack
The last pop up box he was in broke, and he was set free...to scare little pets everywhere!

Aren't I handsome? A Scary Jack is what's colloquially known as an UC petpet. UC petpets are a select handful of petpets who were changed into different petpets a long time ago. Only those of us who were already attached to a neopet kept our original forms. We can't be bought or sold because, like zapped or glitched petpets, we change if you remove us. Therefore, the only way to get an UC petpet now is to adopt or trade for a neopet who has one of us attached! Check out this page for more information:
