Connie's Nostalgic Petpet Match


Welcome! What is this? Well, it is Connie's guide to nostalgic petpet matching!

Wait but... who's Connie? Constanze is a socially isolated korbat who enjoys finding looks-alike. This time, she'll help you find the perfect partner for your unconverted nostalgic neopet!

Why unconverteds nostalgics? Well, it is 2021 and TNT announced they will release ucs that will be it's 2024 and unconverted neopets, now called nostalgic tokens are now obtainable through the NC Mall as items, which means that more users will be are able to enjoy this nostalgic pixels. Connie wants to help you find the perfect companion for your new nostalgic pets.

What criteria does Connie uses to pick the perfect match? She looks for color, pose and species resemblance, but mainly she uses her own personal and subjective criteria, so you might not agree with many some of the options given on this guide.

Press CTRL+F and write color + species in the text box to search for an specific pet!
