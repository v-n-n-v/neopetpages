Welcome!



We are all supporters of mystery island and we work together to win. We face each team with as much energy, dedication, and confidence as possible. By playing lots of Yooyuball, Slushie Slinger, Make Some Noise, or Shootout Showdown, we try our best to win the gold. It doesn't matter if we lose, we'll just pick ourselves up and continue moving forward. We have so much team pride and team spirit; we're like a huge family and it's just amazing. Even if we don't place as high as we hope to, we'll still look back and congratulate ourselves for working hard and trying.

MIYP: Mystery Island Yooyuball Pros


Created during the Altador Cup II, MIYP members are a group of Mystery Islanders who obviously support Mystery Island during the Altador Cup. At the end of the Altador Cup II, they found that the Altador Cup brought them all closer together with one goal: to beat the team they were against. It was extrememly hard for MIYP members to leave and lose contact for another year, so a guild was created in order to stay in contact.

Want to join the MIYP guild? Neomail kayangel31 for an invite!
