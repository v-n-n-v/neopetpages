Welcome:


Hello! Welcome to Krazy Kute Kreations, where you can find premade graphics and layouts made by me, Trisha, for decorating any and all of your pages here on Neopets. I worked hard on everything here, so please leave my credit (as small as it may be) on everything. And, if you feel so inclined, you may link back, as well.

I don't take requests, per se, but if you have an idea for something you'd like to see in a premade, I'm all ears. Neomail me here.

Note:


This site may take a while to load because it is VERY image heavy, so please be patient. In addition to that, it spans many pages (19, at the moment), so clicking a link on the bar above may take you to a different page and start the loading process all over again.

Image Previews:


A lot of my images are larger than my coding will allow me to place here reasonably (by design). If an image looks a little funky (or small) to you, try clicking and dragging it to a tab on your browser to load the full size preview. OR, you can right click and select "View Image" or "Open Image in New Tab" or something of that nature (it'll depend on what browser you're using). For just an image, it opens up the whole, full size image. For layouts, it opens up the thumbnail, which is still a smaller version, but will at least be relatively akin to scale, rather than potentially being skewed, stretched, or just generally weird, which happens when I do what I do to make all the previews as uniform as possible.

Getting Codes:


In order to pick up the codes, go to the box to the right of (or underneath) the graphic or layout that you'd like and either press the Ctrl key and the letter A at the same time, right click and go to "Select All", or triple click with your mouse/cursor. Then, either press Ctrl and C or right click and go to "Copy". Next, go to the layout editing page you need (all listed and linked below) and press Ctrl and V or right click and go to "Paste". Then, just save the changes and you're done!
