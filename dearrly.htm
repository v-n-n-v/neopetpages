Welcome

Welcome to Ixi Snacks – a directory for yummy edible ixi!

Ixi Snacks is presided over by Brenda and her ixi, Dearrly, and is is a home - a yummy, delicious, home for all edible ixi in Neopia! There's room for Biscuit ixi, Candy ixi, Cholate ixi, Custard ixi, Jelly ixi, and even Strawberry ixi!! There's room for everyone. Now mind you, when we say everyone, we do mean everyone. I mean, everyone has their own particular tastes. And we try not to judge around here. Edible is edible, even if you shouldn't eat it… but then again, we're not judging.

But if you really want to push to proverbial custard and get judged, just try noming on one of our delicious Snacks. Their hooves are sharp-ish and their horns are mean. So NO nibbling any of the ixi!

Please have a look around. There's lots to see!

By agreeing to list your ixi at this or any of Brenda's Ixi Directories, you agree for your ixi to be listed at any and all of the appropriate ixi directories run and maintained by Brenda of Ixi Inc..
