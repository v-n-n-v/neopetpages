The Top 5 1-P Opponents

I do this page as much as for me, as for anyone trying to beat them.

If you have any ideas as good or even better on how to do it mail me at claudej

Opponent Weapons Comments
Chiazilla

Render its Trident useless with a Bent Fork

My set : Bent Fork, Flame Reflectorzap & Triple Turbo Dryer

But 15,000HP takes a long time to dig in, while it spams with its abilities preventing me much of the time to heal. The only solution I see is a pet with a lot of HPs with a Wodf, healing only once in a while.

Result : UniClaude at 7000HP - 0/10 The lowest I was able to take it down was 5.61k without using my freezer & Lens flare. If I use everything the best I was able to do is to take it down to 2.1k. It means if I'm lucky and it uses its jade soon I may be able to take it down once in a while. Not worth yet for UniClaude

UniClaude at 9900HP : Much better and with a Hubrids box : 4 wins 2 losses

Update 2019 - I got a Reflectozap 2000. UniClaude 12,000 HP. 9/10 on strong difficulty

Update 2022 - UniClaude 15500 HP. Got it on mighty :)

Qasalan Mummy

I used a Pirate Captains Hat & Hanso Charisma Charm while waiting for it to use its Jade. Then a Kings Lens & Wodf.

It uses its Jade only once
It uses its Warlock Rage, Lens Flare & Rejuvenate as many times as it wants

UniClaude 7000 HP : 2/10 on Strong

A Rod of Dark Nova would be better while waiting for it to use its Jade.

Update 2022 - UniClaude 15500 HP. Got it on mighty :)

The Snowager

My set : U-Bend of Great Justice,Triple Turbo, Wodf & Hubrids Puzzle Box

It takes a long time to dig in through 17,500HP. It has many weapons with different types of icons.

Luckily it doesn't use it's Golden Butter Knife too often (maybe never on Strong). And it doesn't have a healer nor use Rejuvenate.

Result : UniClaude 7000 HP 0/10 I need more HPs for the Wodf to save me.

Update 2020 - UniClaude at 13,000 HP : not much better

Update 2021 - UniClaude at almost 14,000 HP : About 50/50 (I started to use the Hubrids Puzzle Box)

Update 1/2022 - UniClaude at 15,000 HP , added a Thyora Tear Result: 8/10

Update 4/2022 - UniClaude at 15,500 HP. Got it on mighty 7575 HP left but after 21 losses

Space Faerie

Render her Attack Fork useless with a Bent Fork

Defend as much as you can while waiting that she uses her Jade.

My set : Bent Fork, Wodf, Flame Reflectozap. A Reflectozap 2000 would help.

At 7100HP (UniClaude) score is 7/10 on Strong ( full HP)

At HP 7800 I added Superior Reflection Shield

Score 9/10 full HP(from 7800 HP)

I tried with a Hubrids box : Not much better

Change of tactics : As she can use her tempest as many times as she wants, you can't rely on the Wodf to heal. So I sacrifice my Jade , .. and it works .. no more losses (but less full HP wins). I added a Frozen Cyodrake Shield to block 100% her Fire while waiting that she uses her Jade.

But as she can use her Rejunevate as many times as she wants, it will never be a 100% sure win.

Update 2019 - I got a Reflectozap 2000. No need for the Frozen Cyodrake Shield and Flame Reflectozap. A lot better 9/10 on Mighty difficulty

Update 2022 - UniClaude 15500 HP. Got it on mighty :)

The Drenched

My set : Hanso Charisma Charm, Altadorian Swordbreaker, Ultimate Dark Reflectorb, Flame Reflectorzap 2000 and Wodf

I use the Hanso Charisma Charm & the Altadorian Swordbreaker until it uses its Jade and Muffin

Then I switch to the Ultimate Dark Reflectorb & Flame Reflectorzap

Result : UniClaude 7000 HP - 0/5 The lowest I was able to take it down was 14k(out of 15k) (without using my freezer & abilities). It uses the Tempest ability too often. I would need a really big pet to be able to heal once in a while.

Update 2022 - UniClaude 15500 HP. Got it on mighty :)
