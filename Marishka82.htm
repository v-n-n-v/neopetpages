Fun Facts!

  • There are 493 unique Petpet Species!
  • There are 59 unique Petpet Colors (+12 other cases for example: Frozen Raindorf, Dirt Symol, etc.)!
  • There are 50 unique Petpet Paint Brushes!
  • There are 7 unique Lab Ray Colors!
  • Slorg is the most colored petpet at 57 different colors variants (+2 unreleased versions).
  • There are currently 97 active species that do not have a second colored variant (Lab Only: 8, Cardboard: 4, Cooking Pot: 21, Cog: 15, and Regular: 48).
  • There exist two active Christmas Baby Blu! There is the the retired version of a green colored base and the newer version with a pepermint colored appearence.
  • A Grey Snuffly is called a Sniffly!
  • Excluding Lab Exclusive and Cardboard petpets, Neopian Petpets has the most amount of petpets at a total of 715 petpets.
  • Aside from Lab Exclusive and Cardboard petpets, Moltaran Petpets have the least amount of petpets at a total of 38 petpets.
  • For Lab Colors, Ice has the least amount of colors at a total of 4 species.
  • For Paintable Colors, Elderly has the least amount with 8 species.
  • Base color and painted combined, Blue has the most colored species at 226 petpets.
  • Excluding painted species, Blue also has the most base colored species at 63 petpets.
  • Excluding base colors, Faerie has the most painted species at 169 petpets.
  • For Lab Only Colors, Chocolate has the most species at 36 petpets.
  • Pirate Marafin and Tyrannian Blorbis might possibily be the most elusive painted petpets due to their original rarity from being one of the top redeemable reward from Curse of Maraqua Plot.
