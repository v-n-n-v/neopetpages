IT'S BUZZIN' TIME!

Welcome to The Swarm: A Buzz Life. Here we celebrate one of our favorite species in all of Neopia!

Buzz use their keen eyesight and aerobatic expertise to help themselves to the tastiest wild berries and fruits, much to the dismay of the Neopian farmers. They can zoom to the top of the Tigersquash tree and pluck three of the plumpest fruits and whizz off in the blink of an eye.

Requirements

We would love to list your buzz! The only requirement is that they are a permanent pet.
