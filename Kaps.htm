FAQ

➤ How do you earn Neopoints?
The best way to earn Neopoints is by playing the game persistently as it's going to be a long journey. You can do any or all of the following:
- Site Dailies (and Trudy)
- Playing Games
- Battledome
- Restocking (and Reselling)
- Stock Market
- Food Club

Guides for these activities can be found in the Other section of the Helpful Resources section.

➤ How do I go back to the old site?
You can add a backslash "/" or "/x" to the end of the URL string to temporary return back to the classic pages, but this (and any other workarounds) will probably be patched in the future. To view the classic Customization page, page you would visit here. The classic Gamesroom page can be found here.

➤ How do I enable Flash? What happened to all the maps?
Just like many features of the site, the Maps of Neopia are currently being converted to HTML5. If you wish to view the classic Flash maps, then you'd want to do the following above. It is NOT recommended to enable Adobe Flash post-Flash EOL, but you can find a comprehensive guide on how to enable Flash at /~Xochitlicue.

IMPORTANT: Be aware of the risks that come with enabling Adobe Flash post-EOL as enabling outdated software could make your computer vulnerable to a variety of unwanted dangers such as security risks, malware, and more. If you wish to enable Flash, it is best advised that you only enable it for Neopets to reduce these dangers.

➤ How much does X cost?
There are three common methods on how to check the price of an item:
- Check the item on the Shop Wizard (or Super Shop Wizard or SSW) for the lowest or if there are any available sales listed.
- Check Jellyneo's Item Database as a possible price reference while also checking the currently available Trading Post / Auction House listings.
- Create a Price Check (PC) thread on the Trading / Auctions NeoBoard Chat.

➤ How do I recover my old Neopets account?
You would want to gather as many identifying details as you know of your account, mainly the UN, all e-mails, and the birthday. You would then want to create a ticket through Neopets's support page and send the ticket number to the Highway to Help thread on the Help NeoBoard Chat and/or their social media's private messages. Over there, a TNT staff member will assign your ticket to support and further steps will proceed when you're messaged back. If you do not hear a response within two weeks, you would want to send in another ticket.​

➤ How can I view all my pets in my userlookup?
