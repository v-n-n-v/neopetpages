    FAQ

    Q: How the heck did you make all this?

    A: I've been on neopets a long time. I learned stuff. Some of this is new, some of this is older stuff I'm throwing back up. I tried to convince my friends that my old stuff wasn't good anymore. They overruled me.

    Q: Why do you have such a long credit section for resources?

    A: Cause when I was in my early graphics phase I used a lot of crap from a lot of different places. I try to be more selective now, but...I want to give credit where it's due, plus it helps y'all find resources for your own stuff.

    Q: Can you teach me to do graphics?

    A: If there's ever enough people begging me I might write a guide on how to do simple stuff and throw it on a petpage....maybe.
