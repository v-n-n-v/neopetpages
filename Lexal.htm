About the pets

Stuck pets are abandoned pets which do not show up at the Pound. You would need to manually search their names in order to find them. You can learn how to search for stuck pets here. It requires patience and determination but it's great fun!

About the list

Just click on any pet to adopt. If you are looking for a new lab rat, do consider choosing a basic pet instead of a painted one! And if you end up adopting one, let me know! I'd love to know what you are planning for your new pet!

Thanks for visiting and best of luck!
