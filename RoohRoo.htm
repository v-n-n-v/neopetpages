Welcome!

Hi! You can call me Dorito. I'm an artist living in the U.S.! ♥

I work in the video game industry as a Concept Artist, and my hobbies include playing video games, watching or reading nerdy stuff, and gardening.

Do you take requests?:

Sometimes! It's worth a shot to ask. I'm more inclined to do general artwork for requests, no pets or personal characters pleaseeee.

This should go without saying too, but if you suggest an idea to me and I don't end up drawing it, please don't be upset! It's just a request after all :) I'll do the ones that interest me.

Do you do art trades?:

Currently no :( Check back later!
