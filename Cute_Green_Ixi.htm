Tips

Host Yourself:

I'm not saying you have too, but I really recommend it. You can upload images to photobucket.com, and many other FREE places. (Just google "free image-hosting" if you of some reason don't want to use those above.) And then you just save the images I've used, upload them and replace the url in the coding. At the moment, you don't need to host stuff yourself, as not that many people use these lookups. But if I ever delete them yours wouldn't disappear then. But NEVER, ever claim to have made my images, as I've used a lot of time painting them.
