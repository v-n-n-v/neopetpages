Hello, hello! How are you? Welcome to Clara's Rehoming Project! Here I train, paint or zap pets in the hopes of making them interesting enough to find a new owner. I started this petpage at the end of April 2023, and I'm excited to see how far I can take this hobby. The intention is to train some little critters to at least 300 HSD + get them a nice color and species combo, recording everything down below in the meantime. I also added the option of taking potions, brushes and NC items home to beautify your pets. Feel free to neomail me if you see a pet or custom you'd like to take home, explaining a little bit about your plans and goals. Retrading pets is perfectly fine and 1000% encouraged, as long as you are honest about it ~ ♡

Project guide:

This project's goal is to send pets to nicer homes. I'd love for them to be customized and taken care of once adopted, even if you plan on retrading. Obviously not everyone enjoys the dressing up game, so it's okay if you don't wish to do so.

Speaking of retrading, as it was said before, that's more than alright. If you can get a dreamie with a pet found here, go for it! Those looking specifically for permie homes will have a note right below it.

You might come across one or more icons. At the top left of the page you can see what they mean.

Potions and brushes are meant to be used by you, not resold/retraded. Same thing goes for the NC section.

If you don't want the pet/item anymore, please consider readopting/returning the items. You can also swap for another one available, don't worry.

It's okay to adopt multiple pets and/or take home multiple items, but, to ensure everyone has a chance, please wait for at least 30 days.

Remember: if you want something, neomail me (@clorew), writing a little bit about your plans. I'll reply as soon as I see it.

Oh, by the way, I'm always in need of help! If you have a pet you want to donate, please, feel free to reach out.
