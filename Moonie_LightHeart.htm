How to Run/Host a Theme Week

Hosting a Themed Week is really easy and anyone in our Community can. Feel free to host any theme(as long as it is Neo friendly of course) you would like. Below I will list steps/ how to of how to go about Hosting a Themed Week

  • Step 1:Gauge Interest ~ Create a post about your Theme idea to see if you get enough interested people
  • Step 2:Plan Dates ~ Set up some dates. Sometimes dates may change depending on what may already already be happening in the community. Dates can be mentioned while gauging interest.
  • Step 3:Create Board ~ Once all above is set make a board with all the info.
  • Optional Step:Create a Petpage to host the info and gallery(gallery is not a must. just an option)
  • Final Step:Use the NM link above to Nm me, Zouz, about the theme week. Though I will try to catch it in the boards.
