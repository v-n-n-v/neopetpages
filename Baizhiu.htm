Legends of Neopia


Welcome, Warlords!

What is Legends of Neopia?
Legends of Neopia is a 1-player Battledome activity hosted by Legendary Warlords.

How do I participate?
Fight! Win 100 times for bronze. Rack up more wins to upgrade your trophy!

Do I need to be a part of the Legendary Warlords guild?
No. Trophies are available to the public and guild members alike.

A new challenger was announced. Does this mean I missed my chance to upgrade my trophy from the previous challenger(s)?
You are free to complete the challenges and earn trophies at your own pace in whatever order you choose. Only challengers marked "Limited Time Event" are unavailable after the month is over.

Does it matter which toughness level I choose?
It does not.
