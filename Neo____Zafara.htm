NEOPIAN LOTTERY - Pick 6 of 30

Pay 100 NP for a Neopian Lottery Ticket (new draw every day, normal maximum of 20 tickets per day)

Quick Picks by vancouverite2010 : Quick Picks by suolucidir

There are 593,775 different neopian lottery tickets.
There is 1 ticket that matches all 6 drawn numbers.
144 tickets match just 5 drawn numbers. 4,140 tickets match just 4 drawn numbers.
40,480 tickets match just 3 drawn numbers. 159,390 tickets match just 2 drawn numbers.
255,024 tickets match just 1 drawn number.
There are 134,596 tickets that match no drawn numbers.

THE SECRET LABORATORY & THE PETPET LABORATORY

If you have already found and assembled all of the nine different Secret Laboratory Map pieces,
you can go to the Mad Scientist in The Laboratory! (one pet per day) Guide

If you have already found and assembled all of the nine different Petpet Laboratory Map pieces,
you can go to the Creepy Kookith in The Petpet Laboratory! (one petpet per day)

You can assemble your maps by having all of the correct map pieces in your inventory,
then go to the Neopets Treasure Hunt, and then click on the Laboratory or Petpet links.

IMPORTANT - You must completely assemble the Secret Lab map before you can assemble the Petpet Lab map.

WARNING - Severe damage may happen to your pet and to your petpet. Use both laboratories at risk!
