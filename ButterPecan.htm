FAQ

All compound names are welcome here, all name formats, converted and unconverted pets alike!

Please note: the pets listed in this directory are permie pets!

Q: What is a compound name?
A: A compound name is two or more words put together

Q: Do you accept *insert here* format?
A: Of course! All compound names are welcome here as long as they are permie pets!

Q: Why won't you list pets UFT?
A: It would be too hard to keep track of trades, and new owners may potentially be upset about being contact if they have permied the pet.

Q: I want to add my compound pet, but I'm zapping/unsure of what to make them. Can I list?
A: Absolutely! Just note that they are a labbie and I will add them to the labbie section. If I see them stay with a color change for more than a few weeks, I'll move them to their respective section ♥

Q: Can we be Affiliates?
A: Well heck yes! Send me a neomail
