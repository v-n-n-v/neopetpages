Welcome to my untaken compound names site!

I see you are curious as to what I do here. When the rain falls, each droplet contains a word. I combine the words, and I write it down into runes for those to see. You are free to take any runes you wish, I only ask for you to listen the next time the rain falls. Who knows what the rain is trying to tell you...


Touch the droplet to see the runes!
