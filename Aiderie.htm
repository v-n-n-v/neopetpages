    Get Listed at Guildly

    Note: We are currently only listing guilds with at least 11 members. If your guild has less than 11 members, you are welcome to send in news at any time for our news section on the homepage.

    To get listed, please send me a neomail with the following information. For your convenience, a description of each question is below and a textarea just containing the questions is below that. Please do not submit a guild for listing if you are not a council member of that guild.

  • Guild Name: The name of your guild.
  • Public/Private: Simply tell me if your guild is public or private.
  • Theme: What theme is your guild?
  • Member Count: How many members are in your guild?
  • Guild ID: Your guild ID can be located by going to your guild homepage and looking in the URL. Example: https:/guilds/guild.phtml?id=4184150
  • Website link: If your guild has a website, go ahead and provide the link to that here. Please only provide the part after neopets.com. For example, for this page, you would put /~Aiderie
  • Portal link: If your guild has a portal, go ahead and provide the link to that here. Please only provide the part after neopets.com. For example, for this page, you would put /~Aiderie
  • Description: This is where you can provide an up to 3-5 sentence description of your guild. If your guild is private, please plan to use at least one sentence to describe the requirements to join (or simply provide a short link to where those requirements are on your webbie)
  • Button: Please provide me the shortened link (ex: /~Aiderie) of a page where I can grab a button for your guild from. If there are multiple and you have a preference on which one I grab, you can describe the correct one here. If your guild does not have a button, please put N/A or none. If your guild does not have a button, feel free to check out our resources page to find guides on how to make one or a site to make one for you!
  • Contact People: Please provide anywhere from one to five people (usernames please) that can be contacted via neomail regarding your guild. If your guild is private, these people should preferably have the ability to accept applications/give invites.



    • If you need to edit your listing at all, simply neomail me with the subject update, the guild name, and any relevant updates.

    Guides and Resources

    All options below go to external (neopian) sites.

    The Popsicle Stand is a one-stop resource for all things site and guild related. They even have several guild ownership tutorials located in their tutorials section. If you're only going to check one resource out, this is a good one!
    Unwound Clock is a request status directory you can use if your guild would like a custom graphics/buttons/layouts made. You'll be able to see what sites have open versus closed requests on this site.
    Directly. is another directory site, but I believe you really can't have too many directory resources. This one is actively updating as well, which is supremely useful.
    Awakened Advertis.ing. is a guide I wrote up years ago, back when the GC was in its prime, on how to effectively advertise. There are some broken images on there and some of the information is now a little extra in a slower moving GC, but much of it is still good advice (in my biased opinion) and worth a read.
    Izzie's Guild Tutorial has a lot of valuable information about how to make a guild, what kind of pages to include, how to advert your guild, and how to keep members. Amazing resource I wish I had known about when I started creating guilds.
