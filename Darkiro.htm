► What pets can be listed?
Any pet in the categories below can be listed. If you are unsure that your pet would be okay to list, please feel free to neomail Lup with questions.
► Can non-permanent pets be listed?
Pets can be listed regardless of trade status. I would very much appreciate if you do trade with someone that you inform them that the pet is on this page - then the new owner can decide to take the entry down if they so choose.
► Can level 0 or -1 pets be listed?
These pets can be listed, but this glitch can be solved easily by the user or even by random events. If this glitch does not apply to your pet any longer, please contact me to take down the entry.


Finally - these pets are NOT UFT unless specifically listed. Please do not neomail owners asking to trade.

⋙Item Petpets⋘

Occasionally, TNT misflags items as petpets rather than their intended category. This results in items that can be attached as petpets! There have been four major misflag events over the years: the Jagged Ink Wings, Darigan Pteri Plushie, Petpetpet Comb, and Lutari Knight Tunic. There are other petpets out there that are not one of these four and are from events, the lab ray, or from windows of time where any item could be attached. Also included in this section is the 'TBD Goat Petpet', a glitch resulting from attaching a Muyang in another language.

You can find more detailed information about these item petpets here.

⋙Over-Aged Petpets and Trophies⋘

There are a few petpets out there that are older than the pet they are attached to. Most of these petpets came about because of the purge - when old pets were deleted from the system and later recreated, they could have the petpet of the old neopet attached. These petpets can be years older than the pet itself!!

Newly added to this section are pets that have the aged pet trophies (given to any pet that was brought into existence on or before April 26th, 2007) while being too young. The current theory of why this is happening is that the pets are unborn pets that were stuck in limbo and not fully created yet. If you have any info, please let me know!

⋙Multiple PPL Trophy Pets⋘

There are pets who have a glitched Petpet Protection League trophy, which results in the trophy being displayed multiple times. 2 or 3 trophies are commonly seen on these pets.
According to information gathered by Shark and n_t, this glitch happens when the script to award PPL trophies is run multiple times. There are some pets with upwards of 9 trophies, some with only 1 extra, with the total amount of pets with extra trophies being around 3000. This number continues to grow, with the glitch continuing to happen around once a year up to this point. Shark's directory has MUCH more detailed information and a listing of these pets!

⋙Negative Stat Pets⋘

There are two types of negative statted pets: 0cm/0lb pets and negative/zero level pets.

The first type of negative stat pets have a height and weight of 0 in addition to other stats, notably health, being at -1. I will call these pets the "A" type. These pets cannot enter the battledome, cannot lose stats to the lab ray, and whenever healed simply return to -1 health. These pets are a result of the now-revamped pet creation system, created by interrupting the creation process at the perfect time. There are also reports of pets that have been baduser'd and restored having these stats.

The other type, pets with a 0 or negative level, are more common and are replicable now. I will call these pets the "B" type. These pets can lose levels due to certain random events added in the 2014 RE revamp. With some luck, you can get one of these pets yourself with some patience!

You can find more detailed information about these pets here (first type) or here (second type).
