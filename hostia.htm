Frequently Asked Questions

#FoodClub #odds #gates #hold #trophyrun #trophiestime #betamount #15bets #WannaBetOnIt #follow #didnotwin #gift #TER #F.A #OOGA #guides
What is food club?
It's like horse racing but instead of horses, it's pirates and instead of racing, it's eating food. Food Club in a nutshell: 20 hungry pirates randomly assigned to 5 arenas daily. One pirate from each arena will win in each match. In order to collect winnings, all of the pirates in one bet have to win!

I'm following someone's bets but the odds are different. What's going on with that?
Odds range from 2:1 to 13:1 and may change throughout the round. Odds going up or down is most likely due to users putting that pirate in bets. More NP put on a pirate causes the odds to go down while less less NP on the less favored to win pirate causes their odds to go up.

The gates are closed? What does that mean?
Many moons ago, food club used to close the round between 1:45 and 2:15 p.m NST (so you cannot view any food club page) to determine which pirates won then the results were out at 2:15 p.m NST but now the gates still close for those 30 minutes but the results aren't out until 3:38 PM NST but this shifts forward a few seconds each day so by June 28, 2020 results will be by 3:39 PM NST.

How many rounds of results can I hold?
8 consecutive rounds. Once it's midnight NST, day 1's results will be permanently removed so you'll lose out on those winnings.

When does the trophy run start?
Trophy run starts on the 25th of every month that has 31 days, the 24th of every month that has 30 days and the 22nd for February only (the 23rd of February if it's a leap year, i.e 2020, 2024, 2028, etc.) Thirty days hath September, April, June, and November; Thirty-one the others date, Except in February, twenty-eight; But in leap year we assign February, twenty-nine.

When are trophies handed out?
Trophies are awarded by game i.ds starting around 8:45 p.m NST a majority of the time. They may skip a night and there's nothing you can do about it.

How much can I bet?
You can check it out here. Take that number and multiple it by 10 (the maximum number of bets everyone is allowed to make each round) but you DO NOT have to bet this much. Bet however much you are willing to lose. All 10 bets can be the same amount or you can change it for each bet depending on if you feel those pirates, together, will win.

What about those players who can do 15 bets? How?
If you had enough points through 2019's Charity Corner for the Oh, Blimey! perk (1,000 points for 7 days, max of 4 applications), you can have 15 bets total for a limited time.

What's 'Wanna Bet On It?
Again, from 2019's Charity Corner if you had enough points to gamble on Perk Me Up for a chance at the Wanna Bet On It? perk (14 days, max of 2 applications), you can bet 20% more than your max bet (for example, 6778 times 1.2 is 8133.6 rounded down to 8133) for a limited time.

I'm trying to follow someone's bets but the pirates don't match up?
Each bettor updates/posts at various times throughout the day, check to see when they update. If they still haven't then they may post later or they might be skipping. Check today's date and which round number we're currently on.

I'm sure I had the right pirates but I didn't win?
Check here for any bets, if yours are still there, the round hasn't ended yet. If there's nothing there, you didn't have all the right pirates in any bet. Try to screenshot that page then compare with winners.

I won from someone's bets!! Can I pay them or gift them something?
Using their bets does not put you under any obligation to thank, reward, or even pay them. Gifting is allowed on Neopets, but paying for services is not. Please don't put your -and their- account at risk by crossing this line!

What is T.E.R?
TER stands for Total Expected Return. The mathematical average amount you're expected to win from that set of bets compared to your investment. Generally, the higher the TER, the better. Greater than 20 TER is a good chance of doubling your investment. Anything above 10 TER is a profit, and anything below 10 TER generally means that the bet is risky with not a great chance of paying off. Note that TER is only the expected payoff, not a guarantee.

What is F.A?
F.A stands for Food Adjustment. When the pirates get randomly placed in any arena, so do 10 (out of 40) random foods. Each pirate has favorites and allergies (such as meats, dairy, smoothies, etc.) and the 10 random foods gets compared to the pirates in that arena. All 10 foods that the pirate favorites get added up then gets a point knocked off for each foods that are their allergies. Some people believe F.As have no further bearing on the results as they believe it's factored into the opening odds.

What does OOGA mean?
OOGA stands for Only One Good Arena. All 5 arenas odds are inversed (for example, 5:1 becomes its fraction 1/5) and the 4 from one arena are added up and gets subtracted from 1. If it's a positive, there's an arbirtrage while negative arenas have too many pirates with well enough odds to cover more likelier winners. more about arbitrage FC guide 1 FC guide 2
