Names from Earth




Welcome to Names from Earth! Here we have a large variety of names for your perusal.
Unfortunately there have been no recent additions, but rest assured that we will continue our endeavours to transcribe every existing name!

If your name or nickname is not on the list or you know of any alternative spellings for a particular name, please Neomail us!
New names will be put up ASAP. We accept all names except surnames.

Feel free to browse!

You will find all female names in the top half of the page and all male names in the lower half.
Use ctrl + f to find your name.

A huge thank you to everyone who has added names!


This page was originally a premade, however the source page has been deleted.

Listers and buttons are at the bottom of the page.
