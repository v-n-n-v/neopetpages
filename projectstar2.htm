✦ WELCOME TO PROJECT STAR ✦



Hello there! My name is Ashe and welcome to Project Star! The goal of Project Star is to draw every species/color of every neopet! You can find my collection here, all available for usage! If you see a color not drawn yet, feel free to request it to be drawn if my requests are open! If you have any questions, feel free to send me a neomail! Enjoy browsing around!

RULES
➔ If you use an adoptable, remember to link back to this page.
➔ Feel free to edit the adoptables/makeables to your hearts content. You can use them in premades, your own graphics, anything you like.
➔ You are not allowed to enter any work from this site into contests or redistribute them anywhere.



　　 FAQ
Q: Do you take custom requests?
➔ I take requests for official color/species when I'm open. I don't do "customs" (like adding wearables) or fan-made colors of the neopets.

Q: When will you be open next?
➔ I try to be open every Monday or Tuesday! If I'm not open on those days, I'm probably taking a break that week!

✦ NEOPET ADOPTABLES - CLOSED ✦



- Please look above to see if I'm open or closed. Requests sent when I'm closed will be ignored.

- Please leave credit back to this page if you request an adoptable.

- The adoptable to the left is a sample of the style I'm doing!

- I don't add clothes/hair/accessories to the pets, sorry! I only do the basic colors. (If it's a pet that COMES with clothes (ex: desert, elderly, etc) then I will add the default clothes to them. (except I dont draw stealthy clothes)

- You can request up to 2 adopts per request. You can request ANY COLOR of ANY species. (an official color). I don't do fan-made colors. Once I finish your 2 requests, you may request again! (when Im open)

- *NEW* I'm now doing invisible pets! They will have a small silhouette of the pet and be invisible.
