As seen in the news,
this is when the bands made their appearance in Neopia!

The only bands I did not find any 'news' information on is, Blue Kacheek Group, 2 Gallon Hatz and M*YNCI.
Please neomail me if you have information, or if you see any information here that is incorrect. :)


29th March 2001
TICKETS ON SALE for tonights concert with the Sticks N Stones, Tyrannias greatest Rock Band! Click here to buy one of the 80,000 tickets now!


3rd April 2001
Yes Boy Ice-Cream are the greatest new band to appear in the Tyrannian Concert Hall. They only play for one night a month, so catch them while you can!!!


8th August 2001
IMPORTANT UPDATE - Tonight's show at the Tyrannian Concert Hall has been cancelled due to a suprise appearance by the Jazz band Jazzmosis. Have you got a ticket yet?


17th June 2002
Now you can see Tyrannia's loudest and most imfamous heavy metal band live and uncut! That's right WOCK TIL YOU DROP are kicking off their world tour in their local Concert Hall - Get your tickets now!


7th August 2002
The Tyrannian Concert Hall is proud to announce the arrival of Neopia's loudest punk band - Moehawk! Get your tickets NOW before they sell out!


10th December 2002
Must See Concert!!! - Neopia's smoothest jazz musicians have decided to do a seasonal special at the Tyrannian Concert Hall. They will only be performing until the end of the month of Giving, so make sure you don't waste your tickets.


14th November 2003
There is a new band that has taken to the stage in Tyrannia. Twisted Roses are loud, angsty and if that wasn't enough to make you go and see them, they are all girls!


9th December 2003
Tyrannia's grooviest band - Chomby and the Fungus Balls have just unveiled a new smooth seasonal sound. Get your tickets while you can. UPDATE - Sorry, a few technical problems :( SCATFB will launch tomorrow, along with a whole load of new stuff (and Lenny Conundrum we promise!) Did we mention it was Donna's birthday too? :) Don't forget to pick up your free Seasonal Chomby and the Fungus Balls merchandise on your way out.


22nd January 2004
If you manage to get hold of one of these snazzy looking tickets, you could get into see the hottest classical band in Neopia - The Neopian Philharmonic!


23rd April 2004
New Band!!! Straight from Mystery Island The Hikalakas are performing live at the Tyrannian Concert Hall. If the sweet sound of tiki melodies sounds appealling you should rush out and buy tickets now!


21st May 2004
They are loud, orange and they come from Kreludor - introducing the one and only Gründo!!! Catch them at the Tyrannian Concert Hall now!


24th March 2006
New Band! - The Tyrannian Concert Hall would like to introduce Jub Zambra and the Cobrall Charmers! All the way from the Lost Desert, these guys (and gal) put on a show like no one else! Head over to the Ticket Booth if you'd like to catch a show.


14th September 2006
New Band! - The Tyrannian Concert Hall would like to introduce the Mellow Marauders! Head over to the Ticket Booth if you'd like to catch a show.
