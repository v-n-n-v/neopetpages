Welcome to the Need-A-Name Center! Do you need a new labber? Want to create the perfect name for the perfect new pet? You've come to the right place! All of the names listed in the Need-A-Name Center are available. If you're unsure whether a name is taken or not, click the link. If you get the message, "Error: Sorry, there is no Neopet by that name in Neopia," then you're ready to create your dream neopet!



Neopets can be created in the Create-A-Neopet under the "My Account" tab on the top bar. Remember, Limited Edition neopets might not always show up. Keep checking back; maybe they'll be available soon!

If you want to create a Grundo, you can find the Space Adoption Agency in the Virtupets Space Station.

Krawks can be created by taking a Krawk Petpet to the Fungus Cave on Krawk Island. Make sure you have a blank space open for your new Krawk!

Draiks can be created at the Draik Nest. Take your Draik Egg to this location and hatch it. Your brand new Draik could be either gender, so keep that in mind. Make sure you have a blank space open for your new Draik!

Now that you know where to create your new pets, why not pick out a pretty name for them? Names are listed in Alphabetical Order. If you use a name listed here, drop me a neomail and let me know! I'll remove it from the list. Please Note: With the captchas, I am unable to update frequently. This page is likely to undergo a revamp in the near future. Thank you.
