Inspiration

Participation in this theme week requires no adherence to rules. You are welcome to design your own beautiful attires and masks for this Winter's Gala, and participation in this theme week requires no specific adherence to any of the images below. Eloquently put, they are servants in your own creative endeavors.

Should you need further insight into the mysterious world of the masquerade, a list of searchable motifs and keywords has been provided: half masks, full masks, quarter masks, venetian carnival, rich, full bodied color palettes either heavily saturated with color or deep blacks on metallics, feathers, plumage, full brimmed hats, heavy accent jewelry, intricate designs or patterns, gowns and attire that purposefully obscure the wearer's face and body, harlequin patterns, stripes that add a touch of illusion to fabrics, and ornamented fabrics placed around the head and neck.
