My goal is to colorize all my favorite villain and hero images from The Gallery of Evil and The Gallery of Heroes. It's a personal project, I guess its just always been a wish of mine to see what the images in those books would look like if they were in color.

If you have any suggestions, comments, or questions please neomail me!

Feel free to use the colorized images as resources for your graphics or other projects! All I ask is that you give some form of credit and link back to this page. Example: Coloring by Tenshi
