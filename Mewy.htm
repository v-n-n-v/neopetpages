FAQ

Can the chibi be transparent?
- Yes! Simply screenshot the image (Windows + Shift + S on Windows or Shift + Command + 3 on Mac) and upload it to remove.bg. The website will remove the white background for you!
Can we edit the adoptables like changing the colors of clothes ourselves?
- Yes, feel free to edit colors or add items yourself. You must still link back even if edited by you!
Can we request items to be added to the maker?
- We don't take requests but you can Neomail us and let us know any suggestions you might have! We can't guarantee what you suggest will be added but we are always welcome to feedback!
