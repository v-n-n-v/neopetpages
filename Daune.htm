Overview

You've made it this far! Now what? This page is a summary of what Ether is about and what this guild offers!

What is Ether?

Ether is an art/writing guild with a plot theme. Our main focus to help you towards your own personal creative journey, and to foster a creative environment in which we can all push ourselves to working towards our own content. By creating artwork/writing for your own personal characters/project, you can collect artifact trophies in your portfolio treasury as well as guild points that you can redeem for prizes. There is also an option to upgrade your medallion and increase your guild rank.

If this all seems overwhelming already, fear not- all of these activities are optional and there are no activity requirements to be part of the guild. If you are looking to be a part of a creative guild just to relax and chat, we would be more than happy to have you here with us!

Portfolio

The Ether portfolio is the first step towards collecting artifacts and earning guild points. This will be your own page to keep track of your personal progress: your artifact collection and medallion. Get started with your portfolio here.

You do not need an Ether portfolio to be a part of the guild, but only members with portfolios are eligible to receive credit for artifacts and guild points.

Artifacts


Artifacts are trophies that can be earned from completing quests, missions, and participating in our special events. More information and a list of all currently obtainable artifacts can be found on the Artifacts tab.

Medallion

The Keeper's Medallion is a sacred artifact kept by every member of the collective, and those who choose to push themselves to greater heights may go on a journey to unlocking the medallion's secrets.

Build your medallion and raise your guild rank by completing medallion tasks and collecting special artifacts through a series of art/writing prompts and guild related challenges. Start your journey here.

Monthly Quests

Once a month, a faerie will present you an opportunity to prove yourself worthy of keeping their artifact. These optional quests are art/writing prompts you can do in order to collect monthly exclusive artifacts. You will find the current faerie quest here.

Everyone is welcome to participate, but you will need an Ether portfolio in order to receive artifacts/guild credit.

Ether: Hero's Story

The Hero's Story is your character's personal journey into the world of Ether. Complete missions in order to collect story exclusive artifacts.

You will need to be an Ether portfolio in order to be eligible for artifacts/guild credit.

Page Spotlight

Let us celebrate your hard work! Keepers may submit their pages to be randomly featured in the guild front page for 1 week. Click here to submit your page!

The Keeper's Library

The Keeper's Library is a hub of resources gathered for the collective, including: writing and art tutorials/processes generously contributed by our members, a compiled list of collected pages for your lookup and petpage needs, and a Codex full of information of Ether's faeries and artifacts, and last but not the least our Archives; a gallery of illustrations created by our members from Ether's past events.

Special Events

Special events are hosted throughout the year for a chance to collect artifacts and get into the guild spirit, such Art Exchanges and BC Weeks, as well as Annual Character Bootcamp.

Keep an eye out for special event announcements through our bulletin, which is updated at the first of every month.

Contribution

Ether members can design faeries and artifacts to be featured for our Monthly Faerie Quests. Guild contribution is one way to gain guild points and artifacts, you can find more information here!

At the end of the day, Ether really is what you to choose to make out of it! You can participate as much or as little as you'd like, we are here for whenever you have the time and schedule. This guild is created to be of service to anyone who has an interest in creative content and those seeking to develop their pet/original characters!

Come let us be a part of your creative journey and join today!

Ether's Mission

To foster a safe and positive environment where we can all push ourselves to working towards our own personal creative journey.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Please be respectful of your fellow Keepers! First and foremost, we want this guild to help support and motivate you in progressing your own personal creative journey. It is our responsibility to work together to create a safe, positive, and supportive environment for everyone.
  • Please take any personal conflicts through neomails. Anyone found to be disrespectful or demonstrating harmful and negative behavior towards others may be subject to being banned from the guild.
  • Guild participation and activity is optional. We understand that not everyone has the time to participate in guild challenges. We will not remove anyone for inactivity. We'd love to have you join us whenever your time or schedule allows you to!
  • While you do not need an Ether portfolio to participate in guild events, one is required in order to receive credit for guild points and artifact collection. You must send in any type of submission to the proper pages/inboxes in order to receive credit!
  • Side accounts are more than welcome to join! However, please note that side accounts cannot redeem their guild points for any type of NP related rewards from Ether.
  • Ether reserves the right to make changes to the guidelines, guild events, or remove memberships in order to stay faithful to our guild standard. We will not tolerate any kind of harmful, discriminatory, or offensive behavior.

If you acknowledge and accept the rules and guidelines above, and would like to join, please send a message to any of our council members or to the inbox below to request an invite:

Artifacts

Artifacts are guild trophies that are acquired from participating in Ether events/activities. They can be displayed in your Ether portfolio. While all members are welcome to participate in guild events/tasks, they must have a guild portfolio in order to be eligible for artifacts.

Each standard artifact collected is worth 500 Guild Points (The exception being the Studio artifacts and familiars, which have tiers that reward higher points). Guild Points can be redeemed for special rewards in Nadia's Inn.

Hover over the Artifacts tab from the navigation menu to see a complete list of released artifacts and the technical corresponding tasks to collect them.

ARTIFACT GUIDELINES

  • An Ether portfolio is required to collect artifacts and receive guild credit/points. Artifacts can only be claimed by those who have submitted their portfolio to the member directory.
  • You must send in your submission for artifacts in order to receive guild credit! This is vital to keeping track of your guild points. All event pages have the proper forms to submit your submission and the right mailbox to send it.
  • Artifacts may only be added to your treasury upon acceptance of your submission. Your guild point count, nor your guild rank will not update until your submission for the proper artifact(s) has been formally accepted by a council member.
  • Please read the submission guidelines to make sure you are following the art/writing requirements for the artifact you are claiming. Changes to your submission may be requested if it does not fit requirements.

Guild Contribution

Designing Faeries and Artifacts is one way to contribute to the guild! Submissions will be saved and featured in the Monthly Faerie Quest and other future events. Please note that artifacts designed by guild members will not be available to be claimed until they are formally released and their corresponding tasks are completed per the set requirements - this applies to the submitter, as well.

Please read the guidelines for faerie/artifact submissions below.

Faerie Submission Guidelines

  • Submissions must be 100% illustrated and written by you.
  • Faeries cannot be an existing character (ex. an illustration of your OC as a faerie).
  • Faeries do not need to be human/female but they must have wings. Feel free to design your faerie outside of the existing faerie elements (ex. Diamond Faerie, Hexed Faerie).
  • Faerie images must by transparent and resized from 400-600px height/width. Please include a full size and resized version (if original size is larger than 400-600px).
  • Please make sure the image is neo-friendly.
  • Art style does not need to be similar to existing faerie artwork.
  • Please include name and a brief description of the faerie (2 sentences minimum).
  • You have the option of writing an introduction for your Faerie to be featured for our Monthly Faerie Quests. You may check here for examples of our previous quest introductions for inspiration. Otherwise, a council member will be assigned to write an introduction for you.
  • You may submit more than one submission. Upon acceptance of your first submission you will receive the Kadoatie Statue artifact and 500 guild points. Future submissions will receive 500 guild points each.

Send your submission using using this form:

Participate in Guild Contribution by drawing and designing a Faerie NPC (to be featured on the Monthly Faerie Quest).

Artifact Submission Guidelines

  • Submissions must be 100% illustrated and written by you.
  • Artifacts can be any item you'd imagine in a fantasy rpg game, such as trinkets, weapons, jewelry, statues, familiars, etc.
  • Artifacts cannot be of an existing original character. (ex. An image/statue of your character) They can be trinkets inspired by an official neopets character. (ex. Vira's mirror, Nabile's ring)
  • Please make sure the image is neo-friendly.
  • Artifacts must be transparent and will be resized to 150x150px. Please include a full size and resized version (if original size is larger than 150x150px).
  • Art style does not need to be similar to current faeries/artifacts.
  • You have the option of writing an description for your artifact to be featured for our Artifact Codex. You may check here for examples of our artifact descriptions. Otherwise, a council member will be assigned to write an introduction for you.
  • You may submit more than one submission. Upon acceptance of your first submission you will receive the Lotus Potion artifact and 500 guild points. Future submissions will receive 500 guild points each.

COUNCIL POSITION

ETHER BOARD MODERATORS - APPLICATIONS Closed

Applications for council positions for Ether are Closed! We are currently looking for 1-2 sub-council members who can take on the role of Board Moderator Responsibility, in order to keep the boards regulated and active. Board mods will play a huge role in shaping a safe and positive environment for Ether.

We are looking for members who:

  • Have a positive and friendly attitude to help our members feel welcome!
  • Can engage with Ether members and assist with questions and clarifications about guild events/prompts, as well as directing members to the proper pages.
  • Keep an eye out for harmful messages in the guild that need to be deleted/require corrective action.
  • Are very active, reachable through neomail and can respond at a reasonable timeframe (ideally no later than a week)
  • Able to attend meetings to stay updated with future Ether updates/events.
  • Run recruitment boards when needed.

While a board mod's main responsibility is keeping the boards active and regulated, they will also have opportunities/may be invited for the following:

  • Assist with brainstorming ideas for future Ether events/prompts as well as the direction of the guild. (Through bi-weekly council meetings/one-on-one discussions)
  • Assist with hosting events and running pages.
  • Assist with creating content for the guild.

APPLICATION FORM

Please answer the following questions below. You can be as brief or go in depth as you like - if the mail exceeds over 1 neomail please paste it on a petpage!

  • Please write a brief description about yourself! What are your hobbies and goals?
  • How active are you usually on neopets?
  • Do you feel you can take on the responsibilities/expectations asked of a board mod?
  • Are willing to dedicate time and effort to Ether's mission?
