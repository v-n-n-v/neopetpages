    What is a stuck pet?

    Stuck pets are those that are in the pound but won't show up while "pound surfing".
    Pound surfing is when you go to the pound and "view more neopets" to see all pets that are available for adoption.



    However, the pound only shows you a fraction of the pets actually in the pound! All the hidden ones are called "stuck pets". You cannot find them by simply browsing the pound.

    Pets get "stuck" depending on what their name starts with! These are called "stuck strings" and are the first three letters of a pets name.

    The only way to find these pets is to manually search for their name. You can do this by manually typing in every name you can think of - or use the tileset method! That looks a little like this -



    Essentially, these pets are totally hidden until somebody goes and finds them!

    Pound surfing is still a great way to find awesome pets, it's just everybody else on neopets can see pets in the pound at the same time making it harder for you to get them. You can read more about stuck pets here

    What are stuck strings?

    Stuck strings are the first 3 letters of a pets name.

    You can find all the possible stuck strings here.
    Essentially, if ANG is a stuck string, then ANH, ANI etc will also be stuck too!

    Something ~~super important~~ to remember is that stuck strings are always moving which means some strings will eventually become unstuck (meaning you will be able to see them by regular pound surfing!) For example, one day the word "angel" will be stuck but the next day it might not be!

    This can be confusing if you are making your lists to search with, or if you are hoping to move pets around on side accounts hoping they stay hidden from other users. You can always double check on the Lost and Pound board who are in the Pound Chat.
    We look like this!


    The first page in the Lost and Pound thread will have an updated list of what the stuck strings are, but you can always ask in the boards and somebody can double check for you!

    If you didn't know, the Lost and Pound is a huge stuck pet database run by volunteers and is a certified Neopets Fansite! You can find a link here .

    Now for the fun part, you want to find these pets yourself! The most known method is the "tileset" method! I'm going to refer you to two guides here.

    Now, once you have your spreadsheets ready to go, there are two ways you can use them. I'm sure there are actually more but these are the most known. Most stuck guides will show you how to search your sheets using your petpages. This is really easy to do, and is explained in the above two guides!

    Using a browser instead of petpages


    Another way , and what I recommend, is using your own browser to search for pets! I do this for two reasons : you are not restricted to working around the neopets filters by searching through petpages (there are words that are blocked that are perfectly fine to have in pet names) and I find it's much faster. I also accidently close the pet pages *all the time* so this helps for me.

    Making your lists

    The next part of this guide is meant to be used in conjunction with ~megablade or ~_UM, the stuck pet searching guides. It is recommended that you read those pages, and ideally you've got your formulas ready to go and are now working on making your lists!

    There are two types of lists on this petpage: PREFIXES and SUFFIXES

    The PREFIX lists are real names / real words / short prefixes that can get stuck in the pound.

    The SUFFIX lists are just a bunch of random word endings - some real words, some short gibberish that looks nice (it will make sense as you keep searching!)

    If you're following megablade, you'll put Prefix names in the "b" column— and suffix words in the "c" column.

    Keep in mind that you are not restricted to searching any kind of way, and if you're just starting out, you'll eventually find your own groove and searching method.

    All of these lists are petpage-friendly so you definitely want to keep adding on to your own! I also HIGHLY recommend building your lists using word association tools! Especially if you love compound words, certain themes, or are simply just stuck for words/inspiration.

    Pound release.

    NOTE:

    As of 2022, you can't see if pets are on briek5 or pound_release anymore. Instead, it will show pets on vaction OR "Error: Pets could not be loaded. Please reload the page ". I'll leave the next bit in, incase it gets fixed and goes back to normal:

    You can also use tilesetting to find pets that are stuck on pound release or breik_u5. These are again pets that aren't owned by anybody but are stuck on TNTs own accounts until they are released back into the pound.
    Using the tilesetting method as i do, i search for pets directly in the pound. But trying to find these other hidden pets requires you to check the pets petpage to see who owns them.

    Dream pets

    Unfortnately, you cannot search for pets just for their color, or battledome stats or anything like that. Through the Lost and Pound website, you can search through the database based on pet species. However, if you were looking for a certian colour your best bet is to list your dreamies on pet pages below. Alternatively, it's a good idea to keep an eye on the stuck pet threads on the Pound Chat because you might have luck snagging something amazing that way! Don't forget regular pound surfing can also find you gold!




    If you were looking for some kind of theme or name that have certian words you like, that is entirely possible using the tileset method when you're making your own lists!
