Getting a Drugal

Getting a Drugal is not terribly difficult. They can be found in four places in Neopia.

  • Test Your Strength
  • User Shops
  • Trading Post
  • Auction House

It is unknown at this tme exactly what score you ned to reach in order to win a drugal from Test Your Strength.

Drugal prices in user shops fluctuate between 2000 nps and 8000 nps. (as of the launch date of this page, they are currently priced under 3000 nps)

Drugals typically found at the Trading Post are painted and can be immensely more expensive. Prices of painted drugals are entirely dependant on petpet paint brush prices.

In order to paint your drugal, you will of course need to give it to your pet and take it to the Petpet Puddle with an appropriate petpet paint brush in your inventory.

And that's it! That's all you need to do in order to get your very own Drugal!

Caring for your Drugal

Caring for drugals is not difficult. But it does take a little special know how.

Caring for Drugals 101

  • The first thing you should know about caring for your new drugal is that it needs regular brushing. Drugal coats get very tangled and frizzy and pick up all sorts of dirt and debris. Brush their coats at least twice a day to keep them looking their best.
  • Because of the length and thickness of a drugal's coat, regular bathing is required. Two or three times a week will be sufficient. Use a gentle shampoo and condition once a week. You can find specialty shampoos and conditioners at your local Petpet Supply Store.
  • Drugals can be very social creatures. They enjoy playing with other petpets and especially with their owners. Please be sure to give your new drugal plenty of activity.
  • Drugals, when scared or over-stimulated, need a place to hide. They prefer soft, covered, quiet places in a corner of your neohome. A Deluxe Canopy Petpet Bed, Disco Petpet Bed, or Pink Domed Petpet Bed are perfect for a drugal to relax in.
  • Drugals are not particularly finicky when it comes to food. However, they really do not like slimy or crumbly food as it gets in their fur easily.
  • They do love treats though! A drugal will learn all manner of tricks just to get treats.
  • Drugals love to run about the house and chase bouncy and wind up toys. They are not fond of tug-of-war games as they tend to trip over their trailing coats.
  • Drugals love to be petted and cuddled. They will sit in your lap all day long.
That's about all you need to know about caring for drugals. Enjoy your new drugal. If you care for them properly, they will reward you with their love forever!
