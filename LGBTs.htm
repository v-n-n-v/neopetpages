History

the pound chat uprising


  The story began on December 17 Y22. The Pound Chat was abuzz with activity, including a new and exciting event: Wifemas (male). This event was focused on adopting out UCs in cool and exciting ways every night. One of the hosts received negative attention for mentioning their same-gendered partner. They were silenced by TNT.
  Even still, their partner decided to keep the event going and spirits high. Neopians flocked to the Pound Chat in support, protesting by using rainbow-coloured signatures despite multiple warnings. A powerful petpage resource was spread, providing neosignatures and more. Another user gave out Neoboard Pens to anyone interested. The boards, and social media, exploded in an uproar against TNT. 		  On December 18 Y22, an exciting Editorial was published in the Neopian Times. It declared that certain terms and topics would no longer be banned. Neopians proudly and freely identified themselves as LGBT. Additionally, names which were previously flagged as inappropriate were released into the Pound. Today, Neopians are free to express how they choose, and that's because they chose to come together.

❝Neopets should always be a safe place to express yourself, regardless of your identity or orientation, and many of the filters we had in place in the past did not allow for that expression.❞ - TNT
