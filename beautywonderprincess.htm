Introduction


ʽʽWhat can I do for you today?ʼʼ ~ Marina


You may have heard her say this when you visit the Healing Springs! She's always trying to help others, without asking for anything in return (except a few books if you stumble upon her outside of the Healing Springs, but even then she will reward you with more!).

This site is dedicated to Nereid and Marina, the water faeries! There are other water faeries in Neopia as well: The Drenched and Naia.

For now we will talk about the greatness that is both Nereid and Marina, the Water Faeries! I hope you enjoy and love them, as you find out more about then, like I do! Enjoy.

About Faeries


Faeries are tiny magical creatures that live in Neopia. Meeting one is said to be very lucky indeed, as they can bless your pets with all sorts of powers and abilities. A pet must be strong enough to control the faeries spells though, so if your pet is inexperienced then the faerie may just fly away. There are many different types of faeries with differing powers, abilities and temperaments.

Background


According to this page, Water Faeries swim in the rivers and streams, and are unseen by most. They can be heard singing quietly at night, but only if they aren't aware that you are near. They specialise in healing and defensive powers that will protect your pet from harm.

Everyone might think that there's just one Water Faerie, but that's actually not the case.

Earlier I mentioned the Drenched Sisters and the Fountain Faerie. While I won't talk much about them, here are some pictures for you to see if they jog any memory in case you stumbled upon them while exploring Neopia!

　　　


The first image is of Nereid and the second is of Marina!
The third is of Naia, the Fountain Faerie!
The fourth features three Dark Water Faeries, The Drenched!



About Nereid: Nereid is an elusive faerie who prefers to remain hidden. If you go sailing off the coast of Mystery Island you can sometimes hear her singing softly at night.

Quote on her TCG card: Three hundred years under the water, and not a wrinkle in sight!


About Marina: She runs the Healing Springs in Faerieland! In old pictures, Marina was depicted as sitting on a rock, on land (just like now).


Naia and the Drenched Sisters: Quick information so you can know the differences and similarities!

Unlike Nereid and Marina, only Naia controls the Rainbow Fountain located in Faerieland. She can change your chosen pet to any colour available in Neopia except 8-bit, Ice, Magma, MSP, Robot, Royal, Sponge, Quiguki and Usuki.

As for the Drenched, unlike Nereid, Marina, and Naia, they do not help your pets. In fact, ''Like the deadly sirens of Greek mythology, the Drenched swim along the waves waiting for the unlucky sailor to be trapped within their grasps. Little of their personalities were revealed during the Maraquan plot, but according to their individual plushies, each faerie increases in evil with her tail color. ''



















Healing


If your Neopet is wounded or ill, the Water Faerie (more specifically, Marina) can help heal your Neopet back to perfect health!

But where can she perform such sorcery? Why, here in the Healing Springs, good fellow Neopian! ;)

The Healing Springs is located in Faerie Land.

You have to wait every 30 minutes though once you ask her to heal you for free! That's right, for free. However, if you can't wait half an hour, she has a little store where you can buy potions to heal your pet yourself.

Quests


Wisest of the faeries, Water Faeries guard the ancient secrets of magic, which has given them a reputation for haughtiness. Because they prize all knowledge, they will ask you for books, and if you help them, the Water Faeries will toughen your defences in battle.




Sooner or later, the Water Faerie will ask for your help! She will ask you to bring her a book. Isn't she so admirable? Knowledge is power!

As a reward, she will increase your active's pet defense up by 2 whole points!
