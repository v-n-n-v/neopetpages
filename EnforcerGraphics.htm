Art and Graphics

*80s crime show announcer voice* One's a classically-trained, by the book Neovian constable. The other joined the squad because they wanted to fulfill their fantasies. Together, they fight crime and keep the Enforcer's domain safe.

Graphix is...a character. In the sense that they have a unique and bombastic personality and the sense of applying storytelling tropes to themself. They're the kid who will yell at you in languages they don't understand and try to run through the school halls like a ninja. Now they've grown up and gleefully joined one of the Enforcement squads in hopes of being able to fight monsters and bad guys like a real hero. They even get to wear goggles that don't do anything and carry around a giant sword!

Art is their supervisor and is hoping to keep them from getting themself kil1ed long enough that they realize this isn't a game and there are real stakes. Thus far, it hasn't been very effective. He's also tried to get them to put down the giant, glowing sword before they put someone's eye out. They refuse because they have the sword for ~gender affirmation~.
