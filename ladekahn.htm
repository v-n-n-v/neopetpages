1337 boards 1337 means "lEET", from "leet," from "elite," and now means a superior gamer, with connotations of intelligence and daring. It was originally used by computer hackers, when early messaging programs wouldn't allow certain words, phrases, and letter combinations. A whole alphabet and slang language grew out of this, including using numbers to represent letters and words. No, not chat speak; chat speak is lazy.

AA boards Adoption Agency. They collect painted or well-named pets from the pound and then turn around and "offer" them for adoption. Usually require applications and other hoops.

CATFB Chomby and the Fungus Balls. A Neopian band.
Codestones Rocks with symbols carved on them. Regular (tan) codestones are used as payment in the Mystery Island Training School. Red codestones are used as payment in the Secret Ninja Academy. You can get codestones from the Tombola or Deserted Tomb on Mystery Island or in random events; you can also buy them from other players. You can get Red Codestones by throwing a full set of regular codestones in to the volcano.
Collateral trades/lending Also, "Collat". When you want to borrow an item for an avatar, people will usually ask for collateral. Collateral is items or NPs worth the same as the item you're borrowing. This way, if you don't return the item, the person doesn't lose all the NPs they'd invested in the item.
Cookies safety These are set on your browser, and they keep track of your username and p/w. That's how you can see every page on Neopets. Many off-site scams will target your cookies because that gives them access to your account. cookieDefender

Galleries Galleries are their own thing now. You cannot keep items in your Shop "just for display." Most items that people keep in their galleries are very expensive. Asking to buy those items, unless the person has said they would be willing to sell, is very rude. Galleries are full of items that someone is collecting.
Guide A petpage that helps you with something (like mine!).
Guild A club on Neopets. You can hang out with your friends there. (Note: Guild and Guide are not the same word. Please stop mixing them up.)
GUP boards Green Uni Project. They adopt painted/LE pets from the pound, morph them to green unis, and then abandon them again. There are two types who participate: GUPpies (see next entry) and the original, true GUPers. The GUPers do it because they believe everyone can and should earn their own pets and not have one handed to them from the Pound.
GUPpies my word Spammers and attention-seekers who claim to be a part of the GUP but who do it for the infamy. The Guppies are the ones who create boards about "Watch me GUP this pet".

Hacked No, you were not hacked. Hacking is not breaking in to one measly account; hacking is infiltrating entire systems. For your account to be hacked, Neopets would also have to be hacked; since they haven't been, you haven't been, either. You may, however, have been cracked or scammed. If someone guessed your p/w, you were cracked. Next time, make it much more difficult; in fact, don't even make it a real word. Add numbers and random capital letters to confuse even yourself. NoYouWereNotHacked
Haggle trades Neomail them with your offer, and they'll consider it. They may ask you to go higher, and you can ask them to go lower. Just remember to be nice and polite and to make "real" (that is, close to the asking price) offers.
HTS trades Hard to Sell. In Trades. HTS items include TCGs.
HP boards Hit Points. To increase your max HP (the bottom number), train in Endurance at the training schools, complete random Dark Faerie quests, or use the Lab Ray.
HP battledome Honey Potion. Battledome Item. Prize for Illusen's Quest #35.

Petpets You can give your pet a petpet to play with. Choose the petpet from your inventory and, on the resulting pop-up, choose "Give to (your pet's name)".
Petpet Lab Ray In order to use the Petpet Laboratory, you first have to collect all 9 pieces of the Secret Laboratory Map. Then when you take all 9 pieces of the Petpet Laboratory Map, click on the Petpet link. The Petpet Lab Ray can only zap petpets that are attached to your pets. When the zapped petpet is removed, it reverts back to what it was when it was first attached. If you get a Krawk petpet from the PPLR, no, you cannot change it into a regular Krawk pet.
Petpet Painting Not only can your pets be painted different colors, but their petpets can be painted to match. Petpet paint brushes can be used only on petpets. To paint them, visit the Rainbow Pool, where you'll find the Petpet Puddle. You can paint your petpets here.
Petpetpet You can give a petpet its own pet, called a petpetpet. Put the petpetpet in your Inventory and be sure that the petpet you want it on is on your Active pet. Now, start refreshing your Inventory. This might take a looong time. Once the petpetpet finally attaches, you can't unequip that petpet or the petpetpet will disappear.
PPL Petpet Protection League. TNT picks a random petpet species and color and gives out awards based on how long the petpet has been attached to a pet. Only a certain number of users will win the prize, however.
Premium Neopets that you pay for. You need an invitation from a current member to join. Has lots of features including no ads, the Super Shop Wizard, and more. See Leto's petpage for more information. NeoMail Al if you need an invitation. Leto
Pure trades Pure means they want the whole price in NPs. Offer a junk item (less than 10 NPs) and all the NPs.
Pwn Pwn means "own", which means to dominate or be better than or to totally rock. "Own" has been gaming slang for years. "Pwn" came around after a Warcraft user manual misspelled own; the 1337 game players started using the misspelling, instead.

Rate boards Anything can be rated these days, it seems. The usual reason for wanting a rating is to get a warm fuzzy feeling that someone else thinks they're valuable and talented. It's usually just a form a attention-seeking spam.
Restock Also "RS". Buying items from the main shops or user shops and reselling them for more than you paid. Mavrodaphne
Roleplay Also "RP". In roleplaying, you make up a character (on Neopets, one of your pets) and give it every characteristic you can think of: hair/eye color, age/gender, opinions on world peace/ broccoli/ Jhudora, whatever. Basically, you need to know how your character will respond in any given situation. Then, you find other people to RP with. Someone makes up a story situation, and then everyone posts as their character, responding both to the original situation and to the situations that grow out of other users' posts.
Rot trades In "Junk offers will rot". They will neither accept nor refuse if you offer worthless items. Your bid will sit there until you withdraw it or the lot times out.
RS Restock.
RS Help & Chat boards A post title typically found on the Help Board. They restock, announce restock times, post their best results, and hang around and chat.

Separate trades If their lot contains lots of expensive items, often they'll let you neoMail them and offer on just one item. If they like that offer enough, they'll take down the current lot and make a new lot just for you with that single item.

Some people will have the option to only accept items from NeoFriends, however, and for those, you're out of luck. Also, if someone already has 50 items in the Inventory, you can't send another item to them.
