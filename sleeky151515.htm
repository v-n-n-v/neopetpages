About Coltzan's Shrine

(Back to top)
The shrine can be found in the Lost Desert and is one of the more mysterious structures in Neopia. Built by a curious race of beings called TNT, the Shrine has proven in the past to grant gifts of high value including Dubloons, Neopoints and also some lower value things like, well, nothing.


The aim of this project is to find the optimum time to visit the Shrine so that you, fellow Neopian, can be granted the best of gifts with the least amount of effort. But I cant do it alone, I need you to help me and along with others, we will find this time.

Coltzan's myths

(Back to top)

Coltzan's myths is used for finding out whether a particular myth is true or not. Got a myth? Neomail me here telling me what it is.

Myth: Based on X results Status
Wheel of knowledge: Go to the Shrine at exactly 55 seconds for a Dubloon. Send results! Busted!

How myths are defined

Confirmed = The myth has been proven to be correct.
Inconclusive = The myth has neither been proven right or wrong.
Busted = The myth has been proven to be incorrect.
Unknown = The myth is still being researched.
