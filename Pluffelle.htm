Last Updated: November 4, 2022



Thanks for taking the time to stop by! Welcome to Pastel Palette, a place where people from all over Neopia can come and show off their beloved Pastel pets. Pastel is such a pretty colour, don't you think?



Do you have a Pastel pet you'd like to add to this directory? Neomail me the NAME and SPECIES of your pet using the mail button above. The only requirements are for your Pastel painted pet to be permanent, and to be wearing at least a background. Please note that if your pet loses its background and it remains without one for more than a week, your pet may be removed.
