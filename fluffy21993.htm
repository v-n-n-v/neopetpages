Creative Roots

A Name Game
BN = Badly Named and DN = Decently Named

I knew that when trading for an UC the name didn't really matter. Very few BNs wouldn't have been worth it. I was inspired by a petpage about someone who collects BN UCs because the BNs are fun and nostalgic. My perspective was completely changed. These names are a part of Neopets history and references all the children who couldn't come up with unique names and settled for the suggested numbers to be added on. This is why a BN with numbers is perfect for a Y2K pet. Their nickname F1UFF includes leetspeak, a popular trend in the 2000s. Normally I'm really picky about names, they have to be DN or funny, but this is my one exception at the time of writing.

Sentimental

I was obsessed with Neopets from ages 10 to 13 and I played it A TON. I started a few months before the conversion. When it happened, I had no idea how to keep my pets from converting. Back then I was obsessed with making petpages. I had a link directory and a collection of Team Maraqua graphics from others. I learned a little bit of coding from The Neopets HTML Guide and really enjoyed it. I remember how good it felt the first time I got an image to be clickable. As an adult I often create references for myself to my childhood days on Neo. I love digging through petpages made in the early 2000s and seeing how different and quirky they look. Neopets's history is also my passion. F1UFF is a tribute to all of this!

Prompt Response

Questions from SNOWFLAKELE

- what kind of errands does F1UFF run for sloth? do they enjoy it?
F1UFF visits Kreludor to smuggle food and weapons to Dr. Sloth's space stations. They feel neutral about it. F1UFF thinks of it as just another job, just like any other.

- why does F1UFF not like tyrannia? Did something happen to them?
F1UFF cannot understand how any Neopians could live without technology. Nothing happened, they just have a completely different culture from Tyrannia.

- how would an ideal afternoon work for F1UFF?
F1UFF likes to visit the Virtupets Space Station to buy their own weapons. They also like to try their luck at the Lever Of Doom and the Virtuvend 2000 MAX. The rest of the afternoon is best spent in their space ship playing video games.

- Since F1UFF can eat food, do they have a favorite? what sort of food does F1UFF prefer, is eating a big deal to them? have they tried to cook there own before? how did it go?
Their favorite food is the Space Slushie. They like foods based on Noils since it reminds them of their petpet, which helps when they can't spend time together. Sometimes their belly screen doesn't work. Eating is not a big deal to F1UFF since they do not require it to function. They once tried to microwave an Astronaut Meal only for the microwave to accidentally short-circuit and break.
