Introduction

Over the years, The Neopets Team (TNT) have been releasing Stamps for us all to collect. This page is dedicated to these released Stamps. Currently there are 598 stamps total and 29 page albums including all of the above in the Navigation. To visit your own stamp album you must click on your userlookup followed by clicking on the icon that looks like a stamp as shown below:
There, you will be directed to your own stamp collection. The High-Score Table can be located there as well as the Post Office Kiosk.

The Post Office Kiosk is the shop that sells stamps ranged from rarity 1 to 100. If the rarity of a stamp is higher than 100 then it would have been released through a special event that occurred on Neopets in the past. Information is provided in each section on how these stamps were initially released so you can gain an understanding on how rare a particular stamp is. To obtain these stamps which are no longer available to restock you must purchase them off the Shop Wizard, Auctions or Trading Post as linked in the navigation.

Although some higher rarity stamps are no where to be seen! This question and answer from the Editorial may answer your question(s):

Will you be re-releasing some of the older stamps? Many of the rarer ones from early albums are almost unavailable, or retailing for tens of millions of Neopoints.

We've actually discussed this several times before, and we are considering a couple of options that would help reduce the price of the item while also not horribly damaging how rare and difficult they are to acquire.

The good news is that TNT has kept to their word and introduced Dr. Sloth Auctions which is explained further throughout this guide.

Adding Stamps to your Album

Once you have purchased or restocked a stamp it will be added to your inventory. To access your inventory you mist either visit the Main Shops and click on the first link on the second row in the Toolbar, or click on your Neopoints in the top-right hand corner of any page. This is next to "logout" and "the_freebie_man_." Now you should be at your inventory. Your inventory is a display or list of all of the items you've found, earned or purchased through Neopia that have not yet been stocked, deposited, donated, discarded, added for display in your gallery or added to your closet or storage shed.

Within your inventory, to add a stamp to your album you need to click on the image of the stamp. If the item was placed in your inventory recently, then it should be located toward the bottom of the page. Once you've clicked on the image, wait for a pop-up window to appear. Underneath the Estimate Value there is a long bar which displays Choose an Action. Click on this and select Put in Stamp Album and submit.

The stamp should now be added to your stamp album. Now, you need to either view your album or refresh the current page.

Remember, once a Stamp has been added to your album, it cannot be taken out!

The Tarla'd

Who is Tarla?

Tarla is a Red Ixi who lives atop Terror Mountain to manage the Shop of Mystery. As its name suggests, the Shop of Mystery sells random items using "Mystery Item" bags. If you purchase one of these bags you will obtain a random item from a set pool which may or may not be worthwhile.

But Tarla is not renowned for being the shopkeeper for the Shop of Mystery but instead for handing out free items using the Neopets Toolbar.

The Neopets Toolbar can only function properly in Internet Explorer or versions higher than Mozilla Firefox 2.0. On the far right-hand side you'll notice an "Alert" button. If this button flashes Yellow repetitively you should click on it as quickly as possible. If you make it in time, you will be able to obtain a free prize by clicking on the link provided. The link does change every time Tarla visits. This means that there is no way of being able to cheat by using the same link provided in case you missed a giveaway.

About Tarla:

If you click on the image of Tarla, you'll be taken to her Neopedia Article.

When Tarla arrives on Neopets she gives you one set main prize which is the same for all users for that particular day. The main prize is usually available for three minutes or less. If you miss out on the Main Prize then you will be awarded with other various items called droppings.

Tarla does not come everyday but rather at random, meaning she could come twice a week, take a long break and not return for a month or so.

You do not have to download the Neopets Toolbar to be eligible to access these free prizes. If you do not have the Neopets Toolbar and you would like these prizes, I suggest lurking on the Help Chat as the people there are usually aware of Tarla and post rather quickly. When Tarla does come on a certain day she will continue awarding these prizes until the clock hits 12:00 am NST again. Tarla awards these prizes for approximately five minutes. You are only able to claim one prize per day.

The Prizes:

The main prize usually deflates (drops in price) more than the droppings as the main prize is awarded more commonly than the droppings in comparison. When an item has been Tarla'd and you missed out on it, it is best to purchase the item a day after. The items tend to inflate after a set period of time if it is not given out again and this is especially true if the giveaway involved a Collector's item. These include items such as: stamps, coins, sea shells, charms, books, scrolls, paint brushes and laboratory map pieces.
