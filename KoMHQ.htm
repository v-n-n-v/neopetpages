About Us


Welcome to the Knights of Meridell Head Quarters! The Knights of Meridell is a public chat group originating from the Altador Cup I. The chat group publicly supports the team of Meridell and is run year-round. It was also founded by Grand Master Mystic I. It is the oldest active Team Meridell support group.

Customs


Titles
In the Knights of Meridell, titles are used. If it's your first time at the Knights of Meridell, please make yourself known and please mention your name! Usually, regular visitors to the Knights of Meridell have their title in their siggy, so be on the look out! Titles are as follows:

  • Sir/Lady - everyone who is not a member of the Knights of Meridell or at the rank of a Knight or Champion in the Order.
  • Duke/Duchess - those who are members of the Order and at the rank of Duke.
  • Archduke/Archduchess - those who are members of the Order and at the rank of Archduke.
  • Count/Countess - those who are members of the Order and at the rank of Count.
  • Master/Mastress - those who are members of the Council of Masters.
  • Grand Master - the leader of the Knights of Meridell.
Please note, that there may be extraordinary titles accepted in the Knights of Meridell depending on joint-agreements, treaties, and accords between other groups.

Hail Skarl
Whenever someone posts "Hail Skarl" on the board it is customary to reply with the same thing. It's a generic Meridellian slogan. Feel free to post it on your next visit!

Greeting the Grand Master
If you enter/leave the Knights of Meridell public board in the presence of the Grand Master or if the Grand Master enters/leaves the Knights of Meridell public board in your presence then there is a specific greeting procedure you should follow. Even though the Grand Master will not mention your greeting it is EXPECTED.

One should *bow* or *curtsy* before the Grand Master and greet him.

Example: *bows to Grand Master* Greetings Grand Master!

Principles

The Throne of the Grand Master
The Grand Master is the sovereign of the Knights of Meridell. They hold absolute authority over the Knights of Meridell and all of her assets. The Grand Master also has the final word in regards to any possible treaties, changes, etc. that may affect the Knights of Meridell. The Grand Master's reign can only be terminated if their account is frozen (reclaiming the Throne is possible) or upon resignation.

The Council of Masters
The Council of Masters is a group of ten members that are the backbone of the Knights of Meridell. The head of the Council is the Grand Master. The remaining nine Masters are appointed by the Grand Master. Masters are known as the Guardians of the Knights of Meridell. Some duties of the Masters are to regulate the Order and the Knights of Meridell board, as well as, participating in important events (councils, assemblies, etc.) regarding policy and diplomacy of the Knights of Meridell. The most important feature of the Council of Masters is that they are the ones who vote for a new Grand Master when needed! Below of members of the current Council of Masters:

The Order of the Knights of Meridell

The Order of the Knights of Meridell is the official Knights of Meridell guild. It was founded at the start of the Altador Cup II on June 1st, 2007.

It is private and invitations are only given out upon request. Please be active at our public board for a while so we can confirm that you are a person of good stature.

Well known visitors have a higher chance of gaining acceptance in the Order.
