in real life

Hi there! My name is Ani--I have a different name IRL, but I've been known by "Ani" for so long on here that I decided to stick with it. I am a queer lesbian and she/her/hers are my pronouns! I am currently in my 30s (yikes) and hold a BFA in illustration and print making and a M.Ed. in higher education. I currently live in the South in the USA with my wife and doggo and my work has nothing to due with either of my degrees anymore lol.

I love comic books and reading alternative & indie zines and comics. I have gotten into hiking recently and have been doing more of it since the pandemic started. I like things like Lord of the Rings, Harry Potter (but not JKR), Marvel Comics, Animal Crossing, and Pokemon.

about me on neo

I have been on Neo for like.. forever. I have a few of my original accounts from when I first started playing so they are all really, really, old. I started drawing my pets a few years after joining when I saw some awesome Ixi with custom designs. I wanted to be just like them, so I started drawing my neopets! A few of those pets are still with my on these accounts today. As I grow so do their stories and characters! You can find a lot more about my pets in the next few sections. test

On Neo I really like creating and developing my pet's characters. My accounts are getting pretty full, but there's a lot still left to do! I like drawing and creating petpages for my pets. One of my favorite things to do is to enter the Beauty Contest. You can find me on the Art Chat, Pound Chat, and sometimes the NC Chat. I have a Beauty Contest Guide and run NeoBadges, a achievement reward button page. I love making things for the neo-community to use and hope to have more resources in the future! I was also picked by TNT to be an original beta tester for the new site design, which was very cool!

do you do all your own coding?

For the most part, yes! There are sometimes where I use a bit of free coding snippets to accomplish things I like (i.e. art hover code from my character pages). I also do not like coding petlookups or userlookups, so I usually edit premades for those. Besides that, I do all my own coding. I have been doing it since I got a "HTML for Dummies" book when I was like 12. I am no means an expert, but do enjoy making my own layouts for my petpages.

If you're having an issue with your petpage coding, or want to know how to do something on your pages, just ask. I'm always really willing to help. I'd rather you ask than just take my coding from my petpages as well...

can i use your layouts from your petpages?

Sometimes I'm willing to let friends use my pages or my coding, but if you don't think I'd call you a friend the answer is probably no. Sorry! If we do know each other please shoot me a NM and ask!

do you take art requests or trades?

I barely take the time to draw my own neopets, so I don't usually take requests or trades. You can check the status of my art requests and trades in the left-hand bar on this page. I might have a board up on the Art Chat or PC for art requests or trades, or sometimes I will draw fan art for people's pests if I am feeling up to it!

is x pet uft/ufa?

Unless I am actively advertising a pet on the pound chat, they are not UFT or UFA. If a pet is on this portfolio, has art, or a petpage it is safe to assume they are not UFT/A. Please don't mail me asking for my pets. Even if you are "over offering" I am not interested.
