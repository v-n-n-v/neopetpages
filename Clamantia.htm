Introduction

Welcome to Fruits Basket ! (or Vegetables Basket, depends how you see it) This directory is dedicated to Chias, our little edible friends.

Please, keep your Lupes outside during your visit.

I created this directory after seeing a board dedicated to least favorite pets. Surprisingly, a lot of people seem to dislike Chias. I created this directory as a ''Let's give Chias some love'' project.

You don't have Chia yet and would like to own one ?
Lucky you, you have the option to create a Chia at any time ! You just have to click ''Create a Neopets'' and pick one of the four basic colors. Then, you can use a Paint Brush like any other pets. But what makes Chias so special are their exclusive colors. Pea, Avocado, Lemon... You will need a Magical Chia Pop to get these colors. There are 29 different ones, each giving your Chia a unique fruit-vegetable look. The majority of them are relatively cheap, but if you are looking for a Pea Chia, it will cost a lot of NPs.

History

The informations provided below are from Jellyneo.

There's not a lot of information about Chias' origins since they were created before anyone could keep tracks of Neopets events. We can find Chias pretty much everywhere in Neopia, like in Terror Mountain, but some seem to suggest that they could be native of Neopia Central. Chias are known for being really sociable pets. They love to make new friends and are known for making large group of friends very easily. Chias were designed, according to TNT, to look like living neggs. Through the years, the design of Chias didn't change a lot, as you can see on this page. They mostly made Chias look "cleaner" and more 3-dimensional.

One of the first Chia lover was one of Neopets' founder, Adam Powell, also known as Number 6. He was a big fan of Asparagus Chias and he used Chias a lot as Neopets' mascot back in the day when he was still in charge of Neopets. The first Chia that was ever owned was Thyassa, and he is owned by Donna, who was the co-owner of Neopets with Adam back then before they both left.

Fun Facts

Here's a list of fun facts about Chias, in no particular order.


  • Before January 22 2024, there was only one type of UC Chias, which was Plushie. Luipey was the only one listed here. After this date, pet styles were released and Nostalgic Faerie Chia can now be created with the unreleased UC Faerie Chia style. Even though they are not a formal color, Nostalgic Chias will be listed in the directory as such.

  • Chias are currently available in 91 colors (90 + 1 UC), Banana and Clay being the most recent ones. (Y25)

  • Chias cannot be painted Alien, Burlap, Candy, Elderly Boy/Girl, Eventide, Garlic, Invisible. Mallow, Mosaic, MSP, Oil Paint, Origami, Pastel, Polka Dot, Quiguki Boy/Girl, Royal Boy/Girl, Skunk, Steampunk, Swamp Gas, Transparent, Usuki Boy/Girl, Valentine and Wraith.

  • Chia day is on 18th February and is actually named Chocolate Chia Day

  • Both of these Chias are called "Jelly Chia". Which one is the right color ? Neither. Jelly Chias are actually Purple. (So, the Altador Cup Pratice Team is actually a team of Gold Chias?)


  • If you want to show your love for Chias on the boards, there are 11 avatars featuring Chias.

    Rules

    You own a Chia and would like to see your pet added to the Basket ? First, let's make sure you meet the requirements.

  • Your pet must be a Chia (Duh.)

  • You must be the owner of the Chia.

  • Your Chia must be a permanent member of your Neofamily. He/She can be on your main, or on one of your side account, it's ok.

  • It's ok if you intend to change your Chia's color, but you must also intend to keep your pet as a Chia. Please mention the future color in your application.
