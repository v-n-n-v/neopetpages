♥ hello there! ♥

welcome to the plushie aisha playground, a directory of the cutest neopets in town! here to find inspiration for customizing your own lovely plushie aisha? or to find some new friends for your babe? or simply to admire the cuteness and beauty oozing from every pore (or stitch)? you've come to the right place!

reach out to add yours today! i will gladly list and love any permie plushie aisha; converted or unconverted, male or female, customized or uncustomized.

hope you have lots of fun scrolling through and enjoy your stay! n__n
