This is a page celebrating the majesty of the Moehog. Available in 68 stunning colors, these babies are the absolute pinnacle of Neopian fashion. Yes, they're kind of wonky looking... but then, aren't we all, deep down? And anyway, you can take their whole face off with the right cleaner. I'd like to see a Grundo* do that.

*Moehog-Grundo rivalry is an unofficially recognized aspect of Neopian history.

If, however, you would prefer the metamorphosis of a pet into a majestic Moehog: there is a chance that any creature undergoing experiments at the Secret Laboratory, unlocked with a completed treasure map, will be zapped into a basic Moehog! Some people even adopt out their zapped Moehogs to other Moehog aficionados! Or, for a perhaps even more expensive option, once a year any pet can be turned into a Moehog while retaining their painted color with the Premium Species Change feature! Prefer the lottery method and want to take your chance against the entire Neopets userbase? The Holiday Dream Neopet Giveaway may be right for you!
