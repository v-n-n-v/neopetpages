ABOUT PETPETPETS


Im here to talk about petpetpets.Firstly,Let me clear up the differences between pet,petpet and petpetpets.A pet(called in neopets a neopet) is the "main chracter" in neopets.
They can be made free by clicking "create a neopet" at the neopets site. On the other hand, Petpets are Pets to the neopet which can be bought at
a petpet shop in the neopia central or from private shops by searching the petpet's name via the shop wizard. You might be wondering by now what is a petpetpet? Now, im going to tell you more about it. A petpetpet is a "pet" to the petpet. Thus, it is called a petpetpet. It comes in mostly bug forms, ranging from A motix to a scoach.(see below for more infomation).
In the Neopets site, they claim it as a small creatures whose existence, up until very recently, was unknownand Many of them resemble bugs and insects.How Do i get a petpetpet? There are 2 ways to get a Petpetpet.
One of the ways is to buy them from private shops by searching the petpetpet's name via the shop wizard, or the other is to get them from random events. Nowadays, petpetpets are rare and hard to get. Its rarity are mostly 99.Thus, they are very expensive( cheapest at 26000np). However, it is a honor to obtain one of them for your petpet. Currently, there are 34 petpetpets in existance (with 3 unable to almost not in existance).

    You can either get it through a random event when you find a petpetpet on your petpet or buy it from a shop
    -------------------------------



  • How do I get a petpetpet on my petpet?
    You just leave it in your inventory and it would automatically "jump" on your petpet
    --------------------------------

  • How do I remove a petpetpet?
    You just unequip your petpet and the petpetpet would automactally disappear(not recommended)
