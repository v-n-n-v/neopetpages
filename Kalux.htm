WELCOME

Hi there! Welcome to Kalux, a link directory run by Neo. I know the struggle all too well: you're looking for the perfect PNG to finish your lookup, but you have no idea where to start. You start to search the web, but many of the sites you find are outdated. My goal with Kalux is to fix that problem and provide an easy, accessible collection of Neopia's wonderful sites and resources, whether you're seeking a petpage layout, Altador Cup font, or anything in between.
