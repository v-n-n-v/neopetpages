The essence of a great room is its creativity. What makes it stand out from every other room of its kind? Why is it special? On the Creative Building Ideas page, we've covered unique takes on furniture design. Well a lot of these projects are best seen as a whole, rather than just a piece. So here you can find a compilation of epic proportions of some of the greatest Classic Neohome rooms to date.

Each room has something that makes them unique. Fantastic Flooring. Wallpaper that makes you drool. Incredible use of space. A unique twist on a classic design. Or, of course, all of the above. It is recommended that you click on each link that is available (those that aren't linked no longer exist, but if you drag the image to your URL bar or page tab of your browser, you can still see them in at least full-size view), because these screenshots don't do them justice.

Of course, I also recommend touring full homes, so please don't hesitate to look further than what we've linked here, as there's only so many images I can give you. Some users even have full maps of their homes on their petpages so you can preview everything at a glance. Some of these petpages are linked within this one.

If you feel that there's an amazing room or ten I may have overlooked, please feel free to Neomail me and I will do my best to rectify this grave injustice as soon as possible.
