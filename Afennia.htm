My interest in playing card games (of all types) led me to create a set of correspondences
between the 54 Neopets species and the standard deck of playing cards. Intrigued?
Visit the petpage of Siyamon for details on the assignment process,
OrenWing for the simple list along with specifics on the current month's Pet days,
and Jezeira for basic keyword definitions about the cards themselves.
