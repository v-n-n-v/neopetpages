I pause rubbing my eye, taking in a good look of him. Easily the same size as Luchairt and nothing but a teenager. His paws are easily twice the size of my body. He looks at me concerned and nudges me with his paw. Are you ok? A thick Russian accent attached to his voice.

Behind him Trapandor comes jogging up with Bolzar in tow. Both laugh at my reaction to the giant teenage lion. Trapandor, still giggling says, Zeneloc this is Missy who prophesied your arrival, shes probably just surprised because she doesn't even weigh as much as your head.

Bolzar knocks Trapandor on the head, a much deserved smack.

Missy gathers get thoughts and points towards the large doors in from of them, not taking her eyes off Zeneloc. Lets go in Luna is waiting.

Zen nodded and pushes the door open. Both Luchairt and Dedeck are in their bodyguard positions, eyes open to the size of dinner plates. Luna sat at her throne, stone face with no emotion. Zeneloc slowly walks in, taking in his surroundings. The pillars, the pews, the soft carpet, and the tiny dark pelted house cat sitting on a throne protected by two lions. Trapandor, Bolzar and I follow behind and make our way to the pews to spectate.

Finally Zen locks eyes with Luna and bows her. He took in a mighty breath, Once the blizzard and the fiery ruby ally, a descendant is revealed as the raven's regent.
I am Zeneloc, son of Zahure the blizzard and Rubinder the fiery ruby. I believe I am the regent the prophecy called for. He lifts his head and slowly lowers his body lying on his stomach to show respect to the master of the castle.

Luna stood from her throne, made her way down the steps, and passed her body guards. They dare not speak during this meeting. Luna was still emotionless, walking around the teenage lion and clearly gauging his credibility and strength. He only moves his head slightly to keep his eyes on her. Luna makes it all the way around and to his face, even laying on him stomach it was two feet in the air. Luna then stood on her hind legs and placed a paw on his nose. She switched back to her motherly smile and said, Welcome Zeneloc, lets work together well.

The giant chocolate lion sweetly smiled back and mumbled a thank you.
