THE KETCHUP PACK

A Stuck Pets List


Thank you so much for visiting! ^^

To find pets over level 4, search for LV!
To find non-basic pets, search for colour!
To find RW, RN, and MS, search for alert!
Misspells are classified as such primarily by spelling, not pronunciation!

Format of this page is (generally) as follows
and names are subject to personal opinion:

VWN/Capped (Located directly under species)
Uncapped
Organzied as:
Name, NameN, Name_, Name#

Compound
Organzied as:
NameName, Namename, namename, Name_Name, etc.

Framed
Organzied as:
__Name__, __name__, __xNamex__, etc.

with each subsection organized alphabetically!

PLEASE NOTE!!!

Any names that have banned words within them
will have a '.' in the middle of the name!
Otherwise I wouldn't be able to list them!
These specific pets may also not have clickable names!

Names starting with numbers or the letters
D, E, H, I, J, N, O, Q, and U-Z don't get stuck!

Any pet species not included in the list either have no WN options, are too recently added to the site, or aren't stuck at all!

Repounding will result in the pet getting stuck
again! If you repound a zapped pet, please inform
Lost and Pound of any changes!

Please take these points into consideration when searching! ^^
