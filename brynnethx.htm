About

The Faeries' Ruin Archives is a directory for all TFR content found on Neopets, whether it be official or user-submitted.

It originally began as a way for me to satisfy my longing for more TFR content, specifically stories. Since we were not likely to get anything new from TNT, I turned to the Neopian Times. Beginning in late 2015(?) to early 2016, I began the long task of sifting through back issues of the NT, making sure to record every relevant entry I found. I didn't want to miss a single one!

This project eventually expanded to include everything I could find on site about The Faeries' Ruin: both canon content and user-submitted entries to various competitions. It's been a labor of love, and I hope it proves a useful and fun resource to you! ^^

~ Kentucky

Sections

  • The Neopian Times - (external link) Contains separate pages for articles, short stories, comics, and series submitted to the NT. Also includes editorials and a listing of issues that are themed to something TFR or contains a related image in the background. Page holding editorials / series / issues is a WIP. (Total: 4 pages)

  • Art Gallery - (external link) Features artwork submitted to the Art Gallery, all grouped by character.

  • Other Contests - (external link) Records TFR-related entries for the Caption Contest, Poetry Contest, Random Contest, Storytelling Competition, and Lenny Conundrum. Page is a WIP.

  • Official Content - (external link) Archives canon material about or related to the plot, including plot pages, Neopedia entries, site events, site features, and mobile apps.

Contact

If you find an entry that I've missed and think should be included here, please feel free to me! Same deal if you see an error on any entry (wrong title, issue number, URL, etc.) On the other hand, if you are the creator of one of these entries and would prefer if yours were not listed, please let me know and I'll remove it.
