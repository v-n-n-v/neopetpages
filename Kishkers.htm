Welcome to one of the cutest clubs around!

This is a membership club for all baby aishas that would like to be involved. The purpose of the club is to bring a smile to your face, inspire your own baby customs, and to unite our aishas together as a little friend group. ^_^

If you're interested in having your baby added to the Club, please see the section at the bottom about joining!

The Baby Aisha Club will also list baby aishas that are up for adoption - neomail L if you have a baby aisha you wish to list as UFA.

If you are looking to adopt a baby aisha, scroll through or use ctrl + f and search for "adoption" to see who is up for adoption.

There is currently no Baby Aishas up for adoption.
