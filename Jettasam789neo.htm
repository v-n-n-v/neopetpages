｡･ﾟﾟ･ Jetsam History ･ﾟﾟ･｡

　ʺJetsams are Neopia's mean fighting fish. They are mostly unfriendly and vicious but if you can befriend a Jetsam then you have a powerful ally.ʺ - Jetsam Pet Info.

　Jetsam were one of the original species of Neopets, added on November 27, Y1 (1999) - they looked like a crudely drawn piranha, and an "evil" version of their "good" counterpart, the Flotsam, who resembled a small, kind fish. Jetsam is an English word meaning ʺcargo intentionally thrown overboard from a ship in distress.ʺ

　The Jetsam was revamped into its most recognisable shark self on June 22nd, Y2 (2000), but was soon revamped again through an onsite poll on February 16th, Y3 (2001). You can still view it here! Winners were announced on February 20th.



The poll options. #2 was the winner!
This became the Jetsam art up until the conversion (Y8).

　Flotsam were originally the Limited Edition pet of the two, due to the sheer amount of them being eaten by Jetsam. Jetsam became more scarce due to lack of their main food source, and on May 17th, 2001 (Y3), the Jetsam became Limited Edition.

　Jetsam Day is on October 16th. This is the only day you can create a Jetsam of your own! If you want one any other day of the year, your options are either Jetsam Morphing Potions, Jetsam Transmogrification Potions, adopting one, or a lucky zap from the Lab Ray.

　　　

｡･ﾟﾟ･ Famous Jetsam ･ﾟﾟ･｡

　Though not as renowned as other species, there are still plenty of important Jetsam who have made their mark on Neopia!

Spectre
　ʺThe self-appointed, undisputed ruler of cheat.ʺ Spectre is the 9th and final Cheat! opponent. He also may or may not have been involved in the Brucey B and the Lost Coin plot, but he does share a resemblance to the scarred, card playing Jetsam in the plot, and in the game Tug 'O' War.

Spectre is a Neopian millionaire, and according to the description of The Spectre Legacy, has enough money to play Cheat full time. He is featured on the Jetsam Chomp! avatar.

Items
　　　　　　

Mr. Irgo
　A Neoquest NPC, Mr. Irgo will trade you parts of slain monsters for the 2nd last armour upgrade in the game - the Robe of Protection or the Energy Absorber. He can be found in the first of the 7 Techo Caves.

　Mr. Irgo is featured on the Stamp Collector - Neoquest avatar.


Items
　　　


Major Zeg "Ace" Blackfin
　First introduced in the Return of Dr. Sloth TCG set, the Jetsam Ace has gone on to become a Battledome challenger. He is automatically unlocked for battling in the Cosmic Dome by all Premium users.

　His Action Figure item description says he is ready to "save" Neopia, but the Virtupets insignia on his spaceship seems to imply he works for Dr. Sloth.

Items
　　　



Reshar Collifey
　The goalkeeper for Darigan Citadel's Yooyuball team, he took the place of former goaltender Mungo Lifler after his retirement in Y9. His self admitted weakness is inexperience, but his strengths are his quickness, energy, and good hands. He remains on the Darigan team as of Y24.




Relle Felson
　Relle played forward for Kiko Lake's Yooyuball team in Y8. He did not return to play in the Altador Cup the following year.
