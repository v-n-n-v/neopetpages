    • Read The Rules Please

  • Fill out the form and send it to the correct place Especially I have a board up, make sure you click the direct link to mail me on the account listed. Do not mail it to the account I am posting from. On that same note, do not post your listing request on my board unless I state you can do so. I tend to lose boards before I get around to listing.
  • Please only list Cybunny that are permanent This should be an obvious rule. Unfortunately I've had problems with my other directories with people pounding/morphing within even days of listing with me.
  • Only send in listing requests for Cybunny you own I don't want any trouble with angry users coming to me about a pet of theirs that's listed when they didn't ask for him/her to be
  • Do not list cybunny that are being zapped The risk of a species change is too high and cybunnies don't have a nice cheap morphing potion like other species do.
  • If you one day trade/pound/adopt out your listed cybunny please tell me This makes it easier for me to keep the directory up to date. And also to make sure I am not listing any cybunny that shouldn't be listed anymore.
