Welcome

 Welcome stranger to Tenshi's Neocola Machine Statistics page! This is a project I've been meaning to do for quite some time now so I am happy to finally have the page up. I get a ton of Neocola Tokens from the Battledome, gambling is fun and since I get the tokens for free I thought why not gamble with the Neocola Machine? All I've gotten is junk, but maybe some day I'll get one of those Trans Potions its supposed to give out.

Follow my adventure as I document which tokens I use, how many buttons I press, and how much junk I get out of all this.
