Fountain Of Pride

This is a pride themed directory for listing anything LGBTQIA+ related on Neopets. Please Neomail me about any feedback or submissions for these things.

The Rainbow Fountain in Neopia acts as a source of change for a Neopet's presentation. I believe it's also a good metaphor for the changes a person might go through due to discoveries about their gender and attraction. The rainbow water represents how this is a diverse and fluid set of concepts.
Indroi is the mascot. They are themed to match this page!

This section makes no attempt at hiding criticism or missteps. If you want to find history older than this please visit Pride Project because prior to late 2020 Neopets was not accepting.

The binary gender stated on your pet's lookup does not have to match the one they were created with or the paintbrush clothes they're wearing! This applies to unconverted pets as well. A gender change can reflect how they express their gender, their identity, or both!

Your Pet's Gender Identity

I recommend using Dress To Impress to preview what your pet would look like with a gender change and a mix of paintbrush clothes.

I'd love an "other" gender marker for pets, but the best we can do is to state your pet's LGBTQ+ identities. The adoptables tab on this page has some badges for this!

I recommend drawing your pet(s) with their pride flag(s) or "painting" them a given flag in your art. You can submit pride art to the Art Gallery in June and in the Beauty Contest year round. Pride art is always perfect for petpages, portfolios, and pet lookups.

How To Change Your Pet's Gender

Risk Free Methods:

These will only affect your pet's eyes (if they're converted) and gender marker. Everything else, including their paintbrush clothes, are unaffected by these methods. I recommend using these options for unconverted pets, so they aren't accidentally converted.

Fight the Scientist in the Battledome until your pet's gender is changed. This can only be done on your main account, so you may need to transfer your pet. When he uses a Strange Potion as a weapon, then you'll see the gender change. I recommend withdrawing from the fight immediately after he uses the potion to avoid the risk of it being reversed. Quitting will count as losing the battle. It may take you many battles before it happens.
To get him as a challenger you need to unlock the Lab Ray. Refresh the zap page until he unlocks. It takes a while for some.
This option is a great value for your Neopoints because you can use it for many gender changes.

Use a Strange Potion. This is a good option if you do not want to transfer your pet to your main. It's also a very expensive option. It's only a good value if you want 1 gender change and do not care about the lab ray's benefits.

The Royal Boy/Girl Kyrii Morphing Potions will change their entire appearance to the respective gendered color.


Risky Methods:

Zapping your pet with the lab ray has a chance of changing your pet's gender. Of course, it also might change your pet's stats, color, and species. Some days you may not see any changes at all.
If your pet is a gendered color such as elderly or royal, a zap will change their entire appearance to the color of the opposite binary gender. The pet's eyelashes (or lack of) and paintbrush clothes will change. The chances of getting different paintbrush clothes from the Lab Ray are really low so I do not recommend it for that purpose.

Feed your pet a Witchy Negg. It has the chance of changing your pet's gender, species, raising intelligence, raising hit points, and curing them. It will only change one of these things when fed. This is technically the cheapest option, but could easily add up if you don't get a gender change from it the first time.

You are allowed to have someone borrow your pet so they can use them to battle the scientist for you. According to TNT, if you lose anything in a loan it is your own fault.

Mixing Gendered Paint Brush Clothes

Pets can be painted more than once to get both paint brush sets. Paint brush clothes ONLY transfer with user to user. Only clothes that match the current color will transfer this way. For example, if your pet has both royal sets and they're set to male, then only the male clothes will transfer. If you want to cross paint a pet, I highly recommend planning carefully.

These colors have gendered variants: Elderly, Quiguki, Royal, and Usuki.

In my opinion, these are the best ways to get both sets:
Make sure the pet is on your main account. Paint them. Battle the Scientist until their gender is changed. Paint them again.
Make sure the pet is on their permanent account. Paint them. Use a Strange Potion. Paint them again.

If you want a gender change and both sets:
You can always change their gender again after painting them twice. Fighting the Scientist is the best way to get multiple gender changes. The only downside of this is that the pet will need to be permanently on your main account. This is for reasons discussed earlier on this page.

I could not confirm if a pet already painted a gendered color can immediately be painted the same color again after a gender swap. If not, you may need a cheap color as an in between. If anyone can confirm information about this, please Neomail me!

Other Interesting Gender Facts

If your username has your dead name in it, TNT will help you migrate to a new account if you send them a support ticket. Your pets, items, NC, NC items, avatars, NP, gallery items, gallery size, trophies, site themes, and stocks bought can be transferred to a new account. If you bought pet slots, TNT will give you the NC to repurchase them. Your account age, game scores, and battledome challengers cannot be moved.

At least in 2024, on pet lookups many pieces of the pet often do not load. Often times the eyelashes on female marked pets won't load making them look like male marked pets.

If you hatch a Draik, their gender will be random. You can use a Draik morphing potion to avoid this.

If you type #GENDER into a petpage, then the pet who owns the page will have their current binary gender shown in its place. This is great for genderfluid and lab ray pets.

You are allowed to cover the gender of your pet on their lookup and you're allowed to replace it with "other". Find code for it here. It may not work with all layouts. Adding style tags around the code with no layout for sure works.
You are NOT allowed to misrepresent your pet's image or stats.

On your user lookup, you can set your gender to "other". This is great for anyone who does not want to disclose or choose a binary gender (like me). This setting can always be changed.

Since LGBTQIA+ terms became unfiltered many pets are stuck. If you want a pet or name to be released you can submit a ticket.

Canon LGBTQIA+ Characters

You can find this in the description for Team Shenkuu on the Altador Cup teams page. She's a brown Gnorbu.
