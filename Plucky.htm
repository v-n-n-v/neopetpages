Laura's Game
Ratio Guide
Ratios change on the 25th of every month.

Blue games are ones I consider worth playing this month. Some are fast games but some are simply consistent.

Grayed out games wouldn't load or I couldn't send a score. I play on Chrome and don't use any tricks (outdated browsers, scripts, etc) to force Neopets games to run. If your setup is different, your experience may be different. Neopets and the Ruffle emulator will (hopefully) be updated over the next few months. I may update periodically but I'll primarily only update when ratios change.
