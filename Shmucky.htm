If you are interested in adopting a pet, please neomail me with the following information:

1. Name of pet you are interested in:
2. A short blurb about yourself! (ex., your name, what you like to do on Neopets, etc. ) No need to write me a novel! you can keep it as short or as long as you like, I would just like to know a little bit about you!
3. OPTIONAL: Any plans you have in mind for the pet (ex. customizations, character plans, why you like this pet, etc.)


✮ Rules ✮

 
  • Please only apply for a pet if you are intending to keep them!
    •  
  • No applying to re-trade!
    •  
  • Changed your mind on a pet you've adopted? That's okay, things happen! Please either neomail me to return them, or adopt them out on your own!
