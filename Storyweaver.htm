Storyweaver Directory

A mobile-friendly, searchable directory

Last updated: May 29 2024

Welcome to Storyweaver Directory: a site to gather all the stories of Neopia on petpages and weave them together in one place! What makes Storyweaver Directory unique is that it's both searchable and mobile-friendly. Petpages are divided into broad categories, then each petpage has up to five tags that best describe what you'll find in them. This directory accepts all petpage types, so you never know what you'll find here. Enjoy!

Search

To search the directory on mobile, tap the browser menu and select "Find in Page," then type in a search term to find all pages with that tag. To search the directory on desktop, press ctrl+f on your keyboard, then type in a search term to find all pages with that tag.

Pages that haven't been updated in over a year are marked [inactive]. Pages that haven't been updated in over six months are put on a watchlist and marked [inactive?]. Pages that are on hiatus are marked [inactive] regardless of timeframe. Pages that are broken and unusable (default petpage, all broken images, etc) are moved to the Broken but not Forgotten category.

Common Tags and what they mean:

  • #adoptables - Bits of artwork free to use with proper credit
  • #art - Art page or page that contains a lot of art
  • #character - Page that's about the Neopet, see also #ocs
  • #collaboration - Page with many users contributing
  • #guide - Instructions on how to do something
  • #neo - having to do with Neopets
  • #ocs - original characters, see also #character
  • #premades - lookups and petpages that are ready to go
  • #requests - accepts requests on this petpage


Add Page

To add your page to the directory, please neomail june_scarlet with the following information:
Category:
Link to Petpage:
Petpage Title:
Main Account:
Up to 5 tags that describe your Petpage:

If you need help filling out any portion of the form, such as choosing a category or tags, please neomail june_scarlet. Storyweaver Directory will not accept petpages that do not fill out the form.

To prevent blank neomails, please send petpage links in this format: /~storyweaver#link
Links sent with the full neopets address plus an anchor tag make neomails break.
