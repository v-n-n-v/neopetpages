About the Avatar



You can get the Pteri - Darigan avatar by visiting the petlookup of a Darigan Pteri that is exactly 644 days (15,456 hours) old. Upcoming Darigan Pteris are tracked on this page and the board on the AC. If you still need the avatar, I recommend that you set a reminder on your phone or calendar so you remember to check back on the correct day.

A Darigan Pteri becomes eligible the second it turns 644 days old and will give the avatar any time during the next 24 hours. When the petlookup changes to 645 days and the petpage changes to 15,480 hours, it will no longer give the avatar.

Fun fact: It turns out that if a Pteri is 644 days old during the daylight saving time changeover in the U.S., you'll get one "extra" hour to get the avatar in the spring and one "fewer" hour in the fall. (In reality, the pet is still 644 days old for 24 hours, but the time on the clock when it turns 645 days old will be one hour later in the spring and one hour earlier in the fall.)

Want to Help?

If you would like to help out with this project, I'm always looking for:

  • Pets that are under 644 days old that could be morphed/painted into a Darigan Pteri in the future. This can be a pet in the pound or a pet you own, if you don't care about its color and species and are willing to morph/paint it (temporarily or permanently).
  • People who have a free pet slot and would be willing to hold a Darigan Pteri until it ages. The amount of time is negotiable; a couple of months is ideal, but if you can't give up a slot for that long, we can usually work something out.
If you'd like to help with either of those things, please send me a Neomail.
