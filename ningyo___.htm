Welcome to ningyo.

an anime graphic & request site by jewlz

Hi there! Here at Ningyo, I make anything and everything anime and manga related for graphics. Feel free to browse the premade icons, banners and backgrounds or request a custom graphic. Have you fallen in love with one of my premade graphics? I can even add in text so an existing graphic on Ningyo will be just for you.

I do not do button or layout requests here. If you want one of those, you'll have to go to Jewel for that. Or you can check out our amazing affialiates in the sitely section.
Ningyo (人魚, "human fish", often translated as "mermaid") is a fish-like creature from Japanese folklore.


If you need to contact me, you can do so by neomailing me. Please re-send your neomail if I don't respond within 3 days. Your neomail may go unnoticed as I get a lot of messages.
