Welcome!

Hello! Welcome to a directory for pets in cosplay/+fanpets! This is a work in progress- especially categories. Generally, individual media will be given their own section if there are enough pets to list OR if I deem them to be large enough to be separate. Some things may be grouped into more general categories, like "anime" rather than grouped by series. This is subject to change :)

❤ To list, pets do NOT need to be named for a character, only dressed as them. The pet must be a specific character, however, not a general theme (like a Dungeons and Dragons class, a Harry Potter house, etc.). Monsters from different media can be counted - i.e. a Pokemon species.
❤ However, just because a pet has a character name does not mean they are a cosplay pet. Example: my pet Furfrou is a Pokemon RW but is not dressed to look like a Furfrou. He would not be listed.
❤ All media can be listed here - books, movies, TV, games, whatever!
❤ Permanent pets only please - the pet will be removed if the custom changes from a cosplay custom or if the pet is naked/traded.
❤ This is first and forrmost a customization directory - please make sure your pet has a background and a quality cosplay. At my determination, some pets may not be listed if their custom is not quality or not close to the character. This will happen very rarely. :)
