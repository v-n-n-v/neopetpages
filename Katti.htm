DYEWORKS; (DW) - A great feature that was introduced to us in June of 2014. This feature allows you to dye certain NC items and in return you keep the original item and receive a colored version of it.

In order to dye items you'll need an original NC item (that is currently active) and a Hue Brew Potion from the NC Mall. Potions are always on sale in the mall for 125 NC.

Visit Loic's studio HERE to see the active dyework items available.



Have you recently traded for a dyework item? We would love to hear ALL recent trades and how you felt about the trade! C: Fill out the following form and click below to send it in.




1. When I dye the original item will I receive it back?
Yes! You will receive the original along with a dyed version of it! The original item stays in your inventory at all times, untouched.


2. What do I do after I purchase/trade for dyework potions?
You simply just need to visit Loic's studio HERE in the NC Mall with the potion(s) AND original item in your inventory. No activation is required.


3. Do I get to choose what color I want?
Nope. Unfortunately you can't choose. It is random.


4. I see people lending their dyework items, is this allowed?
Yes! Everyone has different rules when it comes to lending but it is allowed. Common ways to lend;
a. You send 1 GBC and 1 dyeworks potion and they will dye the original and send the dyeworks item back to you.
b. You send 1 GBC to the lender and they will send you the item(s) to dye yourself. You will then need another box to send it back.


5. I have an original item but it's retired, can I dye it still?
Nope. You can only dye active items. Visit Loic's studio HERE to see them.

6. What is the difference between Dyeworks Hue Brew Potion and the following potions?
Dyeworks Holiday Hue Brew Potion
Dyeworks Winter Hue Brew Potion
Festive Dyeworks Hue Brew Potion
The holiday themed potions were given out by the advent calendar. All potions give you the same reward and are valued the same. The image is the only difference.

7. Where can I find Retired Dyeworks Mystery Capsules? What are they?
TNT released Retired Dyeworks Mystery Capsules on 12/13/17. They retired on 12/17/17. The only way to obtain the capsule now is to trade for one. When opening the capsule you will be RANDOMLY awarded one/two items. Visit Jellyneo HERE to see a full list of possible items you can obtain.

8. What are Dyeworks bundles and how do they work?
Dyeworks Hue Brew Potion 5-Bundle - 500 NC Dyeworks Hue Brew Potion 10-Bundle - 900 NC Dyeworks Hue Brew Potion 20-Bundle - 1,500 NC

TNT typically releases potion bundles when they release new dyework items. After purchasing a bundle, they will no longer be a bundle and you will get that amount of potions in your inventory. For example, if you bought the 20 potion bundle and plan to trade them away - you will need 20 boxes to send all of the potions. These bundles are great when it comes to saving some NC! You will save 1,000 NC by buying the 20 bundle instead of buying each potion separately at 125 NC each.

ALL data submitted to us regarding dyework items will be added.
