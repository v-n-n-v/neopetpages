Hello the_freebie_man_

You can call me Kaius or Potatoes. I originally played Neopets at age 10 from 2006 to 2008 because of the McDonald's toys. I came back in 2020.

With Neopets as a whole, I take a "souvenir hoarding" approach. My galleries have adoptables featured on them and random nostalgic pics are on my main lookup. I use layouts and page decorations of various kinds on all of my pages.

I try to make art and Neo projects that are memorable in some way.
