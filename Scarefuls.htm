It's the Burlap Life!

New Burlap Pet Ideas!

AHHHH! I love burlap pets so much! They're the plushies for the creepy kids! In my obsession to uhh, catch them all, I started coming up with ideas for future burlap pets. I've been playing Neopets since my college days back in 2001 (please don't do the math!) and I always thought it would be amazing to work for them. TNT, if you use any of my burlap ideas, it would literally be a dream come true!

Enough with the rambling, let me present, THE NEW BURLAP BUNCH!







Burlap Grundo

This cutie ended up somewhat like a pincushion with stuffing sticking out all over! Instead of the usual Grundo antenna, I popped in a couple of giant pins. I used different shades of blue and a star pattern to give it a spacey look.




Burlap Shoyru

My favorite species! And my very first attempt ever at creating a new color. I used purples and oranges simply because it's a favorite color combination of mine. I also took some inspiration from voodoo dolls, with the heart patch and pins.







Burlap Gnorbu

I think this is my favorite so far! I tried to mimic the funny twirly yarn texture for the mane, but not sure if that comes across. The feet are supposed to be little wooden pegs rather than hooves.




Burlap Tonu

This was a request from the PC, can you guess what inspired this one? I'll give you a hint! He's from a favorite movie about Halloween and trick or treating!





Burlap Skeith

I wanted to try out something a little darker and spookier on one of my burlaps. I thought Skeith would work well because of their big size and intimidating look!





Burlap Techo

This ended up being another favorite. His coloring is inspired by a real life species of lizard! Can you figure it out? It's a type of skink!




More coming soon!
