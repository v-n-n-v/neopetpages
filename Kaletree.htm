Welcome to the Golden Paw!

This petpage is dedicated to keep track and list all the Kougras of Neopia !
The Kougra (koo-gra), was first introduced in neopets on August 10th 2000, and its species day is on April 22nd! Kougras are known for being Neopets native to Mystery Island. They primarily eat big, juicy fruits, and love to tackle and pounce when they play.
The Kougras have always been one of mine (Onyx) favorite species, and ive always wanted to make a petpage for them. However to keep things organized and on track, there are some rules that must be followed for the directory to keep running those being:

RULES:

1 - Our directory only lists neopets that are Kougras, well, obviously.
2 - Only pets on permanent homes can be listed. No pets that are currently UFT/UFA please. Thats just to keep track of species changes.
3 - Kougs can only be listed by their rightfully owners, this is just for safety so people that dont want their pets listed end up being listed anyways. I will double check every time before updating the petpage.
Also, in case anyone wants their pet removed from the directory, just shot me a mail and i will do it right away !


UPDATE AS FOR 28TH JAN 2024: Since the release of NC UCS, and now that unconverted is a removable "skin", ive made the decision to completeley delete the UC sections of this directory, adding the pets previous added there on their colour counterpart section (ex: "UC Grey Kougras" moved to "Grey"). I will try my best to keep this directory as updated as possible but since theyre bent to change visually more it might not be always updated.
To list your kougra on our directory, please, refer to the form below.

KOUGRA DESIGN HISTORY


The first kougra design was released in 2000. Their first version used to be facing upfront, with big paws to complete de design.





A few months later, a new version of the kougra was released,offering a different new design, that for TNTs surprise wasnt very well received by the userbase. This time, the kougra design was similar to what we have today, both UC and converted versions. And this design marked the official release of this fantastic species !



Just a few months later, After the first site conversion from classic to the second most known layout (before beta), the kougra was just sligthly adjusted to fit customization items, but its one of the pets that mostly kept its characterists from the first conversion.
