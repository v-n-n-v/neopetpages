Welcome

Greetings, esteemed visitor, and welcome to The Royal Palace. This is where you can meet and address members of the monarchy, from Meridell to Roo Island to Brightvale and elsewhere across Neopia. I am Amy, a humble clerk in charge of the registry.
Founded on 2020.02.22, the Royal Palace currently lists 266 royals from around Neopia. I beg your pardon? Your Highness isn't listed at the Palace? This must be a terrible mistake. To rectify this, please contact us here with the following information: their name, species, and color. We will update our register posthaste.

Requirements

1. Everything must be in order
Female or male, pets will be displayed regarding their painted color. UCs and Crosspaints, on the other hand, will be listed regarding their species.

2. Royal members must stay with their families.
To be listed, your pet must be a permanent member of your Neofamily.

3. Royal pets must be dressed properly.
To be listed, your pet must at least wear a background. However, they don't have to wear their painting wearables (except for Crosspaints, of course).
