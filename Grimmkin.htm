FAQ

1. My dream pet is UC! Can I list it?
Sorry, I will not take listings for UC pets at this time. It's simply not likely a UC pet would be adopted out at this point.

2. I don't see my dream pet on the UFA list. Do I need to check back constantly?
Not at all! I will reach out to everyone requesting a certain pet if one is listed that matches the criteria. :)

3. Nobody is looking for the pet I have UFA! What should I do??
Don't worry! I will reach out if a listing matching your pet's criteria is made. If not, you are always free to continue searching for a home on your own.

4. What happens if I am no longer adopting out my pet / found a home / got my dreamie?
I ask that any changes to your listing status please be reported to me via NM, so it can be removed. I will check through listings periodically just in case.

5. How can I be sure a user is still active to reach out to?
I will comb through listings periodically to help keep things current. Users that are inactive for over a month will be removed and notified. If you will be on hiatus please let me know so I can add a note to your listing!
*for users in stealth mode, I will remove + notify if people have not heard back in over a week.

Other Questions? Shoot me a NM!
