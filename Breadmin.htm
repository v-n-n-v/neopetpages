Gallery Help!

If you don't want to unintentionally overthink your gallery's theme, you can stick to something simple like cute items or anything that you would enjoy collecting. There's nothing wrong with making a gallery for yourself.

Gallery Tips

Really unique gallery ideas usually come in the form of a theme where you can't search a specific category or keyword in the name. This makes for a much more memorable collection of items. Pairing a noun with an adjective is the best way to get a very specific theme.

You can use the categories function to have multiple different collections making it so you have multiple galleries in one. If you chose not to link to a category, then you can have a "secret gallery" which can come in handy.

Come up with a gallery name before you choose items. A silly phrase or a scenario could easily inspire a unique theme.

Try to think of synonyms to the theme you picked and search those on Jellyneo's item database and search these same words in item descriptions.

Search for characters in Jellyneo's Book Of Ages using keywords that relate to your gallery's theme. Some characters have items based on them that you can include. For example, if you want to make a Grundo gallery, then you can find Grundo characters. Search the character name in Jellyneo's Item Database.

bookofages.jellyneo.net/species/ - This page has articles for every species to help you find official lore. You can encorporate this lore into your gallery.

Don't be afraid to ask for item suggestions on the Spotlights and Galleries board and off-site.

Galleries where the items are organized are much more fun to look through. You can either rank your items based on color or group them based on their similarities. Items can be arranged so the matching colors align vertically or horizontally in each row. You can create categories for specific types of items or for completely different themes.

It's much easier to look for a gallery keeper if you use Dr. Sloth's Image Emporium:
drsloth.com/search/?category=10

While you're shopping, skim the stock of user run stores. This is a great way to find obscure items that match your theme or inspire an entire gallery!

Plan your gallery by making a wishlist for it using Jellyneo's Item Database. This makes it easy to keep track of what to buy and gives shortcuts to the shop wizard links for your items. You'll also know roughly how much things cost in advance.

Buy gallery space along side adding items. I do not recommend buying gallery upgrades in advance. This way you're only buying the space you know you'll need.

If you want to know or share how much your gallery is worth then use this tool:
items.jellyneo.net/tools/gallery-price-checker/

If you want to know exactly how many NPs it'll take to get from a specific gallery size to another one then use this tool:
sunnyneo.com/shopgallerycalculator.php

Don't worry if someone else used the gallery idea as you. The kind of gallery you make should first and foremost be fun to make!
