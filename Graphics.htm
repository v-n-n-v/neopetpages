Welcome

Hello and welcome to my resource page! Here you will find a variety of resources and codings that will help you to customize and personalize any page of yours to your liking, as well as offering templates for button borders, my personal neosignatures and special characters for personalized board titles. All items here are either created or edited by myself in my free time and then put onto this page so that others may benefit from them, as well.

No credit is necessary when using any resources listed on this page unless you would absolutely like to do so. However, please do not claim to have made/edited items here, yourself. If this issue is brought to my attention, I will report it to TNT for stolen content.

This page is meant to be fun and resourceful, I hope you find everything that you need here! If there is something you would like to see listed on this page or you have questions/comments/concerns, please feel free to contact me here.
