Information

In spring of 2007, TNT converted the majority of existing Neopets to accomdate the NC Mall. While they consider the remanants of the old-style art to be long-gone history, many users still covet the pets which were left "unconverted".

  • Raz's Guide to Unconverted Pets

Trading & Adoption

Since UCs cannot be created, trading is one of the only two ways to obtain a UC if you do not already own one. The other method is adoption. Both require you to receive the pet from another user, who in turn either created the pet before April 26th, 2007 or also accepted the pet from another user.
