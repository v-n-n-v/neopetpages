Hello and welcome to all new and old Fishies!
It's that time again to come together and play for the cup. With the start of the new season, we all need to get motivated.
When we all play to the best of our capacities, we become an unstoppable wave! So let's show all other teams what Maraqua is able to do! GO MARAQUA GO!

We would also like to inform you that sign ups (and pledges) will open on May 22nd at noon NST. Until the start of matches, stop by WAM boards and chat with our team veterans and our new members.
