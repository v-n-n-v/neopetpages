ADOPTION AGENCY RULES:
1)You must be willing to pay the adoption/transfer fee that neopets.com will charge you.

2)We DO NOT give a GUARANTEE that you will get the pet if you choose to have us drop it in the pound for you, since anyone who is in the pound can find it first. We strongly suggest transferring/trading pets instead.

3)DO NOT request adoption if you do not plan on keeping the pet, even if they are an avatar pet.

4)DO NOT adopt if you only want the wearable clothes some pets might be wearing, unless the foster agrees to that purpose.

5)You must have 5 pets or LESS to adopt. DO NOT dump a pet in the pound to adopt one of ours. If you absolutely want to get rid of a pet, give us its name and we will take it in, if we can. Some fosters may even be willing to trade. If adopting on a side account, please let us know.

6)If you wish to adopt from us, your account MUST be older than 4 months.

7)You must be willing to wait 1 week for your new pet. This will allow the current foster to decide if you are a good fit, if they want to. It also gives fosters a chance to have a real life.

8)Fosters have the right to turn down anyone they do not see fit as adopters. They may have their own rules and requirements. They also DO NOT have to transfer a pet to the new owner, since it does cost them/us 1000 NPs and every account can only do that once a month (more depending on age of account).

9) Please only neomail the foster once. We will get back to you as soon as we can. Also, in your first neomail please write more than "I want your pet." Good impressions will help you be chosen.

FOSTERS!
Want to be a foster parent for this agency? Send a neomail to rozlynnwander.

TWO KINDS OF FOSTERS
A) One foster is with the agency on a more permanent basis. The agency can ask these fosters if they have room to take in a pet. They also get the benefits of the agency's funding. These fosters may request things like Lab Map Pieces, Morphing Potions, and Paint Brushes.

B) The second foster is temporary. Instead of putting their pet in the pound, they have the agency post their adoptable pet on this page and in any other adds for the agency.

LONG TERM FOSTER RULES:
1) The agency reserves the right to decline anyone.

2) You need to be able to foster at least one pet at a time.

3) You will be held responsible for keeping that pet fed and happy.

4) Your account has to be 4 months or older.

5) Having the Secret Lab Ray is a plus!

6) If you put your pet up for adoption, REMEMBER to take all the equipped items and the petpet, if you don't want to get rid of them.

7) You must inform the agency once the pet is adopted so we can update our page.

8) Permanent Fosters need to keep in touch with the agency about extended absences. If gone without contact for 60 days or more, we will assume you no longer foster with us.

Any questions, please ask!

CURRENT CHANGE OPTIONS:

We are also currently working on earning the funds to buy more paint brushes to use on the pets and make sure all our permanent fosters have the zapping option.
There are also a few morphing potions available. Like a name? Ask if we have the morphing potion you'd need. FREE OF CHARGE. That's what they're there for.
