WELCOME!

This is the rules page for the Wonder Trade Art Trade game. Feel free to take inspiration from this page and start your own versions. The rules written below were agreed upon and are subject to change. So check back before each event as some things may have changed. Most importantly, HAVE FUN!

Each Event is on three week cycle.

  • Week One: Sign Ups
    - An extension may occur if only 3-4 people sign up
    - The extension will be one week and will be determined by the few people who have already signed up.
  • Week Two: Drawing!
  • Week Three: Feedback, Rules Editing, etc.
Events start on Sunday and end on Saturday. Please plan accordingly.
The active thread will be updated on this page as well as the participants page. You will always be able to find the active thread if there is one.

Each participant will receive a random user for which they can pick one pet to draw. In the event that someone cannot complete their piece, one of our back up artists will step in and use an 'off' week to complete that piece of artwork.


RULES

  • Each user must submit 3 pets.
  • Each Pet must be a different species.
  • Pets may be the same species if you do not have any other species on your account.
  • You may use outside ref sheets as long as the design is neo-like.
  • The over-all design should read "neopet", but the colors may be drastically different.
  • Feral/Quad as well as Anthro/Humanoid are all welcome.
  • May use any form of art as long as it is original art.
  • Traditional and other non-digital artwork must be photographed or scanned.
  • The photo must then be sent in place of the artwork itself.
  • During the 'off' week, you may exchange the pets that are listed.
  • BE KIND! We all have different ability levels and that's great!
  • Keep your giftee a secret until you see the reveal thread. We don't want to ruin the surprise.
  • If you get a species you are not confident in, please try your best. This is just for fun.
  • If you really feel you cannot work with the 3 given pets, please NM the host.
