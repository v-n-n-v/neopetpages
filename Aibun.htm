Bunny's Burrow


Welcome to the Burrow! This is my rabbit hole of untaken names that I've dug up (hehe) over time trying to make my own. It's not a whole lot, but I hope you like one (or a few!) of them to make into a new friend!
I was inspired by several untaken name pages and wanted to create my own with the list of names I've made over the past few months. Pets that are available will have a BLANK box. I'm always making up names, so please check in every few weeks ^^ (or months I can be lazy)
Thanks for visiting! Next to the name there will be a note if it was made up with anything in mind or is a compound/RN/RW/MS. Most names are 5-7L as I'm very fond of them! Some are bunny-related since, well, I love Cybunnies!! (and bun puns) There are some fun exceptions to this, because I found the name cute or something hehe.
