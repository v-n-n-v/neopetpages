Remember?
Livechat rooms in all the main neoshops and hanging out in them waiting for main shops to restock
Faerie Quests were unlimited
There was no quickstock, everything was stocked one item at a time into your shop or deposit box
User Theme Parks where your pets paid to go on rides and found random items like balloons

You could buy wine from the food shop for about 120 neopoints
Discount Cards where you got a percentage off when buying items from main shops

Anyone having over 100,000 neopoints was considered rich
The Battledome didn't open to everyone until January 2001
You couldn't build a Neohome until March 2001
It cost 3 totems to go to Mystery Island when it first opened and you went by boat

Players could create a new item and submit to Neopets and some where used as real neoitems (jabbathehutt created several neggs, chris22099 made the original snowglobe and I had 2 items, the HRobibear and the HollyNoel ring)
HRobibear became the Cool Purple TeddyBear (atleast it's my favorite colour)

The Story of the HollyNoel Ring and the Fish Negg

Once upon a time in the land of Neopia. A sweet fuzio by the name of Nut_case gave this ring to a pretty lil aisha named HollyNoel in May of 2000. But it wasn't fair for it to be the only one in the land so, in July of 2000 it was changed into the Fish Negg. The image was changed again in the Spring of 2001. We will always treasure the sentiment.
