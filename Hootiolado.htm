Welcome to the H.E.L.P. Dream Pet List! Do you have a dream pet? Then you are in the right place! If you don't have one and you want to see some beautiful pets UFA, you can go to our UFA list. Both the Dream Pet List and the pets we put UFA are open to everyone!

But first, some rules:

  1. You don't need to be in our guild to add your dream pet.

  2. Lutaris are accepted!

  4. If you obtain your dream pet, please send me a neomail so the page will be up to date! Note: I'm slow updating the WL because I'm busy IRL. But worry not, I'll do it as soon as I can

  5. You can add neopets that you can't obtain through the lab, but be aware that we mostly use the lab to paint our pets.

  6. Send a neomail to yohana231 with some details about your dream pet! Please, write in the neomail: colour, species, name (if you want it VWN, WN or you don't mind... etc.), gender or any detail you want!

Do you want to help a Neopian with a Magma wish? Neomail me with your Magma time (NST) and I'll add you to the list!
