Most questions are answered in the guides. The Helpers on this board are happy to answer questions but PLEASE read the guide first.

Post 4:

PT takes place in real time over a month. It always ends at 11:59pm NST on the last day of the month. Prizes are usually awarded and the new game starts on the 2nd.

DO NOT USE the Refresh or Back buttons during your PT game. You can double pay for something that way.

Please DO NOT post plushie names. It can make the prices drop.


Post 5:

DO NOT answer other new players' questions if you are a beginner yourself. Leave the helping to the experienced helpers. Giving wrong or inaccurate advice may ruin another player's game.

KNOWN GLITCHES
Nimmos often cause all plushies in your store to be invisible. They still sell, but you can only see them on your front page.

Meeting taxman with over 50 million COH *WILL* crash your game.
