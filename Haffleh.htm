FAQ

Who is Kelly?

Kelly is a young Yellow Bori who helps me manage the directory, Find A Laugh, of which The Triv' Quiz is a sub-site. Her general knowledge may not be the best but she will help you where she can.

Can I have a hint?

Yes. If you are very stuck on a question then you are welcome to neomail me for a hint if you would like one. On the other hand you might prefer to complete the quiz with no help at all. It's entirely up to you.

I only won't give out hints for bonus questions. You can skip these questions if you cannot answer them.

How many quiz questions are there?

Currently there are 200 regular questions and 2 bonus questions.

What are bonus questions? What can I do with my bonus codes?

Bonus questions appear occasionally in the later stages of the quiz. You can choose to skip them when you find them. They are designed to be particularly difficult, so if you do manage to answer one correctly then you will be presented with a bonus code as proof of your awesomeness.

The bonus codes are secret and should not be dislosed to anyone other than myself, Weapon. You can neomail me your bonus code(s) if you would like to have your Scoreboard status updated.

What are secret ranks? What can I do with my rank codes?

Secret rank codes are proof of where you are up to in the quiz. Your rank first upgrades after Q5 and will then upgrade after every tenth question: Q10, Q20, Q30, etc.

Rank codes are, as noted, secret and should not be disclosed to anyone other than myself, Weapon. You can neomail me your latest rank code if you would like to have your Scoreboard status updated.

What are guest questions? And how can I contribute one?

After Q100 you might stumble across occasional questions devised by people other than myself. Unlike bonus questions these do count as regular questions and must be answered correctly before you can continue.

You can neomail me to submit a question at any time, but please play the quiz a little first to get a feel for how it all works and so that your question will be a good one.
