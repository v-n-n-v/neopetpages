So. You want to make a pet.
There are so many species to choose from, then you have to pick a color, and a personality, and on top of all that,
a NAME?!
A name is the one characteristic of your new pet that will last. You can change their stats, species, gender, color, and lookup, but you /have/ to stick with their name. Now, you could make a name like bubblegumsodapop850927057, but that's a little much, don't you think? It kinda looks like you rolled your head across the keyboard and clicked the submit button. Not to say I'm perfect. I've had a few naming disasters before I knew what a nice name was. But put some thought into your pet's new name. Here, I've listed some names, without the dreaded numbers and underscores, that you can use to adorn your new pets. Please, if you use one, which you're free to do, Neomail me. If you see one on here that's taken, and I don't know it, please tell me. I check all of these before I put them up. So go ahead. Scroll down. Find a fantastic name for a companion who you'll be proud to call your own. ♥

Again. If you use one, please Neomail me, so I can take it off of this page. It's hard to look all of these up. Some people will take two, three, or even four names without telling me. You're more than welcome to take that many, but PLEASE neomail me so I can take them down. You don't need to come up with some really long message, just send me a message saying "I used the names _____." That's it. Easy, right? Go ahead and take more than one, but please tell me. I know sometimes I can't reply, but I can at least see your messages to delete the names. I spend my own free time to come up with these for you. Thanks! =)

Last updated: March 18th. Clearing of old names, new names.

(it took Neha a full year to teach me how to say a person's name. xD ) They may be pronounced differently than they appear, but feel free to pronounce them however you want! (I have a pet with a Hindi name, Barish, and I constantly mispronounce it.)
Special thanks to Neha, who helped to make sure I got these definitions mostly right. ;) Thanks very much as well to fungirl007111, who submitted some of these. I can't even say half of these names, so thank you two very much. xD
