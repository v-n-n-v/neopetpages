Welcome to Hazy Daze, a permanent only UC Grey database!


You can call me Sonder!

Hazy Daze came into creation when a dear friend of mine suggested I make a uc Grey directory as she was working on a uc Faerie one. Thank you so much to Celica for the inspiration and drive to bring Hazy Daze into reality!

I collect uc Greys, and although I don't have a huge collection... My love for each of my grey pets is paramount. Hazy Daze was created with the intent to show everyone on Neopia the love we have for our sad, tired little grey pets.

A couple of rules if you wish to have a pet listed here!

Firstly, Hazy Daze is for permanent uc Grey pets only. Please try not to list if you are unsure if you'll be keeping your sad little friend forever.
That said, I do know we humans have an uncanny and natural ability to change our minds... Please MAIL ME if you decide to trade your pet, so I can remove them from the database. Likewise, if you recently traded for a pet you see listed here and it's under the wrong username... If you plan on keeping the pet, let me know and I'll adjust the username correctly.

Since the pets listed here are permanent, please do not use this directory for scouting on potential trades or pets you'd like to trade for. Instead, visit my affiliate below who has a database for UC pets for trade! ♥

It is mandatory that your pet is wearing a background or some customization since it really helps the pet stand out! I will remove pets with no background after a week from the time I notice.

That's it! Thank you all very much for your interest and support in Hazy Daze. Grab a button at the bottom of the page and toss it on your pet/user look-up if you'd like to help this directory grow and flourish!
