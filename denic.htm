Welcome

Hello! Here you will find the results of a popularity survey I conducted on the PC in May 2021. This is just a general survey for fun about the PC's likes and dislikes, it is not indicative of trading values!

PCers were asked to pick their 3 favourite pets and 3 least favourite pets from each tier according to the UC tier guide, and add or subtract points accordingly like this (e.g.):

Favourite:
Robot Blumaroo +3
Darigan Tonu +2
Baby Eyrie +1

Least favourite:
Baby Kougra -1
Darigan Lenny -2
Sponge Koi -3

Click on the navigation on the left to see the results!
