Welcome to Crater! Crater is a unique PNG resource site created in 2014 and still used by graphic makers today in 2024. We currently house 176 PNGs made by yours truly, myself, Miranda (fearthecrazy). So what makes us unique besides our large number of images? Crater is the first PNG site to create PNGs from 80px by 80px Neopets items. Each image was carefully traced from its original image and blown up into three different sizes for your convenience.

Before you use, please keep in mind that I have spent hours on hours to provide all these images for you guys to use creatively, with that being said, please please credit it me for my work. So, if you use a PNG, please credit Crater in some sort of way.

To use, right-click on the image and save to your computer. This is simply the nature of PNGs... You must save it in order to preserve the transparent background; just copying and pasting it will give it a black background (aka not easy to use).

What are PNGs? A PNG is a type of image file that preserves transparency. Transparent backgrounds allow you to incorporate the PNG into your graphics seamlessly.
How do you use PNGs? See a PNG you like? Right-click and save it to your computer. Open it in your favorite image editing app and enjoy! If you only copy & paste, it'll show a black background in your editor- defeating the purpose.
How did you make your PNGs? Depends where it came from! Wearables as SWF files are easy peasy :) I just removed the background for you. On the other hand, anything besides wearables was traced using Illustrator so they could be turned into much much larger images with clean quality.
How long did these PNGs take to make? A really really long time! Most of the PNGs here are ones I traced. Time is in your favor though- Crater has been around for over a decade *gasp*, so I've had a lot of time to build the repository you see today.
