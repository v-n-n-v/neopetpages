Bucket of Bolts

Welcome to Bucket of Bolts, a directory for all robot neopets. We offer a directory for robot pets to shine and a casing directory for those who need casings for their beloved pets or for those who have extras. We also offer a crosspaint section for those amazing customizations with casings. We currently have 490 robots listed with a goal of listing 500 robots.

To list your pet, just follow these small rules:

#1 - To list, neomail me the name and species of your pet. I don't have a preference of how you send this information, just be sure that the information is there!

#2 - Your pet cannot be UFT/UFA/being labbed. I will only list permanent pets. If this changes, please neomail me to remove your pet.

#3 - I have no restrictions on the amount of pets you can list but they all must belong to you. I will not list a pet without the owner's permission.

#4- By default, I will link to a pet's lookup. If you would rather have me link to your pet's petpage, please let me know and I'll see what I can do.

#5 - Please check to see if your pet is already listed. Sometimes pets are moved around and I am not notified of it.

#6- Pets wearing a pet style to look like a unconverted robot will go to the crosspaint section. Additionally, any pets that are wearing a pet style for a colour other than robot but are still robots without the style will go to the crosspaint section.

**Don't bother the owners of the pets listed in this directory, please! These pets are not UFT/UFA!**

Get a Robot!

You want a robot neopet? They're not exactly ease to get, considering there's no paint brush! However, there are always a few ways!

The Robotification Zappermajig

The One-Use Robotification Zappermajig was a plot prize at for the Return of Dr.Sloth plot. If you haven't spent your points, you can check them out here.

The Lab Ray

If you have access to the lab ray, you have the chance to get a robot zap. This is how Bluto turned Robot and I believe it's probably the most common way to get a robot.

The Pound

Robots can be found in the pound just like any other pet. The problem is, they will not have their casing if they're pounded. You'll just have the insides. Transferring the robot will allow to keep your casings, so you could always ask if someone has a spare set.

FAQ

Can I add my robot to your directory?
You don't need permission to list a pet - just neomail me after you read the rules on the front page and I'll take care of the rest.

Is there a limit to how many robots I can list?
Nope! I'll list every robot that belongs to you, so long as they are not UFT/UFA/being labbed.

My friends are holding some of my robots. Is that alright?
I don't see a problem as long as it's ultimately yours and you're not adding random robots.

Will people ask if my robot is UFT if they're listed here?
This directory is for permanent robots. They shouldn't ask you, but I can't guarantee anything.

My robot is uncased. Is that alright?
Completely! My robot isn't wearing his casings either (in fact, he doesn't even have them anymore!), so I don't require casings. Besides, some robots look pretty cool without them!

Why can't I be added to the extra casing directory?
Unfortunately, because of a filter update, some usernames contain parts of a word that filter doesn't like. I can't update the page with these usernames on it at the moment. I'm sorry for the inconvenience.

Your inbox is full! Where else can I contact you?
My inbox is pretty big but if it's full, you can either toss an item at me or you can try neomailing any of my sides: snowflakes_side, dioji_, a_l_l_3_y, and lemongummiesbears. However, I do encourage you to wait or toss an item at me because my main is the fastest way to contact me.
