This is an updated, comprehensive site with various kinds of fonts, active font markers, and font-making resources. You will not find the silly photobucket man here!


General Rules Please do not claim fonts on any page or one that someone has made for you as your own. Even if they are edited by you. Many font makers have their own guidelines about whether you can submit them to font competitions, etc. Be sure to read the rules on any page before you start using the fonts.
