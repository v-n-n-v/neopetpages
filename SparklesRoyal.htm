Flash Free Golden Dubloon Menu

Hold up, a few things to note

  • The Golden Dubloon can error upon selecting "Eat ALL the food!" and consume your Dubloons.
  • The Golden Dubloon occupancy numbers can be a little glitchy. The restaurant could be full for longer than it should. There's currently no known workaround.
  • The Dubloon of the highest denomination will be used first.
