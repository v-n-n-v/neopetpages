Welcome!

Upon joining Neopets a billion years ago (or at least it feels like that long ago!), I discovered the Peophin. I still have the first Peophin I ever created. She was one of my first pets, period. I mean, they're literal sea horses. Does it get any cooler than that? My original main, now side, account has been dedicated to one of my favorite species. My gallery is Peophin themed. My lookup is Water Faerie themed because I hated all of the Peophin images (though I intend to rectify that soon). And my Neohome will be an underwater oasis for the five Peophins that live there. So the only thing left to do was create a directory for one of my favorite species. I hope I've done them justice. Welcome to...

Pretty Peophins (The Directory)!



NOTE: These pets are NOT up for adoption, do not neomail their owners for their items, neopoints or pets themselves!

  • Peophins with a strike through them (strike) have been frozen, may they rest in peace.
  • Peophins with bubbles after their names () are owned by me.
  • Peophins with asterisks after their names (*) are Unconverted. (This means their pictures will vary from the ones on this page.)

Join Us



Neomail applications to here to join.

Please include:
  • Pet's Name
  • Color
  • Species
  • Gender
  • Nickname (if they have one)
  • Converted or Unconverted


(Gender helps me determine if your pet is painted in the male or female version in the cases of Elderly and Royal pets. So if he's now a boy, but he's painted Royalgirl, please specify that.

Also note that I want the base color listed. A royal-colored pet wearing Zombie clothes is still a Royal and a Zombie-colored pet wearing Royal clothes is still a Zombie. Hope this clears up some confusion. Please don't hesitate to contact me with any questions you may have.)

Please do not submit your pet if you are currently labbing him/her and make sure to Neomail us with any major changes that may occur due to things beyond your control.
