About & Rules

About

Cosmos Café, founded by ravenaura in 2020 and given a reboot into its current form in 2022, is a guild dedicated to all forms of creation on Neo and encouraging artists, writers, and all other forms of creators to work on their projects and goals through badges and fun events. We're side-account friendly, public, have no activity requirements, and are supportive of all Neopians, including those of all backgrounds and the LGBTQ community.

Rules

  1. Always be polite. Insults toward anyone or their work, cliquey or elitist behavior, or support for hateful or exclusionary viewpoints will not be tolerated.
  2. Never claim someone else's work is yours. If you use any elements in your work that weren't created by you and aren't from official Neopets images (e.g. a character design you commissioned or stock photos), credit them.
  3. Badges can be used anywhere, but only on Neopets.
  4. Please refrain from using badges you did not earn.
  5. If you want to change what account you're in the guild on, you can transfer the rank (based on both badges and post count) from your old account to the new one. Neomail coolkaius to do this.

Ranks & Council Apps

Ranks

The requirements listed for each rank are either/or, not both. You can rank up by either earning badges by completing guild prompts and participating in events or by chatting on the guild boards, since both are participation.

  • Ally - Newbie
  • Noble - 25 posts or 5 badges
  • Knight - 50 posts or 10 badges
  • Royal - 75 posts or 15 badges
  • Divine - 100 posts or 20 badges

Rank Badges

You can also get shiny badges to put wherever you like for ranking up. These don't count for ranking up in themselves, but they are pretty.

Guild Board

We periodically make boards on the Guild Chat, as well as Art Chat boards to announce BC theme weeks or other events. Any member can create a board, not just council, and you'll get an exclusive badge for doing so.

Even if you don't have time to create a board, coming in, hanging out, and talking to people or even just bumping is a huge help for getting the word out.
