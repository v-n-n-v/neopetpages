Conker's Untaken Names

names she likes but won't be using herself uwu

Hi! I'm Conker, & I've been maintaining an Excel doc of all untaken pet names I like since at least 2018. But there's no way I'm ever going to create 1000+ pets LOL. So I'm releasing this collection to the public, & will continue to add more as I discover more ♥

If I got the name from another page (again I pretty much imported everything from my own spreadsheet lol), I will also give them credit. You can hover over the pet image to see what petpage or user those names are credited to; if they're from another untaken names page, then clicking on the name will bring ya to that page. There are also link-backs at the sitely section as well! I'm also happy to remove those names too, if preferred.

Feel free to NM me if you have any name requests, or suggestions to add to my page! I love hunting for good names :D
