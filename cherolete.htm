Wasteland

Overwhelmingly most clothes are glamour or feminine coded, and masculine or more gritty clothes are something people often ask for, along with more expressive options! Wasteland is a warm-tone alternative to Pirate, based on Apocalypse-genre media like Fallout or Mad Max. Warm, darker tones with a focus on very warm browns, more saturated than steampunk, with grit and dirt rather than a single colour base. Clothing items such as leather, face masks, goggles, bags, belts, tattered or dirty clothes. As if these pets have been living in a hot, sandy wasteland scavenging for supplies!

Vandagyres

Maraquan


My Maraquan Vandagyre concept is based on the Sea Angel, a tiny type of sea slug with a transparent body and wing-like appendages. The little nubbies on top of its head and its "wings" reminded me of the basic shape of a Vandagyre, and I felt this was a creature very different from existing Maraquan pets.

Korbat Day Set: The Beast


The Beast is a classic Neo character whose been around longer than most. It and the pteradactyls from ptera attack are Outsiders from the original Tyrannia plot, characters perhaps a bit forgotten in recent times. I adore the Shenkuu Hissi set, and I love that it gives the opportunity to make a unique pseudo-brush for a species that stocks. The Beast set is also a reference to the pre-conversion Tyrannian Korbat who had a cute little cone face and pointy head thing. Miss that guy. This set restricts the tail wings and head layers, but when I looked at other korbat sets, they often restrict wing layer, even if its just a "costume." so perhaps its not as important to keep the ears intact. however, if it is, I've always provided a concept with the ears!
