Limited Edition Neopets

Limited Edition (abbreviated to LE) is the term applied to nine species of Neopet that are not permanently available for creation in Create-a-Neopet. They are often (yet not always) released for creation in limited numbers on one day of the year, e.g. Poogles may be released on 19th September.

Tonu, Chomby, Lutari, Cybunny, Hissi, Koi, Kiko, Ruki, Poogle and Jetsam are all Limited Edition. Draiks and Krawk are also not available in Create-a-Pet, but they are distinguished with the term Restricted. Draiks and Krawk are not released on their Pet Days (Draiks were released in the year they were first introduced and also accidentally in 2009, but not otherwise). They must be obtained through other means, such as hatching a Draik Eggs or taking a Krawk petpet to the Fungus Cave. Grundo are also unavailable through Create-a-Pet but they are permanently available from the Space Adoption Agency (although it says 'adoption' you are in fact creating a new Grundo).

All of the Neopets mentioned above can also be obtained by the use of Morphing or Transmogrification potions, though due to the species' Limited nature some of these potions can be expensive. Sometimes creating one on their Pet Day is a preferred option.

So, how does the release work? Firstly, although a Limited Edition pet is usually released on their Pet Day, it is not guaranteed. Below you will find an incomplete history of LE releases. The green plus signs link to entries of my blog where I go into more detail such as when the release started and how long it went on for.

Typically, the Neopet will be released in small batches throughout the day, though this doesn't always begin promptly after midnight (sometimes there is a wait of hours). Sometimes these batches will be often and large enough that the available total will build up and the species will appear to be continually available, making it easy to create one, while other times they may run out in between batches. If this happens then you could try refreshing Create-a-Pet periodically and/or try checking the Help Chat or another board for alerts that people might post when the species becomes available again. At the other extreme, sometimes the batches build up so much that the species remains available for an additional 24 hours after their official Day.
