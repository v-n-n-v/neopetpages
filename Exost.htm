Welcome to Reborn, a non-application UFA site with goals on giving pound pets a chance at a second life by zapping them into desired colors and sent to their forever families. Not everyone playing Neopets has the neopoints for the inflation of paintbrushes or lab ray map pieces. Reborn strives to give hope to once forgotten and dumped pets a caring home and make users of Neopia, old and new, happy.

All pets that are selected for Reborn are well-named, with a capital letter, no numbers, spaces, or underscores. We display birthdays for those looking for a special one.


Rules:
  • I reserve the right to deny any adoption if I feel an account looks off to me for any reason.
  • Adoption from Reborn is meant to be a permanent part of your pet familiy!
  • Do not use adopted pets as trading fodder.
  • All pets listed are UFA, even before zapping! Feel free to send a neomail if one inspires to be your dream pet.
  • Pets With Clothes: All pets come with proper clothes. Sometimes their previews don't show them dressed.
  • You are welcome to change a pet's color or species after adoption!
