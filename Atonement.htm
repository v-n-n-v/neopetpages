Whispers of the Wind


Welcome to Whispers of the Wind, an untaken name site that hopes to help you find the perfect name for your pet. Here, you will find a variety of names including four letters, real words and made up names. So whatever the reason you may have for needing a name and whatever your taste in names, I hope that you will find this page to be a useful resource in your search.

To navigate, hover over the arrow beneath the birdcage on the left. This will reveal the navigation. Clicking on the arrow itself will bring you back to the top.

All the names listed on this page are linked to the pet's lookup to allow you to check the name's availability with ease. When clicking, you may come across a link where the name has been separated by a period. This is due to a filter error which only affects petpages. Simply remove the period to check the availability of the name.

Please note that while the names on this page are untaken, they may not necessarily be appropriate. I may have missed something when listing the names as I'm only human. Please use your common sense before using a name. If you think someone may be inappropriate, please tell me so I can take the name down.

If you can't find the right name for you, why not try requesting a name?
There is no need to mail me once you have taken a name. :)
