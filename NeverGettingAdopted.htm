Hello! My name is Rebekah. I wanted to make a little page to keep up with the pets I will be zapping and gifting/adopting out. I don't plan on making this a huge site or anything crazy, I just want to help others out where I can. In my neolife, I have met many wonderful people and friends. About NeverGettingAdopted

I found NeverGettingAdopted here in the pound back in 2015. I adopted him because I felt awful for him. Now he will be the host of this page. I love him to pieces, even though his name isn't considered WN and his color isn't always super desired. That is the beauty of neopets, I think. Everyone can find something they like about any neopet - no matter if it is mutant or royal.

The Plan

So, here, I will begin zapping pets and keep record of them here. I will list pets that have found their happy homes and whatnot. Thank you for taking the time to visit. If there is ever anything I can do to help you, send me a neomail here.
