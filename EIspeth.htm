welcome to mispeII, an untaken (i/L) name site!

i'm Lina (crystalunicorndreams) and i'm a huge fan of i/L mispells! ♡ i know they are not everyone's cup of tea but personally i love them for making permies with names that i have fallen in love with but are taken, like Elspeth here!

this is Elspeth's personally curated collection of untaken names, focused on pretty rn/rw/ms names. pets that are available will have a blank box next to them. i hope you like one (or a few!) of them enough to make into a new friend.

note: if you are going to be trading these,
please make sure the other person is aware that
it is an i/L name to avoid any confusion!
