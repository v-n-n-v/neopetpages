Puppet Paint Brush

A neutral-to-spooky paintbrush colour inspired by Pinnochio and puppets from the 1920s-1940s. Featuring a wooden body with slight inspiration from ball jointed dolls, this paintbrush colour comes with a cute euro-styled lederhosen outfit! I designed this to work well clothed or unclothed, but with a special emphasis on working well with existing wearables, to make it a great base colour for customisations and crosspaints.

The outfit parts consist of a shirt, lederhosen, and the strings. If you hover over the artwork, you can see the base style!

Mapleshading made an incredible Puppet Yurble design, check it out! Thank you so much!



Origami Korbat

Not much to say here - its an origami korbat! I love origami and I love korbats, so I just made a design that I felt looked good. The colours are based on one of my favourite colour combinations.

A fun fact, the origami pets are based on origami sculptures, complex figures made by dampening paper so it can be reformed without being torn or damaged. These sculptures are often much more complex than "traditional" origami.

8-Bit Eyrie

Alright I know I know, this isn't *actually* an 8-bit Eyrie - its technically closer to 16 or 32bit - but it matches the style well, and I actually did make a proper 8 bit version as well (at least, the colours). See below!

8-bit refers to a specific art style, based on 8-bit processing power computers and game consoles from the early days of computer games. Game graphics from this era in this artstyle had a limited, shared colourpallete of just 16 available colours.

This was followed by the much more popular and recognisable 16bit era of gaming, which gave such classics as Super Mario World, The Legend of Zelda and many, many more. A lot of the time, people say "8-bit" as a catch all for all pixel art - but true 8-bit art has the iconic limited colour pallette!



Stained Glass Paint Brush

This paint brush colour was inspired by the now retired Glass Paint Brush.

In 2002, TNT released the Tyrannian Paint Brush, and retired the Glass Paint Brush. All existing Glass pets were turned into Tyrannian pets, and the Glass Paint Brush item was made useless.

Although many of the old glass pets were visually identical to what we now know as Ice pets, I still think theres a space to explore for glass-like pets. And so, thus was born the Stained Glass Paint Brush!



Maraquan Vandagyre

Inspired by the amazing Maraquan designs made by Chanty I wanted to have a go at making my own Maraquan Design!

I chose the Vandagyre, and based it on the fluffy-browed and adorable Rock Hopper Penguin! There are a lot of maraquan pets based on aquatic mammals, so I felt an aquatic bird wouldn't be out of place either.

I tried to go for a more realistic design here, but with brighter colours and more simplistic body shapes so it still felt like a Vandagyre.
