    These are additional properties that will allow you to customize the header text and drop down menus(black wording).
      #template_nav - controls the text on the navigation.
      li.nav_image - changes text margins
      ul.dropdown - changes the background colour of the drop down menu, and also change the font and give it a border
      li:hover ul.dropdown a - change the colour of the links in the drop down menu
      li:hover ul.dropdown a:hover - changes the colour of the drop down menu links when you hover over them.

    Custom Requests

    Submit a request for your own unique creation with the theme of your choosing. Any user may submit a request, however all requests are taken upon my disgretion. All accepted requests are placed in queue and completed based on their time submitted and theme (Members of Quirky Aesthetic are prioritized above all else) - If are no longer in need of your request, please neomail me to have your request removed from the queue. Please be aware that I do require credit (buttons available) for all services rendered/used.
