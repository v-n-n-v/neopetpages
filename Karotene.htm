Below are a few tips to keep in mind when creating your shop/gallery:

color=#996600>
  • Keep it neat. A lot of extra pictures can take away from the overall look. Also, moving graphics tend to slow down the shop/gallery loading. Keep graphics limited to those that really relate to your theme.
  • Music adds a nice touch to the theme. However, it can also slow your shop/gallery loading. Therefore, I would not recommend music in a shop where you want to make sales as people cannot shop as quickly. Also, many people like to listen to their own music while playing neopets.
  • Always keep your prices visible. Use a good contrast colour to your background. The code for changing the colour is located below.
