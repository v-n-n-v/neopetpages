frequently asked questions

Q. Do you take requests?
A. Unfortunately, I do not. Please do not neomail me about requests as I don't have time for them.

Q. How do I save my layout? - I can't find the save button!
A. Try zooming out your layout by pressing the buttons CTRL / Command and the - sign. The save button should appear somewhere on the layout. If this does not work, sometimes adding spaces at the end of the code ( br's in brackets) helps getting the save button to appear.

Q. I see the save button, but I can't click it. What do I do?
A. Same as before, try zooming out your layout but pressing CTRL / Command and the - sign.
