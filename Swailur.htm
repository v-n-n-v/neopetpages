[CE03] Introduction

A Mallow Grundo has been a dream pet of mine since childhood. I will always be grateful to own Swailur!!

Swailur's all time favorite treat is A Cup Of Hot Borovan. He likes to float in it and drink it. He is permanently stained with Borovan in some way.

He was born in The Secret Laboratory (the one with the Lab Ray) in Y17 to an unknown owner. In Y24 he moved to Terror Mountain after finding his new owner.

He runs a café where he serves borovan, hot chocolate, coffee, and various desserts. Terror Mountain felt like the best choice to Swailur because there's a market for hot drinks.

Swailur makes all of the beverages and desserts himself. Despite having no arms or hands he can still lift things with levitation as if he had limbs. If you try to dress up a Mallow Grundo you'll find that they can "hold" things, so this is technically a canon thing.

[CE05] Relationships

OldManJinkies has a sweet tooth and is a regular customer to Swailur's cafe. Winter is their favorite season so they always feel at home in Terror Mountain. OldMan frequently drinks Swailur's hot chocolate, borovan, and coffee. They love the lofi music played in the cafe.

Swailur HATES supunu. He always wonders "Why be painted candy when you love gross things?" The combination disgusts him and he feels that supunu is a disgrace to desserts everywhere. supunu doesn't mind this sentiment as she prefers to avoid conflict when possible. The truth is supunu loves sweet and gross foods most.

THELID is always happy to keep Swailur's drinks warm because she's partially painted fire. She doesn't like visiting Terror Mountain much because she'd much rather keep warm to keep her flames from going out. Whenever Swailur is willing to travel is when they'll spend time together.

Bylvo the mallow Grundo and Snowflake the blue Bruce are friends with Swailur. Bylvo and Swailur have been drawn together which can be found in the gift art section. Snowflake visits Swailur's cafe in An Unexpected Win.

[DD04] Routine & Hobbies

Bakes pies, cakes, cupcakes, and muffins for Taelia. She lives a short walk away so Swailur is always up to trade goods. He visits Taelia everyday to see if he can bake anything for her spells. If he gets a snowball or Ice Cream Machine Coupon from her he donates it to the Money Tree. Whenever Taelia visits the café they have a chat about the visitors they get. Taelia always appreciates a hot drink from Swailur.

[DD05] Ambitions & Struggles

Goals: He wants to increase tourism to Terror Mountain by helping visitors escape the cold. Swailur hopes his café will surpass the Grundo Café at the Virtupets Space Station. Always diversifying his desserts to appeal to more people and more dietary restrictions is a passion of his.
Worries: He's worried that the locals would push for cold foods because they're commonly sold all over Terror Mountain. He's always afraid of running into a Snow Beast during his errands. Swailur often worries about tourists getting to the Peak safely due to the weather being worse than the Ice Caves and Happy Valley.

[MI04] Playlist

《 Sweetest Winter 》
1. Sweet by PhemieC
2. Snowcone by deadmau5
3. Snow Dayz by Casey Calhoun
4. ラ・ム・ネ (ra-mu-ne) by Snail's House
5. Hometown by Hokø & Dinis

This prompt was really easy for me because I've been collecting music since my earliest teen years. Song 1 references Swailur's focus on sugar and ignoring its downsides. Song 2 and 3 have winter vibes (Swailur lives on Terror Mountain). Song 4 has the sound of a soda can being opened, referencing Swailur's love of sugary drinks. Throughout the song are upbeat positive vibes which describe what Swailur's café is like to be in. Song 5 is the kind of music Swailur plays in his café.
