Welcome to Rainbow Requests

An art request site!

Welcome to Rainbow Requests. A little site for people to add requests for art, and for artists to fulfill them.

Welcome to the little art request site. A place for people to add and complete requests as they wish. This site is directly inspired by the Art Agency. With the Art Agency on a hiatus, I thought I'd like to bring up a request site in the mean time, for people to use, if they'd like. As doing and listing requests brought a lot of joy to me, so I hope it does to you too.
-Anna ⭐

Not sure where to start? That's okay! If you're an artist here looking to draw, take a look below and browse some of the pets that are looking for art! You can press the requests tab on the left to jump right down to them! Pick a pet and draw/make a creative thing for them in any way you like!
There's no minimum requirement for how good art has to be, any art is good art! When you're finished send off the finished piece to the owner of the pet, and aswell send it to me if you want to be credited for helping out :)

For people looking to Request art fill out the form below and send it in! and we will get you added soon (feel free to come bonk Anna on the art agency if you want a quicker update!)

    Frequently Asked questions

    Some answers for your questions.

    Q: How many pets can I request at one time?
    A: You may only have 2 requests listed here at any one time. As time passes and they're either completed or removed due to time passing, you can then request more. You could even ask me to take down one of your current requests to add a newer one instead! If you'd like!


    Q: How long will requests stay up for on this site?
    A: Requests will stay up until they have either been fulfilled twice OR 2 months has passed since the original date they were listed.


    Q: My request isn't listed on the site yet?
    A: Anna is lazy sometimes. Ask Anna to update the page, on the art agency board on the art chat.


    Q: I can't NM you, your inbox is full?
    A: Anna is lazy sometimes. Ask Anna to clear their mails, on the art agency board on the art chat.


    Q: Can I request art for apps/project pages for a future pet I want?
    A: At this point in time I'm gonna say yes, just please be serious about this and don't request if you plan to change your mind about pets you want in the future. I understand that plans change and people fall out of love with pets. But please we don't want to waste kind artists time.


    Q: I don't have a specific design/reference for my pet beside its custom, can I still list a request?
    A: Yeah of course! Just when submitting the form specify in the reference section that the pets custom is the reference! And maybe comment any specific details in the comment section too!


    Q: I'm an artist looking to draw for someone, is there any requirement for the art I draw for people?
    A: Absolutely not, this site is designed as a fun thing to jsut do if you want, there's no requirement to give a full body rendered shaded piece. All and any art is appreciated, even if its just a small doodle.


    Q: I want my requested art to have X/Y/Z elements and drawn /this/ specific way.
    A: Uh, i mean you can try. I will list these, with slight edits to wording. However though, i cannot guarantee someone will draw your specific request. as this is a site, where artist volunteer their time to draw for others! and some just want something chill and not super strict! All I can suggest that if you are after something specific please consider finding an artist who has commissions open, to draw your specific idea for payment (off neo of course)
