This page is (an attempt) at a complete list of:
- Free and dirt cheap site themes
- Free and dirt cheap battledome challengers
- All annual (still ongoing) events that award something
- Free stuff (other than dailies)
- Free NC and NC items

NC and NC items can be claimed on side accounts. If an NC item gives a Neopoint item it CAN ONLY be redeemed on your main account. Neopoints and Neopoint items CAN ONLY be claimed on your main account.

This page was created in Spring 2022.

Some of this information is widely known and some is obscure. I hope this page will help you have an easy source to write down everything in your own calendar.
If it's against the rules for me to share any of the following information, please Neomail me and I will remove it.

Free Training


● The Swashbuckling Academy offers free training to any pet that's species matches the current pet day. For example, all Aishas can receive training on January 3rd, which is Aisha Day.
● Find all pet days here. If a pet is level 40+ or they're enrolled in another course, they cannot receive the free training.

Daily Puzzle Prizes


● The Daily Puzzle is likely to have themed prizes on various holidays. New Year's Eve, December 25th, Neopet's own birthday, and many pet holidays are all likely to have prizes.









Contests


● Jellyneo has an article called "Creative Contest Prizes" that includes all of the contests.


● Mystery Picture Competition - If you can guess what a small piece of the picture is you'll get Neopoints and possibly an item prize.


● Art Gallery - This can be competitive because many submissions are sent in. To find the current month's schedule look for a post by tnt_contest_judge titled "[Month Name] Art Gallery Schedule" where you'll find upcoming themes, holidays, and random (themeless) art days.
● 10,000 NP, a rare item, and a trophy are given to everyone who gets their artwork published. You will be Neomailed if you win.
● Click here for a well written Art Gallery guide.


● Caption Contest - Create a clever caption for a random image. Starts towards the beginning of each month and submissions are accepted until around the 21st of that month. I couldn't confirm the exact dates. Avoid last minute submissions because there's a risk it will not be seen. Winners are selected at the end of the month.
● You can submit as many captions as you want.
● Depending on how well you place you can win NP, a trophy, a rare item, and the Caption Contest - Funny avatar. You will not receive a Neomail if you win, so you will need to check the contest page.
● The top 5 winners of every non-10th round win the Caption Contest - Hilarious avatar.
● I highly recommend Jellyneo's "Caption Contest" titled guide.


● Poetry Contest - Any style of poetry is allowed.
● Most pet holidays have contest days devoted to them.
● Winners receive 1,000 NP, a trophy, and a rare item. You will receive a Neomail if you win.
● Here is the official FAQ.
● I highly recommend Jellyneo's guide titled "The Poetry Contest".


● Storytelling Contest - A user starts writing a story, then it's up to other users to add continuations of that story.
● The schedule and amount of contributors can vary. A contest can last 5 days to over two weeks.
● At least winners are picked to contribute. Sometimes many people are picked.
● Winners receive 2,000 NP, a rare item, and a trophy. You will receive a Neomail if you win.
● Here is the official FAQ.
● I highly recommend Jellyneo's guide titled "The Storytelling Contest".

Monthly Events


● Once A Month - Visit Freebies For You! to get 2,000 NP total and one random food for each pet you have.


● 3rd Day Of Each Month - Half Price Day - All NPC run shops sell everything at half off. These days are much more competitive for restockers.


● 3rd Wednesday Of Each Month - Hidden Tower Discount Day - Everything sold at the Hidden Tower is 3% off IF your account is at least 5 years old.
● You can use this discount on your side account IF all Neopoints used come straight from your main account. I recommend using the Trading Post for sending Neopoints to side accounts.
● You can use one account to purchase one item from the Hidden tower per day. Jellyneo has a Hidden Tower Price Guide.


● 1st Saturday Of Each Month - Jellyneo's Rubbish Dump Avatar Event - Get the avatar by grabbing 8 pieces of rubbish and then refreshing until you find an avatar item. If you have the avatar you can help by refreshing and grabbing items.

August


● All Month - Blumaroo - Chef Bonju Avatar - Have the Mad About Orange Avatar as your active avatar. To get the orange avatar have 8 different items with the word "orange" in its name in your inventory and refresh. The orange avatar can be obtained all year.

● Jellyneo always has coverage of the free items on Halloween. Some Trick-Or-Treat bags become very valuable and some do not.

Other Freebies

● Island Builders App - If you link your game to your Neopets account and reach level 2, your Neopets account will be given a Lania Plushie which can't be gifted, sold, or traded. You can also get a prize for completing the Crystal Plateau Quests and Mystic Jungle Quests.


● Lampwyck's Lights Fantastic - Visit the shop to get an Empty Lantern. It can't be gifted, sold, or traded.


● Purple Site Theme - View any pet lookup of a purple Neopet with a purple petpet attached.


● Free Books - You can only get these if you have access to Flash. The solutions can be found on Daily Neopets's article called "Free Neopets Items". These books can't be sold, traded, or gifted, except Neopets Puzzles and Games can be traded. Your pets can read them.


● Every account (including sides) get one free NC gift from the NC Mall. If you haven't obtained it you'll find a link saying "Claim your FREE NC Gift" at the top of the NC Mall


● The Qasalan Expellibox - Auto Play Link - Has a chance of giving free NC.


● If you visit the Battledome and your active pet has no Battledome items equipped, then you'll be given a free Obsidian Dagger with this non-random event. It only works once per pet. I do not recommend abusing this as TNT may decide it's worth freezing you over.
