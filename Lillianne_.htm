Welcome to Zappy Accidents!

Hello and welcome!

Due to the holiday season and Liz's work schedule, Zappy Accidents is on Semi-hiatus. Once the new Year comes, I should have time to maintain the page again!

Zappy Accidents is a lab ray adoption agency founded by Liz as a way to give back to the community, after so many people have helped her get her own dream pets.
Now, with the help of a few friends, well named pets from the pound will be zapped to create wonderful combinations, to help others get their own dreams! All pets here at Zappy Accidents are completely free to a good home, but if you want to help support the cause, a small donation of NP to help keep UFA pets in the Neolodge is greatly appreciated, as are omelettes to help keep everyone fed and cheap petpets to zap (we've lost a few, but that's the cost of science)!
Pets here are zapped regularly, so if you see someone you like, make sure to adopt them before they change!

We also have access to the Petpet Lab Ray, and all pets will come with a random petpet attached!

Aside from zapping pets, Zappy Accidents also acts as a hosting site for others to list their UFA pets, sometimes you'll find something special!

The Rules!

✪In order to adopt from Zappy Accidents, your account must be at least 2 weeks old.
✪Pets adopted from Zappy Accidents may not be used as immediate trade fodder. If you decide you want to trade them in the future, that's fine, but please don't adopt simply to use them for trading.
✪In order to adopt from Zappy Accidents, you must fill out the following form and NeoMail it to the owner of the pet you wish to adopt. If you do not use the form, your request will be ignored.


Adoption Form

Why do you want to adopt this pet?: please be honest. If you just like the name and want to zap them yourself, that's fine!
