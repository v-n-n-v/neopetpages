Neopets

Here at Philomela, we collect pet character stories to share, not all set in Neopia, but all in the spirit of Neopets. Whether they are loose personality sketches or full multi-page stories, they are welcome here, even partially completed ones.

If you would like your pet's character story to be included in the directory, you must at a minimum have a character outline written up on a petpage, or part of a petpage, and you will need a 88x31px button for them. See the Sitely page for button resources. (We will now accept 50x50 headshots as temporary placeholders until you can get or make a button.) We prefer tales of permanent pets, of course, but we also have room for lab ray stories! Also, your site should be in English, or have an English translation.

Fill out the form below and neomail it to artemis1974. If you have multiple pets you wish to list, you can use one form, but please list them separately, so we can sort them by name, species and color, unless they share a petpage. In that case, please use the group form. Please keep us updated if you adopt out your pet or erase your petpage for some reason!

Why Philomela? In Greek myth, Philomela was a woman whose voice was silenced, so the gods changed her into a nightingale, with a beautiful song. Her character went on to be used frequently as a symbol in Western art and literature. My Pteri, Philomela74 was named after her with this project in mind.
