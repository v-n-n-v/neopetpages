___Neat Lutari Island Facts___

Lutari Island is a great floating mass that mysteriously drifts with the sea. Its location cannot be charted. It does not appear on any knowns maps.

Because Lutari Island's climate is warm, islanders sleep during the day and socialize at night when it's cooler.

Neopets on Lutari Island paint their bodies with fluorescent colours and adorn themselves with beautiful feathers.

    ___Lutari Details___


    Issue 17 | Issue 11

    This was in Issue 11 of the neopets magazine that described Lutaris.

    Characteristic:
    Lutaris are happy and playful critters. They are quite friendly and are always glad to make a new friend. However get one angry and let's just move on. But when they are having fun or just need a way to relax, you can either see them sliding down the icy slopes of Terror Mountain or swimming in Neopias many rivers and streams.

    Fur:
    The Lutari fur is very thick to help it keep warm in the cold environments where they can be found. However in warm places like the Lost Desert or Meridell the fur can be quite a hassle.

    Shape:
    Even though the fur is thick, the Lutari body is thin and slender, allowing it to swim and slide with remarkable speeds.

    Tail:
    The Lutari tail is very useful and versatile. While sliding down the mountain slope, it uses it tails to keep it balanced and maneuver while going at high speed. In the water it uses it's tail as a rudder, allowing to cut through the water while keeping control on where it's going.

    Claws:
    The Lutari claw is quite similiar to a Bori but still not quite that strong. However it still allows it to dig though snow and ice banks to find a warm place to sleep. When looking for food, it usually goes to the nearest body of water to look for clams or roots. Once it finds it, it uses it's sharp claws to open the clams or cut the roots.

    Ears:
    The Lutari ear can flatten backwards, covering the ears inside while still allowing it to go at high speeds. By doing so, the ears are protected from cold and pressure.

    Head:
    The head of the Lutari is actually the widest bone in it's body! Though the fur may make you think otherwise, when they found a narrow spot they'll try to squeeze their head into it. If the head fits then it's body should be able to fit though unless it has eaten too much.
