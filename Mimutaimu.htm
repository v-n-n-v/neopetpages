Welcome!

Last Updated on 5/16/2023

Hello all & welcome to the new Neopets Reference Guide. This guide was originally created & updated by ilkilkilk3. It has henceforth become outdated. Due to this fact I was inspired to continue the great work they brought to this community as there are so many more items & wearables that have been introduced to us throughout the years!

This guide will have it's main focus on Neopets items, wearables & pet references. As of right now it will not be including references in images, events, etc. If you'd like to see recorded references in those categories up to a certain point you can visit the original guide from the Navigation section to the right.

Items & Wearables will be listed in alphabetical order (Outfit set's in order of the character's name).

Items that are marked with a ★ were found by the original guide, items marked with a ◆ are up for interpretation (meaning either I or someone else found them to look like or referencing something, but that it is not entirely confirmed to be that), items marked with a ♡ are user submitted & items marked with nothing were found through my research.

If there is an item that you do not see here that is referencing something from pop culture (Books, games, movies, tv shows, anime, manga, comics, toys, etc.) please click on the Submit a Reference button & let me know the item's name & what it's referencing. All contributors will be listed on this page. Not every reference will be accepted if it doesn't exactly fit what I'm going for (ex; food resembling normal real life food) but doesn't hurt to try.

If you see a broken image or typo please let me know via neomail.

All affiliates & listers are welcome regardless of if it matches the theme of this page, send requests to me via neomail. You must have a link back button to be featured & feature a button to this page on yours.
