Welcome!

Upon joining Neopets way back in the day, one of the first pets I fell in love with and created was a Uni. I still have her to this very day. She's over 6,000 days old! I mean, what little girl doesn't want a unicorn as a pet?! I have an entire account dedicated to my Unis, plus one that lives on my main account. My gallery is Uni themed. My lookup is Nightsteed themed because Halloween Unis are my favorite (as you may have guessed). And my Neohome will eventually be a wide open space for the five Unis that live there to gallop and play in. So the next logical step was create a directory for one of my favorite species. I hope I've done them justice. Welcome to...

Unique Unis (The Directory)!



NOTE: These pets are NOT up for adoption, do not neomail their owners for their items, neopoints or pets themselves!

  • Unis with a strike through them (strike) have been frozen, may they rest in peace.
  • Unis with horseshoes after their names () are owned by me.
  • Unis with asterisks after their names (*) are Unconverted. (This means their pictures will vary from the ones on this page.)

Join Us



Neomail applications to here to join.

Please include:
  • Pet's Name
  • Color
  • Species
  • Gender
  • Nickname (if they have one)
  • Converted or Unconverted


(Gender helps me determine if your pet is painted in the male or female version in the cases of Elderly and Royal pets. So if he's now a boy, but he's painted Royalgirl, please specify that.

Also note that I want the base color listed. A royal-colored pet wearing Zombie clothes is still a Royal and a Zombie-colored pet wearing Royal clothes is still a Zombie. Hope this clears up some confusion. Please don't hesitate to contact me with any questions you may have.)

Please do not submit your pet if you are currently labbing him/her and make sure to Neomail us with any major changes that may occur due to things beyond your control.
