Unsure of value?

Make a value check board on the Neocash chat. Ask about the specific item, and traders who have seen it trade recently should belp you know what its going for. Don't be afraid to ask! Most Neocash chatters are nice.

Report your trades

Value guides are only accurate when we as a community report our trades. Help new and returning traders know what's up, and report your data on reporting boards. /~Owls normally has one up if you search for it.

Be kind

People matter more than Neocash items EVER will. So treat all traders with dignity and respect, and try to treat others like you would like to be treated. This will help make trading wonderful for all!

Need a Buddy? — The Neocash Chat is Here to Help! —

Are you new to the Neocash Chat? Have you recently returned? Do you wish you had someone to ask for advice or to help lurk your boards when you're starting out so you have an extra set of eyes on your trading? These kind neopians have volunteered their services to help those who need some extra assistance. Details can be found below.

Want to help? Send Chips (mikurubeam297) a neomail and let him know you're interested. He'll ask you some questions then get you added.
