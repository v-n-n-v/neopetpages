Welcome to Treasured Wishes. A brand new Gallery Directory managed and run by Luna.

I love browsing galleries, seeing what other people have come up with, and the items they've found that matches their theme. Some people are just so imaginative and creative that I want to be able to share that with others. I've not seen a gallery directory before, but that's not to say there isn't any. This is just the latest and hopefully I'll be able to keep updating as I'm pretty active.
