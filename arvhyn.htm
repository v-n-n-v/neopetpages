WELCOME

Welcome to the Lupe Directory! I absolutely adore Lupes and I was astonished to see there was no Lupe Directory around! For that reason, I decided to start one up. While my commission for a button was underway, a kind neopian gifted a button for the directory!

The only Lupes allowed to be listed are permanent pets! Please do not list your Lupe if you plan to adopt them out or trade them.

Note: I will list your Lupe with the UN you reached out with. If you want your Lupe to link to your main or your side account then you need to provide that UN to me in your message.

Once a month, I will go through the directory listings and clean up the directory! The first phase will be checking for uncustomized Lupes and in the second phase I will go to see if they are with a new owner. If it looks like a new owner, I will remove the listing.
