1) Introduction

BD2P

BD2P is a page dedicated to everything two player battledome.

The goal of BD2P is to save and grow the battledome. Many of us are waiting on TNT to restore the BD to its glory days. We have begged and pleaded for years. But we can help accomplish this. The best way to do this is to grow 2P and bring more exposure to leagues. League battling provides a challenging and exciting metagame.

There's a league for everyone regardless of your experience. Lets save the battledome!

10) FAQs

I want to do 2P in 2020 what are my options?

I highly recommend training for the League 54 build and playing the 9i variant. Other suggestions would be DDL, TPL, L97 and MBL.

What happened to the old leagues like L2-L24?

When the new battledome was made the old leagues no longer functioned properly due to the change in faerie abilities. Defense had to be shifted to weapons and pet builds. L97 was the pioneer. It was built around icicle and became a catalyst for the new leagues.

How do I join a league?

All you need to do is have a properly trained pet for the league. Follow the required stats and get legal weapons to battle with

What exactly is a league?

A league is a way to battle for 2P on neopets. There are agreed upon rules and pet builds but lots of room for strategy and creativity.

What are the best leagues for a beginner or someone with a low budget?

TPL is the easiest league to train for and many of the weapons are cheap buyables. L54 is also beginner friendly with a bit more training involved. 9i is a version of L54 that is very popular and budget friendly.

How can I get on the Battlers Directory?

Mail fishmas if you would like to be on the list of L54/9i battlers.

Do people battle outside of leagues for 2P

Very rarely. The leagues are designed to work out some kinks in the system and create the most competitive battling atmospheres

How do I find someone to battle with?

Just make a board in the battledome chat saying you are seeking challenges, the name of your pet and the league! Or you can find a pre-existing battle board and join in or ask any questions there.

I saw the weapon prices section. Can you tell me what xyz is worth?

Mail fishmas if you are trying to get a price check. I will consult others to find prices for item not listed in JN database history etc.

L54 Starter Guide

Getting an L54 Pet:

Step one is to get an L54 pet. You want a pet with: 54hp / 13-19 strength / 35-54 defense. You can train your own pet from scratch, adopt an L54 pet or adopt a pet close to league stats and finish training, or you can borrow a pet from someone.

Training an L54 Pet:

There is a few things to consider before training a pet. The most important of which is to not go over any of the stat limits. There is some leeway room for defense and strength as you can be anywhere within the allowed boosts (13 strength boost, 35 defense boost). HP is going to be your biggest concern. You want to have a plan for that. If you are using faerie cookies to train consider saving a dark faerie quest and using it at 51hp. You can also save a pink birthday cupcake and use it at 49hp. Faerie Queen Neggs are a pricey way to get 1hp you could use that at 53hp. You can try various other methods or just gamble gamble that you don't get a training bonus.

L54 Pet Adoption:

If you want to try adopting a pet first look at BD2P section for BD pet adoption. You can also search the pound yourself or make a board on the battledome chat or even the pound chat. Occasionally people post boards on the battledome chat about adopting out league pets some keep and eye out for those.

L54 Weapons

Building Your Arsenal:

You want a well rounded set with many options and variations. You want to get several constants, shields and dual duties. Reflectors are a bit more risky but you can look into those as well. It's good to have enough weapons that you can build different sets and change out weapons between battles to keep your opponents guessing

Setting Up To Battle:

When you battle an unknown opponent make sure you build your set to be able to attack with various icon spreads. Most of the time you will want to use many Dual Duties that both attack and defend. When you select what weapons to use try to stack various icon spreads or block specific combos. Think about what weapons work well together. Also consider there are 7 different icon types. You should aim to have a counter for most of them unless you are taking a more aggressive strategy.

Battling Known Opponents:

When you battle an opponent you should try to remember what weapons they use. If you are really serious you can even take notes during the battle. Having this history might help you be better prepared for the next time you fight. You can try to come up with new counters and adjust your set.

L54 Strategy

Using Constants:

Deciding how many constants to use depends on the rest of your set and your overall strategy. More constants means more aggression. But remember the defense boost in L54 is 1.5x strength. This can be a deterrent from getting too aggressive. In general most constants do best in 2. However many constants pair well a dual duty or shield as well. For example: maractite duck + frozen water daggers

Using and Countering Dark:

Dark is particularly harder to block than other icons due to shade not blocking it. Try using a shield or a Dual Duty for dark defense. For strategy you could attempt to play dark on turns you think your opponent will use shade. For example: wraith scroll x2

Using and Countering Frozen Combo:

Frozen Combo is one of the signature moves in L54. It is the frozen water daggers combined with the frozen wave scimitar. Given the defense boost 1.5x in this league this combo hits hard both offensively and defensively. One bit of advice is to make sure you have a move that can counter Frozen Combo in case your opponent is using it. For example: wheel shield + fiery battle duck

Using Dual Duties:

Dual Duties truly shine in this league. You can use Dual Duties with other DDs, with shields, or with constants. Try to stack various icon spreads and attack the icons your opponent is likely to use. You want plenty of Dual Duties in your set. For example: twisted dark dagger

Earth and Light Icons:

The abilities halitosis and static cling are both pretty popular in this league. So consider have weapons that block earth and light or both. Also be careful not to use too much of either of these icons. If your opponent is using an ability consider blocking that icon type as it is more easily predicted. You might want to bring a weapon that blocks both light and earth. For example: deadly pugio

The Battle Ducks

Icy Battle Duck can pair with frozen water daggers to beat the frozen combo heads up. It is a mid grade level constant and can be strong if used correctly

Fiery Battle is another mid grade constant. The only danger with using this weapon is that the popular twisted dark dagger can shut it down pretty hard. Have a plan for that

Maractite Battle Duck is an upper grade constant. It plays well with other water icons especially another Maractite Duck. It can also be used with Frozen Water Daggers or Frozen Wave Scimitar. And like all the ducks can be played with a shield or healer.

Species Weapons

The most likely species weapons you will face are the Silver Korbat Amulet from korbat opponents and the Kayla's Cloak from zafara opponents. Be ready for those weapons. Also don't be surprised if your opponent chooses not to use them at all. The cloak shuts down frozen combo quite well especially if used with static cling. The Silver Korbat Amulet combines 3 hard to block dark icons with 3 hard to block fire icons while blocking both earth and light itself. Its a tricky weapon but when used effectively it can be powerful.

Using and Playing Vs Meditate

Shade is a bit more popular than Meditate due to it being more reliable. However you should be ready to both use and play vs meditate. For this ability to work you must act before your opponent. It helps to plan ahead for possible moves you could do and it helps to try to go a bit faster than you normally would. When playing vs meditate don't panic. Try to select the best move and hopefully also act first. When used perfectly meditate heals 4hp two rounds in a row.
