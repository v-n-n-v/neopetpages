applications

If you want to adopt one of the pets that is listed as status: UFA, just send me a neomail stating that you agree to the following conditions:
- I plan to keep the pet as a permie
- I have no plans to paint the pet (doesn't apply to basic pets)
- I have no plans to morph the pet into a new species
- I have no plans to trade the pet, and
- If I change my mind about the pet, I will send a neomail to ildacoo before pounding.

Please also include the name of the account you want the pet on! Finally, please only message me about pets who are listed here as status: UFA (unless I've recently posted something contradictory to the boards).
