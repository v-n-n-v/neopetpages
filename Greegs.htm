Tarla Tracking

This page was last updated on 2019-07-05.

About Tarla's Toolbar

Welcome to the Tarla-Tracking Page! This is an ongoing project about a little-known link that was almost forgotten long ago in Neopets history. If you go to your Quick Stock page, you will see something at the top that looks like this:

Quick Stock

Jump to: Closet | Quick Stock | Equipped Items | Stamps | TCG Album | Item Gallery
Item Event Log | Neopets Tool Bar | NC Mall Album

The link highlighted above used to lead to a place to download the Neopets Toolbar for your browser a couple of years ago. The toolbar would alert you to item giveaways. Since Neopets stopped updating the toolbar, the link to download it has been taken down. Once you click the link, you will see Tarla.

This is the same Red Ixi who runs the Shop of Mystery on the top of Terror Mountain.

You might not recognize her at first because you are looking at an older image of her. It gives you a feel of how long ago Neopets stopped updating this link.

Since we can no longer download the toolbar, Neopets has allowed us access to Tarla's prizes as Tarla's Non-Toolbar Treasures. Unfortunately, there is a catch: we have nothing to alert us when Tarla comes by to give prizes!

Catching Tarla

If Tarla is not going to be here at all today, you will see the message below:


Tarla is busy with her other chores today. Check back tomorrow to see if she's back!

when you visit: //www.neopets.com/freebies/tarlastoolbar.phtml.

If you see the message below, you know Tarla will be coming by today:


Tarla is at the prize warehouse right now. Check back later to see if she's here!

The message above means that Tarla will be coming by at four random times today, each time within one of four 6-hour periods, to give out prizes. If she has already visited during a 6-hour slot, even the last one, this message will still appear until the end of the day. Don't let it trick you to keep on searching for her.

Tarla stays for 15-16 minutes at a time. In the first THREE (3) MINUTES she arrives, she will award you the Main Prize of the day. Everyone who finds her within the first three minutes of any of her visits that day receives the same Main Prize. If it is a Secret Laboratory Map Piece, everyone will receive the same piece. Past Main Prize items range from Books to Paint Brushes to Stamps to Furniture to Battledome items to Food.

When you catch her during that time, you will see Tarla holding a box with many items appearing and disappearing very quickly inside it for a few seconds.

Then, you will be awarded the Main Prize of the day.

Afterwards, Tarla starts to leave and drops some things on her way out. If you missed her in the first three minutes, you have a chance to pick up a random rare item she drops while she is still lingering in the next 12-13 minutes.

After several items appear and disappear very quickly for a few seconds, the random rare item you have picked up is revealed to you.

Tarla visits FOUR (4) TIMES A DAY between the following intervals: She will appear between 10 minutes after the hour to 45* minutes after the hour during the following hours. So, check for Tarla at (all times are in Neopian Standard Time):

Interval

Hours

I

1:10AM-1:45AM, or 2:10AM-2:45AM, or 3:10AM-3:45AM, or 4:10AM-4:45AM, or 5:10AM-5:45AM

II

7:10AM-7:45AM, or 8:10AM-8:45AM, or 9:10AM-9:45AM, or 10:10AM-10:45AM, or 11:10AM-11:45AM

III

1:10PM-1:45PM, or 2:10PM-2:45PM, or 3:10PM-3:45PM, or 4:10PM-4:45PM, or 5:10PM-5:45PM

IV

7:10PM-7:45PM, or 8:10PM-8:45PM, or 9:10PM-9:45PM, or 10:10PM-10:45PM, or 11:10PM-11:45PM

* Her visits can start 45 minutes after the hour, but they can end as late as 59 minutes after the hour.

The time Tarla arrives between each interval is random, except that she will only start appearing 10 minutes after the hour. She will also not appear at the hour of 6 or 12 (AM and PM). The best way to catch her for the Main Prize is to refresh the page each minute during the interval she has yet to appear in. For example:

If you're looking for Tarla in between 1:10 PM - 5:59 PM (the 3rd interval), you would start refreshing every minute from 1:10 PM (so refresh at 1:10 PM, at 1:11 PM, etc.) to 1:45 PM. If Tarla has not shown up, you start again refreshing every minute from 2:10 PM until 2:45 PM. Then, again at 3:10 - 3:45 PM, 4:10 - 4:45 PM, and 5:10 - 5:45 PM. If you keep checking this way, you find Tarla sometime along the way.

Another way to catch Tarla is to refresh on the Help Chat NeoBoard OR the Game Chat NeoBoard and wait for someone to make a "Tarla" post topic. This does not guarantee that you will get the Main Prize or any Prize at all, because the user posting may have caught her too late during her visit.

If you have already collected a Main or dropped Prize for the day and catch Tarla again, you will see the message below:


Tarlas says:
I can only give you one prize per day!

You will have to wait until tomorrow to receive more prizes. You are not allowed to use your side accounts to visit Tarla as this is earning items. Please, collect items only on your main account!

Collecting Tarla Data

I will no longer be keeping track of the times Tarla comes and goes. I will not be keeping track of which prizes she gives out each day. I have concluded:

  • Tarla's start time is always somewhere in between :10-:45 minutes after the hour, so her end time will never cross over into the next hour.
  • Tarla will never appear anytime between 12-1 or 6-7 AM or PM.
  • There is no way to predict which day Tarla will appear and which prize Tarla will give out in any day... Random is Random!

Here is what you can do to help out:

  1. If there is an item missing on any of the above lists or information you have about Tarla that I do not have, please let me know. NeoMail ranfanlan.
  2. Most of my data collection comes from the Help Chat NeoBoard and the Game Chat NeoBoard. If you catch Tarla, please post ASAP (as soon as possible) to help other users get prizes too. If you catch her late and miss her Main Prize, still post.
  3. Post any prize you received on the Help Chat NeoBoard or the Game Chat NeoBoard.

Again, this is all completely voluntary.

Good luck catching that Red Ixi!
