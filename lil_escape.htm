Welcome

THE PETPETPET DIRECTORY OFFICIAL OPENED AT JUN. 3RD, 2006

Hey the_freebie_man_, nice to see you here. x3 You are very welcome in this great directory of those small bugs, known as petpetpets. I will be listing them alphabetically by species. If you've got a petpetpet, you'd like added, please read below. If you are having any trouble with your petpetpet or don't know how to get or attach one, you can visit my Petpetpet Guide for help and info about those. (:
Also, I love feedback! xD You should always feel free to send me a neomail telling me what you like, and may not like about this site, so I can make worse better, and improve it. (:

NOTE: Since I no longer get online on neopets as often as I used to, I might not be able to read all neomails before they get deleted. -.- So, if there has been like a month with no answer from me, feel free to send me another nm. It's not because I don't like you, I just don't get all of them. Thank you.




How to Join


If you'd like your petpetpet signed up on this page, just neomail me saying so. Please, do also the name of the pet and petpet, who owns the petpetpet and the specie of the petpetpet, using this form:

Petpetpet Specie: Cooty
Pets Name: Zichee
Petpets Name: Daisy

When I recieved your mail, I'll reply as soon as possible, when I've signed up your petpetpet (please have patience, I may not be able to update every day). :)
If your petpetpet is in the directory, please link back with one of the link buttons I've made, so more people can know about the directory. :3 -pokes left-
