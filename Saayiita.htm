Requests

Requests are: OPEN

Send me a neomail with: Your name, what sort of font you want (words/picture), what avatar you are planning to use with it and/or the color scheme you want, extra information about what you do/don't want in the font

Say "it is strawBERRY nice to meet you" somewhere in there so I know you paid attention ;P

It is a lot easier when I have an idea of what you want. Very vague requests will probably get you a very vague font.
I can also edit one of my fonts for you! Same rules as above ;)

I reserve the right to say yes or no to a request.
