Welcome to Bori Burrow! A small Bori directory that I (You can call me Nidrew, or Andrew, whichever you prefer) decided to start, mostly as a small hobby. As some of you may know, or will learn after you've seen this directory, Bori are my favorite species! It's been that way for years, and it all started with the pet that's actually hosting this directory, abismall. It is thanks to him Bori are my favorite species.

I've been in Neopets for quite some time, and at the point of writing this, i only have one pet goal left, so I started to wonder, what should I do once I get that goal? Well, after some time of thinking, I came up with a small Bori directory. I was rather surprised by it, and excited to list my pets! Unfortunately neither the directory or the owner seemed to be active. This, though unfortunate, gave me the idea to start a directory of my own, this way I would have something new to do in Neopets once I've achieved all my pet goals!

Anyway! I'm not here to tell my story, but to introduce the directory, right? So, that's what I'll do! At the top of the page you'll be able to find a small navigation bar where you'll be able to see all the listed Bori, how to add your pet, and some other information you might find useful! Hope you enjoy the directory! And remember, the pets listed here are permanent pets, so do NOT message any of the owners asking for trades, that is rude and is not the purpose of this directory! The purpose is to showcase all the great Bori in the site!

So, what do you say? Want to list your Bori?

Origin

Bori are one of many different species that exist in Neopia. As a general rule Bori are quiet peaceful creatures who are rather timid. They originated from the frozen region of Terror Mountain, but can now be found all over the world.

The Bori species is native to the Ice Caves, and it was originally introduced to Neopia on the 13th de of Collecting, Y6 (Or 13th October, 2004). Their release was accompanied by the Hannah and the Ice Caves Plot, in which their story was officially presented.

The bori originally came in the 4 basic colors, with the later addition of the elusive Ice Bori. This pet could only be obtained by downloading the Yahoo!/Neopets toolbar. This gave you access to morph one of your current pets, or create a new one from scratch, in order to obtain a basic or an ice bori. This is the reason why, until recently with the introduction of Premium Perk, Ice Bori used to be quite rare and valuable.
