Welcome to BoB!


Welcome to BoB, or Brightvale on Toast!

You might be wondering how in the world the acronym "BoB" can come from the phrase "Brightvale on Toast". Well, King Hagan has earnestly elected to tell you all about it. You can actually see him at the bottom at the page. Here what he has to say...
~ "Toast has a silent, liquid and invisible B in front of it! That's common sense."

History of BoBers


Brightvale on Toast was founded back in the summer of 2006, when the first ever Altador Cup showed up. Us Brightvale supporters had to find a place where we could flaunt our intellect and be able to support each other throughout the tournament. Back in AC I, the group wasn't called "BoB", but the UBF, or United Brightvale Front. Sometimes, our boards were simply titled, "Brightvale" Boring, huh?

Because of our remarkable trait for being drawn to toast and any other type of bread, we soon decided on a new name for our Brightvallian chat group. It was, of course: "Brightvale on Toast", where the Toast had a Silent, Liquid, and Invisible "B" in front of it, making the abbreviation "BoB". Yes, THAT's how quirky we are, but it's what makes us unique and different from the other teams. B)

Now, during AC II, we faced some stiff competition from another Brightvale chat group, the MOTES. We soon merged the two groups, but still kept our reknowned name, and have kept it for the last four years with pride.

BoB isn't JUST a chatgroup. We're MUCH more than that. We're a family, with people who will strive to help anyone that is need of aid. We band together and get through thick and thin, and we can always rely on each other for the biggest or the smallest of things.

We can currently be found either on the Altador Cup boards, or the Virtupets boards. We hope to see you there, Brightvale supporter or not!

Toasty Etiquette


These are just some basic rules that you need to follow if you want to be a welcome part of BoB. They were made by a lot of the BoBers together, exemplifying our team spirit even more!

1. Stay Toasty: This means that you must be courteous to everyone that hops into the BoB boards, whether they are a part of our family, or not. Brightvale has a long history that includes culture and diplomacy, with visitors coming from all over Neopia. Hospitality, just like toast, is for EVERYONE.

2. Hail King Hagan: As proud citizens of Brightvale, we represent the wise old King both at BoB and abroad. Let's make him proud! Work hard. Play fair. Eat toast.

3. The Toast Pops Together: Whether Wheat, Cinnamon, White, Whole Grain or Raisin; topped with butter, Jam or Nutella; eaten alone or used to create sandwiches: toast is indeed the perfect food. And even with all our differences, together we strive to be the perfect team. It doesn't matter if you Sling Slushies, Make Some Noise, Shootout, or Yooyuball to the best of your own ability, you can still become one with the toast. No slice is taken for granted.

4. The New Piece of Toast: Whoever posts the first post on Page 24, is entitled to make the new board, according to the Board Rules listed in the next section. If you do not wish to make the board, please say so on the boards, and someone else will make it.
