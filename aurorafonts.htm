    welcome, welcome!

    Hello there! My name is Mandie and I am the owner/creator of Aurora Fonts. Aurora Fonts was established on August 6th of 2014. This site is the home of many premade fonts which I have created for you to use. I also have provided some extra templates for you to create fonts on your own, and offer request services.

    rules

    When using my fonts, there are a few rules that I require you to follow:
    • do not claim my fonts as your own. just don't do it.
    • if you feel you must get one of my fonts rated, please just give credit to me.
    • even if you edit the crap out of my font, I still made it, so you must still give credit.

    requests

    None of my premades are exactly what you like? No problem. I will happily make a personalized font just for you. Just fill out the forum below and send it to me via neomail.
    When requesting a font, please be specific. I cannot stress this enough. I have every right to just ignore a request when I have no idea what you want. Please understand.
    *It is important to note that I make all of my fonts in Safari on my Mac, so my fonts will be best suited for this browser unless otherwise requested.
