8: Reading Scrolls - Take Three!
This time, your light was exactly the same - so you didn't need to see it. First, you took all of the colours that you gained from the second crystal, and put them onto the tablet as before. So this time, you could ignore all the colours. So cross them all out. You were only interested in the blank spaces. You needed to read all of the scrolls (from left to right, top to bottom) for your blank symbols in order to get the location of the white scroll for that symbol.

For each of the blanks you had to read a matching blue, red AND yellow scroll. Now at the time, we thought it was random which order to read these in. It wasn't until the solution came out that we learnt the truth.


Apparently the chef got bored and started to paint his toenails. You were supposed to read the scrolls in the order that his toenails were painted, so in this case blue, yellow and then red. This revealed the location of the white scroll. Read a white scroll that matches your symbol. You could then move on to the next blank symbol (and the chef's toenails would have changed). However, it seems almost no- one discovered this, and it was only a timesaver. It was perfectly possible to read the scrolls at random and move on.
