Intro

Former home of Maureen - a UC Value Guide run by Asellus.



Dear PC,

Maureen's guide has been retired and it is so hard to know what to write here! On January 17, TNT announced that NC UCs were officially being released, but they threw us a curve ball – all UCs were being converted. This means that UCs would no longer be tradeable, and while UC owners get an NC item to give pets a UC look, that item is separate from the pet. This means UC tiers and UC trading is over – and the era of UC tier guides is over too.

Hosting the tier guide only to oversee the end of UC trading entirely definitely has me feeling some kinda way, and I know for many of us we all definitely have feelings here. The PC will still be around but UC trading was a huge component of the PC. It is fair to say that the PC will never be the same as it was before TNT cancelled UCs. As we close one chapter and move to a new era, I want to say two things:

1) The PC is an incredible community. Don't ever undersell your contribution to neopets.com – remember that pet trading, the pet trading economy, shuffles and frenzies – these were *user driven activities created by and for users*. That is INCREDIBLE – it means one of the busiest aspects of the site was created by site users, for site users! This organic creation of site engagement is worthy of noting – it shows how much the PC truly loves neopets.com.

2) I have played for over 21 years and been a member of various neo communities, but none have held my heart like the PC. The PC has some of the most genuinely kind, caring people on the site. I am going to miss this community SO much! It's not going to be the same – and I am genuinely emotional over it.

Continue to shine bright, PC!

Love,
Asellus

Sitely
