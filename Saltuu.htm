Standard Battledome Keywords

HSD - Means Health, Strength, Defense. By adding these together, you gain your BD stats.
Even - Your HSD are trained together and within 3 points of each other.
Labbed/Uneven - HSD is zapped through the labray or RE's; the stats are several points different from each other.
Capped - Strength and Defense are maxed at 750.
Untrainable - Stats are not evened out and requires leveling.
E/T - Even/Trainable.
