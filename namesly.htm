Cecilia's Future Friends / Name Inspiration

Welcome: to Cecilia's Future Friends. This petpage helps users to find an untaken name perfect for their future pet.

Note: As of June* 2024 this page is still active and regularly updated. Over 800 new friends have come from this page!
* I am having a career change with extensive training (little to no neo access) and a cross country move between the months of June 2024 to August 2024. I will not be updating the page during this time. Thank you for understanding.

Most of these names with be made up by yours truly. Or other helpful neopians. A special shoutout to Tal who has donated many names to this page.

I have organized my names into 3 categories: Feminine, Masculine, and Neutral.

Pronunciation is up to you but I will add suggestions in parentheses after each name. In the parentheses I will add any "need to know" details about the origin.

Please, neomail me if you use a name! Please consider this as I work full time and have a life outside Neo! This page is a hobby in my limited free time but I still want to provide Neopia with an amazing list of (updated) untaken names!



Acronyms:
RW = Real Word
RN = Real Name
Pl = Plural
MS = Misspell


Disclaimer: No names will contain underscores or numbers unless extremely fitting of the name. This is my personal preference, there is nothing wrong with pet names that contain underscores or numbers.

This page was created on December 29th 2022.
