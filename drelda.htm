welcome to bejeweled everything from bullets to layouts

Well hello there! This nifty site is Bejeweled. As an extension from Jewel, Bejeweled is a resource and premade site aimed to providing whatever you need to spruce up your site. We have everything from bullets to dividers to premade layouts and much more. My name is Jewlz and I am open to suggestions for premades as well as comments or questions. My inbox is open 24/7 so don't hesitate to contact me at any time! Bejeweled has been open since March 12, 2014.

What editing program do you use to make graphics?

I use Gimp 2.8.10. I would LOVE to have Photoshop, but it's rather pricey and I'm used to Gimp now.

Can you teach me how to make... ?

Personally, no. I do have my tutorial site up on this page. You can learn how to make petpages, guild layouts, and tons of buttons. If you need more, try our sister site Beetle or Scarab.

I'm only using your coding, is that okay?

You are free to use the layouts how you like. I only ask that you credit me and do not edit/alter any of the graphic images.

I think I've seen someone using your content without credit. What should I do?

Simply neomail me with the information on where you have seen it improperly credited and I will take it from there.

Am I supposed to use your other sites when making a layout/graphic?

Of course not! They are there for your convenience. Use them however you would like. If you choose not to, that's okay!

Can we make something together?

I have recently opened a collaboration section on Jewel. If you would like to make something together check out that page and neomail me info.

Have a question you'd like to add? Please feel free to contact me.
