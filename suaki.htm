questions

got some questions? hopefully i answer anything that's not clear here. if not, feel free to mail!


do you take custom font requests?

requests are open for a very limited time! go to the request section on the sidebar and read the rules and notes! afterwards, i have no intention of having a regular schedule for font requests because i don't want to overpromise. i might open them again if i have a lot of time, but it really depends on my schedule irl. i will always be open to taking suggestions though! any suggestions i take will be premades open to everyone on this site.


i'm new to neopets. what are fonts here?

fonts refer to the styles that people can customize their posts when they're on the neoboards! if you browse around, you can see that a lot of users post with different styles, which means they're using a font. some just change the color of their text, some have song lyrics, others have links where they advertise their pages - there's a lot of ways to customize. for a lot of people, having a font shows their personality more on the boards, and they might purposely have a font to match their favorite avatar or even their active neopet that shows when they post!

this is what your default post on the neoboards would be without any fonts applied or anything.

this site offers premade fonts (styles) that you can apply to decorate your posts on the neoboards!


how do i use these fonts?

go to your neoboard settings if you want to change your font and avatar for posting on the neoboards. for this site, you'll see a preview image of a font that i've made along with two textboxes. the contents of the first textbox should be copied into the "Enter NeoHTML:" section of your settings, while the second textbox is meant to go in the "Enter NeoSignature:" section.


i don't see anyone with fonts when i browse the neoboards?

check your neoboard settings! there's options to show and hide fonts that can be toggled whenever. fonts aren't for everyone and that's okay - some people can find certain styles hard to read and some people chose font colors that aren't exactly easy on the eyes for others, so there's an option to turn them off. i'm not sure if it's off by default for new accounts, but if you want to use and see fonts, check your settings!

are these fonts free?

yes! all of these premades are free to use :)


um some of the fonts don't look like the preview picture?

hould be close to the preview, but there may be slight differences between browsers. for the older fonts i have here, they may also appear bigger/smaller than the previews simply because those preview images were taken a decade ago. if you notice a gap when you're using any of the older picture fonts, try setting the size to 4. you can do that by looking for s=# (typically s=1 on older fonts), and instead, edit it so it's s=4 which means setting the size to 4.

however, if you notice something looks really wrong or if you just need some additional help, please let me know! i will try my best to be helpful and keep things updated, but some (esp older fonts) might slip through.


what's the whole alt+255 thing and dots in the picture section?

so a lot of block/picture fonts rely on proper spacing, but we can't just use a regular space multiple times in a row. that's always been how it works, and our workaround is using a special space character, and if you have a number pad, that space character appears when you press alt+255. the caveat is that this character is not pasteable, so if we tried to post our font code for others to use and it included that space character, it would be all messed up. a lot of fontmakers (myself included) just used a different symbol like a period and . would indicate to a user that they need to replace it the special space character themselves. if you want even more details, i would recommend isaac's guide!

when i came back from hiatus, i noticed that these spaces were pastable now. i'm not sure when or what exactly changed, but i guess it's a non issue now? i went ahead and removed the periods from my picture fonts, so they shouldn't require any extra work other than the normal copy/paste now.


will you make me a font if i gift you an item?

nope! i appreciate the sentiment, but it's actually against the rules for me do this for profit. for more information, you can check out the editorials from neopian times issue #250 and issue #347. i don't make these fonts expecting people to give me stuff or anything - please just enjoy them if you use one!


do i have to link back here on my userlookup if i use a font?

while that would be appreciated, it is definitely just optional! i don't require anyone to explicitly give credit for using my fonts - all i ask is that you never take credit for them or imply in any way that you created them if anyone ever asks.


what is a nb pen? do i need one?

a neoboard pen is a neocash item that allows you to have an extra 30 characters each for your neoHTML and neoSignature. it will also act as another set of settings/preferences, so you can essentially "store" another font if you ever wanted to switch it up on the neoboards. example: say you like a font that goes with a blue avatar, and one that goes with a purple one. if you have a nb pen, you could set your default avatar to the blue one and match your font to that, while you can name your nb pen "purple" and set the purple avatar to it along with the purple font. on the neoboards, with every post you can choose to post with your default or the purple pen and switch it up!

anyways, it is completely up to you if you want to purchase one from the nc mall! for my fonts, i have a variety of both nb pen and non nb pen fonts, so you definitely do not need one to use my fonts!

picture fonts

the other thing i've noticed is that spacing a bit weird now compared to how it was before. line heights can be weird and it's device/browser specific but if you're on chrome desktop, you're likely fine. for mobile, it's not as bad when the picture is size 4, so that's why all newer picture fonts will be size 4 (s=4), and i've switched some older ones to that as well because i used to almost always use size 1. if you don't like a huge font, please feel free to play around with sizes! more on this in the questions sections if you're curious.

however, if you do run into any problems or if i'm outdated, please let me know! i love working on picture fonts and i would love to see more of them around on the boards :)
