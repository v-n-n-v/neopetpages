PAINTING YOUR NEOPETS

Welcome to the page all about painting Neopets! I'm compiling an up-to-date list of all the available colours of Neopet, including how many Neopets are currently available in each colour. This should help people trying to decide what colour to paint their pet, work out whether they can use their premium perk to change their current pet's colour, and predict the next rainbow pool releases! Please note that premium perks are not affected by restrictions such as colours only being available at the Lab Ray - when using your perk, all colours are available if you use the method that allows you to switch colour as well as species.

Morphing Potions

You can buy Morphing Potions which change your pet's colour (and species) from Kauvara's Magic Shop. Any potion called a Morphing Potion is likely to change the colour of your pet. To use a Morphing Potion, have the potion in your inventory and click on it to bring up the action menu. Select "Use on a pet" a choose the pet you wish to morph into the new colour and species. Do NOT click feed the potion to your pet if you have a Grarrl or a Jetsam - the potion will not work!

Transmogrification Potions

Transmogrification Potions are a special type of Morphing Potion which will turn your pet into a Mutant. You can only get these through Random Events, buying from other players (see where to buy Paint Brushes, below), or occasionally from winning one at the Neocola Machine. You use the potion in the same way as a Morphin Potion and again make sure not to accidentally feed it to your Grarrl or Jetsam!

Magical Chia Pops

There are also Magical Chia Pops which can change the colour of your Chia! Feed them a Magical Chia Pop and your Chia will turn the colour of the pop. You can buy the pops at Super Happy Ice Fun Snow Shop or as usual from other players. Some colours are cheaper than others and some are associated with avatars. Magical Chia pops only work on Chias - feeding them to another pet won't change their colour (but will count towards their gourmet food count, as it will with Chias). If you want a Garlic Kiko or Jubjub (or Coconut Jubjub), you'll need the Lab Ray, the Fountain Faerie or the Premium Perk. Chokato pops do work on kikos, however.

Paint Brushes

You can't search for Paint Brushes on the Super Shop Wizard. You can buy Paint Brushes from The Hidden Tower and from other players on the Shop Wizard, the Auction House and the Trading Post, as well as being able to win them in the following places:

  • Alien Aisha Vending Machine (needs Nerkmids, awards paintbrushes r150 and lower)
  • Anchor Management (Maraquan and Pirate Paint Brush, Pirate Petpet Paint Brush)
  • Art Gallery competition (many different Paint Brushes and Petpet Paint Brushes in the prize pool!)
  • Caption Contest (many different Paint Brushes and Petpet Paint Brushes in the prize pool!)
  • Faerie Caverns (awards Faerie Paintbrush 1:1,000,000 chance).
  • Fruit Machine (can award ANY paintbrush!)
  • Grave Danger (can award Zombie or Maraquan Paintbrushes, and Maraquan Petpet Paint Brush)
  • Lost Desert Scratchcards (currently unavailable until converted to Flash, previously could win Lost Desert Paint Brush)
  • Meridell Rubbish Dump (Blue Paint Brush and Silver Paint Brush)
  • Poetry Competition (many different Paint Brushes and Petpet Paint Brushes in the prize pool!)
  • Storytelling Competition (many different Paint Brushes and Petpet Paint Brushes in the prize pool!)
  • Test Your Strength (Halloween Paint Brush)
  • Tarla's Non-Toolbar Treasures (Amongst the prize pool are Speckled Paint Brush, Cloud Paint Brush, Scritchy Sketchy Paint Brush)
  • Wheel of Extravagance (ALL Paint Brushes available!)
  • Wheel of Monotony (Tyrannian Paint Brush and Tyrannian Petpet Paint Brush - if you don't want to wait hours, here is an instant play link but don't like it if you don't want to spin!)
  • Ye Olde Fishing Vortex (ALL Paintbrushes possible!)

The following items can also award Paint Brushes:

  • Discovered Treasure Chest (Pirate Paint Brush, Pirate Petpet Paint Brush and Pirate Draik Eggs)
  • Legendary Chest (Pirate Paint Brush, this item is no-trade and was only available from the mobile game Legends and Letters)

To paint your pet with a Paint Brush, have the brush in your inventory and take the pet you wish to paint as your Active Pet to the Rainbow Pool. Select the pet and the brush and go!

Faerie Quests

If you get a Faerie Quest from the Fountain Faerie, she will allow you to take dip in the Rainbow Fountain and change your pet to almost any other colour (lab exclusive colours cannot be chosen). Faerie Quests are given out as Random Events but to increase your chances of getting a Fountain Faerie Quest, you can buy Faerie Quest Fortune Cookies from the NC Mall Fortune Cookie Store. These grant you a random Faerie Quest each day for 9 days and cost 100nc a cookie normally.

Premium Perk

Note: This is the only way to achieve an Ice Bori! Premium members are granted a Premium Perk once a year in which they can change the species of their pet for free. However, if you wish to change the colour as well you can! What you have to do is this:

  • Make sure your current pet is a colour that is unavailable in the target species' colour range. Paint them with a Brush, Morphing Potion, Transmogrification Potion or Magical Chia Pop if you have to.
  • Select the pet to change species to at and select the new species you want to change to.
  • If you have done stage 1 correctly, you should now be offered the chance to change the colour as well! A drop down will appear offering you every available colour for the new species. Pick the one you want and go ahead with the change. Your pet should change straight away.

The Lab Ray

There is also the Lab Ray, which can randomly change the colour and/or species of your pet if you zap them. You will first have to buy or acquire through Random Events all the Lab Map pieces, and then when you hand them in you will gain access to the Laboratory where you can zap your pets. Normally you get one zap a day, but for 125nc you can buy a Lab Ray Cookie which will grant you extra zaps each day for more chances at changing your pets colour. Remember, changes are random! There are colours which are only available through the Lab Ray (or premium perk) so the Lab Ray is a great resource. However, at the moment you cannot turn in new Lab Maps because the turn in page is broken. The Lab itself is unaffected.

Annual Holiday Dream Pet Giveaway

Once a year in December, TNT offers 40 users the chance to have one of their pets changed to any species and colour of their choice in the Holiday Dream Pet Giveaway. Go to this page to find out the relevent dates and enter the competition to win the chance for your pet to be changed. Any pets changed by the giveaway will be converted even if they were previously unconverted, and any wearabled previously on the pet will be placed back in your closet. You can only enter with one account (it doesn't have to be your main account) so don't try and enter with multiple accounts!

Other Methods

  • Half Eaten Glowing Jelly - Eating a half eaten glowing jelly has a very small chance of turning your pet glowing (though it has a greater chance of making them ill).
  • Flask of Rainbow Fountain Water - using this potion will paint your pet a random colour.
  • Mysterious Swirly Potion - this potion will turn your pet into a random limited edition pet.
  • 8-Bit Power Up Potion - this potion will turn your pet 8-bit if the colour is available for their species.
  • Turnip Tonic - this potion will randomly turn your pet to one of the four basic colours (red, yellow, green or blue).
  • Potato Potion - this potion will turn your pet into a Brown Kiko.
  • Mutox Syrup - this potion will turn your Grundo into a mutant.
  • Rainbow Swirly Potion - this potion will turn your pet into one of the four basic colours (yellow, red, blue or green) and raise their level by two.
  • Vengeful Scroll - reading this Scroll to your pet will turn them into a Pink Lenny (and also count towards the Book Award).
  • Plaid Negg - When fed to your pet, this negg will raise the level of a random ability your pet has or it will change the colour of your pet to a random colour.
  • Kaleideonegg and Vortex Neggs - these Neggs will change the maximum HP of your pet. The Kaleidonegg will also change your pet's colour to one of the four basic colours, and the Vortex Negg may do this instead of changing the maximum HP. There is also a risk of disease.

Have fun painting your pets! :)
