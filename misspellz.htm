Welcome, welcome!

Welcome to Misspellz - an untaken names page with a slight twist. All names found on this page are variants of actual names/words. The site is broken down into four main categories: female RNs, male RNs, themed words and fandoms. Fandoms you say? Why yes! Fandoms often have very unique and peculiar names that lend themselves well to fantasy based names. Besides fandom love is a thing I take part in. x3

You will not find perfect misspells on this site. I'm more interested in the way a name sounds rather than how it's spelled. So misspelled maybe used a little bit loosely. Or maybe a lot loosely.

a friendly note

You do not need to neomail me if you have used a name. All names are linked to their subsequent pet lookups for both of our ease. You can simply click, check, and create! ;D
** If a name is not clickable, then it was recently created and I've been too lazy to archive it yet.
