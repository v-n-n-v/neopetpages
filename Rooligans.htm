Welcome to Rooligans! — Roo Island's Premier Chat Group —

Rooligans is a chatgroup where users come to make friends, chat, and enjoy the overall Neopets experience. If a user is spoiling the experience for other Rooligans, they can expect to be ignored or asked to leave.

The Rooligans are a diverse group with people of all ages and backgrounds, and as we are human beings, there will be disagreements. We ask that you be reasonable, open minded, and willing to give others the benefit of the doubt. If your goal is to instigate arguments, put others down, brag, show off, or pursue some other endeavor that reasonable people would not call friendly fun, Rooligans is not the place for you.

This page has some guidelines for posting on the Rooligans boards. If you are a new member, please read through it and keep it in mind when visiting the boards.

We kindly ask you to remember that the Rooligans are our chat group and not just Roo Island supporters. A Rooligan member does not have to play for Roo Island in the Altador Cup. Our boards last all year round on the site events boards.

Thanks for taking the time to get to know more of what being a Rooligan is all about, and remember, when in doubt, use the golden rule:
Do unto others as you would have them do unto you.


Roospect

Always be respectful and kind to others, including non-Rooligans.

Roocruiting

A bigger team is not necessarily a better team. Rooligans do not actively recruit.

Rools

Always abide by the rules of the Neopets.com website to the best of your ability.

The Altador Cup — What To Do When June Rolls Around Again —

  • Please try to play more than you chat. Try chatting as a break between games. Remember that points do matter in the cup, so try and do your best, instead of scoring one point and waiting for the game to end.
  • Remember that many users chat between goals and games, and it is not necessary to post rude messages demanding that others leave the boards to play.
  • Please don't join Roo Island if you are not going to be playing. There are many ways of supporting us, but not playing will only bring down scores. You don't have to join the team to be a great supporter!
  • If someone from another team comes on the board trying to taunt you, ignore them — they most likely want attention. If it gets really bad, don't be afraid to report them.

Board Creation — What To Do When A Board Is About To Explode —

The Rooligan who pwns (makes the first post on) page 22 creates the next board. Anyone spamming to earn the pwn will be asked to forfeit it.

If a scoreboard is created separately from the main chat board, it should not be labelled with the chat board number.

If the person who pwned the page is unable to make the board, the pwn will be passed along to the next poster.

If all eligible board makers are not present, designate a board maker amongst yourselves.

Always use the proper title format. You can copy & paste the title (make sure to update the number and add a punny title!) and first post below:

Try to have the board ready in a timely fashion. You may want to start preparing it soon after you pwn.

However, please do not create the new board until the old one is near the end of page 25 or has just exploded.
