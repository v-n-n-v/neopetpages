UC Shuffle Info:

How to play UC Shuffle:


Post one or more UCs you'd like to put in the pot.

Other people will bid their UCs on your UC for 30 minutes. You can also receive UC AND converted bids on your UC if you specify it by putting a *quiggle* next to your pet's name during listing. At the end you will have to pick one of the offers you received

-UNLESS-

THIS PART VARIES DEPENDING ON WHAT VERSION YOU ARE PLAYING:

No Pass UC Shuffle: you MUST select an offer that you received.

All other variations:If you do not receive a valid bid according to the variation you are playing, you may pass on all offers. If you recieved a valid bid however, you must select one of your offers (It does not have to be a valid bid that you accept)

After bidding ends, but before you accept an offer, some choose to post a Short List of their favorite bids and ask for advice on which to choose. You may do so to ask for help on trading values. PLEASE NOTE: not everyone will give you unbiased advice as many PCers are friends and may have friends who bid on your pet. So try to ask a trusted friend or get more than just one or two opinions on your shortlist. And remember, this is a gambling game for pet trading, it's not meant to get you uptrades. Don't play unless you accept the chance of a downtrade.

Variations of UC Shuffle:

There are many variations of UC Shuffle! Make sure before you play, you read the first posts to understand the rules of the version you're playing.
Here are some of the most popular versions of UC Shuffles being played:


No Pass --- There are no stipulations, no safety nets. Everyone who lists in this version WILL BE PICKING A BID AT THE END. This variation is higher risk, but usually attract WAY MORE BIDS than any other version.

Free Pass/Pass --- Everyone who plays is allotted a pass on their pet. This variation often needs to have a capped pot, because there will be 20+ people who want to list due to no risk. These are usually more popular during slower trading times.

Same Tier --- If you do not receive a bid that is at least the same tier as your listed pet, you may pass. (Eg. if you list a t5 and only get t4 or lower offers, you may pass)

Half and Half/ H&H --- List 2 pets to put in the pot. If both pets receive bids, you must trade at least one of them. You may pass on the other pet's bids. If you list one pet in this version, you will have no pass.

Color Exclusion --- You may pick 1-2 (depending on the host) color exclusions on your listed pet from your tier. If you list a T5 and choose Faerie as your exclusion, if your only same tier bids are t5 faerie pets, you may pass.

Pet Exclusion --- You may pick 1-2 (depending on the host) specific pet exclusions on your listed pet from your tier. E.g: If you list a T1 and choose Mutant Jetsam as your exclusion, if your only same tier bids are Mutant Jetsams, you may pass.

Goal --- Choose 1-2 goal pets in your tier or below. If either are not bid on your pet, you may pass.

Shuffle Extravaganza --- This is a special event held on the 1st of the month due to everyone having fresh transfers. It is usually hosted by many users throughout the course of the whole day.

Ladder --- Each pet listed may pass, UNLESS they receive a fair ladder bid. A fair ladder bid is: Same tier w/ same name format, or lower tier with a better name format, or higher tier with a worse name format. Extensive rule explanation of this variation can be found HERE


There are other variations and clauses to these such as:
Name swap clause:If the only eligible bid you receive would be a nameswap, you are allowed to pass on this offer.
Spicy Rule: Refers to Half and Half Shuffles. Even if only one of your listed pets receives bids, you still MUST trade that pet.

Other variations pop up from time to time, But these are the most common. When in doubt, READ THE FIRST POSTS!

OTHER HOSTING TIPS
Do not bully or harass newcomers to the game!!! If someone breaks your first posts, if someone misunderstands the rules, or ghosts, or backs out: LET. IT. GO.
This is a FAN MADE game, run by FANS. We cannot enforce any of these made up rules, so let's be civil and douse any torches that come out if you're hosting. Keep it upbeat and positive.

Make sure you're hosting at a busy time on neo! If the boards are dead, then there won't be a lot of good bids and people won't list. If the PC is hopping, this is the perfect time to host!

If you're unsure if there's enough interest, host a UC SHUFFLE INTEREST BOARD first! Just flat out ask the PC "Hey who would like to play UC shuffle, listing or bidding? Let us know what you have so we can see if there's enough interest to play!" This helps listers be much braver to know they have a lot of support and won't regret playing :3

Know the rules well This is just so that if a question comes up, and you have to make a call, you already know what to say. read over the rules page and be a good host! I already believe in you :3
















FAQs

Q: Do I need a pet in the pot to play?
A: No! You do not need a pet in the pot to play this game.


Q:Can I bid a pet I put in the pot on other pets in the pot?
A:No. If you put your pet in the pot, this pet can no longer be bid on other pets in the current pot.


Q:If someone plays and breaks the rules, can you do something about it?
A: Plain and simple, no. The users who choose to volunteer their time to host can't force anyone to trade. However if someone intentionally breaks the rules or ghost you, you can mail the current host and let them know the situation privately. They may choose not to let the person list in games they host anymore, or mail them to ask they stick to bidding. Even though we are all adults left playing this children's site, some people choose to behave like children still so please don't let them ruin your fun by breaking the rules. Just make a mental note and move on :)


Q:Can I host?
A: YES! Anyone and everyone is welcome to host. Just please make sure you have read the rules thoroughly and know what's what! Also make sure you clearly specify what version of UC shuffle you'll be hosting so there's no confusion :3


Q:Can I bid my pet on multiple shuffled pets?
A:Yes you can bid as many of your pets on as many of the auctions as you want. (As long as none of your offers are currently pets in the pot)


Q:What happens when TWO or more shuffled pets pick the same bidded pet?
A: The person who bid the pet must then choose which shuffled pet they want to trade for. The shuffled pet(s) that is not chosen must then pick another bid (or pass if applicable)


Q:If I list and the bid I choose finds out they are out of transfers/Miss-types their bid/for some reason CANNOT trade the pet they bid on my pet/Is chosen for two pets and does not pick mine, DO I NEED TO PICK A NEW BID?
A:YES. YES. YES to all of these questions.
ESPECIALLY IN A NO PASS UC SHUFFLE
If the bid you select for whatever reason does not work out, and you have another eligible bid (And in the case of no pass, any bids whatsoever) YOU MUST SELECT ANOTHER BID. That is the whole point of these games is to make trades. And if this rule wasn't in place, people would abuse it to get out of following the rules and just trading. THIS GAME IS MEANT TO MAKE TRADES. **NOT** UPTRADES!!! Please don't ever list unless you understand that simple fact.
