Orin's Converted Cuties UFA List

Hi, this section is for listing cute converted pets available from various users from around the Pound Chat, to help their pets find new homes. Please take a look if you're interested. And also check some of the other linked UFA sites and dream pet listings if you're looking to adopt or adopt out. ^^

Last Updated: 2022, April 28

Add a Pet

Feel free to neomail me to add/remove/update UFA pets!
Please include the following: Pet(s) name(s)
Whether there is an application deadline or if they are UFQA
Contact account (I can also link to a board if you have a board you will be keeping up with for a few days)
Any requirements or preferences you have for their new home(s)

If I see you advertising around the PC, I may offer to add you here. If I post on your board and offer to add your pet, I'll just use the info you already provided and ask if I need anything else. c:

Removal: Previous/new owner of a pet can contact me at any time to remove a pet. For pets whose deadlines have passed, I'll remove them within a day or two after their deadline. For pets with no deadline: I'll periodically check if the pet is still on the same account they were before, so if you simply move them to another side account or something, please maybe have UFA note on their lookup w/ contact account at least. Thanks! I might also remove UFQA pets after a month if you don't stop by update me that they're still UFA.
