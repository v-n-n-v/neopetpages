How this page works
The purpose of this page is to bypass the need for Flash to order food at Kelp (until TNT converts Kelp to the new layout). Process links will add the food directly to your Eat page. Simply click the item name to order. Selecting another item in the same category will overwrite your previous choice.

** IMPORTANT: You will NOT get a "Something Has Happened!" event for this avatar! **

As soon as you get leftovers, DO NOT leave the leftovers page!
Please check your list of avatars in a separate tab.
You may need to refresh the leftovers page a few times until the avatar shows up!
