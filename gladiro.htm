The Fortunate Ones ミ★

Adoption and Dream Granting Agency. Est 2021


Hello there, and welcome to The Fortunate Ones Adoption and Dream Granting Agency! Here we aim to connect pets with their forever homes and help Neopians to get their dream pets. We offer adoptions, pet zapping, magma dipping, and, on occasion, Rainbow Fountain dips or species changes. Let us know what your dream pet is, and we will help you get it!

WANT TO JOIN US?

We'd love to have you join our group if you would like to help us grant those dream pets! Do you have a lab ray? Do you know your Magma Pool time? Would you like to just help us advertise and bump our boards on the PC? Let us know! If you're interested please send us a neomail. :)

Our History

The Fortunate Ones was started by our founders, Otto and Mia. They put in a ton of time and effort to conceptualize, create, organize, and run the agency to get things off the ground. The name Fortunate Ones was chosen specifically to carry on the legacy of their former long-time running guild, Fortune's Footpath, which they ran many years ago and hold very dear to this day.

Due to real life situations, our well known original page is currently inactive. Our Adoption Agency page is now located at /~FortunateOnes and our Dream Granting page can be found at /~Clurisa. We apologize for any confusion or inconvenience this may cause. However, we are eager as ever to help you with your adoptions and dreams!

Rules and Info

Please take a minute to read through this section

1. Anyone is welcome to list spare Paintbrush clothing for donation.

2. Anyone can make a request for Paintbrush clothing. In order to obtain spare Paintbrush clothing, you MUST own the unclothed pet in the corresponding color & species or have a name of a pet in that color & species that is currently in the pound.

3. Please ensure all NP clothing has been removed BEFORE transferring your unclothed pet. This will ensure no NP clothing you own gets stuck in the donater's closet should the PB clothing share the same Zones.

4. You CAN transfer a pet the same day as long as the person requesting clothes can offer a different Neopet (can adopt one from the pound) for their own pet in a pet-for-pet transfer.

5. We are not listing Lutari, Magma, basic colors (Blue, Green, Red, Yellow), or basic Aisha collars, Elephante hats, and Ixi collars.
