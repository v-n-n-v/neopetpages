    Fun Facts

    TNT first released the transparent color on September 25th, 2010 - starting with transparent skeiths for Skeith Day!

    There are 4 ways to create a transparent pet: by painting your pet with a Transparent Paint Brush, morphing your pet with a Transparent Morphing Potion, zapping your pet Transparent using the lab ray, or painting your pet Transparent by using a Fountain Faerie Quest.

    There are currently only 5 transparent morphing potions available; elephante, korbat, lenny, skeith & wocky.


    The Transparent Paint Brush is currently listed on TP at 5M. Last Checked: July 31st, 2023.
    Before that, my last check on September 19th, 2019 = 3.5M.

    All About Boneyard▼

    bone·yard /ˈbōnˌyärd/ noun informal 1. A cemetery. 2. A place where the bones of wild animals gather.

    Hello and welcome to Boneyard - a Transparent Pet Directory that was created by molarz on November 25th, 2013. Can you believe that we celebrated ten years in November of 2023!? Whether you have a whole family of transparent pets or simply admire from afar, I hope you enjoy your stroll through the Boneyard ♥

    As of January 29th, 2024 » Boneyard finally has at least one pet in every species section! Currently, Boneyard has a total of 580 pets listed with the most popular species being: Aisha, Blumaroo & Draik!

    Before sending in your submission form, please consider the following:
    ▶ 2024 NCUC UPDATE: Pets currently wearing UC skins are not accepted to Boneyard, even if that pet is technically painted transparent. You can submit your transparent pet even if you think you may use a UC skin on them in the future, but they will be removed during my regular clear outs without notice. You can always submit again later!
    ▶ Pets must be painted transparent (with no UC skin) to be added to the Boneyard.
    ▶ Only submit pets you currently own.
    ▶ Pets that are no longer painted transparent, or are now using a UC skin, are removed during my regular clearouts. Pets that are still transparent but have changed species will be moved to the new species.
    ▶ Pets do not need to be customized to be added, but those that are customized with at least a background will be listed first, while uncustomized pets will be added at the bottom.
    ▶ I do not add pets without permission, but if you see your pet was submitted by a previous owner and would like them removed, please neomail me and I will remove them ASAP.
    ▶ Pet Spotlight is chosen mostly by random at least monthly. I don't typically notify owners - so only submit if you are comfortable with your pet being featured! Owner listed will be the account the pet is currently on.
    ▶ Buttons are available but linking back is in no way required or expected :)
