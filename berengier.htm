Rules

Augment is a graphic creation site run by Jewlz. Here, you can pick and choose pieces of coding and graphics to create your own layout. This way you have your own personal style, but yet a graphic designers look to the finished piece. Augment features many things from backgrounds to styling CSS for the smallest of things. To compliment your site, you can even visit Augments family sites to pick up premades, resources, coding and graphic tutorials, or even request a custom graphic. Our family sites are below.

color palettes

You can use these color palettes for layouts. Simply copy and paste into your image editor, ms paint, or an in-browser color eyedropper to get the hex codes ex. #a1a1a1. Some paletees have 7 colors, whole some have 8. You dont need to use all the colors, it just gives a nice variety that go well together.
