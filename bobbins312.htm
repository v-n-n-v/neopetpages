How to become the featured gallery:
In order to be the featured gallery you must be part of the guild Curators of Neopia and get voted as the gallery of the month.

How to be listed as a favorite:
Favorites are picked at my digression. They are galleries that I find unique, original, amazing, intensely full etc. All the galleries here are awesome but the favorites are stand out galleries that I would visit if you are on a time crunch.

Information

Eye Candy is a page that was originally started in 2007. Notomatoforu wanted a place to compile a list of sweet galleries both found and submitted from around Neopia. As an avid gallery collector and lover, galleries are the reason she continues to play Neopets. Eye Candy was resurrected and got an overhaul in 2013 with the primary goal being to share galleries and help keep other like minded lovers motivated as well as inspired and to cultivate a new generation of gallery advocates.
