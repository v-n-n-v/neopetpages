Evil

neopets shade evil
The Jubjub is not menacing. Or rather, you clarify, the Jubjub's stature is not menacing. Standing on flat ground, he can't be any taller than knee-height. Yet, for whatever reason, you're perturbed by the grey Jubjub.

Maybe it's the fact that he doesn't really have a face. Well, he has a mouth, but he lacks eyes. Instead, a gaping hole sits in the middle of his face, oozing some sort of black sludge that looks to be the consistency of a smoothie. It's mesmerizing to watch it ooze, if not a little revolting. You'd like to look away, but there's not much else to see.

Below the floating Jubjub with his surreal black wings,the sludge is pooling on the ground. It takes you a minute to realize that the larger pools are shifting, and from them form nameless monsters. Inky black, dripping like their creator, they shuffle forward with unsteady purpose. Your heart beats faster as you plan your escape route. From above, the Jubjub cackles.

I am Evil, and it's about time you fools learned to respect me.

Profile

Creator of All That is Dark, Evil's mere existence has become something of a legend. From the goopy substance that drips off his face, all manner of dark creature spring forth, their purpose only to bring chaos to the world.

Evil is as vain as he is powerful. He believes that he is the one true ruler of the world and that others should bow to his every command. He delights in watching chaos unfold, and will do what he can to aid it.

Despite what some legends say, Evil can, in fact, speak. His words are garbled by a bubbling gurgle, but he remains fairly easy to understand.

What are the origins of such a vile creature? No one can say for sure. The most prevalent theory insists that he came from the collective evil amongst the hearts of all living beings, while other theories contest that he created evil, and thus existed before anyone else. His true origins remain a mystery.

Short as he may be, Evil's appearance remains unsettling to most. His fur lacks colour, as do his feet. He has no eyes, only a gaping black hole on his face that constantly drips an oily-black sludge. His mouth, jaggedly formed and black as night, changes form constantly. He maintains an almost wraith-like appearance, despite not being a wraith at all.

Seth

Ugh wait, you actually want to hear about this lazy thing? Seth /never/ listens to me, even when I have the best ideas! If only he weren't so much larger than I am...

Name: Sethuzan
Nickname: Seth
Species: Faerie Seti
Age: Immortal
Gender: Genetically none, but often referred to by masculine pronouns
Occupation: Ancient Sakhmetian Deity

Lots of mystery surrounds Sethuzan's origins, his eonic disappearance, and subsequent re-emergence. Several Sakhmetian wall carvings exist of him, depicting scenes of chaos and disaster but also reverence. Modern Sakhmetian culture seems to all but have forgotten about him.

In his normal form, Seth takes on the appearance of the elusive Faerie Seti petpet. He stands at 6 feet tall (about 4 feet at the shoulder), making him not much taller than many neopets.

That being said, it is rumoured that he possesses a larger, deadlier immortal form more closely resembling the wall art of him.

Although once rash and hotheaded, Seth is now lazy to a fault. He avoids as much conflict as possible, and generally ignores the world when he can. This bugs Evil, whom often tries to recruit Seth as a pawn in his schemes of chaos and destruction but ultimately fails.
