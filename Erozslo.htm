WELCOME TO PIXELATED! A directory for your 8-bit wonder.

Hi! My name is Reena and I run this new directory called Pixelated. The story behind the start of this directory is due to my admiration for 8-bit pets. It is also partly due to an insufficient amount of funds, I only managed to get one 8-bit Quiggle to be called my own (I'll try to get another one). I also like to see how other people customize this wonderful pixelated pet. Although the customization is quite limited for NPs, a basic background is sufficed enough for your 8-bit friends to be listed here. According to NeoCodex, this color is to pay homage to sprites of 8-bit video games which is very simplistic-looking, made up of large pixel blocks.
