About The Apptionary

The Apptionary is currently run by Eliza, and has been revamped as a combination application hub and dream pet wishlist as of 15 March 2014.

The original founder of The Apptionary was Claws.

Claws was one of those zap-and-gifters who frequent the PC. Too often, though, she had transferred pets to people who claim that she have their "dream pet", and later she found the pet rotting on some account. She found that frustrating. She used "dreamie lists" to find new owners in the past, and then she thought... how about a dream pet list with some proof that this really is their dream pet? Just a smidgen of dedication.
And so the idea of the Apptionary was birthed.

Eliza had applied for a few pets using pet applications, and knew many people who did, as well. She had always thought that something was wrong with the current system. How could people spend so long working on pet applications, just to throw them out when they typically were not accepted? It seemed to her wrong, unjust. Then she heard about The Apptionary. She loved the idea, and wanted to do whatever she could to help it flourish.

Rules

1. Do not submit anything that isn't yours. You will be blacklisted.

2. To add, remove, replace, or do anything else related to an app, neomail The Apptionary. You MUST neomail from the account that the app is on, OR from an account that specifies that another account belongs to you.

3. When you have successfully adopted a pet, please contact us so that we can remove your app!

4. If you are submitting an application that was previously used for a particular pet (as in, that pet's new owner has already been chosen), you must replace that pet's name in your application. This is to protect that pet's owner from harassment. Nicknames used in the app are okay. ('Wolfy' for xxWolfy621xx)

5. To list an application with Apptionary, you need to save your coding in a location different than the place where you have it on display. A text doc on your computer is a great place. Try to keep it up-to-date. You would be amazed how many people accidentally lose their coding and all their hard work. In addition, it is a lot of work for me to keep adding and removing applications thanks to lost coding. So, SAVE YOUR CODING!

FAQ

Can I submit an incomplete app?
We will accept apps that show that you've made your point about the pet, but may still need touching up; like, you haven't included pics yet, or coding needs to be fixed, etc.

Am I allowed to submit an app that I'm currently using to apply for a specific pet with?
Yes, but do consider if this will impact your chances with that pet. Some fosters may not like that you are / intend on reusing an app.

Still have a question? Neomail The Apptionary.

Why was my app removed?

Your application may be removed for any of the following reasons:

You have broken one of our rules.

Your main account is inactive for over 21 days, and you have not contacted me about a reason for the inactivity.

Your main account is set to stealth, and all your pets are sad.

We have noticed you already own the pet you are applying for.

The link you provided no longer leads to your application.

If you would like your app relisted, simply send in the form again with your updated application.

Adopt Out

To adopt out your pet using The Apptionary, all you need to do is look through the applications and neomail your chosen applicant(s). After you adopt out to one of the applicants, it would be amazing if you could neomail me and let me know, so that I can update this page. Thank you and good luck finding the perfect home!
