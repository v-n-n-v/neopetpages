Welcome to Masquerade! ♥

We are a customization-directory founded in the summer of 2013.

We are dedicated to listing extraordinary customizations we find throughout Neopia and helping fellow fashion-fans to keep up with the newest items and events.

A great customization doesn't need to be NC or expensive at all. It all comes down to the effort you put into it. With enough skill you can turn any cheap item into part of a great theme.
If you need proof, just have a look at our listings! ♥

Also one Aisha is not like the next. Every pet gets their character and individual look from their owner and the story behind them.
We are doing our best to show you that one species of pet can be dressed in an endless number of different, amazing styles.

If you need some inspiration for customizations you're planning, we also make requested customs, check our "Requests" section!

Who can apply?

Applying is easy.
As long as your neopet is customized with at least 4 items of clothing (those CAN include NC, NP, Painbrush Items, Garlands, Foregrounds, Trinkets and Backgrounds), you are free to apply for a listing on our directory.

Should your pet make the website, I will inform you personally via neomail.

If your pet doesn't show up on the website after a week, chances are it wasn't chosen for display.
You can of course require to make sure or ask for customization-tips should you want some. I'd be happy to help you out :)

As always, rating is of course not objective and if you don't make the website, that doesn't mean that your customization is bad or wrong.
Everybody has a different taste when it comes to customizing, so don't be offended. As long as you love your pet the way it is, that's all that counts.

Applying

To apply, simply send me a link to your neopets lookup and include a few lines about the customization.
(For example: What theme were you going for?)
If you want to submit more than one pet, I will just have a look at your lookup and your side-accounts and tell you which ones I will list. That makes it easier for everybody! :)

Please make sure you place our button on your or your pet's lookup before you apply! ♥

Note: Don't worry about re-customizing a pet that I have listed. I use permanent images so you can change whatever you want without consequence.
For more info see the FAQs!

Frequently asked questions

Here's a collection of things you might have asked yourself or asked me, so if you're unsure about something, maybe this will clear things up!

Listing Questions

Q: What do I have to do to get my pet listed?
A: You will find all the info that you need in the "get listed" section!

Q: How do you decide which pets you list?
A: It's simply a question of taste. But I also list many pets that I personally wouldn't customize that way, simply because I think that the outfit is very original or uncommon!

Q: I have applied and still haven't gotten an answer. Why is that?
A: I get a LOT of neomails every day, and don't always have time to reply to all of them. However, if your pet should get listed, you will of course receive a message telling you about it!

Q: Do I have to add a button to my lookup to get listed?
A: If you want your pet to be listed, please, yes, add one of our buttons to your lookup or your pet's lookup.

Q: Why are there so many listings of some species and none of others?
A: It's not because I dislike those pets, it's simply a question of who applies. It seems that some species are much more popular with customizers that others, for example: Everyone seems to love Draiks and I haven't found an item yet, that doesn't look good on an Aisha... But that only means that it's even more important that the owners of all the other gorgeous pets out there apply as well!

Q: Is it a problem if I change my pet's customization? Should I tell you about it?
A: No and no, unless you want to of course. If I list your pet, I use a permanent image, so it will not change if you re-dress your pet. If you want a different or additional customization to be listed, you can of course inform me!

Q: Can I get more than one customization of the same pet listed?
A: Yes! But please send me a link to the actual pet lookup each time, so I know you really customized an actual pet of yours and didn't just use DTI.

Q: What should I neomail you about?
A: Please tell me if you plan on giving your pet away. Everything else is not important unless you wanna talk about it. :)
If you see a link that is no longer working, please tell me about it as well, I'm a little behind on checkups sometimes!

Q: Does my pet need to wear NC items to be listed?
A: Of course not! As long as it's a good customization, every pet is welcome!

Spotlight Questions

Q: What's the point of the spotlight?
A: Mostly it's about fun. If you found this website, that means you probably like customizing and/or looking at other people's creative outfits, so the spotlight gives you the chance to show your skills and vote for your favourites!

Q: What can I win?
A: Eternal glory! Neopets forbids all sorts of contests, meaning that the spotlight is purely for fun and you can only win a winner-button and an entry on the "past winners" page.

Q: Is it free?
A: Of course!

Q: How do I enter?
A: You can find all info on the spotlight page!

Q: Can I enter a pet that is also permanently listed?
A: Yes!

Q: How often can I vote?
A: Once per category! And yes, you can vote for your own entry!

Q: I can't see any contestants or a voting page...?
A: That means it's not yet time to vote. Once all entries have been submitted, the voting page with all the images will appear!

Q: What is Open Neo?
A: An amazing website where you can plan your pet's wardrobe, or any pet's wardrobe really. If you have an account there, you can permanently save those outfits and use them to enter our spotlight!

Q: Can I enter for both categories?
A: You are allowed one entry and one vote per category, so yes!

Q: What's with the berries in the voting form?
A: Well if you'd read the rules, you'd know.

Premade Questions

Q: Can I use your premades for my own pet?
A: Of course, that's the point! Please credit with our outfit-button of you do!

Q: How do I know which items you used?
A: The Open Neo addresses can be found on hover!

Affiliates Questions

Q: What kinds of websites do you take as affiliates?
A: Any kind really, as long as the page is active and you link back!

NC questions

Q: How do I get NC items?
You can buy them at the NC Mall. You will need NC to buy them, those can be bought for real money. You'll find all info at the NC Mall!

Q: The item I want is no longer for sale, what do I do?
A: You can trade NC items with other members by using Gift Boxes! Those can be gained via Gift Capsules or at Target Stores! The kind folks on the NC Board will help you out!



Still unanswered questions? Send me a neomail and I'll answer asap! :)
