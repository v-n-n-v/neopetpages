Hello and welcome to The Zafara Spectrum: a Zafara Directory. Here there's just a sampling of some of the loved Zafaras in Neopia, but who doesn't love seeing a bunch of something they love in the same place?
This place is run by me, Micah (or Bebe or Videl). Please don't hesitate to contact me about getting one of your Zafaras listed here, or updating an already-listed Zafara's information. That's what I'm here for; to show my love for everything Zafara! I hope you like it here.

Andy's owner has such an interesting and vivacious style that I had to choose one of his Zafaras -- and probably more in the future. I see Andy in the BC frequently and what a unique looking guy!
