★ PERSONALITY ★

Evzy likes to joke around and have fun, but it's often at the expense of others. She's not necessarily a mean person - just very snarky and sardonic. She's rather hot-headed (though her anger tends to manifest outwardly in a passive-agressive way), and if you make her angry she'll probably hex you or something...Even if you don't anger her she'd probably still do it just for kicks honestly. Still, for the most part, she's more or less harmless.

She's very flirty, but seldom has any actual interest in the people she flirts with, she just likes having fun and seeing others get flustered. It's to the point where she, on occassion, will get genuinely surprised when others reveal they thought her flirting was serious or that she liked them. In all actuality, she just enjoys teasing others.

She's very lazy and procrastinates heavily on almost everything she does, and then later rushes things, so they don't turn out as good as they could have - she very rarely puts her full effort into anything.
She sells potions and assorted items with magical properties, as well as offering to perform charms or curses for others, and while the quality of her works is not neccessarily bad, it could still be a lot better if she just bothered to put in more effort.

Evzy complains a lot, has little-to-no empathy, is completely full of herself and will almost always put herself before others, but....if someone is willing to put up with her despite all of this, she's not a bad friend.



★ OCCUPATION ★

Evzy owns and runs a magic shop out of her own home. She sells potions and offers to perform enchantments (or curses), as well as selling various ingredients needed for both. She's a fan of the bartering system, and usually instead asks for items instead of money as payment. The item in question can range anywhere from the person's most treasured possession (even if it's not something of monetary value) to something the customer just happens to have on their person that catches her eye. It's more due to her being impulsive and a bit of a hoarder, rather than her being materialistic, though. She thinks money is "boring", and only cares to make enough to get by and to gamble with.

★ ABILITIES ★

Evzy was born a witch with magical abilities, and though the powers she possesses are quite strong, she rarely does anything to full extent of her abilities. Her magic does not require any wand, staff, spell, or incantation of any sort to be performed. She does however use gestures occassionally, such as hand waving or finger snaping, which are more for show than anything else.
Though her magic is powerful, there are rules and exceptions to what she can do, even if she DID give it her 100%. Evzy cannot give life or sentience to anything (including bringing back the dead), though she can animate inanimate objects. She cannot make anyone truly fall in love with another person, though she can cause them to be temporarily infatuated (in which the person will act similarly to one under the influence of a love potion from the Harry Potter series). She cannot grant anyone talents they do not already possess, but she can perform a luck charm or create a luck potion to greatly increase their chance of success in whatever they choose to do (though these potions are tricky to make, and easy to have go awry). Finally, she cannot create new matter from nothing, although she can transfigure items into something else, or teleport an item, as long as she knows the exact location of the item in question.

★ APPEARANCE ★


Evzy is tall (6"1) and curvy (hourglass shaped), with pale skin, and has a long, pointed face with slightly downturned golden almond-shaped eyes. Her hair is a gradient of blue with glittering flecks of gold and white, and it fans out dramatically at around shoulder length (it would go just slightly past shoulders if straightened). She has long, floppy dog-like ears, small wings, and a long tail that are the same color as her hair, and her tail ends in an almost feather-life tuft of fur that fades from deep blue to yellow. For casual attire, she typically wears loose shirts (especially big sweaters) and airy skirts, usually complete with tights or nylons and high heels.
Human au evzy looks more or less the same - without the ears / wings / tail, of course. Her hair is orange with yellowed tips, and her eyes are a medium-light brown.
