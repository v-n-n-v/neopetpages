*80s crime show announcer voice* One's a classically-trained, by the book Neovian constable. The other joined the squad because they wanted to fulfill their fantasies. Together, they fight crime and keep the Enforcer's domain safe.

Art is the picture of a dapper Neovian gentleman. Respectful to all genders, species, and walks of life. Takes breaks in the afternoon for tea and crumpets and works steadfastly until and after them. Is never seen in anything less than a perfectly tailored and crisp suit. Knows how to use a saber, but will always attempt to negotiate beforehand. He was a prime candidate for the Neovian Constable...so how did he end up in some freakish la la land under the iron-fisted rule of an overgrown emo kid with a questionably sane anime fan as his partner?
