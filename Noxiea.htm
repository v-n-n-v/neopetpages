SPELLBOUND

— A DIRECTORY OF MAGIC FOLK IN NEOPIA —

****

— WELCOME —

Welcome to the place where the most magical pets of Neopia gather. Here they shall discuss and practice magic while they show off their brilliantly enchanting customizations!

As the directory grows, I would like to add different categories for pets, either based on their species, magic profession, elemental alignment or mythical species.

SPELLBOUND is a customization directory for the witches, wizards, warlocks, sorcerers, sorceresses, mages and mystics of Neopia.

Additionally, SPELLBOUND is a place where all creatures who harness magic powers are welcome, such as pixies, nymphs, and elves.
